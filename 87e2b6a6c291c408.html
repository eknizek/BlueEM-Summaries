Peru Secures $37.5 Million from Green Climate Fund for Environmental Strategy

22 April 2026 · 06:37 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru has consolidated its environmental strategy against climate change by securing $37.5 million from the Green Climate Fund (GCF), the primary international financing mechanism for climate action. The funds are allocated under the Natural Heritage of Peru Initiative (PdP), which aims to strengthen Peru's capacity to address climate-related challenges. This financing represents a significant commitment to environmental conservation and renewable energy development in the country.
