Elida mining project achieves 28,000+ meters of drilling, emerging as key exploration focus

05 May 2026 · 06:22 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
The Elida mining exploration project, located 86 km north of Lima and operated by Globe Trotters Resources Inc, has completed over 28,000 meters of drilling exploration work. Vice President of South America Exploration Manuel Montoya presented these results at the proEXPLO 2026 event, positioning Elida as a material early-stage mineral discovery play. The extensive drilling campaign suggests solid prospectivity and could attract downstream development capital or strategic partnerships in Peru's copper-gold sector.
