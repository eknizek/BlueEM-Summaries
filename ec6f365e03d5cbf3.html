Mexico's Treasury Department Projects Moderate Economic Growth for 2027

20 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Mexico's Secretaría de Hacienda (Treasury Department) released preliminary economic policy criteria projecting moderate economic growth for the coming years. The ministry forecasts 2026 real annual GDP growth between 1.8% and 2.8%, followed by 2027 growth between 1.9% and 2.9%. These projections suggest economic momentum will remain subdued, reflecting ongoing structural challenges and external headwinds. This guidance sets the baseline for fiscal and monetary policy planning and has implications for investment returns and business cycle expectations in Mexico.
