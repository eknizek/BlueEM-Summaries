Mexico's Economic Recovery to be Moderate in 2027; Finance Ministry Releases Pre-Economic Policy Criteria

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Mexico's Finance Ministry released pre-criteria for 2026–2027 economic policy, projecting real GDP growth of 1.8–2.8% for 2026 and 1.9–2.9% for 2027. The forecasts indicate modest economic recovery following slower growth in prior years. These growth projections will inform government spending and monetary policy decisions. The moderate outlook reflects ongoing challenges to Mexico's economy including external headwinds and structural constraints.
