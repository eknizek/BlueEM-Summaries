Get into cherries and pistachios, raise cattle: provincial producers abandon wine and switch sectors

04 May 2026 · 10:07 PM  ·  La Nación
Facing severe headwinds in Argentina's struggling wine industry, Mendoza producers—particularly smaller operations—are diversifying into alternative agricultural products and sectors. Many are transitioning to livestock farming, higher-margin premium wine varietals, or emerging crops such as pistachio and cherries that are gaining market traction. This sectoral shift reflects both the structural challenges facing traditional viticulture in the region and emerging opportunities in adjacent agricultural value chains that offer better economic prospects for rural producers.
