Energy storage systems are growing 30% in Mexico

Energy storage systems for photovoltaic energy in the residential, commercial, and industrial sectors are growing more than 30 percent in Mexico and Central American countries, according to Chinese company Solis Latam.

Min Ye highlighted that this energy storage market in Mexico is divided into residential, commercial, and industrial segments.
