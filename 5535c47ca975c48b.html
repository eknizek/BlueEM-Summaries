Moody's places ISA under review due to Ecopetrol credit deterioration amid country risk downgrades

26 April 2026 · 10:10 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Moody's has placed Interconexión Eléctrica S.A. (ISA), a major Colombian electricity and infrastructure company, under review for downgrade. The action reflects deteriorating credit metrics at parent company Grupo Ecopetrol, heightened Colombian sovereign risk, and concerns regarding governance, dividend policy, and leverage ratios. ISA's credit quality is critical to Colombian infrastructure financing and has broader implications for investment-grade exposure to the country's energy and utility sectors.
