Ecopetrol Confirms Temporary Departure of Ricardo Roa; Return Scheduled for June 28

07 April 2026 · 01:45 AM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia's state-owned oil company Ecopetrol announced that CEO Ricardo Roa is temporarily leaving his position while facing criminal charges from prosecutors, with return expected June 28, 2025. Juan Carlos Hurtado, executive vice president of Hydrocarbons, assumes the acting presidency. This development affects governance and continuity at one of Colombia's largest state enterprises and a major regional energy producer.
