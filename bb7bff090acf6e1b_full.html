# Raúl Marcenaro Appointed as New General Manager of Retail at Ripley Perú

Ripley Corp announced that, starting April 13, 2026, Raúl Marcenaro Rouillon will assume the position of General Manager of Retail at Ripley Perú, reporting to Alfonso Lobato, Corporate Manager of Retail.

Rouillon has more than 20 years of experience at Adidas, where he served as General Manager in Peru. The company specified that between March 16 and April 13, an induction and handover process will take place.

"From that date, Raúl will assume leadership of Retail in Peru with the support of the Peru Retail Committee. We wish him the greatest success in this new stage, always focused on fulfilling our purpose of improving the quality of life of our customers," Ripley stated in its announcement.

Additionally, the creation of the Management of Transformation and Corporate Development was announced, with special focus on the implementation of Artificial Intelligence. In this context, Rodrigo Guajardo, current General Manager of Retail Peru, will assume this new role working with teams from different countries and business units, reporting to Lázaro Calderón, CEO of Ripley Corp. Furthermore, Guajardo will be part of the Peru Retail Committee.
