New Exchange Wants to Challenge B3 Monopoly: 'Whoever Has One Has None,' Says Base Exchange CEO

30 April 2026 · 09:00 PM  ·  MoneyTimes
After over a decade without competition, Brazil's equity market monopoly held by B3 is set to be challenged by Base Exchange, a new exchange controlled by the Abu Dhabi-based Mubadala Investment Company. The Base Exchange, based in Rio de Janeiro, is in final regulatory approval stages and expects to begin operations in 2027. The CEO invoked the phrase 'whoever has one has none' to characterize the transformative impact of introducing competition. This development is significant for capital markets infrastructure investors and those tracking competitive dynamics in Brazilian financial services.
