Mercado Libre Announces Strategic Investment in Gradient Capital Fund to Accelerate AI Innovation in Latin America

20 April 2026 · 07:04 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Mercado Libre announced a strategic commitment as Limited Partner in Gradient Fund 5, an early-stage venture capital fund based in Silicon Valley that focuses on artificial intelligence innovation. The investment signals Mercado Libre's commitment to backing AI-driven startups and technologies across Latin America. This move allows Mercado Libre to gain exposure to emerging AI innovations while supporting the startup ecosystem in the region where it operates.
