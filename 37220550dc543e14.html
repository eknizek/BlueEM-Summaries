Telcel Stories: AI-Powered Competition for Content Creation

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Telcel, Mexico's major telecommunications operator, announced a content creation competition leveraging artificial intelligence tools. The contest focuses on four thematic pillars: impact with purpose, connected humans, reimagined reality, and envisioning the future. This initiative reflects the telecom sector's broader shift toward digital ecosystem expansion and engagement of creative communities through AI-enabled platforms, positioning Telcel as an innovation-forward brand in Mexico's competitive telecom market.
