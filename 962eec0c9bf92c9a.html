Finep releases additional R$2.1 billion for innovation, reaching record level

06 April 2026 · 07:49 PM  ·  O Globo
Finep, Brazil's innovation financing agency, has released an additional R$2.1 billion in funding, bringing total allocations to a record level. This capital is directed toward innovation and technology development projects across Brazil. The increased funding reflects the government's commitment to supporting R&D and entrepreneurship. For LatAm investors, this signals growing public sector support for innovation ecosystems and potential opportunities in tech-enabled ventures backed by Finep financing.
