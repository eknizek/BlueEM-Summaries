Flora & Fauna opens new store with US$500k investment on Dos de Mayo in San Isidro

24 April 2026 · 12:10 AM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
The Wong family is reopening a retail presence on Avenida Dos de Mayo in San Isidro, Lima, with a new Flora & Fauna store focusing on conscious food retail. The store opening on April 23 represents both a symbolic return to where the family's ancestors started with a small bodega 80 years ago and a strategic expansion move. The US$500k investment reflects the family's continued commitment to retail growth in Peru's upscale market.
