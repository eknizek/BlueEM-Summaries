Flora & Fauna opens new store with US$500k investment on Dos de Mayo in San Isidro

24 April 2026 · 12:10 AM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Flora & Fauna, the conscious-food retail concept of the Wong family, is opening a new store in San Isidro with a US$500,000 investment, located on Avenida Dos de Mayo—the same street where the family started 80 years ago. The April 23 opening marks both a symbolic return to the family's retail origins and a strategic expansion within their growth plan. The investment in conscious/organic food retail reflects shifting consumer preferences in Lima. This represents retail sector expansion by a major Peruvian business family.
