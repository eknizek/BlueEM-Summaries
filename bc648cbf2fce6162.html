Flora & Fauna Opens New Store with US$500k Investment in San Isidro, Lima

24 April 2026 · 12:10 AM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Flora & Fauna, the conscious food retail concept backed by Peru's Wong family, is opening a new store in San Isidro with a US$500,000 investment, scheduled for April 23, 2024. The location holds symbolic significance as it marks the family's return to Dos de Mayo Avenue after 80 years, where their grandparents began with a small bodega. The store opening represents a strategic expansion of the Wong family's retail footprint and reflects confidence in the premium sustainable food segment in Lima. This capital deployment signals continued growth ambitions in Peru's upscale consumer market.
