Gás do Povo: Government sets payment date to 10th of each month

08 May 2026 · 11:59 PM  ·  Infomoney
The Brazilian government has established a standardized payment schedule for the Gás do Povo (People's Gas) program, setting the 10th of each month as the fixed payment date. The new schedule begins this month when credits for May will be released to 2.71 million Brazilian families. The program provides fuel subsidies to low-income households, and the standardized payment schedule aims to improve predictability and financial planning for beneficiaries.
