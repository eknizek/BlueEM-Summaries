Record Trade Deficit in Colombia Triggers Alarm Among Internationally Exposed Companies

10 May 2026 · 11:00 AM  ·  Portfolio - Negocios
Colombia has recorded a trade deficit of USD 16.377 billion, marking the largest deficit in available records dating back to 1980. The widening deficit is triggering concerns among Colombian companies with significant international operations and export exposure.
