Chilean Government Delegation Leads Tech Sector Mission to US with Meetings at Nvidia, Google, Meta, OpenAI

30 April 2026 · 11:50 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Chile's government sent a high-level delegation led by the Foreign Minister and Minister Lincolao to California to conduct strategic meetings with executives from leading technology companies including Nvidia, Google, Meta, OpenAI, Cisco, Salesforce, Grok, and Apple. The mission aims to strengthen ties with global tech leadership, potentially exploring partnerships, investment opportunities, and innovation collaborations. This diplomatic initiative reflects Chile's strategic focus on positioning itself as a regional technology hub and securing relationships with key players in AI, cloud computing, and digital infrastructure.
