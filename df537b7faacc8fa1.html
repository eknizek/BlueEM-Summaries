Government Awards Airport Train Contract to Trusted Outsourcer without Bidding

25 April 2026 · 11:00 AM  ·  Reforma
The Mexican government has directly awarded the operational outsourcing contract for the Suburbano Lechería-AIFA train project to a contractor company without conducting a public bidding process. The company has a history of winning government contracts dating back to the previous administration (4T). This arrangement raises questions about procurement transparency and competitive bidding practices for major infrastructure operations.
