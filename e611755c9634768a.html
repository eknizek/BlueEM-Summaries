Caban Energy Advances Renewable Energy in Colombia with Zero-Capital Investment Model for Enterprises

14 May 2026 · 08:34 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Caban Energy, which has operated in Colombia for over four years, is expanding renewable energy adoption through a capital-light financing model that eliminates upfront investment barriers for corporate clients. The company provides integrated services spanning design, construction, and ongoing operations of renewable energy systems, allowing enterprises to transition to clean energy without initial capital expenditure. This business model addresses a key adoption barrier in Colombia's energy transition by shifting risk and investment to specialized developers.
