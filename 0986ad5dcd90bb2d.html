Global Payments Giant Invests US$100 Million and Opens Brazil's First Non-Asian Factory

06 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Neofeed
Fiserv, the world's largest payment processor, opened its first manufacturing facility outside Asia on May 6 in Betim, Minas Gerais, Brazil, representing a US$100 million investment. The facility has annual production capacity of 100,000 Clover payment terminals, which will serve the Brazilian market with focus on small and medium-sized businesses. This expansion signals Fiserv's confidence in the Brazilian market and its commitment to localized production.
