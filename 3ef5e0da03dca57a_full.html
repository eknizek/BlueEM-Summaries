# MinExpo 2026 in Medellín Projects Over $30 Million in Business Deals and Expands Multisectoral Reach

The fourth edition of MinExpo Colombia is taking place in Medellín with the expectation of driving business worth more than 30 million dollars, bringing together companies, suppliers, and allies from the mining sector at the Plaza Mayor event center.

## Greater Business Participation and Event Growth

The fair, organized by the Colombian Mining Association, is being held at Caja de Madera and Gran Salón at Plaza Mayor over two days, with the participation of 114 brands and strategic allies. For this edition, organizers project the attendance of more than 3,500 visitors, reflecting an increase compared to previous versions.

The event is consolidating itself as a space for articulation between the mining industry and other productive sectors, integrating companies from food, beverages, hospitality, and services. This growth is evident in the increase in exhibitors, the expansion of exhibition areas, and the involvement of new commercial allies.

According to organizers, the fair seeks to respond to sector needs through the supply of business and technological solutions. In this context, interaction between companies allows for identifying business opportunities and strengthening commercial relationships at different levels of the productive chain.

"Mining is a motor of opportunities, as efficient and innovative solutions develop around it," stated Juan Camilo Nariño, president of the ACM. According to the executive, the event facilitates connections between companies interested in participating in the national mining market.

## Business Roundtable and Projection of Commercial Agreements

One of the central components of MinExpo 2026 is the business roundtable, projecting approximately 600 meetings between companies from various sectors and suppliers of goods and services. This figure exceeds the more than 550 business meetings held in the 2025 edition.

The dynamics of these encounters aim to generate commercial agreements and strengthen the value chain of the mining industry. In the previous edition, negotiations resulting from this space reached commitments of more than 30 million dollars, which sets a benchmark for this year's expectations.

Organizers note that the structure of the business roundtable allows optimizing the time of participating companies, facilitating direct contacts with potential clients and strategic allies. This mechanism has become one of the main attractions of the event.

"The mining sector is an ecosystem that drives the economy in territories and at the national level. Connecting with mining is opening the door to new markets and growth opportunities," said Nariño, referring to the economic impact of this type of business encounter.

Beyond the commercial agenda, MinExpo 2026 incorporates interactive spaces designed to bring attendees closer to mining operations. These activities seek to broaden understanding of the sector and encourage participation of companies that traditionally are not part of the industry.

The event also highlights the existence of a network of companies that provide solutions, innovation, and knowledge to the mining sector. This network includes small, medium, and large companies that participate in different links of the productive chain.

According to data presented during the fair, the mining industry in Colombia makes annual purchases close to 14 trillion pesos from companies of different sizes, demonstrating its capacity to generate demand across multiple economic sectors.

"Behind the mining sector exists a network of companies that provide solutions, innovation, and knowledge. MinExpo allows making this ecosystem visible and showing that mining is also a large market for Colombian business talent," indicated Nariño.

The event organization highlights that the expansion of its reach responds to a strategy to position the fair as a meeting point between different productive sectors, beyond mining. In this sense, the inclusion of new industries seeks to diversify business opportunities and strengthen business relationships.
