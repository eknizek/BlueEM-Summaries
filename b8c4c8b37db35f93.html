Superservicios fines Enel Colombia COP 2.847 billion for energy market price distortions

24 April 2026 · 12:38 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia's Superservicios regulatory authority has issued a COP 2.847 billion fine to Enel Colombia for market manipulation in the wholesale electricity market. The regulator determined that Enel submitted artificially high price bids that forced more expensive thermal generation to be dispatched to meet demand. This enforcement action reflects tighter regulatory scrutiny of Colombia's electricity market and highlights compliance risks for generation companies.
