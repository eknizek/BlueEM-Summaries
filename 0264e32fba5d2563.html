Belo raises Series A of $14M and attracts Tether to cap table

09 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Belo, a fintech specializing in international accounts and payment solutions, closed a $14 million Series A funding round that includes participation from Tether, the stablecoin issuer. The capital will fund the company's expansion in Brazil and across Latin America, where it aims to serve businesses and individuals needing cross-border payment capabilities. Tether's investment signals growing institutional interest in fintech solutions for international transfers.
