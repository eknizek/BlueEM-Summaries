Belo Closes Series A of $14M with Tether Investment

06 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Belo, a fintech focused on international accounts and cross-border payments, has secured $14 million in Series A funding with investment from Tether, the stablecoin issuer. The capital will accelerate Belo's expansion across Brazil and other Latin American countries. This funding signals investor confidence in cross-border payment solutions for the region and Tether's strategic interest in payment infrastructure plays.
