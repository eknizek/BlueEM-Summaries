Inpasa Ships 45,000 Tons of DDGS to Turkey

16 April 2026 · 09:14 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
Inpasa, a major Brazilian grain processor and agribusiness company, has completed the shipment of 45,000 tons of DDGS (distillers dried grains with solubles), a protein-rich animal feed byproduct derived from ethanol production. The cargo is destined for Turkey, reflecting strong export demand for Brazilian agricultural commodities and animal feed supplements. This shipment demonstrates the scale of Brazil's agribusiness exports and integration into global supply chains for livestock feed.
