Coteminas' judicial recovery accepted by court after two years of proceedings

05 May 2026 · 11:22 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Coteminas, a major Brazilian textile and home goods manufacturer, has had its judicial recovery plan accepted by the courts following a two-year process. This approval allows the company to proceed with its restructuring plan to address financial difficulties and liabilities. Judicial recovery acceptance is a significant milestone enabling the company to reorganize operations, renegotiate debts, and resume normal commercial activities under court supervision.
