# Startup Summit 2026 Opens Registration with 20% Early-Bird Discount

Florianópolis will once again be the center of attention for founders, investors, accelerators, public authorities, and companies in the innovation ecosystem in August, when it hosts the Startup Summit 2026. This year, the event, organized by Sebrae Nacional in partnership with ACATE (Catarinense Association of Technology), reinforces its international vocation with the creation of the Startup Summit Global, expecting to attract participants from 30 countries.

In 2025, the meeting brought together more than 32,000 people, with 10,000 attending in person, and generated more than R$ 350 million in investment intentions.

The Summit Global will be a dedicated space within the trade fair, focused on the presence of representatives from partner countries, innovation hubs, international organizations, and foreign startups interested in connecting with the Brazilian ecosystem. In 2025, 22 countries were represented, with participants from Germany, Angola, China, the United States, Sweden, Finland, and India, among others.

"The Startup Summit has grown along with Santa Catarina's innovation ecosystem. Our focus is not to expand the audience, but to ensure the quality of connections and deliverables for entrepreneurs," highlighted Renato Campos Carvalho, president of Sebrae/SC's Deliberative Council.

## Startup Summit Week

Another novelty is that the Startup Summit will expand its reach beyond the CentroSul Convention Center and will incorporate into the official programming more than 50 side events independently organized by communities and companies in the innovation ecosystem in 2025.

The Startup Summit Week will feature brunches, networking rounds, meetups, happy hours, technical visits, and other activities between August 19-29, while the Summit takes place on August 26, 27, and 28.

"The proposal is to integrate these initiatives and expand the event's impact across the entire local ecosystem," explains Alexandre Souza, Innovation Manager at Sebrae/SC.

## Confirmed Speakers

In addition to connections and business generation, the list of confirmed speakers includes Alexandre Messina from Lovable, neuroscientist Miguel Nicolelis, near futurist Neil Redding, Rodrigo Akira, Google's Developer Ecosystem Manager in Brazil, Claudia Rosa, CEO of Rede Invest and one of Brazil's largest angel investors, Rafael Kiso, founder of mLabs, Konrad Dantas, founder and CEO of KondZilla, and Deborah Folloni, serial entrepreneur and founder of Epic.new.

## Content Tracks

There will be 17 content tracks dedicated to topics such as artificial intelligence, SaaS and new software models, venture capital and M&A, deeptech, healthtech, energytech, retailtech, team building and startup culture, and internationalization.

**Service:** Startup Summit 2026

**Dates:** August 26, 27, and 28, 2026

**Location:** CentroSul Convention Center – Florianópolis – SC
