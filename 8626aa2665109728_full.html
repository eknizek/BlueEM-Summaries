# Alcoa's Profit Disappoints Despite Aluminum Price Strength

Alcoa Corp.'s stock fell in the afternoon of Thursday (16th) after the aluminum producer reported first quarter earnings and revenue that fell short of expectations, despite higher aluminum prices.

For the first quarter ended March 31st, Alcoa reported adjusted earnings of $1.40 per share on revenue of $3.19 billion, a 5% decline compared to the prior year.
