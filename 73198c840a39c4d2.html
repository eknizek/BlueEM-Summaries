Supersociedades confirms Presto reorganization to address 31 billion peso debt

30 April 2026 · 06:22 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia's Superintendence of Corporations (Supersociedades) has confirmed the reorganization of Presto, a major retail chain, following creditor approval of its restructuring plan. The company obtained backing from 81.39% of creditors to reorganize liabilities totaling approximately 31 billion Colombian pesos while preserving 694 jobs and 180 retail locations. This restructuring represents a critical test of Colombia's insolvency framework and demonstrates the viability of out-of-court debt restructuring for distressed retail firms in the region.
