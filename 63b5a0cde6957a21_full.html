# Yape and BCP Banking App Users Report Access Disruptions

Various users reported that the Yape digital wallet application and the BCP mobile banking app are not available. It should be noted that, for the moment, this is not a massive outage. That is, not all users of the application are being affected.

Users expressed through platforms such as X, Facebook, and Instagram that they are having problems accessing their money and being able to make transfers in the morning hours. At the moment, the cause of the reported problems is unknown and the brand has not yet commented.

It should be noted that this outage comes after maintenance was performed last weekend.

"As allies of our customers, we seek to keep you always informed. We want you to know that on April 19, between 0:00 a.m. and 6:00 a.m., several of our products and/or channels (including Mobile Banking, Yape) could present unavailability and/or intermittencies," BCP communicated.

BCP had scheduled system maintenance that prevented its users from performing some operations normally. However, a day after this maintenance, Yape and the BCP app presented issues.
