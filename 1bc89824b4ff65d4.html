Colombian gym chain Action Black plans 28 new premium fitness centers across 5 countries

04 May 2026 · 11:07 AM  ·  Portfolio - Empresas
Action Black, a Colombian premium fitness chain, plans to open 28 new training centers as part of an international expansion into five countries during 2026. The brand will enter the British market with a London location and the Caribbean with a Santo Domingo center, alongside additional regional expansion. This growth reflects rising demand for premium fitness services among middle and upper-income consumers across Latin America and emerging markets.
