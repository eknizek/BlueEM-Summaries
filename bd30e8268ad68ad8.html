Aegea, Brazil's largest private water utility, released its delayed 2023 annual results on April 11 after a comprehensive accounting restatement affecting revenue recognition methodology and Expected Credit Loss (PECLD) calculations. The restatement resulted in lower reported revenues and significantly higher provisions, reflecting revised accounting standards. The delayed filing and substantial restatements underscore operational and financial reporting challenges at the utility, raising questions about asset quality and accounting practices among investors.
