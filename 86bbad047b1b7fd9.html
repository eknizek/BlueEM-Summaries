Axia (Former Eletrobras) Earns R$3.7 Billion and Prepares Leadership Succession

06 May 2026 · 10:41 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Axia Energia, the privatized successor to Brazil's Eletrobras, announced adjusted net profit of R$3.7 billion for the first quarter of 2026, a significant reversal from an R$80 million loss in the same period of 2025. The company is simultaneously preparing for a leadership transition, with current CEO Ivan Monteiro set to be succeeded. The strong Q1 2026 results demonstrate the profitability of the privatized entity and come amid management changes at the energy company.
