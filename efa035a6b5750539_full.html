# Copasa Receives Court Approval to Proceed with Public Share Offering

Companhia de Saneamento de Minas Gerais (Copasa) announced on Thursday (16th) that the Court of Accounts of the State of Minas Gerais authorized the continuation of "certain stages related to the company's potential subsequent public share offering." The information was contained in a statement to the market.

According to reports in the media, a monitoring session was held on Thursday, which is a type of inspection process conducted by the court. In the document released Thursday, Copasa states that the actual execution of the offering involves providing additional clarifications, which may be requested by control bodies, "obtaining applicable approvals (including corporate and creditor approvals), macroeconomic and market conditions, execution of definitive contracts and compliance with procedures provided for in current regulations."

The final decision still depends on obtaining all necessary approvals from relevant authorities and market conditions.
