Amermin, Ulterra Take Critical Minerals Recycling Partnership Global

20 April 2026 · 11:44 PM  ·  Mining.com
Amermin and Ulterra have expanded their critical minerals recycling partnership to operate on a global scale, with Amermin now processing waste materials from Ulterra's operations across Argentina, Canada, and the United States. This partnership supports the growing focus on mineral recycling as part of supply chain resilience and sustainability strategies. The deal reflects industry momentum toward circular economy practices in critical minerals sourcing, which is essential for battery, renewable energy, and technology applications.
