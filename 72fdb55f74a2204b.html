Dominican Republic Halts GoldQuest Project Following Protests

05 May 2026 · 12:23 PM  ·  Mining.com
The Dominican Republic has halted GoldQuest Mining's Romero gold project following sustained mass protests against the development. Environmental risks, water concerns, and social opposition from affected communities triggered government intervention and project suspension. The decision reflects rising political and regulatory risk for mining in the Caribbean and Latin America, where environmental activism and social stakeholder concerns increasingly influence permitting outcomes.
