# Mexican Peso Falls: Dollar Price on April 28, 2026

The Mexican peso depreciates this Tuesday against the dollar, amid a global advance of the U.S. currency and investor caution ahead of a key week marked by monetary policy decisions from major central banks, including the Federal Reserve (FED).

The Mexican currency is trading at 17.4160 units per dollar, which represents a loss of 0.23% compared to Monday's reference price, reflecting risk aversion in international markets.

## Central Bank Decisions and Geopolitical Tensions

Although analysts forecast that most central banks will keep their interest rates unchanged, market attention is strictly fixed on economic projections. Specifically, there is interest in learning the reading of officials on how tensions and war in the Middle East could impact:

- The control of global inflation

- The pace of global economic growth
