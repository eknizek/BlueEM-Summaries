MBRF Completes Agreement with Saudi Sovereign Fund and Advances Sadia Halal IPO Plan in Riyadh

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
MBRF, a Brazilian halal meat producer and exporter, has finalized a strategic partnership with a Saudi Arabian sovereign wealth fund, valuing the combined business at US$2.07 billion with operations across 14 countries. The agreement establishes a framework for an initial public offering of MBRF's Sadia Halal subsidiary on the Saudi exchange (Tadawul) starting in 2027. This deal reflects growing Saudi appetite for branded food assets and marks a significant capital markets milestone for Brazilian protein exporters in Middle Eastern markets.
