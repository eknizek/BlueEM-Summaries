MBRF Concludes Saudi Sovereign Fund Agreement and Advances Sadia Halal IPO Plan in Riyadh

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
MBRF has finalized a strategic partnership with a Saudi sovereign wealth fund, structuring a halal meat business valued at $2.07 billion with operations across 14 countries. The agreement establishes a framework for the planned IPO of Sadia Halal on the Saudi Arabian stock exchange beginning in 2027, marking a significant milestone in the company's expansion strategy. The partnership leverages Saudi capital and market access while positioning the halal protein sector for growth through international public markets.
