Backus Launches Guide to Promote Responsible Consumption and Partners with Municipalities and Retailers

24 April 2026 · 01:32 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Backus, Peru's major beverage producer, has launched a responsible consumption guide and partnership program designed to engage municipalities and retailers in promoting drinking responsibility. The initiative moves beyond corporate messaging to include training for government officials and direct engagement with bars and stores, with plans to scale the model across additional districts. This reflects evolving consumer and regulatory expectations around responsible marketing in the beverage sector across Latin America.
