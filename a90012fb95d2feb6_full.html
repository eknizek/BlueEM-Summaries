# Grupo Eco Plaza Invests $20 Million in Mixed-Use Project at Santa Anita Oval

Grupo Eco Plaza, a Peruvian corporation dedicated to the development of commercial real estate projects, launched its first mixed-use development project of 2026, featuring housing and commerce, with over 22,000 m².

The project will be strategically located at the Santa Anita Oval, at the intersection of Av. La Molina and Av. Nicolás Ayllón, in the Ate district—one of the areas with the highest commercial and logistics flow in East Lima.

According to Eco Plaza, the project will feature commerce and mixed urban activities, enabling the development of a housing and commerce format that aligns with their value proposition: formalizing retail commerce and bringing housing closer to areas of high economic dynamism.

## Job Creation and Formalization

The Santa Anita project will generate hundreds of jobs during the construction phase and, once operational, will contribute to the corporation's goal of 10,000 formalized commercial jobs that Eco Plaza projects to achieve with its portfolio of 12 projects by 2026.

The development is backed by strategic alliances that the group has been consolidating with international investment funds and financial entities, as part of an expansion plan that contemplates Eco Plaza's entry into Latin American markets in a first phase.
