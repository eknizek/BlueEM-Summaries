Domino's plan to grow market share in Brazil

23 April 2026 · 02:35 PM  ·  Neofeed
Domino's is pursuing an aggressive expansion strategy in Brazil, planning to open 150 new locations within three years to reach 370 total stores. Currently operating 220 units (22 company-owned), the pizza chain is targeting underserved smaller cities with populations up to 150,000. The company plans to sign at least 35 new franchise contracts and open 20 stores in the near term, focusing on geographic diversification beyond major metropolitan areas.
