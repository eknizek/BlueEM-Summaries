Lula announces on TV general framework of 'Novo Desenrola Brasil,' to be launched Monday

30 April 2026 · 11:35 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
President Lula announced on television the broad framework of a new debt relief initiative called 'Novo Desenrola Brasil' (New Unscramble Brazil), with formal launch scheduled for Monday. This program targets consumer debt restructuring and financial inclusion for indebted Brazilian citizens. The initiative reflects government efforts to address household indebtedness and support consumption amid economic challenges. The program has implications for consumer credit markets and household balance sheets.
