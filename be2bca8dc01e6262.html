Government spending explained 46% of Q1 economic growth and 59% of annual GDP advance

15 May 2026 · 08:54 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombian government expenditure accounted for 46% of first-quarter 2026 economic growth and 59% of the annual GDP advance, reflecting unusual reliance on public consumption to maintain growth momentum. Meanwhile, private investment continues declining and structural sectors including construction, agriculture, and mining remain significantly weakened. This growth composition raises sustainability concerns about Colombia's economic expansion and dependency on fiscal stimulus versus organic private-sector activity.
