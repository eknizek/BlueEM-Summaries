# Roadmap to Exit Fossil Fuels Must Include Renewable Energy Demand

As the First Conference on the Transition of Fossil Fuels approaches, taking place from April 24-29 in Santa Marta, Colombia, the international debate tends to focus on the economic and financial incentives that still sustain this economy. Fossil fuels are expensive, polluting, and unpredictable in an increasingly unstable world – it is time to move beyond them. But for the reduction of fossil fuels to succeed, we must greatly accelerate the solution through renewable energy.

The global competition will be over who creates markets and organizes demand to attract investments, technology, and decarbonized industrial production to their territory.
