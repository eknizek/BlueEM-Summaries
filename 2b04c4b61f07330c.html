Regional airline association rejects air navigation rate increases in Argentina

04 May 2026 · 07:55 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
The International Air Transport Association (IATA) has formally rejected Argentina's new air navigation fee structure under President Javier Milei's administration. The government's resolution imposes a 15% increase on international overflight and landing charges, while domestic air navigation fees jump 359%. IATA has called on Argentine authorities to immediately suspend the charges and convene formal consultations with airlines, highlighting the severe impact on airline operations and regional connectivity.
