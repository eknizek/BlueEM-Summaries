Globant and CMPC Launch AI-Based Supply Chain Traceability Solution Compliant with EU Deforestation Regulation

09 April 2026 · 03:36 PM  ·  Company press release
CMPC, a major Chilean forest products company, partnered with software firm Globant to develop an artificial intelligence-powered supply chain traceability solution that meets EU deforestation regulation requirements. The platform enables transparency and compliance across CMPC's forestry and pulp operations. This initiative demonstrates CMPC's strategic response to tightening environmental regulations and its positioning as a sustainable forestry operator in global markets.
