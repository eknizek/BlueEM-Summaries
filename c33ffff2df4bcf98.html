Astor Raises $5M Seed Led by Monashees to Build AI Investment Advisor

23 April 2026 · 04:00 PM  ·  PR Newswire
Astor, a Brazilian fintech startup, has raised $5 million in seed funding led by prominent VC firm Monashees to develop an AI-powered investment advisor platform democratizing wealth management for retail investors. The round demonstrates strong investor confidence in fintech innovation in Brazil's wealth management sector. Monashees' participation underscores Latin American venture capital appetite for technology-driven financial services disruption.
