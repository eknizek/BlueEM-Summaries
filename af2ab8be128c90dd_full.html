# Customs Authorities Agree to Improve Operations at Mexico City's AIFA Airport

Customs authorities agreed to implement a new operational strategy at Felipe Ángeles International Airport (AIFA).

The director of AIFA, Isidoro Pastor Román, detailed that between 2023 and March 2026, customs generated cumulative revenue exceeding 241 billion pesos.
