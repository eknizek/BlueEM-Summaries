Banobras Ready to Expand Support to Pemex if Sheinbaum Requests

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Jorge Mendoza, head of Mexico's development bank Banobras, has indicated readiness to expand financial support to Pemex, the state-owned oil company, should President Claudia Sheinbaum request additional assistance. Mendoza stated that Pemex currently has adequate budget for its financing and investment needs. This statement reflects ongoing government support mechanisms for the oil sector and potential future capital allocation decisions affecting Mexico's energy infrastructure.
