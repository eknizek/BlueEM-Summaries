# Dollar Price Today Mexico. Exchange Rate Banks April 17, 2026

The dollar price today starts Friday's trading session at 17.1590 Mexican pesos. This represents a 0.50% advance for the national currency compared to Thursday's reference price, strengthening the exchange rate at week's close. With this movement, the Mexican peso is on track to end the week with accumulated gains, adding up to a 0.75% return so far.

## Why Did the Dollar Fall Today?

The main driver behind the appreciation of the Mexican peso is optimism from international investors about an imminent end to the war in the Middle East. The President of the United States, Donald Trump, expressed confidence that an agreement to end the conflict could soon be reached. He stated that while a two-week ceasefire extension with Iran is possible, it may not be necessary. Added to this, Tehran announced that the passage of commercial vessels through the Strait of Hormuz will remain open, easing commercial tension.

"The market has moved from 'hope' mode where an agreement is reached, to 'euphoria' mode. We'll see if sentiment holds throughout the session," wrote analyst Jacobo Rodríguez in a report for Roga Capital clients.

The good global sentiment is also reflected in a solid opening of stock exchanges. In contrast, the U.S. dollar—typically sought as a global safe-haven asset—weakens against other currencies, while oil prices register declines.
