Subsea7 Awarded Offshore Contract for Sépia 2 in Brazil

09 April 2026 · 07:16 PM  ·  ml-eu.globenewswire.com
Subsea7, a major offshore services provider, has been awarded a contract for subsea infrastructure work on Petrobras' Sépia 2 project in Brazil's pre-salt region. Sépia 2 represents incremental production capacity from already-producing pre-salt assets, a lower-risk expansion than greenfield deepwater projects. This contract award validates continued capex deployment by Petrobras on high-margin pre-salt developments. For energy investors, contract awards signal project momentum and Petrobras' commitment to pre-sal monetization, supporting long-term oil and gas revenue.
