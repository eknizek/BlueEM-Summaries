Government Renews Concessions for 14 Distributors; Enel Left Out

06 April 2026 · 04:08 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Brazil's Energy Minister Alexandre Silveira approved 30-year concession contract renewals for 14 electricity distributors, including Light and companies from CPFL, Equatorial, Neoenergia, and EDP groups, representing a major regulatory decision long-awaited by investors. However, Enel was notably excluded from the renewals, signaling potential regulatory or operational concerns. This decision is critical for LatAm infrastructure investors as it provides long-term revenue certainty for major power distribution assets and clarifies the regulatory landscape for utility investments in Brazil.
