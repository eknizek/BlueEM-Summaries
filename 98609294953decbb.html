Mexico City, Coahuila, and Tabasco Lead Unemployment During March

04 May 2026 · 09:53 PM  ·  El Economista
Mexico's unemployment data for March shows elevated joblessness in Mexico City, Coahuila, and Tabasco. Querétaro recorded the largest monthly increase in the unemployment rate, rising from 1.4% to 2.3% of the economically active population. These regional labor market deteriorations may reflect seasonal factors or broader economic softness. The variation across states highlights uneven employment conditions across Mexico's geography, which has implications for consumer spending, wage growth, and monetary policy considerations.
