Record exports propel first-quarter trade surplus to fivefold increase

20 April 2026 · 08:54 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina achieved a record commercial surplus of US$5.5 billion in the first quarter of the year, representing a fivefold increase compared to the prior year. March exports alone reached US$8.645 billion, representing a 30% jump. The strong export performance, driven by agricultural and commodity sales, underscores recovery in Argentina's trade balance as global demand for key export products remains robust.
