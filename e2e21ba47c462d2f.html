Colombia's civil society, industry associations, economists, and experts have collectively voiced support for the independence and autonomy of the central bank (Banco de la República), opposing what they characterize as politically-motivated government interference. The statement emphasizes the critical importance of central bank independence for monetary policy credibility, inflation control, and macroeconomic stability. This is a material development for investors, as threats to central bank autonomy directly affect currency stability, interest rate expectations, and credit market conditions—key risk factors for all LatAm portfolios with Colombian exposure.
