Chile's Fiscal Council Issues Recommendations to Refine Public Spending Rule

29 April 2026 · 09:30 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Chile's Fiscal Council has released recommendations to improve the structural budget rule governing public spending, focusing on refining the cyclical adjustment of fiscal revenues based on economic activity levels. These technical recommendations aim to enhance the framework's accuracy and effectiveness in managing government finances across economic cycles. The proposals are relevant to macroeconomic stability, fiscal sustainability, and the investment outlook for Chilean sovereign debt and equities.
