# Use of FGTS in Desenrola Program Will Impact Job Creation and Housing Delivery, Says Abrainc

The use of FGTS resources for the Desenrola 2 program should generate a direct impact on the economy and cost up to 107,000 direct jobs and 46,000 housing units, according to an assessment by the Brazilian Association of Real Estate Developers (Abrainc).

The entity expressed concern about the government's proposal and said that it only addresses the symptom of default.

By Rita Azevedo, Valor — São Paulo
