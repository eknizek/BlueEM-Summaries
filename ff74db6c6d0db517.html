Eavor Wins Overall CleanTech Innovation of the Year in 2026 CleanTech Breakthrough Awards Program

09 April 2026 · 02:08 PM  ·  GlobeNewswire
Eavor, backed by Singapore sovereign wealth fund Temasek, has won the Overall CleanTech Innovation of the Year award for its proprietary closed-loop geothermal technology platform. The award recognizes Eavor's approach to deploying geothermal energy without the resource depletion risks of conventional systems. For Latin American investors, geothermal represents a material opportunity in energy transition, particularly in countries with volcanic geology (Chile, Peru, Mexico). Temasek's backing and third-party validation strengthen the commercial case for geothermal expansion in LatAm energy portfolios.
