Brazil sets April export record boosted by rising oil prices

08 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Brazil achieved record export volumes in April, with growth significantly fueled by higher crude oil prices resulting from geopolitical tensions in the Iran region. As Latin America's largest oil producer, Brazil has benefited from the price rally in global energy markets. These strong export results support Brazilian government revenues, currency strength, and corporate profitability in the energy and commodities sectors, with positive implications for the country's trade balance and macroeconomic outlook.
