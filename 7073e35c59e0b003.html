AI, data centers and energy: why batteries become the critical link in Latin America

02 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
As demand for reliable, uninterrupted power surges from AI infrastructure and data centers, battery storage and energy transition solutions have become critical investment focuses across Latin America. Investors prioritize jurisdictions offering macroeconomic stability, renewable energy capacity, and grid reliability to support the region's digital transformation. Interviews with renewable energy executives highlight battery technology and storage-as-a-service as key wealth creation opportunities in the region.
