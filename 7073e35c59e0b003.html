AI, data centers and energy: why batteries become the key link in Latin America

06 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Battery technology is becoming essential infrastructure in Latin America to support artificial intelligence, data center growth, and renewable energy integration. Investors seek macroeconomic stability and reliable renewable power supplies without interruptions for these computationally intensive operations. Atlas Renewable and other industry executives highlight how energy storage solutions enable grid reliability while supporting the region's digital transformation. The convergence of AI demand, data center expansion, and renewable energy targets creates significant investment opportunities in battery technology and energy infrastructure.
