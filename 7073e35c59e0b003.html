AI, data centers, and energy: why batteries become the critical link in Latin America

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Latin American investors increasingly view battery storage as essential infrastructure to support AI and data center development, as energy reliability and access to renewable power become competitive requirements. Energy developers and technology companies seek macroeconomic stability and renewable capacity without supply interruptions, driving demand for battery systems to smooth intermittent renewable generation. An Atlas Renewable executive highlights how battery technology addresses LatAm's dual challenge of rapid data center deployment and renewable energy integration, positioning energy storage as a critical sector for private capital investment.
