Foreign capital inflow to Brazilian stock exchange loses momentum in April but remains positive

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Foreign institutional investors continue allocating capital to Brazilian equities in April, but the pace of inflows has decelerated compared to earlier months. The slowdown reflects global portfolio rebalancing and changing monetary policy expectations, though the overall flow remains positive. The reduction in foreign participation could indicate caution about Brazil's macro environment, including inflation and interest rate concerns.
