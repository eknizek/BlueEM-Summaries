GPA brings retail trio to leadership to turnaround operations amid debt restructuring

16 April 2026 · 09:12 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazilian retailer GPA announced the arrival of three new executives to its leadership team on Thursday, April 16, including José Rafael Vasquez, former CEO of Sam's Club, as part of operational restructuring efforts. The management reshuffle occurs as GPA simultaneously negotiates with creditors holding R$4.5 billion in debt to finalize financial restructuring terms. The dual-track approach targets both operational recovery and debt resolution to stabilize the struggling retail operation.
