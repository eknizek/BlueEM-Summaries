Carretera Central in Critical Condition: Ositrán Identifies 21 Structurally Compromised Bridges

20 April 2026 · 07:48 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Peru's infrastructure regulator Ositrán has issued a critical alert regarding the condition of 21 bridges along the Carretera Central (IIRSA Centro – Tramo 2 concession). Increased heavy-vehicle traffic is creating significant structural stress on these bridges, raising safety and maintenance concerns. This highlights infrastructure asset deterioration risks and potential liability for concessionaires, requiring urgent capital expenditure and operational intervention to maintain network integrity and concession compliance.
