Judge Authorizes 12% Tax Collection on Petroleum Exports

17 April 2026 · 11:54 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's Regional Federal Court (2nd Region) in Rio de Janeiro has overturned a preliminary injunction and authorized the 12% export tax on crude petroleum oil, ruling in favor of an appeal filed by the Office of the Attorney General of the Union (AGU). This decision removes a previous legal obstacle to implementing the export tax on crude oil, directly affecting Petrobras and oil exporters' cash flows and competitiveness. The ruling clarifies the government's authority to collect the tax on petroleum exports.
