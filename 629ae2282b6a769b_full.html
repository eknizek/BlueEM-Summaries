# Weight-loss pens come under scrutiny from Brazilian Federal Police and Anvisa

Anvisa and the Federal Police will announce tomorrow a cooperation agreement to intensify the analysis of weight-loss pens seized in inspection operations. The reason is the exponential growth in consumption of irregular products. In recent weeks, numerous seizures of weight-loss pens have been made in inadequate storage and transport conditions — even hidden inside car tires, for example. Initial analyses of these devices have already identified products with no active substance at all. The risks related to Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) used in the manufacture of these products will also be evaluated, generally imported irregularly or disguised as other substances to circumvent health inspection.
