# WTI crude falls $20 following Iran ceasefire announcement

The price of the United States' West Texas Intermediate (WTI) crude oil fell more than $20 per barrel in early Wednesday trading, after President Donald Trump announced that he had agreed to a ceasefire with Iran.

New York, United States (April 7, 2026) — 5:38 p.m.

The price of West Texas Intermediate crude was falling more than $20 per barrel in the first operations of Wednesday.
