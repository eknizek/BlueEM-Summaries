29 April 2026 · 06:42 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Brazil has formed a commission to deliberate on ending the controversial 6x1 work schedule (six days work, one day rest), which will include members aligned with the government as well as authors of related constitutional amendment proposals (PECs). This reflects ongoing political debate over labor reform in Brazil, with potential implications for labor costs and worker productivity across the economy.
