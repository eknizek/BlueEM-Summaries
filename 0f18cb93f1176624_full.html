# Parque Arauco Posts Strong Q1 Results with 55% Net Income Growth

The shopping mall chain Parque Arauco reported its financial results for the first quarter of 2026, consolidating a positive growth trend in its main indicators. Thus, as of March this year, the company's revenues increased 20.2%, totaling US$ 112.3 million, compared to the same period the previous year.

In that regard, the company's Ebitda grew 20.5% to US$ 79.7 million, reflecting operational solidity across its three markets of operation: Chile increased by 20.9%, while Peru grew by 20.4% and Colombia by 19.6%.

All of this resulted in "significant growth of 67.7% in profit attributable to controlling interest," equivalent to approximately US$ 35 million, according to the reasoned analysis. Total profit rose 55.1% compared to the same period in 2025, exceeding US$ 46 million.

"The results of this first quarter reaffirm the solidity of our strategy and our capacity to capture value through efficient operations and the consolidation of strategic assets. Growth was driven by the strong performance of our shopping centers, high occupancy reaching 95.4%, and the successful integration of our new assets in Chile and Peru—Parque Arauco Oriente, Minka, and Parque La Molina," explained Parque Arauco's Corporate Manager of Administration and Finance, Francisco Moyano.

The beginning of the year was marked by relevant milestones such as the approval of a capital increase of approximately US$ 330 million, which brings the company's investment plan to US$ 1.033 billion through 2033, aimed at strengthening the company's future growth and consolidating its leadership in the region's real estate sector.

The company explained that it will continue investing in the expansion of its main assets, the development and acquisition of new shopping centers, and the strengthening of its presence in the multifamily market in Chile, Peru, and Colombia.

Moyano detailed that "of the total investment plan, US$ 277 million correspond to six new projects that strengthen our regional presence."

In Chile, the firm announced the recent acquisition of Arauco Chicureo (US$ 106 million) and a multifamily building in the San Joaquín municipality (US$ 26 million). Additionally, expansion work began on Arauco Premium Outlet Coquimbo (US$ 17 million) and Arauco Quilicura (US$ 13 million).

In Peru, "the mixed-use strategy is strengthened with the development of the multifamily project at the MegaPlaza Independencia shopping center (US$ 34 million) in Lima, and in Colombia, the expansion of Parque La Colina shopping center (US$ 81 million), which is in full development," said the executive.
