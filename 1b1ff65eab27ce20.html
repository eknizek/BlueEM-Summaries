Boticário Closes In on Natura, Narrowing Market Share Gap to Historic Low

14 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Grupo Boticário has significantly closed the competitive gap with Natura & Co, Brazil's largest beauty and personal care companies. According to Euromonitor data, Grupo Boticário achieved a 15.5% market share in the previous year, reaching the smallest distance from Natura in its history—just 0.3 percentage points. According to Nielsen data cited by the company, Grupo Boticário claims to have already surpassed Natura for market leadership. This reflects intensifying competition in the Brazilian beauty and personal care market and represents a major milestone for the Boticário brand in challenging Natura's market dominance.
