# WTI and Brent Crude Prices Fall Nearly 13% in the Week

Crude oil prices remain more than 30% above pre-conflict levels.

This Friday, oil fluctuated between gains and losses as markets awaited talks between Iran and the United States, which will determine the future of a fragile ceasefire.

Crude oil is on track for its largest weekly decline in 10 months. Crude has cut gains and closed below $100. Brent could reach $100 if the Strait of Hormuz remains closed for another month. WTI crude recovered above $100. Oil futures rose 3.10% in the Asian market. Crude prices fell to $94.
