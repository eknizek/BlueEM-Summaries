Mexico Would Lose 220 Billion Pesos in Tax Revenue if Strait of Hormuz Blockade Extends Through 2026

14 April 2026 · 10:00 PM

If the conflict over the Strait of Hormuz exceeds six months and persists through the rest of 2026, the federal government would stop receiving more than 220 billion pesos from the subsidy for the Special Tax on Production and Services (IEPS), estimated the Mexican Institute of Finance Executives (IMEF).
