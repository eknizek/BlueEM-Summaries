AXIA Sells Assets to Colombian Partner in Another 'In-House' M&A Deal

04 May 2026 · 09:03 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
AXIA Energia has agreed to sell its 49% minority stakes in four Brazilian transmission companies to Grupo Energía Bogotá (GEB) for R$451.5 million. GEB was already a 51% partner in these assets, making this transaction a continuation of their strategic relationship. The deal demonstrates Colombian utility company appetite for growth in Brazil's infrastructure sector and reflects AXIA's portfolio optimization strategy. This M&A activity underscores cross-border Latin American investment in critical energy infrastructure.
