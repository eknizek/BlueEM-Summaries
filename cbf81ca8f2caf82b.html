AXIA Sells Assets to Colombian Partner in Another In-House M&A

04 May 2026 · 09:03 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
AXIA Energia has announced an agreement to divest its 49% minority stakes in four Brazilian transmission companies to Grupo Energía Bogotá (GEB) for R$451.5 million. The transaction consolidates GEB's position as majority owner with 51% in these assets, demonstrating the Colombian utility group's appetite for expanding its Brazilian operations. This in-house consolidation deal reflects strategic rationalization within the partnership while highlighting growing Colombian investment interest in Brazil's energy infrastructure. The transaction underscores the value foreign investors place on Brazil's regulated transmission assets.
