The Consequences of Urban Center Depopulation

24 April 2026 · 09:30 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazilian city centers have historically concentrated services, commerce, and housing, but since the 1950s many have suffered physical degradation and significant population loss. Strategic activities that once defined these urban cores have shifted to other areas of cities due to a combination of factors. This phenomenon has broad implications for real estate values, urban development patterns, and the viability of commercial districts across Brazil's major metropolitan areas.
