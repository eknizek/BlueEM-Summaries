Vinci Targets R$1.2 Billion in Fifth Impact Fund

20 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Vinci, a Brazilian impact investment manager, is raising R$1.2 billion for its fifth fund targeting healthcare, retail, B2B services, and agribusiness sectors. The fund employs a minority-stake strategy designed to provide liquidity and exits for founders while maintaining operational control with management teams. This structure balances growth capital deployment with founder retention and alignment in target portfolio companies.
