Oil Market Gripped by Desperate Rush for Barrels

11 April 2026 · 06:39 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Global oil markets are under acute supply pressure with traders and refineries globally competing intensely for immediately available barrels, reflecting tight market fundamentals. This supply-demand imbalance supports crude prices and benefits oil-exporting LatAm economies (Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Mexico). For LatAm energy companies, commodity prices remain a critical driver of cash flow, capex, and shareholder returns; tighter global oil markets could extend the favorable pricing environment benefiting regional upstream operators and fiscal revenues.
