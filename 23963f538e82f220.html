Melisa Acquisition Targets Prepaid Telecom Market Following Movistar Acquisition

20 April 2026 · 08:18 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Following its acquisition of Movistar, Melisa Acquisition is positioning itself to capture significant growth in Mexico's prepaid mobile segment, targeting the addition of 40 million new users. The strategy reflects growing demand in Mexico's price-sensitive prepaid market where millions of consumers lack access to traditional postpaid services. This acquisition marks a major consolidation in Mexico's telecom sector, with implications for competitive dynamics and pricing in both prepaid and postpaid segments.
