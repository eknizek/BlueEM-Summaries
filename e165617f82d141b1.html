Colombia's SIC launches investigation into D1 for payment failures, defective products, and electronic channel irregularities following thousands of consumer complaints

09 April 2026 · 12:58 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia's Superintendencia de Industria y Comercio (SIC) has opened a formal investigation into D1, a major discount retail chain, over allegations of systematic consumer rights violations including payment processing failures, defective inventory, and irregular e-commerce practices based on thousands of customer complaints. This regulatory action highlights operational and compliance weaknesses at D1 and may trigger reputational and financial penalties, affecting shareholder value and market positioning. For investors in Colombian retail and consumer sectors, the investigation signals potential governance and operational risks among major discount chains and underscores regulatory scrutiny of e-commerce compliance standards.
