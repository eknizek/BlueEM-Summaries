Vorcaro already used financial carousel scheme when buying bank that became Master, shows Central Bank

18 April 2026 · 03:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazilian Central Bank documents show that Daniel Vorcaro attempted to purchase Banco Máxima—the institution that later became Master Bank—using an embryonic version of the financial carousel scheme currently under regulatory investigation. The carousel scheme involves circular movement of resources between companies controlled by the same owner without genuine external capital infusion. The Central Bank proposed blocking the transaction in February 2019 based on concerns about this irregular financing structure, suggesting the problematic practices predate the current scandal.
