Vorcaro Used Financial Carousel Scheme When Buying Bank That Became Master, Central Bank Shows

18 April 2026 · 03:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Central Bank documents show that Daniel Vorcaro attempted to use an early version of the financial carousel scheme currently under investigation when acquiring Banco Máxima, the institution that became Banco Master. The carousel scheme involves circular movement of funds between entities controlled by the same owner without genuine external capital injection, effectively recycling money to simulate capitalization. The Central Bank had proposed vetoing the 2019 transaction based on these concerns.
