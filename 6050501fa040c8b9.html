Why Stablecoins Are Crypto's Real Payments Test

10 May 2026 · 12:04 PM  ·  Bloomberg - Tech
Stablecoins are emerging as a practical payments solution for cross-border remittances, with Filipino workers abroad sending $35 billion annually and increasingly using blockchain-based transfers instead of traditional channels. The technology promises faster settlement and lower costs than conventional remittance corridors, presenting significant opportunity despite current market penetration remaining minimal. With regulatory frameworks like the GENIUS Act taking effect and traditional banks entering the space, stablecoins are becoming a focal point in the global payments industry transformation.
