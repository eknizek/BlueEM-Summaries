# Callao Port: Ositrán Advances Tariff Revision for North Pier

The Public Infrastructure Investment Supervisory Body for Transport (Ositrán) presented, in a public hearing, the proposal for the Third Official Tariff Revision of the Productivity Factor for the Multipurpose North Terminal of Callao. In the session, which included participation from various users and agents of the port sector, the technical criteria were presented that will govern the update of the terminal's maximum tariffs for the period 2026-2031.

During the hearing, representatives of the concession holder, as well as from companies Petroperú, Terpel Comercial del Perú S.R.L., Blending S.A.C., and Terminales del Perú presented their perspectives on the impact of the proposal on their operations. Participants analyzed the Regulator's proposal, which establishes a Productivity Factor of -2.26%, based on the absence of effective competitive conditions for the regulated services of the terminal, managed by APM Terminals Callao S.A.

After the presentations, Ositrán requested users to submit their technical proposals in writing. In this regard, it was emphasized that the study of these contributions is fundamental to democratizing the decision-making process and ensuring that the final result is balanced, transparent, and responds to the operational reality of all actors in the national port system.

Similarly, the public and institutions were reminded that the period for receiving written comments will remain open until April 28, 2026. Documents can be submitted through the Virtual Information Desk or via email at info@ositran.gob.pe. This mechanism ensures that the procedure has the technical support of those interested, under the principles of integrity and commitment to the user that guide institutional management.

After evaluating the comments received, Ositrán will prepare the Final Tariff Report in June 2026. The new Productivity Factor will enter into force on July 1, 2026, and will govern the update of the terminal's maximum tariffs until June 30, 2031.
