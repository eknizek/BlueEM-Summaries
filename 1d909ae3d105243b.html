04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Analysis by Cinthia de Lamare of Cescon Barrieu examines whether Brazilian corporate restructurings are moving away from court-supervised insolvency toward extrajudicial agreements. Recent examples including Raízen and GPA have pursued out-of-court settlements with creditors rather than formal judicial processes. This trend could reshape how distressed companies manage debt workouts and stakeholder negotiations in Brazil.
