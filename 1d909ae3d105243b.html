Is the future of restructurings in extrajudicial recovery?

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Leading Brazilian groups such as Raízen and GPA are increasingly turning to extrajudicial recovery mechanisms rather than traditional judicial restructuring. Legal analyst Cinthia de Lamare of Cescon Barrieu examines this emerging trend, suggesting a structural shift in how major corporates handle debt and operational challenges. The shift reflects potential advantages in speed, flexibility, and cost relative to formal court-supervised processes.
