Colpensiones backs decree ordering fund transfers: resources belong to transferred affiliates

23 April 2026 · 11:17 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colpensiones president Jaime Dussán affirmed the state pension fund's support for a government decree ordering the transfer of pension resources from private funds, arguing the measure addresses regulatory gaps while the Constitutional Court reviews the pension reform. The move affects billions in assets and millions of pension fund members. This escalates the conflict between public and private pension managers over control of Colombia's pension system.
