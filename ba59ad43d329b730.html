Frisby Spain announces legal victory against Frisby Colombia over trademark dispute

23 April 2026 · 01:27 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Frisby España announced a favorable legal ruling in its ongoing trademark battle with Colombian company Frisby Colombia, according to reports from the Spanish firm. The ruling reportedly involves the cancellation of Frisby Colombia's trademark registrations in Europe, representing a significant legal blow to the Colombian brand. The Colombian company has not yet responded to the announcement. This dispute reflects ongoing competitive tensions between the two regional fast-food brands operating under similar identities.
