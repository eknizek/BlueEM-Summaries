BTG Projects First-Quarter Results Deterioration for Nearly Half of Companies Under Its Coverage Due to High Interest Rates

20 April 2026 · 07:48 PM  ·  Neofeed
BTG Pactual projects that nearly half of the Brazilian publicly-traded companies it covers will report worse results in the first quarter of 2026, attributing the decline to persistently high interest rates and increased corporate debt levels versus the prior year. The earnings season begins with Usiminas on April 24, followed by major names including Santander, Suzano, and WEG. Higher debt burdens combined with elevated borrowing costs are expected to pressure profitability across the market, affecting both financial and industrial sectors.
