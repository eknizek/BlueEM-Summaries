BTG Projects Q1 Earnings Deterioration for Nearly Half of Covered Listed Companies

20 April 2026 · 07:48 PM  ·  Neofeed
Investment bank BTG projects that nearly half of the publicly listed companies under its research coverage will report worse results in Q1 2026, with elevated interest rates and high debt levels cited as primary headwinds. The earnings season begins this week with Usiminas and continues with major names including Santander, Suzano, and WEG. The forecast reflects concerns about corporate profitability in Brazil's high-rate environment and rising debt burdens among listed enterprises.
