Customs authorities agree to improve operations at Mexico City's AIFA airport

13 April 2026 · 08:46 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Customs agencies and authorities at Mexico City's Aeropuerto Internacional Felipe Ángeles (AIFA) agreed to implement new operational strategies designed to improve processing efficiency and reduce bottlenecks. The initiative focuses on streamlining customs procedures for both passenger and cargo operations. Improved customs functionality at Mexico's second major international airport could reduce logistics costs and improve supply chain reliability for importers and exporters.
