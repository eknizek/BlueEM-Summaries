Customs Authorities and AIFA Agree to Improve Operations at Mexico City Airport

13 April 2026 · 08:46 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Customs agencies and Mexico City International Airport (AIFA) authorities agreed to implement an improved operational strategy aimed at optimizing cargo and passenger processing efficiency. Enhanced customs operations at a major Mexican airport gateway directly support logistics and supply chain efficiency for exporters, importers, and multinational operators using AIFA. Operational improvements reduce dwell times and costs for traders and manufacturers reliant on air transport for time-sensitive goods.
