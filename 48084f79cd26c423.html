Mixue arrives at 25 de Março and will open its first factory outside China

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Mixue, a Chinese tea and ice cream beverage chain that rivals McDonald's and Starbucks in scale, is expanding into Brazil with its first international factory. The company is opening its initial store in São Paulo's 25 de Março neighbourhood this week, marking a significant entry into the Latin American market. This expansion represents Mixue's first manufacturing facility outside China and signals the company's ambitions to capture market share in Brazil's growing quick-service beverage sector.
