Mixue Arrives at 25 de Março and Opens Its First Factory Outside China

16 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Mixue, a Chinese beverage and ice cream chain with over 20,000 locations globally, is expanding into Brazil with its first store on 25 de Março street in São Paulo and a dedicated manufacturing facility. The rapid expansion reflects the company's aggressive international growth strategy and confidence in the Brazilian market. Mixue is now larger by unit count than McDonald's and Starbucks globally, highlighting the scale of Asian consumer brands entering LatAm. For investors, this signals opportunities in food and beverage franchising and the competitive dynamics of the Brazilian QSR market.
