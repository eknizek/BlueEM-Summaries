# Mexican Peso Achieves Strongest Weekly Gain Since April 2025

In the Forex market, the national currency recorded its largest gain in 51 weeks, as between April 11 and 18, 2025.

Amid uncertainty over the Middle East conflict, the exchange rate threaded its fifth consecutive day of decline, a variation that allowed the Mexican peso an appreciation between April 3 and 10 of this year.
