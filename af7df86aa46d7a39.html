Inca Rail Announces Transfer of 80% Stake to RBB Andes Holding Limited

18 April 2026 · 02:34 AM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Inca Rail, Peru's leading luxury tourist train operator, announced the initiation of an 80% stake transfer from majority shareholder Track Investment Holding Limited to RBB Andes Holding Limited. The sale agreement was signed on December 4, marking a major ownership transition for the iconic Peru rail business serving the Machu Picchu route and other tourist corridors. This M&A transaction signals investor confidence in Peru's tourism and hospitality recovery post-pandemic and the value of specialized tourism infrastructure assets. The transaction is relevant for investors tracking transportation, tourism, and Latin American M&A activity.
