# Peso recovers and advances! Dollar price today April 14, 2026

The Mexican peso recovers and advances during this Tuesday's session, driven by a better environment for risk assets due to expectations that the United States and Iran could resume talks this week to end the war, with the dollar price today April 14, 2026 trading at $17.22 units.

The Mexican currency registers a gain of 0.43%, while the US dollar fell against a basket of currencies. Investors were also assimilating a report from the United States showing that producer prices grew in March less than expected as the cost of services remained unchanged, although rising energy prices are fueling inflationary pressures.

For now, the International Monetary Fund (IMF) lowered its forecasts for the global economy due to the impact of fuel prices and supply disruptions caused by the conflict in the Middle East.

For the rest of the session, various officials from the US Federal Reserve, including Michael Barr, Susan Collins and Austan Goolsbee, are expected to participate in different public events.
