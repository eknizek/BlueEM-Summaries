Vitru launches follow-on offering to raise up to R$300 million

08 April 2026 · 02:07 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Vitru Educação, owner of higher education brands Uniasselvi and Unicesumar, has launched a follow-on offering targeting up to R$300 million in capital raises. The primary offering comprises 13.6 million shares (approximately R$200 million base) and aims to increase trading liquidity in Vitru's stock while funding acceleration of the company's organic growth initiatives. This equity capital raise is significant for investors tracking education sector consolidation and growth financing strategies in Brazil.
