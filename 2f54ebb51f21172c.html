Pressured by debt, Kora Saúde reaches creditor agreement for out-of-court recovery

30 April 2026 · 12:51 PM  ·  Neofeed
Hospital group Kora Saúde announced an extrajudicial recovery agreement on April 29 as it grapples with mounting debt and financial pressure. The HIG Capital-backed company faced auditor concerns about operational continuity given its financial strain. The out-of-court restructuring approach allows Kora Saúde to negotiate with creditors outside formal bankruptcy proceedings. This move reflects broader challenges in the Brazilian healthcare sector where multiple operators face leverage pressures and liquidity constraints.
