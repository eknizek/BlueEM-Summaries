Labor reform: Argentine government considers pooled severance funds for SMEs

01 May 2026 · 05:32 PM  ·  Clarin
Argentina's labor reform implementation continues as the government analyzes regulatory mechanisms for the Fondo de Asistencia Laboral (FAL, Labor Assistance Fund), which will become mandatory for most employers except the public sector and household employers. Under consideration are provisions allowing SMEs to jointly contribute to and draw from shared severance funds, potentially reducing individual employer burden. This regulatory step is critical as 45% of registered employees in Argentina work for small and medium-sized firms.
