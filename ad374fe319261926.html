RappiPay Bets on Closing Financing Gap with 100% Digital Loans for Small Merchants

05 May 2026 · 04:59 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Finanzas
RappiPay, a fintech joint venture between Rappi and Davivienda, is launching a digital lending product aimed at closing financing gaps for small and medium-sized merchants in Colombia. The platform offers loans ranging from $1.8 million to $61.3 million pesos with repayment periods up to 24 months and interest rates starting at 17.4%. The initiative targets approximately 35,000 merchants who currently lack access to traditional financing channels, leveraging digital processes to reduce time and complexity in loan approval and disbursement.
