# Exclusive: Mak Capital secures majority to nominate Oncoclínicas board president, sources say

On the eve of the assembly that will define Oncoclínicas' new board of directors, the company is facing a new controversy. Mak Capital, which holds 6.38%, secured a majority — by just one vote — to nominate Marco Grodetzky for the presidency of the board. However, the result displeased Latache, a shareholder with nearly 15%. The vice-president, Marcel Cecchi, a partner at Latache who had been running the company, resigned, according to what Valor found out.
