Petroperú appoints Edmundo Lizarzaburu as board chairman

03 May 2026 · 02:51 PM

Petróleos del Perú (Petro-Perú) announced the appointment of Edmundo Raúl Antonio Lizarzaburu Bolaños as president of the board of directors of the state-owned company, a position he will assume starting May 3, according to a material fact reported to the Superintendencia del Mercado de Valores (SMV).

The decision was adopted at a General Shareholders' Meeting in universal session held on May 2. In that session, the removal of Roger Arévalo Ramírez as board president was agreed upon, with his last day in office being May 2. Likewise, the resignation of Carlos Augusto Villalobos Dulanto as a board member was formalized, with his term ending on May 2.

In the same agreement, Lizarzaburu Bolaños was appointed as a board member in the category of independent director, as well as board president. Berthin Enrique Gómez Vela was also appointed as an independent director, beginning his duties on May 3.

Lizarzaburu Bolaños is an industrial engineer from the Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú and holds a Global MBA. He also has a master's degree in research in management sciences and a doctorate in management. He has over 22 years of experience in companies and in research and consulting work in sectors such as banking, capital markets, hydrocarbons, and retail in Latin America and the United States.

Throughout his career, he has held positions in the financial sector and in private companies, as well as positions on boards and committees related to risk management, compliance, and corporate governance.
