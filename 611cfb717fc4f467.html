Felipe Harboe: Companies Purchasing Clipping Services Must Require Agencies to Comply with Law

20 April 2026 · 11:42 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Attorney Felipe Harboe has issued a legal warning regarding press clipping service compliance in Chile. Both companies that cut and commercialize news content and their buyers face potential penalties including fines up to 1,000 UTM units and criminal sentences ranging from 61 to 540 days imprisonment. The statement emphasizes that purchasing companies have a responsibility to ensure their service providers comply with applicable laws, otherwise they may be complicit in violations.
