Brazil's Embraer CFO Garcia resigns, CEO Gomes Neto to take interim role

06 April 2026 · 12:49 PM  ·  Reuters
Embraer's Chief Financial Officer Garcia has resigned from the Brazilian aerospace manufacturer to join competitor Azul as finance chief. CEO Francisco Gomes Neto will assume the interim CFO role while the company searches for a permanent replacement. This executive reshuffle reflects ongoing leadership transitions at both major Brazilian aviation companies and may signal operational changes at Embraer as it navigates the post-pandemic recovery and competitive pressures in regional aircraft manufacturing.
