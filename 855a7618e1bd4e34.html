Anthropic, OpenAI's main rival, targets Brazil office amid surge in Claude AI adoption

17 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Anthropic, OpenAI's principal AI competitor, is eyeing a Brazil office expansion as part of its global strategy to capture enterprise clients in key markets. The company views Brazil as one of the world's most important markets for AI adoption and corporate use cases. This expansion reflects the intensifying competition in generative AI and Anthropic's commitment to serving Latin America's rapidly growing corporate technology spending, particularly in financial services, technology, and professional services sectors.
