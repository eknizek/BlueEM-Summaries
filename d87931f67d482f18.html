Rocha Case Will Damage Investment Climate: IMEF

04 May 2026 · 09:42 PM  ·  Reforma - Portada
The Mexican Institute of Executives (IMEF) has cautioned that high-profile corruption cases linking political figures and government officials to narco activity—particularly the Rubén Rocha case—will further erode Mexico's investment climate and business confidence. Such allegations undermine institutional credibility and increase perceived sovereign and political risk for investors. IMEF's warning reflects growing concern about governance risks affecting capital allocation decisions.
