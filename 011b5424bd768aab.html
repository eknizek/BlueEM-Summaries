ARTICLE: The new playbook for startups

05 April 2026 · 07:30 PM  ·  Neofeed
The article examines how the traditional startup playbook—focused on product-market fit, venture capital raises, revenue growth at any cost, valuation increases, and eventual IPO or acquisition—is evolving. Startups are now adopting alternative strategies that prioritize sustainable business models and profitability over rapid scaling. This shift reflects changing market conditions, investor sentiment, and lessons learned from failed high-burn ventures. For LatAm investors, this signals a maturation of local startup ecosystems and potential opportunities in profitable, lower-risk ventures rather than unprofitable unicorn-chasing.
