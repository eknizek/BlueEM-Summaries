IMF Debt Payment: USD 800 Million Due Friday as Government Awaits New Disbursement

26 April 2026 · 11:04 PM  ·  Clarin
Argentina is scheduled to make an USD 800 million interest payment to the International Monetary Fund on Friday, while the government awaits a potential USD 1 billion disbursement contingent on IMF board approval in coming weeks. To shore up dollar reserves, the government is evaluating options to refinance or issue new sovereign bonds. These moves reflect continued reliance on external funding to manage foreign exchange pressures and service IMF obligations under the current program.
