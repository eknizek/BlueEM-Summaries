Rise in Agricultural and Road Machinery Imports Concerns Brazilian Sector

15 April 2026 · 11:15 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazilian grain production has achieved record harvests over the past decade with minimal climate-related setbacks, yet domestic agricultural machinery sales show a concerning disconnect. In the first three months of 2026, internal retail sales totaled only 9,800 units, representing a 13.1% decline compared to the same period in 2025. This divergence suggests rising imports may be capturing market share, raising concerns among domestic equipment manufacturers about competitive pressures and market consolidation.
