Economic Activity: Key Industrial Sector Rebounds in March Despite Low Levels

20 April 2026 · 02:26 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's metallurgical sector, a critical input for manufacturing industries, showed modest improvement in March 2026 with 1.5% monthly growth according to industry chamber Adimra. However, the sector remains under significant pressure, with first-quarter cumulative output down nearly 7%, reflecting broader industrial weakness in the Argentine economy. Metallurgical production is a key indicator of downstream manufacturing health and capital goods demand.
