# D1 Opens Internship Positions: Here are the Profiles They're Looking For and Requirements to Meet

Tiendas D1 opened a new call for university students interested in completing their professional internships in different areas of the company during 2026. This initiative is part of the linking programs that the company has been developing in recent years, with the objective of offering work experience to young people who are close to completing their studies.

"At Tiendas D1 we are searching for great university talents who want to live a unique practical experience in the largest 'hard discount' in the country. This is your opportunity to develop your skills to the maximum and apply everything you have learned during your academic training, in a dynamic, innovative environment with purpose," the company stated.

## Academic Programs Convoked

- Process, industrial, systems, mechanical, chemical, and environmental engineering

- Law and psychology

- Logistics and occupational health

- Business administration, business, and international commerce

- Social communication and graphic design

## General Requirements:

- Be an active student in the internship stage

- Have endorsement from your educational institution

- Availability to complete the internship period

- Basic knowledge of office tools

- Interest in the retail sector and teamwork

The internships will be held Monday through Friday, from 7:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., with compensation equivalent to the current legal minimum wage, plus affiliation to ARL and EPS.

The vacancies are available in different regions of the country, especially in areas where commercial and logistics operations have presence. Selected candidates may be linked to administrative, operational, and support areas, according to their academic profile.

Registrations are made through the company's official channels, where interested parties must complete the corresponding form and attach the requested documentation. The call is part of the young talent recruitment strategies being promoted by the company, with the purpose of supporting professional training and facilitating the transition of students to the working world.

Interested parties can consult the details of available vacancies and the application form directly on the company's official employment portal, where the current offer is constantly updated.
