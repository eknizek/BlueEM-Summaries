Agro-Industrial Exports Surged 31.4% in March, Q1 Exceeded $12.2 Billion

23 April 2026 · 07:07 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Argentina's agro-industrial exports surged 31.4% in March, reaching over $4.7 billion, with first-quarter exports exceeding $12.2 billion. The strong performance was driven by shipments of wheat, beef, and sunflower, demonstrating resilience in the country's core export sectors. This growth reflects improved global demand for agricultural commodities and Argentina's competitive export positioning, supporting the government's foreign exchange generation targets.
