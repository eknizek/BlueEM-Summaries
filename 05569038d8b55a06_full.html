# Spanish Prima Developers Attract Bradesco in R$545 Million Project

Based in Salvador but with investors from Catalonia, Grupo Prima secured a R$188 million check from Bradesco to finance 80% of the construction work on the Villaggio Jardins residential project, which totals R$545 million in Gross Sale Value (GVV) in the Cidade Jardim neighborhood in the Bahian capital.

The work, consisting of two high-end towers plus a private forest and leisure areas, began in January and is scheduled for delivery in 2028, with more than 90% of the units in the first phase already sold, according to the developer that has been operating in Brazil since 2005 and says it has already invested R$1 billion in the country with its own resources during that period.

The most recent credit was structured based on a business plan, with part of the funds coming from savings accounts, and has receivables assignment and property mortgage as guarantees.

"This financing represents a seal of confidence in our project and in Grupo Prima's execution capacity," evaluates CEO and partner Rubén Escartín. "More than an important step for the company, it is a boost that allows us to deliver a differentiated project and move forward with our investment plan," says the executive.

Beyond Villaggio, Prima has R$1.2 billion in development, including two in licensing phase with combined GVV of R$352 million, one in Salvador's Historic Center and another in Ondina.

Still in Salvador, the Sublime Horto House is under construction, with GVV of R$260 million and delivery expected this year.

In its first project outside Bahia, the group launched Brise Studios Design – a retrofit of the João Ursulo Ribeiro Coutinho building, designed by architect Lúcio Costa, next to the Church of Candelária in Rio.

Prima has already announced R$5.6 billion in investment to develop a tourist destination in Vila de Baixio, on Bahia's north coast, over ten years. The first phase, budgeted at R$2.1 billion, includes three resorts – including one under the Anantara flag – with completion of the first two expected by 2028 and the third by 2030.

The group plans to launch high-luxury residential projects in São Paulo and Rio soon.
