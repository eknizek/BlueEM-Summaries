# Spanish Prima Developers Attract Bradesco in R$545 Million Bahia Project

Based in Salvador but with investors from Catalonia, Grupo Prima secured a R$188 million check from Bradesco to finance 80% of construction for the Villaggio Jardins residential project, which totals R$545 million in gross sales value in the Cidade Jardim neighborhood in the Bahian capital. The works, comprising two high-end towers plus private forest and leisure areas, began in January and are scheduled for completion in 2028, with more than 90% of first-phase units already sold, according to the developer, which has operated in Brazil since 2005 and says it has invested R$1 billion in the country with its own resources over this period.

The most recent credit was structured based on a business plan, with part of the resources from savings accounts, and is guaranteed by assignment of receivables and property mortgage. "This financing represents a seal of confidence in our project and in Grupo Prima's execution capacity," evaluates CEO and partner Rubén Escartín. "More than an important step for the company, it is an impulse that allows us to deliver a differentiated development and advance with our investment plan," says the executive.

Beyond Villaggio, Prima has R$1.2 billion in development, with two in licensing phase with combined gross sales value of R$352 million — one in Salvador's Historic Center and another in Ondina. Also in Salvador, the Sublime Horto House is under execution, with R$260 million in gross sales value and delivery scheduled for this year.

In its first project outside Bahia, the group launched Brise Studios Design — a retrofit of the João Ursulo Ribeiro Coutinho building, designed by architect Lúcio Costa, next to the Candelária Church in Rio. Prima has already announced R$5.6 billion in investment to develop a tourism destination in Vila de Baixio, on Bahia's northern coast, over ten years. The first phase, budgeted at R$2.1 billion, includes three resorts — among them, one with the Anantara brand — with completion expected for the first two by 2028 and the third by 2030. The group plans to launch ultra-luxury residential developments in São Paulo and Rio soon.
