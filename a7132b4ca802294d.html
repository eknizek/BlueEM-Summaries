Cocamar sues Patria over grain storage units dispute

13 April 2026 · 10:51 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Cocamar, a major Paraná-based agricultural cooperative with annual revenues exceeding R$11 billion, has initiated legal action against Patria, an alternative asset manager, over a disputed transaction. The conflict centers on four grain storage units in Sabáudia that Patria listed for sale in December. Cocamar alleges that Patria accepted the purchase proposal but subsequently failed to respond to communications, effectively abandoning the deal. The case highlights potential disputes in Brazil's agricultural infrastructure asset sales and the broader agribusiness market.
