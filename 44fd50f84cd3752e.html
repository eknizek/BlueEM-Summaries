Peru Ministry of Economy: Public Investment Surpasses S/5.818 Billion in April 2026

01 May 2026 · 08:13 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru's Ministry of Economy and Finance reported that public investment execution in April 2026 totalled S/5.818 billion, representing a significant 49.4% year-on-year increase from S/3.894 billion in April 2025. This substantial acceleration in public capital spending signals expansion of state infrastructure and development projects. The robust public investment growth suggests improving fiscal execution and increased economic activity in Peru, supporting infrastructure and construction-related sectors.
