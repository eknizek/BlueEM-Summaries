Rede D'Or Raises US$500 Million with Ten-Year Bond

22 April 2026 · 08:22 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Rede D'Or, a major Brazilian healthcare operator, has successfully raised US$500 million through a ten-year bond offering priced at par with a yield of 6.55%. The offering attracted strong global investor demand during a challenging period for Brazilian companies in international debt markets. The roadshow commenced on Monday, demonstrating management's commitment to engaging with the investor base and securing capital for growth and refinancing needs.
