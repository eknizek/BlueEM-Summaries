Techint and Mindlin lose: key gas pipeline awarded to Víctor Contreras and Italian SICIM

29 April 2026 · 07:44 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
A consortium led by PAE, YPF, and Pampa Energía has awarded the construction contract for a key gas pipeline to Argentine firm Víctor Contreras and Italian company SICIM. The award excludes Techint and SACDE, companies linked to Paolo Rocca and the Mindlin family, marking a significant loss for Argentina's major construction firms. This decision follows Techint's earlier loss in the pipeline tube procurement bid to an Indian company.
