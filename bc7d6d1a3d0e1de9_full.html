# Oncoclínicas: 'We Will Support Those With Financial Capacity', Says Latache

Two days before the assembly that will elect Oncoclínicas' new board of directors, Latache co-founder Renato Azevedo affirms that the management firm will not nominate any of its executives for the position. The name of Marcel Vieira, former CFO of Oncoclínicas and Latache partner, appears on the slate indicated by the company.

"I can guarantee that Latache will not nominate any board member linked to the management firm. If we vote, it will be for an independent member," says Azevedo.

According to him, the decision is meant to make clear that the firm does not want to have any interference in Oncoclínicas and has no interest in managing it. "We don't have the capacity to manage and we don't want to, our focus is on the takeover bid."

Azevedo rules out any confrontation with MAK Capital, which nominated names for the board and requested multiple voting rights. However, he states that the company has not yet proven its financial capacity.

"If MAK proves it has financial capacity and willingness to do so, we could even support it. Either them or any other shareholder who makes a firm commitment and proves management capacity," says Azevedo. "The issue is that no binding proposal has yet appeared that meets the company's needs."

Regarding the strategy of betting on a takeover bid, he evaluates that the extrajudicial recovery guaranteed the company's existence despite the legacy of the previous management.
