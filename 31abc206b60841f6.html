Government Launches Single-Window Commerce Initiative and Streamlines Procedures

04 May 2026 · 06:48 PM  ·  Reforma
The Mexican government unveiled a regulatory reform package designed to accelerate investment project approval and reduce bureaucratic bottlenecks. Key measures include the establishment of a single-window commerce office and significant streamlining of administrative procedures for business registration and licensing. These initiatives aim to improve the business environment and attract domestic and foreign investment by reducing time and cost barriers to market entry.
