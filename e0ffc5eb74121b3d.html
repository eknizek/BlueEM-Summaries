Pernambuco startup seeks R$50M revenue with 'agentic assembly line'

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Volund, a Pernambuco-based startup, is leveraging AI agents to build what it calls an 'agentic assembly line' for enterprise operations. The company, located in the Porto Digital innovation hub, has integrated AI agents throughout its own operations, including establishing an Artificial HR department. The startup targets R$50 million in annual revenue by scaling this AI-driven operational model for enterprise clients.
