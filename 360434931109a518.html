French Saint-Gobain Sells Telhanorte to Tauá Partners

04 May 2026 · 08:05 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
French multinational building materials company Saint-Gobain announced a definitive agreement to sell Telhanorte, its Brazilian network of construction materials retail stores, to Tauá Partners. The transaction represents a strategic divestment by the French group as it realigns its portfolio. Tauá Partners, a Brazilian alternative investment firm, acquires an established retail chain with significant market presence in the construction materials segment.
