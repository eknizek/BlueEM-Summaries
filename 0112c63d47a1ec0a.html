Intercorp and Plaza Center: The Next Growth Steps for the Real Plaza Sister Brand

27 April 2026 · 11:26 PM  ·  Gestion - Economia
Plaza Center, a power-center retail format developed by Peruvian conglomerate Grupo Intercorp, is pursuing aggressive expansion focusing on secondary cities and peripheral districts where traditional shopping malls face operational constraints. Launched in 2017, the Plaza Center model prioritizes essential retail services, quick-shopping convenience, and lower capital intensity than conventional malls, positioning itself as a complementary format to Grupo Intercorp's flagship Real Plaza brand. This strategy addresses growing consumer demand in underserved markets while optimizing limited urban real estate availability in Peru's major metropolitan areas.
