Rumo Seeks to Eliminate 20% Voting Rights Cap

13 April 2026 · 08:02 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Rumo, a major Brazilian rail and logistics operator, is proposing to amend its bylaws to eliminate a clause capping shareholder voting rights at 20% of company capital. The move is interpreted by investors as an effort to attract strategic investors at a time when Cosan, Rumo's largest shareholder, may consider diluting its stake. The proposal signals potential governance changes and could facilitate M&A or capital restructuring.
