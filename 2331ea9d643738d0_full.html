# InRetail and IFS Announce $130M Acquisition of InFinance XP (Formerly Financiera Oh!)

InRetail Perú Corp. (InRetail) and Intercorp Financial Services Inc. (IFS) announced today the acquisition of InFinance XP S.A. (formerly Financiera Oh!), an operation materialized through the purchase of IXP Holding Corp. from IFH Retail Corp. for US$130 million.

The transaction has been structured as a strategic partnership between InRetail and IFS. This transaction leverages IFS's financial positioning, a platform that integrates banking services, insurance, wealth management, and payment methods.

InRetail, for its part, has established itself as the leading retail platform in Peru, operating a network of more than four thousand stores under various formats and brands. This broad geographic presence allows it to be close to millions of consumers nationwide.

The integration of these capabilities will allow the construction of a robust ecosystem of payments and consumer financing, with a superior value proposition for customers and a scalable platform of digital solutions, expanding access and convenience for millions of families through a customer-focused financial offering.

It is worth noting that InFinance XP has approximately three million customers, loan placements of S/ 1.7 billion and deposits of S/ 1.5 billion. Recently, the company launched the Sip brand, an application that consolidates its financial and payment products, as well as its loyalty program.
