Food Labeling Changes Would Create Costs and Impact Colombian SMEs, Says Business Group

24 April 2026 · 07:20 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombia's ANDI business federation has raised concerns that proposed changes to food labeling regulations would impose significant compliance costs on the country's business sector, affecting more than 50,000 companies including many small and medium-sized enterprises. The group argues that the increased regulatory burden could strain SME margins and operational efficiency. The issue highlights the tension between public health labeling initiatives and business competitiveness in Colombia.
