New Food Labeling Regulations Would Increase Costs and Harm Colombian SMEs, Says ANDI

24 April 2026 · 07:20 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombia's business federation ANDI has raised concerns that planned changes to food labeling requirements would impose significant compliance costs on over 50,000 enterprises, disproportionately affecting small and medium-sized businesses. The regulatory measure aims to improve food transparency but could burden domestic manufacturers and increase consumer prices. The issue highlights tension between public health objectives and business competitiveness in Colombia's food and beverage sector. Implementation costs and timeline remain key concerns for food producers and investors in the Colombian consumer sector.
