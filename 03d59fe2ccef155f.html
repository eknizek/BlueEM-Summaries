Receita Previsível acquires Meetz portfolio and reinforces ecosystem thesis

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Receita Previsível has acquired the customer portfolio of Meetz, a prospecting platform, in a transaction that demonstrates consolidation within the Brazilian sales and revenue operations software ecosystem. Following the sale, Meetz is pivoting from a prospecting platform toward a sales consulting firm targeting higher-value contracts with larger enterprise customers. This transaction exemplifies the "platform ecosystem" strategy where specialized software companies acquire customer bases to accelerate growth. The deal reflects maturing market dynamics and the ability of well-funded software companies to execute strategic M&A.
