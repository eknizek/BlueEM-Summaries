# Copasa receives approval from Minas Gerais Court of Accounts to proceed with share offering; final decision still pending approvals

The Companhia de Saneamento de Minas Gerais (Copasa) announced on Thursday (16) that the Court of Accounts of the State of Minas Gerais authorized the continuation of "certain steps related to the potential subsequent public share offering" of the company. The information is contained in a statement to the market.

According to media reports, a monitoring session was held on Thursday, a type of inspection process by the court. In the document released Thursday, Copasa states that the actual implementation of the offering involves providing additional clarifications, which may be requested by control bodies, "obtaining applicable approvals (including corporate and creditor approvals), macroeconomic and market conditions, the execution of definitive contracts and compliance with procedures provided for in current regulations."
