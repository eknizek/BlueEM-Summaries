SUNAT's mystery shoppers will monitor receipt issuance in digital sales enforcement

21 April 2026 · 10:20 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
SUNAT is implementing a mystery shopper program to monitor receipt (comprobante) issuance compliance among digital retailers, with tax officials identified via electronic SOL mailbox communications to prevent impersonation fraud. The enforcement mechanism targets informal sellers and tax non-compliance in rapidly growing e-commerce channels. This administrative initiative strengthens tax collection and reduces revenue losses in Peru's expanding digital retail sector.
