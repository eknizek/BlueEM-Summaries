Andreessen Horowitz and Kaszek invest in Segura, AI startup for insurance brokers

27 April 2026 · 09:00 AM  ·  Neofeed
Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), alongside Kaszek Ventures, has invested in Segura, a Brazilian startup developing AI solutions for insurance brokers. Segura has been operating for two years and represents a strategic entry point for top-tier Silicon Valley venture capital into Brazil's fintech and AI ecosystem. The investment reflects growing interest among global VCs in AI applications across financial services verticals in Latin America.
