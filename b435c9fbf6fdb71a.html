Embrapa announces Agrishow partnership for free planting in exchange for carbon credits

27 April 2026 · 08:56 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Brazil's agricultural research agency Embrapa announced at Agrishow a partnership program offering free planting services to farmers in exchange for carbon credits generated from their operations. This initiative aims to incentivize sustainable agricultural practices while creating a revenue stream from carbon markets. The program represents a novel approach to monetizing environmental benefits in Brazilian agriculture and expanding carbon credit participation among smallholder and mid-size farmers.
