Fitch Ratings warns Colombia's next government will inherit high public debt constraining its entire term

03 May 2026 · 07:59 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Credit rating agency Fitch Ratings has cautioned that Colombia's next government will inherit elevated public debt that will constrain fiscal policy flexibility throughout its four-year mandate. The agency estimates required fiscal adjustment equivalent to 3% of GDP to restore fiscal sustainability. High debt levels limit government capacity for new investments, infrastructure spending, and social programs while raising debt service burdens. This rating agency warning signals sovereign credit risks and may influence Colombian sovereign bond spreads and foreign investor sentiment toward the country.
