Fitch Ratings warns next Colombian government will inherit public debt that will condition entire mandate

03 May 2026 · 07:59 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Fitch Ratings has issued a warning that the next Colombian government will inherit elevated public debt levels that will constrain its policy flexibility throughout its four-year term. The rating agency estimates that Colombia requires a fiscal adjustment of approximately 3% of GDP to stabilise debt dynamics. This has significant implications for credit ratings, bond markets, and the fiscal trajectory of Latin America's third-largest economy.
