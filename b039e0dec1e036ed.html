Mexico Unemployment Rate Rises to 2.8% in March

24 April 2026 · 04:12 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Mexico's unemployment rate climbed to 2.8% in March, marking the highest level in 27 months and signaling deteriorating labor market conditions. The increase in joblessness reflects the broader slowdown in economic activity and weakness in employment growth. Rising unemployment could weigh on consumer spending and corporate profitability in Mexico.
