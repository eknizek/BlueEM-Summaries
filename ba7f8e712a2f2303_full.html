# Company Hero Aims to Bring Order to Independent Contractor Management

In many companies, managing independent contractors (PJ professionals) still involves spreadsheets, email exchanges, various contracts, and manual invoice verification. When this operation grows, what once seemed manageable becomes a bottleneck. To address this scenario, Company Hero launched Gestão PJ, a platform that centralizes the journey of these professionals — from contracts to invoices and from payments to compliance and benefits offerings.

Among the platform's features are batch invoice requests and automatic validation using artificial intelligence, identifying and correcting discrepancies, as well as complete management of contracts, documents, and payments in a single digital workflow. The proposal is to organize and automate an operation that currently happens in a dispersed manner between HR and finance departments, with manual processes and little control.

According to Company Hero, clients like Wellbe and FindMe have already adopted the platform and reported a reduction of up to 80% in operational processes related to managing these professionals. This move aligns with the expansion of independent work in the country. A Datafolha survey from August 2025 showed that 59% of Brazilians prefer to work independently, a trend that has pushed organizations to better structure their relationships with their independent contractor base.

With Gestão PJ, Company Hero expands its presence in the independent contractor universe and now offers companies a more robust and structured layer of operations, compliance, and benefits, delivering greater convenience to all involved parties.
