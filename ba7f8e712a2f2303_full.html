# Company Hero Introduces PJ Management Tool to Professionalize Contractor Operations

In many companies, the management of PJ contractors still involves spreadsheets, email exchanges, various contracts, and manual invoice verification. When this operation grows, what once seemed manageable becomes a bottleneck. To address this scenario, Company Hero launched Gestão PJ, a platform that centralizes the journey of these professionals — from contracts to invoices and from payments to compliance and benefits offered.

Among the platform's features are batch invoice requests and automatic validation using artificial intelligence, identifying and correcting discrepancies, as well as complete management of contracts, documents, and payments in a single digital workflow. The proposal is to organize and automate an operation that currently happens in a dispersed manner between HR and finance, with manual processes and little control.

According to Company Hero, clients such as Wellbe and FindMe have already adopted the platform and recorded a reduction of up to 80% in the operational process related to managing these professionals. The move aligns with the expansion of independent work in the country. A Datafolha survey from August 2025 showed that 59% of Brazilians prefer to work independently, a trend that has pressured organizations to better structure their relationships with their PJ contractor base.

With Gestão PJ, Company Hero expands its presence in the PJ universe and now offers companies a more robust and structured layer of operations, compliance, and benefits, delivering greater convenience to all involved. Companies interested in understanding how the platform works in practice can learn more about Gestão PJ on the company's website.
