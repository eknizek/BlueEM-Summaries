# Company Hero Streamlines PJ Contractor Management with Management Tool

At many companies, managing PJ contractors still involves spreadsheets, email exchanges, various contracts, and manual invoice verification. When this operation grows, what once seemed manageable becomes a bottleneck. To address this scenario, Company Hero launched Gestão PJ, a platform that centralizes the journey of these professionals — from contracts to invoices and from payments to compliance and benefits offered.

The platform's functionalities include batch invoice requests and automatic validation of invoices using artificial intelligence, identifying and correcting discrepancies, as well as complete management of contracts, documents, and payments in a single digital workflow. The proposal is to organize and automate an operation that currently happens in a dispersed manner between HR and finance, with manual processes and little control.

According to Company Hero, clients such as Wellbe and FindMe have already adopted the platform and reported a reduction of up to 80% in the operational process linked to managing these professionals. The move follows the expansion of independent work in the country. An August 2025 Datafolha survey showed that 59% of Brazilians prefer to work for themselves, a movement that has pressured organizations to better structure their relationship with their PJ base.

With Gestão PJ, Company Hero expands its presence in the PJ universe and now offers companies a more robust and structured layer of operations, compliance, and benefits, delivering greater convenience to all involved.
