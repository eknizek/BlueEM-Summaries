# Mexican Fintech Klar Integrates Apple Pay into Its Payment System

Klar—the Mexican fintech that acquired the digital unit of Mexican bank Banorte last year—announced the incorporation of Apple Pay into its payment system for its customers in Latin America.

Through a statement, the company indicated that to pay in stores, customers must double-click the side button, authenticate themselves, and bring their iPhone or Apple Watch close to a payment terminal to complete a contactless payment.

Additionally, customers will also be able to use Apple Pay on iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Apple Vision Pro to make purchases more quickly and conveniently within applications or on the web, without needing to create accounts or repeatedly enter their personal information, card data, or shipping and billing information.

With the announcement, Stefan Möller, CEO and founder of Klar, stated that the company's priority "is to offer technology that makes life easier for our users." Along these lines, he added that "offering Apple Pay to our cardholders is a fundamental step toward modernizing the payment experience in Mexico, allowing our customers to operate with the highest global security standards in an extremely simple way."
