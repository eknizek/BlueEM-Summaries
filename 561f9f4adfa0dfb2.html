BNDES approves R$140 million for Transjordano to implement green corridor in São Paulo

18 April 2026 · 05:24 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
The Brazilian Development Bank (BNDES) has approved R$140 million in financing for Transjordano, a logistics operator, to implement a green corridor project in São Paulo state. The initiative aims to improve transportation infrastructure while incorporating environmental sustainability measures. This represents a strategic investment by BNDES in logistics and infrastructure development, aligning with Brazil's sustainability objectives.
