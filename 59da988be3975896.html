ABB sees 'short circuit' in infrastructure but plugs US$45 million investments for data centers

08 May 2026 · 06:15 PM  ·  Neofeed
ABB, a Swiss electrification leader, is targeting Brazil for expansion, primarily in data center infrastructure installation. The company has allocated US$45 million for investments in Brazil, including portfolio expansion and operational enhancements at its Sorocaba, São Paulo facility. This deployment reflects growing data center demand in Brazil and ABB's confidence in the country's digital infrastructure market.
