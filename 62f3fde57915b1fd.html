Fictor Receives Court Approval for Judicial Recovery but Company Will Be Monitored

18 April 2026 · 01:47 PM  ·  O Globo
Fictor, a Brazilian company, has received approval from São Paulo courts to proceed with judicial recovery (recuperação judicial), a formal restructuring process under Brazilian bankruptcy law. While the company has obtained the necessary judicial authorization to implement its recovery plan, it will remain subject to court monitoring and oversight throughout the process. This is a significant development for creditors and stakeholders in the company's capital structure.
