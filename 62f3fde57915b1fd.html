Fictor's judicial recovery request approved by São Paulo court, but company will be monitored

18 April 2026 · 01:47 PM  ·  O Globo
Fictor has received approval from São Paulo's judiciary for its judicial recovery request (recuperação judicial), a Brazilian insolvency mechanism allowing companies to restructure while continuing operations. The court has granted the petition but imposed monitoring conditions to oversee the company's financial rehabilitation process. This legal approval is a critical milestone for the industrial company as it navigates debt restructuring obligations.
