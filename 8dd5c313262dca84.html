Banks criticize Federal Audit Court decision to suspend INSS payroll deduction loans

04 May 2026 · 05:31 PM  ·  O Globo
Brazil's banks have voiced criticism of a Federal Audit Court (TCU) decision to suspend payroll deduction loans (crédito consignado) linked to the National Institute of Social Security (INSS). Consignado credit is a major product for Brazilian banks, particularly for retirees and pensioners. The TCU suspension raises regulatory and compliance concerns for lenders. This decision impacts a significant lending segment and could affect both bank profitability and consumer access to credit.
