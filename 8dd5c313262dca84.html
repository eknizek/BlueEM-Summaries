Banks criticize TCU decision to suspend INSS payroll deduction loans

04 May 2026 · 05:31 PM  ·  O Globo
Brazil's Federal Court of Accounts (TCU) has suspended payroll deduction lending (consignado de INSS), a significant credit product for pensioners and retirees managed by Brazilian banks. The decision triggered strong criticism from the banking sector, which relies heavily on this revenue stream. This regulatory action has major implications for bank loan portfolios, earnings, and the broader private credit market in Brazil, as consignado lending is a core business line for many institutions.
