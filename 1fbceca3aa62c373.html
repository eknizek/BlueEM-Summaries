Free Pension System Transfer Between ONP and AFP to Launch in September

02 May 2026 · 06:15 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru's pension system regulator has announced that free portability between the state-run ONP (Oficina de Normalización Previsional) and private AFP (Administradoras de Fondos de Pensiones) pension funds will launch in September. This policy reform gives workers greater flexibility to switch between pension systems without financial barriers. The move reflects broader pension system modernization in Peru and could reshape asset flows between public and private pension administrators. This development is material for institutional investors managing AFP assets and pension-related financial products.
