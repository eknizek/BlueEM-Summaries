An 'invisible tax' is slowing economic growth and investment in Colombia

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Colombia faces a significant drag on economic growth and investment flows due to an accumulation of regulatory requirements, administrative procedures, and policy uncertainty—described as an 'invisible tax' on business operations. This regulatory overburden increases operational costs for companies and deters new capital inflows, undermining the country's competitive position for foreign direct investment. The structural impediment to economic dynamism stems from complex compliance requirements and lack of regulatory clarity, issues that must be addressed to restore investor confidence.
