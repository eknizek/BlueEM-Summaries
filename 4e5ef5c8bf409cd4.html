Business groups celebrate investment facilitation decree

04 May 2026 · 09:56 PM  ·  Reforma - Portada
Mexican business organizations and chambers have praised a government decree designed to accelerate investment authorization processes, reducing approval timelines to no more than 90 days. The measure is intended to improve the ease of doing business and attract capital to the country. This policy development is significant for investors considering entry or expansion in Mexico, particularly in sectors requiring regulatory approval.
