Court Removes BYD from Brazil's Slave Labor 'Dirty List'

09 April 2026 · 08:37 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
A Brazilian court has removed electric vehicle manufacturer BYD from Brazil's 'dirty list' of companies involved in labor trafficking and forced labor violations. The decision clears BYD of allegations and removes a significant regulatory compliance barrier to the company's operations and expansion in the Brazilian market. This ruling is material for BYD's EV market ambitions in LatAm's largest economy and signals regulatory developments around labor standards enforcement for multinational manufacturers operating in Brazil.
