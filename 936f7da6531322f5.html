Minerva Reopens Market with $600 Million Bond

15 April 2026 · 07:52 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Minerva has successfully completed a $600 million international bond offering with a 10-year maturity and coupon of 7.5% (pricing at 7.625% p.a.). This represents the first major international capital raise by a Brazilian company since late January, when Sabesp, FS Bio, and Azul raised nearly $600 million combined. The transaction signals renewed investor appetite for Brazilian credit and reflects the protein/food company's financing needs.
