DIAN announces changes to withholding tax: Previous rates return by order of State Council

09 May 2026 · 03:43 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's tax authority (DIAN) has announced a return to previously applied withholding tax bases and rates following a provisional suspension of multiple articles in Decree 572 of 2025 by the State Council. Withholding agents must now apply the earlier regulatory framework. This regulatory reversal impacts tax planning and compliance for Colombian businesses.
