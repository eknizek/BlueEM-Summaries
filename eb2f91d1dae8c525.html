Superintendencies Must Be Technical Bodies, Not Political: Billy Escobar, Former Superintendent of Corporations

27 April 2026 · 04:01 AM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
Billy Escobar, the former Superintendent of Corporations (Superintendencia de Sociedades), resigned after nearly five years in the role and raised concerns about institutional politicization and abuse in insolvency proceedings. Escobar advocates for superintendencies to function as technical regulatory bodies rather than politically influenced agencies. His departure and subsequent warnings highlight governance risks in Colombia's financial and corporate oversight infrastructure.
