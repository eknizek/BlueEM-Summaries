# Mexican Peso Advances for Second Consecutive Day

The Mexican peso advances for a second consecutive day following comments about a ceasefire in Iran, with the dollar price today, April 1, 2026, trading at $17.84 pesos; learn here the exchange rate in Mexican banks.

The Mexican currency registers a gain of 0.45% compared to Tuesday's reference price, when it advanced just over 1%, amid a global dollar decline due to growing expectations of an imminent ceasefire in the Middle East.

U.S. President Donald Trump told Reuters on Wednesday that his country "will withdraw from Iran quite soon" and could return to carry out "targeted strikes" if necessary. The president is scheduled to address the nation in the evening in a speech in which, according to the White House, he will offer "an important update" on the war.
