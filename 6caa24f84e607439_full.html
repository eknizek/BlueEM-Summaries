# Peso advances for second consecutive day! Dollar price today April 1, 2026

The Mexican peso advances for the second consecutive day following comments about a ceasefire in Iran, with the dollar price today, April 1, 2026, trading at $17.84 units; here you can find the exchange rate at Mexican banks.

The Mexican currency registers a gain of 0.45% compared to the reference price on Tuesday, when it advanced just over 1%, amid a global dollar decline due to growing expectations of an imminent ceasefire in the Middle East.

The President of the United States, Donald Trump, told Reuters on Wednesday that his country "will withdraw from Iran fairly soon" and could return to conduct "targeted attacks" if necessary. The president is scheduled to address the nation tonight in a speech in which, according to the White House, he will offer "an important update" on the war.
