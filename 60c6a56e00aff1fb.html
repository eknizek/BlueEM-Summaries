Fibra Next enters bidding war for Fibra Macquarie with acquisition offer

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Mexican REIT Fibra Next initiated a tender offer to acquire Fibra Macquarie, a competing real estate trust, with the auction period opening May 4 and closing June 1, 2024, subject to possible extension. The move reflects competitive M&A activity in the Mexican real estate investment trust sector and strategic consolidation among major property funds. The transaction represents a significant capital markets event in Mexico's REIT space, with implications for portfolio composition, fee structures, and distribution yield for investor bases.
