Fibra Next Joins Bidding for Fibra Macquarie and Launches Acquisition Offer

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Fibra Next has announced a competing acquisition offer for Fibra Macquarie, Mexico's major real estate investment trust, intensifying competition for control of the asset. The offer period officially opened on May 4 and will conclude on June 1, 2024, with provisions for potential extension. This transaction represents significant M&A activity in the Latin American real estate and infrastructure sectors.
