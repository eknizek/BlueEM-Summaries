# Gerdau Makes Binding Offer to Purchase Celesc's Stake in Hydroelectric Plant

Gerdau has submitted a binding proposal for the acquisition of all shares in the Dona Francisca Hydroelectric Plant (DFESA) corresponding to 23.03% of the capital, which belong to Santa Catarina Electric Power Company (Celesc). The proposal values the company at R$ 150 million, which was accepted by Celesc on Thursday (16).

According to Gerdau, "the acquisition price will be paid in full on the closing date, using available own resources." The plant has, according to the steelmaker, an installed capacity of 125 megawatts (MW) and physical guarantee of 72.5 average MW, of which approximately 66 average MW are destined for DFESA.

Currently, Gerdau already owns 53.94% of DFESA's capital. With this acquisition, it will hold 76.97% of shareholding, guaranteeing approximately 50 average MW of energy.
