Economist Arriazu Warns Unemployment in Greater Buenos Aires Poses Greatest Political Risk to Government

20 April 2026 · 10:54 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Economist Arriazu cautioned that Argentina's ongoing economic adjustment process is destroying employment faster than it creates new jobs, particularly in the Greater Buenos Aires region. He warned this jobs crisis could translate into significant political pressure and electoral consequences for the government. Unemployment dynamics remain a critical economic and political indicator in Argentina as the administration pursues structural reforms. Rising joblessness could complicate the government's ability to maintain social cohesion during the adjustment period.
