Cargo Transport in Mexico Faces Critical Challenges from Insecurity and Infrastructure Deficits

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Mexico's cargo transportation sector confronts a critical operating environment marked by escalating insecurity and inadequate road infrastructure, according to industry association Canacar. Security threats including cargo theft and violence against drivers compound logistics costs and operational risks. Infrastructure deficiencies further constrain efficiency and competitiveness of freight transport operations across the country.
