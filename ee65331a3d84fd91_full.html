# Dino's Judicial Reform Proposal May Reduce STF Criticism and Contrasts with Fachin Approach

Members of the Supreme Federal Court (STF) consulted by Valor state that the proposal by Justice Flávio Dino for a judicial reform can contain criticism of the Court, in addition to being a counterpoint to the code of conduct defended by STF President Edson Fachin.

In an article, Dino stated that the intensification of debates about the judiciary created a favorable moment to consider a new reform.

Supreme Court justices made different interpretations of their colleague Flávio Dino's article, according to sources consulted by the publication.
