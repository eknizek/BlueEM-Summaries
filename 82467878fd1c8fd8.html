Roadmap to Exit Fossil Fuels Must Include Renewable Energy Demand

24 April 2026 · 10:00 AM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
An effective decarbonization pathway requires more than supply-side renewable energy development; it must actively build market demand for renewable energy. The article argues that policy and investment frameworks should couple renewable capacity expansion with mechanisms to increase consumption and market pull for clean energy. This demand-focused approach is essential for successfully transitioning away from fossil fuels in Brazil and globally.
