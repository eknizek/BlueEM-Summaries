USA Rare Earth CEO on 'Transformative' $2.8B Serra Verde Deal

24 April 2026 · 10:20 PM  ·  Bloomberg - Tech
USA Rare Earth announced a $2.8 billion acquisition of Brazil's Serra Verde Group, expected to close in Q3, as the US and allied nations seek to reduce dependence on China-dominated rare-earth markets. Rare-earth elements are critical for high-strength magnets used in consumer electronics, automotive, and defense applications. This deal represents significant foreign investment in Brazil's mining sector and reflects geopolitical efforts to diversify critical mineral supply chains, with major implications for Brazil's mining industry, technology supply chains, and US-LatAm strategic partnerships.
