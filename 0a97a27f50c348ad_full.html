# Peso Falls from Best Level in Six Weeks; US Dollar Price Today, April 15, 2026

The Mexican peso retreated on Wednesday after reaching its best level in nearly seven weeks, driven by optimism over the resumption of peace talks between the United States and Iran. The dollar price today, April 15, 2026, is quoted at $17.28 pesos.

The Mexican currency registered a loss of 0.15% compared to Tuesday's reference price, when it had advanced to 17.1610 pesos, a level not seen since late February.

"We anticipate that optimism will increase throughout the session stemming from a potential peace plan in the Middle East, with investors seeking confirmations of alleged new conversations between the United States and Iran," indicated Grupo Financiero Ve por Más in a report.

According to US President Donald Trump, the war could end soon, and in an interview he asked the world to pay attention to "two incredible days," while US forces impose a blockade that forced Iranian port vessels to turn back.
