Former Central Bank Official Received R$3 Million from Banco Master in 2025 After Agency Departure

10 April 2026 · 10:05 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
João André Calviño Marques Pereira, former head of the Central Bank's Financial System Regulation Department (2018–January 2024), received R$3 million from Banco Master in 2025 through his consulting firm JGM Solutions, after taking leave in March 2024 and formal resignation in June 2025. The payment occurred after his departure from a regulatory position overseeing the same bank, raising governance and conflict-of-interest concerns. This highlights potential revolving-door risks in Brazilian financial regulation and may attract scrutiny from market participants and regulators monitoring governance standards in the banking sector.
