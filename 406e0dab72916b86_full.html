# CBC Achieves Milestone Operating on 100% Renewable Energy Across All Regional Operations

CBC reported that all of its production plants, distribution centers, and agencies in each of the countries where it has a presence now operate with electricity from 100% renewable energy sources.

The company highlighted that this achievement reflects its commitment to protecting the planet, the well-being of the communities where it operates, and building a better future for future generations.

"In Peru, we celebrate this important advance, the result of the collective commitment of all of CBC. Operating with 100% renewable energy reflects our vision and responsible growth, as well as our commitment to continue innovating, investing, and leading initiatives that contribute to building a more resilient and sustainable world," said Mirko Astudillo, General Director of CBC Peru.

More than just a milestone achieved, it represents a tangible demonstration of how a strategic vision and clear purpose can transform the way of operating and generate a positive impact on a large scale.

This milestone aligns with CBC's commitment to the Science Based Targets (SBTi) initiative, to which the company is committed, strengthening its efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and actively contribute to the fight against climate change through science-based objectives.
