Brazilian AI startups rank among world's 100 most promising

10 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Two Brazilian artificial intelligence startups, Tako and Enter, have been recognized among the world's 100 most promising companies in a prestigious global ranking. Brazil is the only Latin American country to achieve multiple entries on this list, underscoring the country's emerging strength in AI innovation and startup development. This recognition highlights Brazil's competitive position in the global AI startup ecosystem.
