Traders Bet on AI to Boost Profits, but Results Disappoint Expectations

02 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Traders and investment firms are increasingly deploying artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to identify market opportunities and enhance trading returns, but early results are falling short of ambitious expectations. The disconnect between AI hype and actual performance reflects challenges in model accuracy, data quality, and rapidly changing market conditions that render historical patterns unreliable. This trend highlights both the promise and limitations of AI in capital markets, particularly when applied to discretionary trading strategies without proper risk management and validation.
