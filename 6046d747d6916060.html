Milei's auction of railroads risks sidelining US-backed bidder - Buenos Aires Times

21 April 2026 · 01:37 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times (Argentina)
The Milei administration's auction model for Argentina's deteriorating freight railroad network has drawn criticism from industry experts who warn it may sideline one of the most significant bidders in the process. The auction structure is seen as potentially problematic for competition and value maximization. A major US-backed bidder appears to be at a disadvantage under the proposed framework. This privatization initiative is part of the government's broader infrastructure reform agenda.
