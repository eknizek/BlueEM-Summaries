Amid Ambipar Crisis, Cetrel Gains Competitive Ground in Brazilian Environmental Services

07 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Cetrel, the second-largest Brazilian environmental services firm, is benefiting from competitive weakness at peer Ambipar, strengthening its market position in water supply, industrial waste management, and emergency response services. Cetrel is owned by Grupo Solví, which operates major landfills across Brazil. The consolidation dynamic in the environmental sector is creating winner-take-more conditions. For LatAm investors, this highlights the importance of operational execution in fragmented environmental services and the value of integrated waste-to-water platforms in Brazil's growing ESG-driven market.
