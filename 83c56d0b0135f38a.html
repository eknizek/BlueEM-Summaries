Amid Ambipar Crisis, Cetrel Emerges as Clear Winner in Brazilian Environmental Services

07 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Cetrel, the 48-year-old environmental services company controlled by Grupo Solví, is positioned to gain market share as a competitor (Ambipar) faces operational challenges. Cetrel provides integrated services spanning water supply, industrial waste management, emergency response, and environmental solutions. Solví also operates major landfill operations across Brazil, creating an integrated platform in environmental services and waste management. This competitive dynamic reflects consolidation pressures in Brazil's environmental sector and the strategic value of scale and operational track records. For LatAm investors, this highlights opportunities in environmental infrastructure and waste management amid rising regulatory focus on ESG.
