02 May 2026 · 04:24 PM  ·  Valor Economico

This statement reflects Berkshire's contrarian stance toward hype-driven market trends and its focus on fundamental value creation. For investment professionals, this signals how a major institutional investor views current AI valuations and deployment decisions, relevant to capital allocation strategy and market sentiment analysis.
