Bill threatens Brazilian airlines and causes 'turbulence' in Amazon skies

27 April 2026 · 09:39 PM  ·  Neofeed
The Brazilian Chamber of Deputies approved Bill 539/2024, authored by federal deputies Coronel Ulysses and Cristiane Lopes, which would authorize South American airlines to operate domestic flights within Brazil's Amazon Legal region. The proposal now moves to the Senate for consideration. The initiative aims to increase air connectivity in the Amazon but faces potential opposition from domestic carriers concerned about competition.
