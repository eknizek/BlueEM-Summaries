Losses estimated in millions as one million hectares flooded in northern Santa Fe

20 April 2026 · 06:25 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Severe flooding affected one million hectares in northern Santa Fe province after rainfall topped 400mm in some areas, with departments of Vera, General Obligado, and 9 de Julio most impacted. The flooding inflicts major losses on the region's primary economic activity—livestock ranching—threatening cattle and pasture production. Cattle losses and productivity disruption will ripple through Argentina's protein export sector. Agricultural insurance claims and credit demands are expected to spike.
