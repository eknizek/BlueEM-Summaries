"Losses are in the millions": estimate of one million hectares flooded in northern Santa Fe

20 April 2026 · 06:25 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Northern Argentina's Santa Fe province suffered severe flooding from recent rains exceeding 400mm in some areas, inundating approximately one million hectares. The hardest-hit departments include Vera, General Obligado and 9 de Julio. The flooding caused millions in direct losses to the region's primary activity: livestock operations. The disaster highlights climate risks to Argentina's agricultural and pastoral production.
