Telecommunications: How to switch mobile operators without losing your phone number

27 April 2026 · 01:27 PM  ·  Portfolio - Empresas
Colombia has implemented regulations allowing consumers to change mobile operators while retaining their phone numbers, a measure designed to increase competition in the telecommunications sector. Number portability empowers consumers by reducing switching costs and barriers to entry for rival operators. This regulatory move aligns with broader Latin American trends toward consumer protection and market liberalization in telecom.
