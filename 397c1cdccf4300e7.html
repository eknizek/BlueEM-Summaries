MBRF and HPDC complete formation of Sadia Halal with share offering on horizon

03 May 2026 · 11:08 PM  ·  Valor Economico
MBRF (world's largest halal poultry producer) and HPDC have completed the formation of Sadia Halal, a new halal-certified poultry enterprise. The companies are preparing to launch a share offering to capitalize the new venture and fund growth operations. This transaction represents a significant development in Brazil's poultry and food exports sector, particularly for halal-compliant products targeting Middle Eastern and Islamic markets.
