ADEX: Peruvian Exports to Japan Grew 14.4% in First Two Months of Year

03 May 2026 · 01:08 AM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peruvian exports to Japan totaled $558.9 million in the first two months of 2025, representing a 14.4% increase compared to the same period in 2024, according to the Exporters Association (ADEX). The growth was primarily driven by traditional export sectors, including mining and agriculture products. This positive trade performance reflects ongoing demand for Peruvian commodities in Asian markets and strengthens bilateral trade relations between the two countries.
