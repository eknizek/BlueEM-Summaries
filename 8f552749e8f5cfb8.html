Older Cattle Beef Gains Market Traction with Ranchers and Haute Cuisine

04 May 2026 · 08:00 PM  ·  O Globo
A shift in Brazilian ranching and culinary preferences is driving demand for beef from older cattle, traditionally undervalued in commodity markets. High-end restaurants and premium butchers are incorporating older-cattle beef into their offerings, citing superior flavor and marbling characteristics. This trend creates a new revenue stream for ranchers and adds value differentiation to Brazil's beef export portfolio, with implications for pricing and market segmentation.
