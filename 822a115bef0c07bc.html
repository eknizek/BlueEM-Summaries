Entel targets 15-20% market share in Peru fiber TV segment by 2027-2028

24 April 2026 · 11:30 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Entel Peru's head of legal and regulatory affairs stated the company targets a 15-20% market share in the fiber TV segment by 2027-2028 to strengthen customer retention. This expansion represents Entel's push into bundled services combining mobile and fixed-line offerings. The fiber TV growth strategy is part of Peru's broader digital infrastructure development and telecom market competition.
