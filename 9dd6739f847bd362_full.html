# 'Wellbeing Investment' Does Not Risk Retirement Savings — AMAFORE

The president of the Mexican Association of Afores, Guillermo Zamarripa, assures in an interview that they will indeed increase the amount of investment in infrastructure projects, but not at a level of 30% of retirement savings nor under an "expropriation" of workers' resources.

According to Amafore, the approval of this new law reaffirms the principles that govern all investments of the Retirement Savings System.
