SEA approves metrotren extension to Quillota and La Calera in project exceeding US$800 million

05 May 2026 · 10:31 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Chile's Superintendencia de Aeronáutica Civil (SEA) has approved a significant metrotren (suburban rail) extension project for EFE (state-owned railway company) connecting to Quillota and La Calera. The project encompasses 26 kilometers of new track, seven new stations, and is forecast to carry over 6 million passengers annually, representing substantial infrastructure investment. With a project value exceeding US$800 million, this is a major capital project addressing regional transportation needs.
