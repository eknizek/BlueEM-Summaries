Northern General Clinic Suspends Surgeries Over $441 Billion Health Insurance Debt

24 April 2026 · 06:12 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Clínica General del Norte in Colombia has suspended scheduled surgical procedures due to an outstanding payment portfolio from health insurance providers exceeding 441 billion pesos. Specialists report payment delays of up to four months, creating operational strain and threatening healthcare service access. The crisis reflects broader liquidity problems in Colombia's health insurance system and its impact on healthcare provider finances. This situation has material implications for hospital operators, health insurance investors, and the broader healthcare infrastructure in Colombia.
