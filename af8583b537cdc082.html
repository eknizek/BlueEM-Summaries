Mexico's Construction Sector Projected to Rebound This Year

08 April 2026 · 09:30 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
BBVA México projects a significant rebound in Mexico's construction sector this year, with growth supported by heightened government spending on public works and infrastructure projects. The bank expects the sector to exceed 650 billion pesos in activity, reflecting strong public sector demand for construction services and project development. This outlook is underpinned by government infrastructure priorities and capital allocation toward transportation, energy, and urban development. The construction sector rebound could benefit equipment suppliers, materials manufacturers, and engineering firms while signaling broader economic confidence in Mexico's infrastructure agenda.
