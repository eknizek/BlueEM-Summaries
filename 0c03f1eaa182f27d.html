Alliance between BVC and Fundación Bolívar Davivienda aims to bring more SMEs to capital markets

25 April 2026 · 09:58 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
The Colombian stock exchange (BVC) has partnered with Fundación Bolívar Davivienda to improve financing mechanisms and expand access to capital markets for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). The initiative targets the financing gap that constrains SME growth and aims to deepen liquidity in Colombia's capital markets. This is relevant for investors focused on market infrastructure, capital formation, and SME-focused private equity and credit opportunities in Colombia.
