Alliance between BVC and Fundación Bolívar Davivienda seeks to bring more SMEs to capital markets

25 April 2026 · 09:58 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's stock exchange (BVC) and Fundación Bolívar Davivienda have launched a joint initiative to facilitate SME access to capital markets. The partnership aims to close financing gaps that have historically limited small and medium-sized enterprises' ability to raise institutional capital. By introducing new financing mechanisms and reducing barriers to market entry, the initiative seeks to deepen and broaden Colombia's capital markets. This development is relevant to investors seeking exposure to LatAm SME growth and capital market deepening trends.
