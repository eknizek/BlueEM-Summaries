Teck flags Chile cost pressure amid fuel squeeze

23 April 2026 · 12:03 PM  ·  Mining.com
Teck Resources is facing significant cost pressures in its Chilean operations due to rising diesel, freight, and explosives costs, creating headwinds for the company's outlook. However, the miner has reported record copper sales volumes and improved profitability, which helps support its full-year guidance. The cost challenges highlight operational vulnerability to commodity-linked input costs in the region, a key risk for large copper producers with significant Chilean exposure.
