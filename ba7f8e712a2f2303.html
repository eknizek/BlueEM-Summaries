Company Hero Aims to Bring Order to Independent Contractor Management

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Company Hero has introduced Gestão PJ, a software platform designed to professionalize the management of independent contractors (PJ workers) in Brazil. The solution targets manual inefficiencies in HR, finance, and contractor administration by automating workflows and providing greater operational predictability. This addresses a significant pain point in Brazil's large independent contractor market, where many companies still rely on manual processes. The venture reflects growing demand for HR tech and financial operations software targeting Brazil's unique labor market dynamics.
