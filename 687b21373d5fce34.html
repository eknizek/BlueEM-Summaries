Sandvik Seeks to Lead in Underground and Surface Mining Drilling: What Is Its Strategy?

04 May 2026 · 09:53 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Sandvik, a Swedish capital goods and engineering company, is pursuing market leadership in underground and surface mining drilling technology in Peru. The company intends to introduce battery-electric jumbos (drilling equipment) for the first time in Peruvian mining operations, along with other technological innovations aimed at improving efficiency and sustainability. Sandvik achieved 20% growth in 2025 and is targeting similar expansion rates by bringing these innovations to Peru's mining sector. This strategy reflects the company's commitment to modernizing mining operations through electrification and advanced equipment.
