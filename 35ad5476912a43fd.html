Is There a Market for Products Using Green Hydrogen?

12 May 2026 · 10:05 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Law 14.948/2024 established Rehidro (Regime Especial de Incentivos para a Produção de Hidrogênio de Baixa Emissão de Carbono), providing R$18.3 billion in federal tax exemptions, reductions, and suspensions for green hydrogen (H2V) production equipment and services. The regime aims to position Brazil as a global exporter of green hydrogen and derived products, particularly for hard-to-decarbonize industrial sectors. The incentive programme reflects Brazil's strategy to leverage its renewable energy advantage and create value-added exports. This represents significant opportunity for energy transition, industrial chemistry, and infrastructure companies.
