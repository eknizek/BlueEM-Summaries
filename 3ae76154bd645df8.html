Terra Verde: 600-hectare megaproject in Punta Negra marks expansion of southern Lima

24 April 2026 · 12:02 AM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Terra Verde, a planned-city megaproject developed by Inversiones Inmobiliarias Global, spans 600 hectares in Punta Negra in southern Lima and will include residential complexes, commercial space, schools, and hospitals. This large-scale real estate development marks significant infrastructure expansion in Lima's southern suburbs. The mixed-use development model targets both residential and commercial segments. Such projects are typical of Peru's real estate market consolidation and suburban growth strategy.
