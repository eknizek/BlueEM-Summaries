Terra Verde: 600-hectare megaproject in Punta Negra marks southern Lima expansion

24 April 2026 · 12:02 AM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Terra Verde is a major real estate development project in Punta Negra led by Inversiones Inmobiliarias Global, designed as a planned city spanning 600 hectares in southern Lima. The project includes residential housing, commercial space, schools, and hospitals, representing significant capital investment in Lima's southern expansion corridor. The development marks a strategic move in Peru's real estate market for mixed-use urban development.
