Excess information redefines purchasing decisions of first-time parents in childcare in Colombia

19 April 2026 · 09:06 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
A new study reveals that 58% of first-time parents in Colombia experience significant difficulty trusting the childcare information available to them. This information overload is materially affecting consumer purchasing decisions in the childcare sector. The findings suggest a market opportunity for trusted information intermediaries and curated childcare platforms that can filter and validate recommendations for Colombian parents.
