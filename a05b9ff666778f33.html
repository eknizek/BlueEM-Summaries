Information Overload Redefines First-Time Parent Purchasing Decisions in Child Care in Colombia

19 April 2026 · 09:06 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
A study in Colombia reveals that 58% of first-time parents face significant difficulties trusting the information available when making childcare purchasing decisions. Information overload is reshaping how parents evaluate and select childcare products and services in the market. This trend has implications for consumer goods companies marketing to this demographic and suggests an opportunity for trusted, simplified brand messaging. The finding indicates potential market fragmentation and brand loyalty challenges in Colombia's childcare sector.
