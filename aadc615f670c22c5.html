Government Approves Regulations for Special Economic Zones to Attract Investment and Generate Employment

22 April 2026 · 07:50 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
The Peruvian Ministry of Foreign Commerce and Tourism (Mincetur) published decree DS 005-2026-Mincetur, approving regulations for Law 32449 governing the creation, authorization, and operation of Private Special Economic Zones (ZEEP) in Peru. This regulatory framework establishes the legal and operational structure for ZEEP development. The approval represents a key government policy initiative designed to attract foreign and domestic investment while creating employment opportunities in designated economic zones.
