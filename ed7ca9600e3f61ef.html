Stock Exchange Integration Advances, but Trust Remains Colombia's Major Challenge: NUAM Manager Interview

29 April 2026 · 07:43 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Finanzas
Juan Pablo Córdoba, manager of NUAM (the integration vehicle for Colombia's stock exchange), discussed progress in a multi-country stock exchange consolidation project entering its final phase. While technical integration advances, the real challenge lies in rebuilding investor confidence in Colombia's regulatory environment and institutional integrity. Córdoba emphasized that sustainable value creation requires resolving trust deficits in local market governance.
