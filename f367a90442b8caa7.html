Grupo Aeroportuario del Pacífico Announces Initiation of the Process to Establish a FIBRA

09 May 2026 · 06:42 AM  ·  GlobeNewswire
Grupo Aeroportuario del Pacífico (GAP), a major Mexican airport operator, has announced the initiation of a process to establish a FIBRA (Fondo de Inversión en Bienes Raíces, or real estate investment fund). This strategic move likely aims to unlock value from GAP's real estate assets and provide a new capital structure for investors. FIBRAs are Mexico's equivalent to REITs and offer tax-efficient investment vehicles for real estate portfolios.
