Cencosud Renews Leadership: Manfred Paulmann Assumes Board Presidency

24 April 2026 · 05:50 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Cencosud, a major Latin American retail and infrastructure conglomerate, has announced a leadership change with Manfred Paulmann assuming the presidency of the board following the next board session. Julio Moura will step down from the presidency but remain on the board as a director. This executive transition in one of Peru and Latin America's largest retail and logistics companies reflects evolving governance at a key player in consumer retail and infrastructure across the region.
