# Itaú (ITUB4), Vale (VALE3) and More: See 8 Stocks to Seek Dividends in May, According to Empiricus Research

Empiricus Research made a change to its recommended portfolio of stocks to seek dividends in May, removing SLC Agrícola (SLCE3) from the selection and adding Vale (VALE3).

In a report, the firm states that the exit of the commodity producer reflects the worsening cost prospects due to the war in the Middle East. The entry of the mining company is based on the consistent evolution of results, especially in the Basic Metals division (Vale Base Metals).

## Foreign Flow

According to Empiricus, the financial market ended April still surrounded by uncertainties about the conflict between the United States and Iran, which has resulted in increased expectations for inflation and, consequently, for the Selic rate.

"Given this, the Ibovespa even showed decent performance in the month, and counted on positive foreign flow. This only reinforces what we have been saying: even in this context, Brazilian assets remain attractive in general," says the firm, noting, however, that not all companies benefit equally from this flow.

In the institution's assessment, April actually demonstrated that investor preference continues to be for more consolidated companies, with low debt, good cash generation, and lower sensitivity to monetary policy.

## Performance

Empiricus Research's recommended dividend portfolio, which is composed of eight stocks, fell 0.8% last month, compared to a 0.1% drop in the Ibovespa.

In the year-to-date accumulation of 2026, however, the firm's selection advances 28.7% and significantly exceeds the main index of the Brazilian stock exchange, which rises 16.3%.

In the portfolio, the positive highlight in April was Axia Energia (AXIA6), with a gain of 7%, benefiting from the upward revision of energy prices. On the negative side, SLCE3 led the losses, with -9.1%.
