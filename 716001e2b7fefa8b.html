IG4 Closes Definitive Agreement to Acquire Braskem

29 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
IG4, a major Brazilian investment firm, closed a definitive agreement to acquire Braskem, one of Latin America's largest petrochemical companies. This transaction represents IG4's largest acquisition to date and is positioned as potentially one of Brazil's largest corporate restructurings. The deal carries significant implications for the chemicals, materials, and broader industrial sectors in Brazil.
