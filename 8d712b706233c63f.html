Globo Launches Vertical Video App for Mobile Phones

13 April 2026 · 05:06 PM  ·  O Globo
Globo, Brazil's largest media conglomerate, has launched a new vertical video application designed for mobile devices, marking its entry into the competitive short-form video segment dominated by platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels. The app represents Globo's strategic pivot to capture mobile-first audiences and monetise content through this channel. This move reflects broader trends in media consumption in Latin America where vertical video consumption is growing rapidly among younger demographics.
