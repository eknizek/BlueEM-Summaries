# Mexican Peso Trades Stable: Dollar Price on April 27, 2026

The Mexican peso began Monday's session with limited movements against the dollar, driven by a cautious environment among international investors. The Mexican currency is trading at 17.3703 pesos per dollar, remaining virtually unchanged from the 17.3770 units registered in the reference price at Friday's close.

## Geopolitical Factors Anchor the Exchange Rate

The current stability in the exchange rate responds directly to global markets' expectations regarding the situation in the Middle East. Investors are awaiting new developments on negotiations between the United States and Iran, which seek to end the war in the region. This uncertainty has slowed the adoption of risky positions.

## What Will Move the Mexican Peso This Week?

Beyond geopolitics, market participants' attention is focused on two key macroeconomic events that will define the currency's direction in the coming days:

**Federal Reserve Decision (Wednesday):** The Fed's monetary policy decision in the United States will be published. Signals about interest rates are vital for the peso-dollar parity.

**Growth in Mexico (Thursday):** At the local level, the market awaits the preliminary reading of Gross Domestic Product (GDP) for the first quarter of the year, a fundamental indicator to measure the health of the Mexican economy.
