Golden Analytics Debuts with $7M Seed Funding from NEA and Madrona to Build AI-Native Business Intelligence

07 April 2026 · 01:00 PM  ·  Company press release
Golden Analytics has completed a $7 million seed funding round led by New Enterprise Associates (NEA) and Madrona Venture Group to build AI-native business intelligence solutions. The company aims to develop next-generation analytics tools leveraging artificial intelligence for enterprise customers. This funding validates investor confidence in AI-powered software solutions and enterprise analytics markets. The capital will support product development and go-to-market expansion for the startup.
