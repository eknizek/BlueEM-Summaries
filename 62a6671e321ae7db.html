End of Chocolate-Flavored Food? When New Labeling Law on Cocoa Content Takes Effect

12 May 2026 · 09:41 PM  ·  MoneyTimes
Brazil's new Law 15.404/2026 requires all chocolate and cocoa-containing food products sold in Brazil to clearly display the minimum cocoa percentage on packaging. The regulation applies to both domestic and imported products and aims to provide consumers with transparency about product composition. The rule affects the entire chocolate industry's labeling and product formulation practices.
