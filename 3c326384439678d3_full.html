# ArcelorMittal has appetite to continue investing in Brazil, says CEO

ArcelorMittal is concluding this year an investment program that began in 2022 totaling R$ 25 billion, but has no intention of stopping there. The company is advancing in technical feasibility studies to invest between R$ 3.8 billion and R$ 4 billion in a new rolling mill line and a coating line at the Tubarão unit in the municipality of Serra (ES).

According to the CEO in Brazil, Jorge Oliveira, the company is moving forward with these studies to expand its capacity in the state of Espírito Santo.
