Five signals to evaluate companies before doing business: how Colombia's first MCP of business information operates

24 April 2026 · 11:28 AM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
A new business information platform in Colombia—the first Mutual Credit Platform (MCP) of its kind—employs artificial intelligence to analyze companies by integrating financial, labor, and sectoral data. The platform aims to support business decision-making in Colombia's challenging economic environment by providing comprehensive company assessment signals. This fintech solution addresses information asymmetries in the Colombian market and facilitates credit risk evaluation. The emergence of such platforms reflects growing demand for data-driven credit and business intelligence in LatAm's financial ecosystem.
