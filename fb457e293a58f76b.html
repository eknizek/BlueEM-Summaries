Bank of America's strategy for carry trade in Argentina

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Bank of America has developed a strategy for investors to execute carry trades in Argentina by combining inflation-adjusted bonds (UVIs) with currency hedges to capture high dollar-denominated returns while limiting exposure to peso depreciation risk. The strategy leverages Argentina's high real interest rates and inflation-linked instruments. This reflects investor appetite for high-yield opportunities in Argentina despite currency and inflation risks.
