Brazil Registers Positive Foreign Exchange Flow of US$9.3 Billion in April

06 May 2026 · 10:10 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil recorded a total positive foreign exchange flow of US$9.3 billion in April 2026, according to data released by the Brazilian Central Bank on June 6. This represents a notable reversal from the negative flow of US$6.4 billion registered in March 2026, which coincided with escalating tensions between the United States and Israel against Iran. The April recovery in FX flows indicates renewed investor confidence in Brazilian assets following the March geopolitical disruption.
