CFE labor liabilities surge 24.3% in Q1 2025: IMCO analysis

30 April 2026 · 09:09 PM  ·  Reforma - Portada
Mexico's Federal Electricity Commission (CFE) saw labor-related liabilities increase 24.3% annually in the first quarter of 2025, according to analysis by Mexican think tank IMCO. The sharp increase in pension and employment obligations represents a growing fiscal burden on the state-owned utility. The trend raises questions about CFE's long-term financial sustainability and potential impact on Mexico's fiscal position.
