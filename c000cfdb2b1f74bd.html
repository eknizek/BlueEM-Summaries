Minem announces new change in Mining Vice-Ministry

07 April 2026 · 06:41 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
The Peruvian Ministry of Energy and Mines (Minem) announced an executive change, designating Mayra Valderrama to the Vice-Ministry of Mines position following the resignation of Juan Samanez Bilbao, who held the post since February 5, 2026. This ministerial reshuffle affects Peru's mining regulatory and policy direction during a critical period for the sector. The change is relevant for investors tracking mining policy continuity, regulatory enforcement, and government stability in Peru's mining sector.
