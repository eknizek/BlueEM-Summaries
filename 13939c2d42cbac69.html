Dairy Union Leader: SanCor Collapse "Not an End, But a New Beginning"

22 April 2026 · 06:59 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Following SanCor's collapse, Héctor "Etín" Ponce, general secretary of the dairy union Atilra, stated the cooperative faced insurmountable operational challenges under current conditions and blamed management. The union is negotiating with industry participants to preserve employment and operational continuity at the failed cooperative. SanCor's bankruptcy represents a significant disruption in Argentina's dairy sector and signals stress in the cooperative model and agricultural credit markets.
