Grupo Aeroportuario del Pacifico Reports on Impact Arising from the Cessation of Operations of Spirit Airlines

04 May 2026 · 10:10 PM  ·  GlobeNewswire
Grupo Aeroportuario del Pacifico (GAP), Mexico's leading airport operator, has disclosed the impact of Spirit Airlines ceasing operations. Spirit's shutdown affects traffic volume and revenue at GAP's airport network, particularly at hubs serving the US-Mexico route. This development is material to GAP's financial performance and reflects broader consolidation in low-cost carrier capacity in North America.
