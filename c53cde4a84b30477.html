Amermin, Ulterra Take Critical Minerals Recycling Partnership Global

20 April 2026 · 11:44 PM  ·  Mining.com
Amermin and Ulterra expanded their critical minerals recycling partnership to include processing waste streams from Argentina and Canada, in addition to their existing US operations. This global expansion positions the companies to capture value from critical mineral waste streams across multiple jurisdictions, supporting supply chain resilience for battery and technology manufacturers. The partnership reflects growing industry focus on recycling as a source of critical mineral supply, particularly as demand from energy transition applications accelerates.
