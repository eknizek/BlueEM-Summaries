As Desenrola 2.0 Stalls, Banks Offer Their Own Renegotiations

06 May 2026 · 11:01 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Brazilian government's Desenrola 2.0 program, launched on June 4, 2026, experienced significant delays in rolling out through bank channels due to delays in finalizing FGO (Fund of Guarantees for Operations) approvals. The final administrative clearance was only completed on June 6, causing customers to encounter difficulties accessing the program. In response, banks have begun offering their own renegotiation alternatives to customers seeking debt relief, as the government program continued to struggle with its deployment.
