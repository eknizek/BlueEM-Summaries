Mexico and US Complete Preparation Phase for T-MEC Review

20 April 2026 · 09:05 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Mexico's Secretary of Economy Marcelo Ebrard confirmed that Mexico and the United States have concluded the preparatory phase ahead of the next review of the USMCA (T-MEC in Spanish). The trade agreement, which replaced NAFTA, undergoes periodic reviews to assess its implementation and potential adjustments. This milestone signals both nations are ready to enter substantive negotiations on tariffs, rules of origin, labor standards, and other key provisions that affect cross-border commerce and investment flows across North America.
