What Desenrola 1 did for Lula 3 and what Desenrola 2 can do for Lula 4

02 May 2026 · 08:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Brazilian government is launching Desenrola 2 ('New Desenrola Brasil'), a debt restructuring initiative with Treasury backing aimed at encouraging households and businesses to renegotiate and reduce outstanding debts. The program builds on the success of Desenrola 1 from the previous administration. This initiative is designed to address consumer and corporate debt burdens while stimulating economic activity.
