Latam reports 62% earnings increase and adjusts forecasts due to fuel cost impact

05 May 2026 · 11:36 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Latam Airlines, Chile's flagship carrier, reported a 62% increase in net earnings but adjusted forward guidance based on revised fuel cost expectations. The airline now assumes oil prices around US$170 per barrel for Q2–Q3 2026 and US$150 per barrel for Q4, reflecting commodity price volatility impacts on airline operations. This adjustment demonstrates the sensitivity of airline profitability to global energy markets and the challenge of maintaining margin guidance amid fuel price uncertainty.
