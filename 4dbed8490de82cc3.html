Government Evaluates Including Self-Employed Workers in Debt Negotiation; Considers Restricting Betting Wagers

04 April 2026 · 02:01 PM  ·  Valor Economico
The Brazilian government is evaluating measures to include MEIs (microentrepreneurs and self-employed workers) in debt negotiation programs while also restricting consumer participation in online betting platforms. This dual policy approach addresses credit stress among informal economy participants and consumer financial health. For investors, debt restructuring programs targeting MEIs signal government concern over household leverage in lower-income segments, while betting restrictions may impact fintech and gaming companies' revenue models. These regulatory moves reflect broader fiscal and social policy priorities affecting consumer-facing businesses and financial services in Brazil.
