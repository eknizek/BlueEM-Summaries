Scotiabank projects inflation in Peru at 3.2% by end of 2026

21 April 2026 · 01:36 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Scotiabank revised upward its inflation forecast for Peru to 3.2% by end-2026, from a prior estimate of 2.2%, citing geopolitical tensions in the Middle East and closure of the Strait of Hormuz. Higher oil and commodity prices flowing from supply disruption risk threaten to push Peruvian inflation above central bank targets. This macro shift has direct implications for LatAm monetary policy, FX, and real asset returns.
