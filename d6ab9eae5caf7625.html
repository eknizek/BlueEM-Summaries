Scotiabank revises Peru's end-2026 inflation forecast to 3.2%

21 April 2026 · 01:36 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Scotiabank has revised upward its inflation forecast for Peru, increasing the end-2026 estimate from 2.2% to 3.2%. The revision reflects global geopolitical tensions, specifically the escalation of conflict in the Middle East and concerns about potential closure of the Strait of Hormuz, which would disrupt oil and commodity supply chains. Higher inflation expectations have implications for Peru's monetary policy, real interest rates, and purchasing power for consumers and businesses.
