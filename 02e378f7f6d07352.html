Itaú Delivers ROE of 24.8% with Controlled Delinquency

05 May 2026 · 11:43 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
Itaú, Brazil's largest bank, reported first quarter 2026 net income of R$12.3 billion, representing a slight 0.3% decline versus Q1 2025 but a 10.4% year-over-year increase excluding the effect of an accelerated dividend distribution in late 2025. The bank achieved a ROE of 24.8%, maintaining strong profitability metrics. Results aligned closely with market consensus expectations. Delinquency rates remained under control, reflecting solid credit risk management despite macroeconomic pressures.
