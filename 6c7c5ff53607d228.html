Investment Fund Administration Industry Grew 23.5% Last Year, Recording Record AUM of $43.85 Billion

20 April 2026 · 11:49 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Chile's investment fund administration industry (ACAFI members) expanded 23.5% in the previous year, reaching record assets under management of $43.85 billion. The new ACAFI president, María José Montero, identified increasing institutional investor participation as a major strategic challenge, framing it as a systemic design issue rather than a market limitation. This growth reflects robust capital market activity and institutional investor engagement in Chile.
