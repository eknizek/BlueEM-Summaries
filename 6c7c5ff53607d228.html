Investment Fund Administrator Industry Grew 23.5% Last Year, Setting Record Assets Under Management of $43.853 Billion

20 April 2026 · 11:49 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Chile's investment fund administration industry achieved significant growth in 2025, expanding 23.5% year-over-year with assets under management reaching a record $43.853 billion USD. The newly appointed president of ACAFI (the association of fund administrators), María José Montero, highlighted institutional participation growth as a key challenge, identifying system design as a critical lever. The robust growth reflects strong demand for asset management services and institutional investor interest in Chilean capital markets.
