Vicuña Accelerates Copper Project with US$800 Million Investments in 2026, Awaits RIGI

07 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Vicuña, the copper joint venture resulting from Lundin and BHP's merger, is accelerating its copper project in Argentina with planned investments of US$800 million in 2026. The company expects to make a final investment decision by year-end and has submitted its project to Argentina's RIGI (Investment Promotion Regime) for incentive eligibility. The total project investment is valued at US$18 billion, representing a major commitment to Argentina's mining sector.
