# Banxico Deputy Governor Heath Warns Rate Cuts Amplify Inflation Risk

The risks to inflation have increased due to the armed conflict in the Middle East, "we lose a lot" if we reduce the rate and it would give the wrong impression of less commitment to the Banco de México's (Banxico) priority mandate, warned Jonathan Heath, deputy governor of the central bank.

Heath was one of the board members who voted against reducing the benchmark interest rate by 25 basis points to a level of 6.75 percent.
