# Insurance Association Backs Government Reactivation Plan: "Measures Point in Correct Direction"

Another national business group joined in supporting the government's reactivation plan, announced Wednesday evening in a national broadcast by President José Antonio Kast. This time, it was the president of the Insurance Association of Chile (AACH), Alejandro Alzérreca, who praised the announced draft law.

"From the Insurance Association of Chile, we value the Plan for Economic and Social Reconstruction and Development announced by the government, in a context where the country faces relevant challenges regarding growth, employment, and economic dynamism," said the business representative through a statement.

"We believe that the measures announced point in the correct direction, by focusing on returning to the path of sustained growth, strengthening formal employment, and creating conditions of greater certainty for investment—key elements for advancing greater well-being and opportunities for people," he added.

Alzérreca concluded by showing the AACH's willingness to collaborate with authorities on matters related to the future legal initiative.
