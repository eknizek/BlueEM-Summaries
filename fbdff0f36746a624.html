'Rebuilding trust emerges as the greatest challenge for the new Government': Comfama

20 April 2026 · 07:36 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Comfama, a major Colombian health and social security provider, has announced plans to increase its investments by 14.5% in the current year, with total capex exceeding $60 billion. According to its director David Escobar, rebuilding trust has become the most significant challenge facing the organization amid the new government administration. The significant investment increase reflects Comfama's commitment to expand its service capacity and institutional presence. This statement underscores broader concerns about institutional confidence in Colombia following recent political transitions.
