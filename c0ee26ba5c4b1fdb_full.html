# Colsubsidio Launches 'Hunt Your Home 2026' Housing Fair in Bogotá: Requirements, Dates, and Projects with Subsidies Up to 52 Million Pesos

A new edition of the 'Caza tu casa 2026' housing fair is coming to Colombia's capital, an event organized by Colsubsidio that seeks to centralize real estate and financing options for citizens. From April 24 to 26, interested parties will be able to access advisory services and projects in a space designed to facilitate the acquisition of personal properties through various forms of economic support and mortgage credit.

The event will take place at Club El Cubo Colsubsidio, located at Carrera 30 # 52-77, in the Teusaquillo sector, with continuous hours from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

## Required Documents and Requirements for Soliciting Home Credit

To advance in the purchasing process and receive immediate financial evaluation from more than 15 construction companies and banks present, the organization recommends that users prepare basic documentation. This step is essential to determine how much can be paid monthly and understand the payment plan for each project.

Required documents include:

- Recent employment certificate

- Certification of severance savings

- Proof of monthly income

## Subsidies and Economic Benefits Available During the Housing Fair

According to official data from the compensation fund, the event functions as a channel for citizens to reduce the final value of their home by combining different economic supports. In this space, one can validate access to various forms of financial aid:

- Up to 52 million pesos under the housing subsidy concept

- A maximum of 24 million pesos for rental subsidies

- Up to 17 million pesos managed through district subsidies

- Guidance for home improvement subsidies or construction on personal property

Additionally, attendees can access 'mega-discounts' of up to 65 million pesos and financing plans covering up to 100 percent of the total property value, including preferential rates that facilitate financial closure for Colombian families.

## VIS and Non-VIS Projects with Immediate Delivery Options in Bogotá

The real estate offering of 'Caza tu casa 2026' includes more than 100 projects distributed throughout the country, with options that adjust to different housing needs. According to event information, buyers can find properties with fixed prices starting from 149 million pesos in areas with nearby services such as schools and transportation.

Among the highlighted advantages for visitors are:

- Housing down payments starting at 200,000 pesos

- Availability of apartments for immediate delivery

- Projects with Edge certification and zero down payment purchase options

- Personalized advisory with social support through the deed transfer

- Home insurance with coverage against damage, theft, and unforeseen events

This initiative seeks to make the dream of homeownership more accessible through the support and solidity of an entity with extensive experience in the sector. Upon closing the fair, interested parties will receive cost-free follow-up and expert guidance to finalize their home purchase process.
