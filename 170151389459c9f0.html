Stellantis Establishes Chinese EV Distribution Network in Argentina Through Leapmotor Brand

21 April 2026 · 04:31 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Automaker Stellantis has allocated a network of 11 dealerships to sell Leapmotor vehicles in Argentina, the Chinese EV brand in which it holds equity. This strategic move comes as Stellantis manages its existing Argentine operations, including Fiat's Córdoba plant and Peugeot's El Palomar facility. However, the El Palomar plant is reducing production from two shifts to one effective May 1st due to declining export volumes and weak domestic demand, reflecting broader challenges in Argentina's automotive sector.
