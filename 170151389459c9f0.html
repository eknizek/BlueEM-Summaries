Stellantis builds Chinese car business in Argentina with Leapmotor distribution network

21 April 2026 · 04:31 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Stellantis is expanding into China-made vehicles through a 11-dealership network for Leapmotor, a brand in which it holds equity. Simultaneously, the conglomerate is scaling back Argentine manufacturing, reducing Peugeot El Palomar production from two to one shift starting May 1st amid falling export orders and domestic demand weakness. The dual strategy reflects industry adaptation to local market contraction and shift toward imported electric vehicle offerings.
