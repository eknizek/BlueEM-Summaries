# Peso Closes April with Losses! US Dollar Price Today April 30, 2026

The Mexican peso registered a depreciation this Thursday, pressured by a challenging economic environment at both the local and international levels. Recent reports on the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) of Mexico and the United States have generated uncertainty in the markets, directly impacting the exchange rate. Despite this setback, the national currency maintains a positive overall balance for the month, but investors remain cautious about the new quarterly figures.

## At what price is the dollar trading today?

During the session, the exchange rate stood at 17.55 pesos per dollar. This figure represents a loss of 0.15% for the Mexican currency compared to Wednesday's reference price. However, the medium-term outlook is not entirely bleak. Despite today's decline, the Mexican peso has accumulated a positive return of 2.1% throughout the entire month of April, demonstrating some resilience amid global volatility.

## Why did the Mexican currency depreciate?

The peso's decline is primarily due to two key economic factors revealed in the first quarter of the year:

**1. Economic contraction in Mexico**

The local factor that weighed most heavily on the currency was the report on the national Gross Domestic Product (GDP), which contracted between January and March more sharply than analysts expected. This decline marks the first negative reading of the Mexican economy since late 2024. The downturn was driven by a widespread deterioration across all components of the economic indicator.

**2. Below-expectations growth in the US**

On the international front, US GDP also played a crucial role. Although the neighboring country's economy managed to rebound in the first quarter—driven mainly by an increase in public spending after overcoming a government shutdown—the actual growth did not meet the expectations that the market had projected.

This combination of a contracted local economy and a less accelerated US engine than expected has been the perfect catalyst for the slight depreciation that the peso experienced today.
