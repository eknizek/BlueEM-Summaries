# Congressional Push to Integrate Mexico Water Treaty with USMCA

Republican congressmen proposed to the Trump Administration integrating the 1944 Water Treaty with Mexico into the text of the North American trade agreement (USMCA) in order to be able to force the Mexican Government to pay its debt in water delivery from the Rio Grande.

Republican congressmen proposed to President Donald Trump's Administration to integrate the 1944 Water Treaty with Mexico into the North American trade agreement (USMCA) to be able to compel the Mexican Government to settle its debt in water deliveries from the Rio Bravo River.
