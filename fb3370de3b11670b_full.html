# Request to integrate Water Treaty with Mexico into T-MEC

Republican congressmen proposed to the Administration of President Donald Trump to integrate the 1944 Water Treaty with Mexico into the text of the North American trade agreement (T-MEC) in order to force the Mexican Government to pay its debt in the delivery of water from the Rio Bravo.

**Washington DC, United States (April 24, 2026) — 17:37 hrs**

Republican congressmen proposed to the Trump Administration to integrate the 1944 Water Treaty with Mexico into the North American free trade agreement (T-MEC) in order to be able to force the Mexican government to settle its debt in water deliveries from the Rio Bravo River.
