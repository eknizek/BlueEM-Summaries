Roncador Lost Money in Its Mines. Then It Discovered 'a Periodic Table'

27 April 2026 · 04:41 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
Grupo Roncador, under CEO Pelerson Penido Dalla Vecchia (Peleco), shifted its business model from traditional mining operations that were generating losses to focus on extracting and marketing macronutrients and mineral compounds from rock for agricultural applications. The company discovered substantial value in understanding the geological characteristics and nutritional content of its mineral reserves. This pivot positions Roncador in the high-margin agriculture input sector, specifically targeting soil nutrients and crop productivity.
