# Dollar Price in Peru: Check the Exchange Rate for Monday, April 27

Check the buy and sell quotes for the US dollar in the country for Monday, April 27.

## How much did the dollar close on Friday, April 24, 2026?

The price of the dollar in the country closed on Friday, April 24 at 3.4885 (opening at 3.4650), according to the Central Reserve Bank of Peru (BCRP).

The interbank exchange rate closed with an average value of 3.4781, with a maximum of 3.4880 and a minimum of 3.4690.

## What factors affect the dollar price in Peru?

Factors such as BCRP policies, political tensions, and fluctuations in international markets affect the exchange rate.

## What is the Ocoña dollar?

The Ocoña dollar is the "street dollar," that is, the informal exchange rate, the one bought and sold in the parallel market or exchange houses. Ocoña Street and the "Ocoña dollar" continue to be in force as a reference for the parallel market for buying and selling dollars.

Source: BBVA

## Where to see the dollar price?

The official closing quote of the day is found on the website of the Superintendency of Banking, Insurance and AFP (SBS).

## What is the difference between the buy and sell price of the dollar?

- **Buy price:** what the buyer is willing to pay to acquire the asset.

- **Sell price:** what the seller is willing to receive to sell the asset.

## What does the dollar price depend on?

It depends on the supply and demand of the market. How difficult it is to get it for someone who needs it and how easy it is to sell it for someone who has it.
