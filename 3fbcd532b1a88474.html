As Consumer Spending Tightens, Divino Fogão Sees Fixed-Price Meals Jump 52% in Q1 2026

26 April 2026 · 01:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Divino Fogão, a Brazilian restaurant franchise chain with over 250 units, experienced 52% growth in its fixed-price meal offering during Q1 2026, with this format now representing the majority of sales. Fixed-price meals, where customers pay one set amount regardless of portion size, appeal to cost-conscious consumers trading down from traditional per-kilogram pricing. The shift reflects weakening consumer purchasing power and demand for predictable, value-oriented dining formats with an average check of R$55.
