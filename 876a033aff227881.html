São Paulo Deposits R$561 Million for Santos-Guarujá Tunnel Expropriation and Demands Federal Support

16 April 2026 · 10:02 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
São Paulo's government transferred R$561.9 million to Portuguese contractor Mota-Engil to cover expropriations and resettlements required for the Santos-Guarujá tunnel infrastructure project. In announcing the payment, Governor Tarcísio's administration demanded that the federal government deliver on its promised funding commitments for the project. The tunnel is a key infrastructure initiative requiring coordination between state and federal resources.
