Why massive AI investment has not yet moved the ROI needle

08 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Despite record-breaking investment in artificial intelligence technologies, corporate AI initiatives are struggling to deliver concrete financial returns. The primary bottleneck is not technology or capital availability, but organizational culture and resistance to change within companies. This cultural misalignment prevents the translation of AI spending into tangible business results, suggesting that implementation challenges are as critical as technical infrastructure.
