Power generator advocates mandatory battery storage for new solar parks to prevent grid curtailment

03 May 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Atlas Renewable Energy, one of Brazil's largest power generators, is advocating for mandatory battery storage integration in new solar power plants to mitigate grid curtailment issues. Excess solar energy generation during peak daylight hours creates significant operational bottlenecks, requiring the National Electric System Operator (ONS) to implement daily power cuts. This policy push reflects the critical need for energy storage infrastructure to optimize renewable integration and grid stability, making it highly relevant for renewable energy investors and power infrastructure developers in Brazil.
