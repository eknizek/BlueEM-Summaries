Russell 1000 Index Inclusion Could Unlock Capital Flow for JBS; Brokers Reiterate 'Buy'

07 April 2026 · 05:30 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
JBS, the Brazilian protein and agribusiness company, is gaining analyst attention due to the potential inclusion in the Russell 1000 index, scheduled for June 26 rebalancing. Inclusion would expose the stock to passive and active capital flows from US institutional investors, supporting the equity price. Santander and other brokers have reiterated buy recommendations on the expectation that index rebalancing could unlock significant capital flows. For LatAm-focused investors, this represents a key catalyst for a large Brazilian agribusiness exporter and signals renewed interest from US-based fund managers.
