JPMorgan Recommends Buying Stocks on Declines and Anticipates New Market Momentum

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
JPMorgan Chase has issued a market recommendation suggesting investors should buy equities during price declines, citing a disconnect between market rate expectations and economic fundamentals. The bank argues that current market pricing reflects an excessively restrictive interest rate scenario that is not justified by underlying economic data. Despite recent volatility, corporate profits and economic activity continue to demonstrate resilience, supporting the bank's bullish outlook and suggesting a foundation for renewed market strength.
