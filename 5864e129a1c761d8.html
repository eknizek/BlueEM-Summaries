Brazil's Tax Burden Rises to 32.4% of GDP in 2025, Highest Level on Record

10 April 2026 · 08:32 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's total tax burden climbed to 32.4% of GDP in 2025, amounting to R$4.12 trillion according to data released by the National Treasury. This represents the highest level in the country's historical records and signals ongoing fiscal pressures and increased taxation on businesses and individuals. For LatAm investors, this trend underscores challenges to corporate profitability, business competitiveness, and return on investment in Brazil, while also reflecting the government's fiscal consolidation efforts.
