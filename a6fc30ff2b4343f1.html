Fictor's Judicial Recovery Request Accepted by São Paulo Court

18 April 2026 · 06:04 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's São Paulo Court of Justice (TJSP) approved the judicial recovery petition for Fictor Holding and Fictor Invest on Friday evening, following the companies' declaration of R$4.2 billion in liabilities. The companies now have 60 days to present a restructuring plan to creditors. This marks a significant corporate restructuring event for a major Brazilian holding company and is relevant for creditors, investors in related entities, and the broader Brazilian credit market.
