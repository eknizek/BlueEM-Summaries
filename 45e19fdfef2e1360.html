USMCA annual reviews could weaken Mexican peso by 7%, BBVA Mexico warns

16 April 2026 · 05:37 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
BBVA México warned that annual reviews of the USMCA (United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement) could exert downward pressure on the Mexican peso, potentially driving it to the 18.0–18.50 range per U.S. dollar—a 7% decline from prevailing levels. The bank's analysis highlights the treaty's significance for currency markets and broader Mexican macroeconomic stability. This directly impacts cross-border investment returns and capital flow dynamics in the region.
