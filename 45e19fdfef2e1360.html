USMCA annual reviews could weaken Mexican peso to 18.0–18.50 per dollar — BBVA México

16 April 2026 · 05:37 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
BBVA México analysis suggests that the annual review mechanism of the USMCA (T-MEC) could trigger peso depreciation of approximately 7%, pushing the currency to a range of 18.0–18.50 per U.S. dollar. The potential weakness reflects uncertainty around trade terms and competitiveness concerns. For LatAm investors with Mexican peso exposure, equity positions, or cross-border transactions, FX volatility tied to trade policy revisions is a material macro risk factor affecting returns and hedging strategies.
