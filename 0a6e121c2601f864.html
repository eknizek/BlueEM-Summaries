Argentina posts 'historic' March trade surplus as exports surge

21 April 2026 · 06:42 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times (Argentina)
Argentina recorded a notable trade surplus of USD 2.5 billion in March, described as historic, fueled by surging exports according to INDEC (National Statistics Institute) data. The energy sector accounted for nearly half of the surplus, indicating strong contribution from oil and gas exports. This performance reflects improved external sector dynamics for Argentina and suggests benefits from energy production increases and export competitiveness in global markets.
