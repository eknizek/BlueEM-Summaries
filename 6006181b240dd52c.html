Movement launched in response to insolvency cartel allegations, rejects system stigmatization

23 April 2026 · 12:57 AM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
A new movement has been launched in Colombia to counter allegations of cartelization within the insolvency system, following formal complaints filed by Asobancaria (the Colombian Banking Association). The initiative aims to defend the integrity of insolvency proceedings and reject broader stigmatization of the system. This dispute highlights concerns about competitive practices and regulatory oversight in Colombia's credit and insolvency markets.
