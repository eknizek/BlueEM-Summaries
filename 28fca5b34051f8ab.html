The Next Decade of E-Commerce in Latin America Depends on Delivery

27 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Mercado Libre CEO Fernando Yunes outlines the critical factors for e-commerce expansion across Latin America over the next decade. The region demonstrates significant untapped potential but faces structural challenges: underdeveloped logistics networks, limited product selection in many markets, and financial inclusion gaps among consumers. Success requires coordinated improvements in last-mile delivery infrastructure, expanded merchant catalogs, and greater access to credit and payment solutions for underbanked populations.
