SMU President Pilar Dañobeitía: Shareholders Committed to Continue Investing

25 April 2026 · 05:31 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
SMU's president Pilar Dañobeitía announced a three-year capital investment plan of 370 billion pesos encompassing three new distribution centers in Chile, additional retail locations, and expansion into Piura, northern Peru. The company plans to finance investments through operational cash flow rather than external capital raises. The statement reinforces shareholder confidence in the company despite recent public challenges facing controlling shareholder Álvaro Saieh and his broader business holdings.
