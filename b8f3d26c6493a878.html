Pilar Dañobeitía, SMU president: 'Our shareholders are convinced they want to keep investing'

25 April 2026 · 05:31 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
SMU, controlled by entities of Álvaro Saieh's business group, announced a three-year strategic investment plan valued at CLP 370 billion. The plan includes opening three new distribution centers in Chile, expanding store count, and strengthening presence in Peru's northern Piura region. SMU president Pilar Dañobeitía stated shareholders remain committed to continued investment despite public difficulties faced by Saieh. Funding will come from the company's operational results. This signals confidence in SMU's growth strategy across the region.
