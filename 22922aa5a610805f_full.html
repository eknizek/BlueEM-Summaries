# SAE Leases Houses, Apartments, and Farms Nationwide with Rates Starting from 80,000 Pesos: Here Are the Requirements You Must Meet

The Special Assets Society (SAE), an entity linked to the Ministry of Finance, is offering houses, apartments, and farms for rent in different areas of Colombia with rates starting from 80,000 pesos. Interested parties can consult the complete offer through its official website. On the platform, users will find a search tool that allows filtering by property type, location (department and city), and price range.

These are special properties that have been subject to judicial proceedings. It is important to note that the assets under the management of the entity are special goods that have been subject to domain extinction processes, so to carry out the rental process, people must meet specific conditions.

## Details of Offered Properties

Upon entering the digital catalog, each property has a detailed technical file that includes photographs, the area in square meters, the exact location, and the current condition of the property.

**1. Rural Property in Córdoba: $80,000**

Among the available offers is a rural property called El Reposo, located in the El Ébano village, in the municipality of Los Córdobas (Córdoba).

**2. Lot in San Bernardo del Viento: $340,000**

In the department of Córdoba, specifically in the municipality of San Bernardo del Viento, the entity offers for rent a lot located at Calle 8C #22-301.

**3. Apartment in San Gil: $580,000**

In the municipality of San Gil, in Santander, the SAE placed an apartment of 75.19 square meters of built area for rent, located in the San Carlos Building.

**4. Commercial Space in Bogotá: $850,000**

In the capital of the country, the entity has a commercial space of 8 square meters of built area, located in the Santa Fe Plaza Business Center (Carrera 19 #12-84, local 2-244).

It is essential that people review whether the property requires any type of adaptation, as many of these are delivered in the condition they were received after the judicial proceedings. The process is carried out directly with the State and without intermediaries.

## Requirements for Renting

- Complete the rental application form

- Have a copy of the renter's and co-debtors' citizenship ID

- Have employment certification and payroll statements or, in the case of self-employed individuals, prepare financial statements and bank statements

- Have one or more co-debtors with real estate property or demonstrated solvency

- Declare no kinship with SAE officials

- Not be a defaulting debtor

It is important to remember that the process is carried out directly with the entity, without intermediaries and free of charge. For any questions, it is recommended to consult through official service channels.
