Association seeks annulment of ANTT resolution imposing fines up to R$10 million

25 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Brazilian Collaborative Freight Association (Abrafrec) filed a petition requesting annulment of ANTT's new Penalty Framework resolution, arguing it contains constitutional defects and threatens small freight operators with potential market exit due to penalties up to R$10 million. The regulatory action reflects tightening enforcement in transportation and logistics, a sector critical to Brazilian supply chains and economic competitiveness. Regulatory uncertainty and potential operator consolidation will impact freight economics, inflation pressures, and supply chain costs. Investors in logistics, transportation, and supply chain services face material regulatory risk and market structure shifts.
