Trucking association seeks annulment of ANTT resolution imposing fines up to R$10 million

25 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Abrafrec, the Brazilian collaborative freight association, filed a petition with ANTT (National Land Transport Agency) to annul a resolution implementing the Penalty Benchmark (Marco das Penalidades). The association argues the regulation contains legal defects making it unconstitutional and threatens to eliminate small transport companies from the market. This represents regulatory risk and operational cost concerns for the Brazilian transportation and logistics sector.
