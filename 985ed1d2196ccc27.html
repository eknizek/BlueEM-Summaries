Santa Fe Livestock Producer Battles Against Flooding of 5,000 Hectares as Agricultural Emergency Declared

21 April 2026 · 04:21 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Producer Josefina Saliva in Santa Fe's Vera department is racing to save livestock as approximately 5,000 of her hectares remain underwater. Regional estimates suggest around 1 million hectares across northern Santa Fe departments are flooded, prompting calls for an agricultural emergency declaration. The disaster threatens significant agricultural output and livestock operations in one of Argentina's key farming regions, with potential impacts on export capacity and rural employment.
