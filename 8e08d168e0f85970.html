American Express Global Business Travel Enters $6.3 Billion Take-Private Deal

04 May 2026 · 12:25 PM  ·  Wall Street Journal
American Express has agreed to take Global Business Travel (GBT) private in a $6.3 billion deal, combining two major players in corporate travel and expense management. The acquisition allows Amex to vertically integrate travel services with its payment and corporate card ecosystem, enhancing customer retention and data insights. This represents significant consolidation in the B2B travel services sector and strengthens Amex's position as a comprehensive corporate financial services provider.
