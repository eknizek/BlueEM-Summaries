Union's pre-sal oil production reaches 182,000 barrels per day in February, PPSA reports

Oil production in the pre-sal region whose share belongs to the Union reached 182,000 barrels per day in February, 38.9% above the 131,000 barrels per day recorded in February 2025, according to Pré-Sal Petróleo (PPSA), the state company responsible for managing production-sharing contracts.

The volume extracted in February this year is 8.9% higher than in January.

By Fábio Couto, Valor — Rio de Janeiro
