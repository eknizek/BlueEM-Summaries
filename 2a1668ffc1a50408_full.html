# Billy Escobar Resigns from Superintendencia de Sociedades After Four Years of Management

Billy Escobar Pérez resigned from the Superintendencia de Sociedades after more than four years leading the entity, a position he has held since December 2021.

"I am informing the country that I have made the decision to advance in other professional projects as of this date," the official stated. He also assured that this is a "personal decision, adopted after considering the commitments demanded by this responsibility to be fulfilled."

Billy Escobar highlighted that during his time in office, his management focused on strengthening the technical capacity of the entity and expanding territorial presence. He equally focused on developing effective tools to support business owners from "a social vision, contributing to the sustainability, productivity, and competitiveness of the country."

"I thank the President of the Republic, Gustavo Petro, for the confidence placed in me and for the guarantees of independence that made it possible to exercise this work with rigor, transparency, and technical criteria," he stated through a statement.

He also highlighted that during his tenure he managed to position the Supersociedades as a recognized benchmark at the national and international level. "I will continue contributing to the country from wherever I find myself, with the conviction that institutional strengthening and business development are pillars of Colombia's progress," he concluded.
