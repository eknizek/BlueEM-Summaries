Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Alert Over Food Warning Label Changes

29 April 2026 · 10:58 PM  ·  Portfolio - Negocios
Colombia's National SME Observatory has raised concerns about the financial and operational impact of new food warning label requirements on micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs). The observatory is calling for adequate transition periods to allow businesses time to adapt to labeling changes. This regulatory shift affects food producers and retailers across Colombia and represents a compliance cost consideration for small-cap food and retail businesses.
