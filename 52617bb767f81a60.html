BOND: AI startup for SME accounting raises R$10 million

15 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
BOND, an artificial intelligence startup founded four months ago to automate accounting for small and medium enterprises (SMEs), closed a R$10 million funding round led by US venture capital firm Propel. The company targets Brazil's highly fragmented SME accounting market, which historically underinvests in technology, positioning itself to capture significant growth as businesses digitalize back-office operations.
