# Indecopi applies anti-dumping duty to steel wire rod imports from China

The Dumping, Subsidies and Elimination of Non-Tariff Trade Barriers Commission (CDB) of the National Institute for the Defense of Competition and Protection of Intellectual Property (Indecopi) decided to impose definitive anti-dumping duties on imports of steel wire rod originating from China.

The Dumping, Subsidies and Elimination of Non-Tariff Trade Barriers Commission (CDB) of the National Institute for the Defense of Competition and Protection of Intellectual Property (Indecopi) decided to impose definitive anti-dumping duties on imports of steel wire rod originating from China. This arises within the framework of the investigation initiated by the CDB in January 2025 regarding these imports.

According to resolution 065-2026/CDB-Indecopi, the anti-dumping duty will be US$81.3 per ton of steel wire rod. This measure applies to Chinese exporters Angang Steel Co. Ltd.; Angang Group Hong Kong Co., Limited; Benxi North Steel Rolling Co., Ltd; Benxi Iron & Steel Hong Kong Limited and other exporters.

The measure will take effect the day following its publication in El Peruano.

As recalled, the investigation arose from a request presented on October 10, 2024, by Corporación Aceros Arequipa to the CDB.
