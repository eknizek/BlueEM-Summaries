# Sip Digital Wallet Launches with Cashback Rewards and Bill Payment Functionality for Peruvian Consumers

Sip, the new digital wallet that improves the way people pay and manage their daily expenses, has been announced. It combines an intuitive experience with concrete benefits, such as cashback of up to 1% on purchases made, in addition to exclusive discounts and other facilities designed for Peruvian consumers, such as paying for schools, basic services like water, taxis, and other daily expenses.

"An important differentiator compared to other wallets on the market is the return of a percentage of purchases made daily by our customers. This cashback can be used to shop at stores like plazaVea, Inkafarma, Promart, Oechsle, Mifarma, and Mass. In this way, our customers are generating recurring savings," said Renzo Castellano, general manager of Infinance XP, a company that is part of the Intercorp group.

"Our customers can continue shopping in the same places, with the added advantage of receiving cash refunds for each transaction made," added Castellano.

It is worth noting that Sip incorporates savings accounts with free availability in soles and dollars with the best market rates, debit and credit cards, QR payments, fixed-term deposits, bill payments, exchange rates, and loans.

Additionally, customers can "sip" (transfer money) to their family and friends immediately and with total security, using their mobile phone number.

"Sip integrates the financial products and rewards of users of Financiera oh! and agora, which allows us to have a user base of 3.9 million users from launch. This positions us from the start as a relevant competitor in the digital wallet and financial products market," added Castellano.

As of today, Sip is available for download on the App Store and Google Play. Users can update or download the application, register, and start using it.
