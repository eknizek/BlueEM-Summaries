Invima had warned about sale of counterfeit yellow aguardiente using Yeison Jiménez's image

18 April 2026 · 05:47 PM

# Invima Had Warned About Sale of Counterfeit Yellow Aguardiente Using Yeison Jiménez's Image

Industria Licorera de Caldas announced this week that a special edition of Manzanares Yellow Aguardiente will soon be released as a tribute to popular music singer Yeison Jiménez, who died this year in an air accident.

According to the company, the initiative seeks to exalt the life, cultural legacy, and trajectory of the artist, recognized for his contribution to Colombian popular music and for proudly carrying the name of his hometown Manzanares (Caldas) to national and international stages.

While this special edition will soon be released to the market (the sale date has not yet been confirmed), Invima had issued a health alert regarding the illegal sale of a counterfeit product that illegally used the name of Industria Licorera de Caldas's flagship brand. According to the entity and the ILC, this product is counterfeited by people who irregularly use the health registry and has no quality or safety guarantees for consumption.

The situation was confirmed and reported to Invima by Industria Licorera de Caldas, the holder of the Health Registry, which stated that it recognizes the product identified as counterfeit and fraudulent, which is why it is confirmed that its distribution and commercialization lacks health and quality guarantees.

Keep in mind that only now will the brand launch the product, which will be available at authorized sales points.

## How to Identify a Counterfeit Product

Invima revealed which elements you should consider to identify whether a product is counterfeit or fraudulent:

- The counter-label does not present the historical reference of the product's origin

- The counter-label does not present the mandatory information "Made in Colombia"

- The counter-label does not present the mandatory information of Manufacturer Name and Address

- The counter-label does not present the mandatory Health Registry information

- The counter-label does not present the mandatory legend "Sale of alcoholic beverages to minors is prohibited"

- The counter-label shows the image, name, and logo of singer Yeison Jiménez, which is not part of the original labeling of the product authorized by Invima

## Special Edition

This week, the liquor company announced the launch of this special edition. According to Diego Angelillis Quiceno, general manager of Industria Licorera de Caldas, it is "a tribute made with the heart, to remember an artist who created a strong bond with millions of fans in the country and who helped us promote as an ambassador the Manzanares Yellow Aguardiente."

The bottle design features a differentiated presentation that highlights the image and history of Yeison Jiménez, in harmony with the attributes, values, and origin of the product, which share territorial identity with the artist.

"On the label we have the image of Yeison Jiménez authorized by his family, whom we approached with great respect to propose this tribute, which we believe is very deserved and that all his followers were expecting," said Angelillis Quiceno.

With this limited edition aimed at both collectors and followers of the artist, Industria Licorera de Caldas reaffirms its commitment to promoting culture and recognizing figures who have marked Colombia's recent history, keeping alive the legacy of those who have contributed to strengthening Caldas pride.
