JHSF acquires Enjoy Punta del Este for $160 million, exports 'Cidade Jardim' concept to Uruguay

14 April 2026 · 01:24 PM  ·  Neofeed
JHSF Península, part of JHSF UK (controlling entity of JHSF Participações), completed the acquisition of the Enjoy Punta del Este complex in Uruguay for $160 million on April 14. The Brazilian real estate developer plans to rebrand and operate the property under its premium Cidade Jardim concept, extending its residential and lifestyle offerings beyond Brazil into the Uruguayan market. This cross-border acquisition demonstrates JHSF's expansion strategy in luxury real estate within South America.
