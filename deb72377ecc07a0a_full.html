# Nobel Prize, Steady Sales: How Fósforo Became a Sustainable Publisher in 5 Years

What do sociologist José Henrique Bortoluci, chef Bel Coelho, and photojournalist Gabriela Biló have in common? They are all published by Fósforo, which is celebrating five years this May.

Among the authors with the best-selling books are Annie Ernaux, José Henrique Bortoluci, Mohamed Mbougar Sarr, and Samanta Schweblin.

The founding partners of Fósforo: Luiz Francisco Carvalho Filho, Fernanda Diamant, and Rita Mattar.
