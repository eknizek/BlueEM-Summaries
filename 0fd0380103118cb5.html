Suzano Selects Avondale Global Gateway as Gulf Coast Hub, Bringing Regular Wood Pulp Imports Back to Louisiana for First Time in More Than 30 Years

30 April 2026 · 08:13 PM  ·  Company press release
Suzano, Brazil's leading pulp producer, has selected the Avondale Global Gateway port facility in Louisiana as its primary Gulf Coast distribution hub for wood pulp imports into the United States. This strategic move marks the return of regular Suzano pulp shipments to Louisiana after more than three decades, reflecting the company's commitment to expanding US market access and reducing logistics costs. The Avondale facility provides direct deep-water port access and positions Suzano to serve downstream paper and packaging manufacturers across North America more efficiently. This infrastructure investment enhances Suzano's competitive positioning in its largest export market and supports volume growth.
