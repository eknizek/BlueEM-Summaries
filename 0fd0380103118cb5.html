Suzano Selects Avondale Global Gateway as Gulf Coast Hub, Bringing Regular Wood Pulp Imports Back to Louisiana for First Time in More Than 30 Years

30 April 2026 · 08:13 PM  ·  Company press release
Suzano, Brazil's leading pulp and paper company, has selected Avondale Global Gateway in Louisiana as its Gulf Coast hub for regular wood pulp imports—marking the first sustained pulp import operation at the location in more than three decades. This strategic move strengthens Suzano's market position in North America, reduces logistics costs, and improves supply chain efficiency for U.S. and regional customers. The decision reflects both the company's growth strategy and renewed competitive positioning in the critical North American market.
