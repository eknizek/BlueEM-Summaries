Petroperú Patrimony Reorganization: ProInversión Launches Divestiture of 55 Non-Strategic Assets

15 May 2026 · 03:54 AM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
In response to Petroperú's financial crisis, Peru's private investment promotion agency (ProInversión) announced the divestiture of 55 non-strategic assets beginning in July 2026. The restructuring aims to improve the state refiner's balance sheet and operational efficiency by shedding non-core holdings while preserving critical refineries and fuel distribution infrastructure. This asset-light repositioning reflects broader efforts to stabilize Peru's struggling state energy champion and potentially attract strategic investors or partners. The sale process represents a significant privatization and infrastructure consolidation opportunity.
