Chile, US to Sign Mining and Security Deals

20 April 2026 · 12:01 PM  ·  Mining.com
Chile and the United States have agreed to formalize bilateral cooperation on critical minerals development, project financing, recycling infrastructure, and joint policing initiatives targeting mining-related crime. These agreements reflect deepening strategic partnership aimed at securing Western supply chains for essential minerals and addressing security challenges in mining regions. The deals underscore Chile's importance to US critical minerals strategy and the role of government-to-government coordination in enabling private sector investment.
