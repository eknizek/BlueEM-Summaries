Amstel Targets Gen Z with Low-Alcohol Portfolio and Social Media Strategy

21 April 2026 · 10:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Amstel, a Heineken brand operating in Brazil, is shifting its marketing strategy to target Generation Z consumers who favor moderation in alcohol consumption. The company is reorganizing its communications approach and expanding its low-alcohol product portfolio to appeal to this demographic. This reflects broader consumer trends toward lower alcohol consumption among younger drinkers. The rebranding effort includes increased focus on social media marketing channels.
