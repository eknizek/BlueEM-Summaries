Unico announces new CEO for Latin America with focus on Argentina expansion

29 April 2026 · 06:35 PM  ·  Neofeed
Unico, a Brazilian identity technology company operating across 20 countries, announced Luis Felipe Monteiro as CEO for Latin America. Monteiro, previously global vice president of institutional relations, takes the role with a mandate to expand into Argentina and strengthen regional presence. The appointment reflects Unico's strategy to deepen market penetration across Latin America.
