Unico Announces New CEO with Mission to Expand into Argentina

29 April 2026 · 06:35 PM  ·  Company press release
Unico, a Brazilian identity technology company operating in 20 countries, announced on April 29 the appointment of Luis Felipe Monteiro as CEO for Latin America. Monteiro, formerly global vice president of institutional relations at Unico since February of the previous year, takes on the mission of expanding the company's footprint in Argentina and strengthening its regional presence. This leadership appointment signals Unico's commitment to scaling its fintech and identity solutions across Latin American markets.
