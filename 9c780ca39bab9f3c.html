Court strikes down levy on power generators created during economic emergency; collected funds must be refunded

06 May 2026 · 07:09 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
A Colombian court ruled unconstitutional a special levy imposed on power generators that was created during an economic emergency period, requiring the Colombian tax authority (DIAN) to refund or compensate all funds collected under the decree within six months. The decision removes a financial burden on electricity companies and creates a significant liability for the government. This ruling has direct implications for Colombian utility sector profitability and government fiscal accounts.
