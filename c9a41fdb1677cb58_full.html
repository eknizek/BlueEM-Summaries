# 'The Taste of Believing': Nutresa Rebrands 17 Products Across 30 Million Packages in Inspirational Campaign

Zenú, Jet Chocolates, Festival Crackers, Tosh, Colcafé, Doria and many others are changing their branding.

In an unprecedented move in its trajectory, Grupo Nutresa announced the launch of 'The Taste of Believing', a campaign that replaces the names and logos of its main brands with messages of optimism directed at consumers across the country. According to information from the company in Medellín, the initiative involves the modification of 30 million product packages belonging to 17 of its most representative brands, with the objective of transmitting messages of hope in daily life.

The proposal is based on the premise that collective confidence is a key engine for social progress.

"Our history of more than a century has taught us that the main ingredient of progress is faith in ourselves. Today, our leading brands are dressed in positive messages to remind us that the flavor that unites us is that of believing," stated Jaime Gilinski, CEO of Grupo Nutresa, when presenting the campaign.

Among the brands involved are Jet, Jumbo, Chocolate Corona, Colcafé, Sello Rojo, Zenú, Doria, Chocolisto, Ducales, Saltín Noel, Wafer Noel, Chococono, Aloha, Tosh, Dux and Yupi. Their traditional packages were temporarily replaced with phrases designed to reinforce confidence in personal and collective goals.

The company noted that its products are present in eight out of every ten Colombian households and in more than 400,000 points of sale, which would allow the message to reach extensive coverage across the national territory.

María Elisa Botero, the organization's marketing director, explained that the strategy seeks to convert daily consumer products into a channel of mass communication: "By changing 30 million packages, we convert our everyday products into a message of optimism that will reach every corner of Colombia," she indicated.

## Artistic Participation and Audiovisual Production

The campaign includes an audiovisual piece narrated by artist J Balvin, who participates as the main voice in a video that compiles scenes of daily life in the country. The production uses an adapted version of the song "Mi Gente," originally performed by the artist. The video was developed by the Sancho BBDO agency along with production company OP, and involved more than 80 hours of filming with multiple teams working simultaneously at different locations.

"A small daily gesture has the power to restore our faith. That's why I'm thrilled to be the voice that shares the purpose of this campaign," J Balvin said about his participation.

The launch of 'The Taste of Believing' coincides with a period of growth for the organization. Grupo Nutresa reported that it is currently the most valuable company in Colombia and projects doubling its sales by 2030, with the objective of consolidating itself as a multinational with global reach.

The corporate strategy is supported by three pillars: consumer experience, innovation and transformation, and sustainable profitability. The company has 169 brands distributed in more than 80 countries, 47 production plants and approximately 47,000 employees.

In terms of sustainability, the company reported investments of 150 billion Colombian pesos, with advances such as the use of 88.2% renewable energy and the implementation of packaging that is 89.3% recyclable or compostable. Through social programs, it also reported benefiting more than 1.6 million people through food banks.
