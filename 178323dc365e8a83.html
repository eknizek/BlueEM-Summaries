Nearly one-third of population reports no emergency savings, Anbima/Datafolha survey shows

25 April 2026 · 09:33 PM  ·  Valor Economico
An Anbima and Datafolha survey found that approximately one-third of Brazil's population does not maintain emergency savings, raising concerns about household financial vulnerability. This metric is critical for understanding consumer credit demand, default risk, and macroeconomic resilience in Brazil. The data suggests significant gaps in financial planning and savings culture, with implications for credit markets, consumer lending, and household consumption patterns.
