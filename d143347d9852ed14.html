Brasília Governor Reports Investment Fund Proposal to Acquire Master Bank Assets from BRB

10 April 2026 · 08:47 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Governor of the Federal District (Brasília) announced that an investment fund has submitted a proposal to acquire assets transferred from Master Bank to BRB (Banco de Brasília). The transaction is valued at approximately R$15 billion, with R$4 billion to be paid immediately and the remainder financed through instruments tied to the acquired assets. This represents a significant M&A opportunity in Brazil's banking sector and reflects ongoing consolidation efforts following Master Bank's regulatory issues.
