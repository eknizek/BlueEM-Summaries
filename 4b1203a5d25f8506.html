Petrobras Elects New Board and Confirms R$8.1 Billion in Dividends

16 April 2026 · 08:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Petrobras held its shareholder meeting on April 16, 2026, electing a new board of directors to be led by Guilherme Mello, the executive secretary of Brazil's Ministry of Planning and Budget. Shareholders also approved a dividend distribution of R$8.1 billion based on the company's 2025 financial results. This action reflects strong profitability and shareholder returns from the state-controlled oil giant.
