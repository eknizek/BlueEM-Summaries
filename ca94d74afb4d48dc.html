India Acknowledges Iranian Oil Purchases, Dismisses Payment Woes

04 April 2026 · 09:46 AM  ·  Bloomberg
India has acknowledged ongoing Iranian oil purchases while downplaying payment settlement difficulties. This reflects India's strategic pivot away from Western oil suppliers amid US sanctions and geopolitical realignment. For LatAm oil exporters (particularly Brazil and Mexico) competing for Asian markets, Indian demand for non-sanctioned suppliers affects global crude pricing and market share. Rising Indian oil demand from alternative sources provides indirect upside to LatAm producers' pricing power and volumes.
