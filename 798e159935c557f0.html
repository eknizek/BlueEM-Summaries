Motorcycle sales growth accelerates 40.7% in April, exceeds 113,000 units in Colombia

02 May 2026 · 08:10 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's motorcycle market demonstrated robust growth of 40.7% in April 2026, with sales exceeding 113,000 units. Bajaj, Suzuki, and AKT lead the market, with strong demand concentrated in low-displacement motorcycles reflecting consumer preference for affordable mobility. This growth signals economic recovery and rising consumer purchasing power in Colombia, benefiting automotive suppliers, dealers, and manufacturers operating in the region.
