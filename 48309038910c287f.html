Copasa to Transfer R$350 Million to Minas Gerais Municipalities in Privatization Deal

23 April 2026 · 09:06 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Copasa, Minas Gerais' state-owned water and sanitation company, has agreed to transfer approximately R$350 million to municipalities across the state to facilitate the renewal of water and sewerage concession contracts. This transfer is viewed as a critical step toward executing the company's privatization. The transaction demonstrates how state asset sales in Brazil often require negotiating compensation arrangements with local governments holding contractual claims.
