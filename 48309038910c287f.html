Copasa to Transfer R$ 350 Million to Municipalities in Deal to Advance Privatization

23 April 2026 · 09:06 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Copasa, the state-owned water and sanitation company of Minas Gerais, is committing approximately R$ 350 million to municipalities in Minas Gerais to facilitate the renewal of water and sewage concession contracts. This financial arrangement is a key step toward advancing the company's privatization strategy. The transfer addresses municipal concerns and regulatory requirements, enabling the company to progress with concessionary contract renewals that are critical prerequisites for the privatization process.
