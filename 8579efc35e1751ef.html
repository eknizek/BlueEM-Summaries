Micro-pensions: Colombia's strategy to close the pension coverage gap amid pension crisis

20 April 2026 · 09:53 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia is leveraging micro-pension schemes (BEPs) as a solution to close significant gaps in pension coverage amid an ongoing pension system crisis. The programs feature flexible contribution levels, government subsidies, and technology-enabled administration to expand access to retirement savings. Colombia is positioning itself as a regional leader in innovative pension solutions. However, persistent barriers including low-income participation and institutional adoption continue to limit impact, requiring ongoing policy refinement.
