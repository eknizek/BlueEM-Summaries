DP World Launches Brazil–Africa Logistics Corridor, Expanding Trade Connectivity

24 April 2026 · 01:00 PM  ·  GlobeNewswire
Global logistics operator DP World announced the launch of a dedicated Brazil–Africa logistics corridor, expanding trade connectivity and port infrastructure utilisation. The initiative aims to improve shipping efficiency, reduce transit times, and unlock new trade opportunities between Brazil and African markets. Rumo, Brazil's major logistics and rail operator, stands to benefit from increased cargo volumes and partnerships supporting this corridor. This development is significant for Brazilian exporters and logistics infrastructure investors.
