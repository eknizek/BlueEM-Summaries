# How to Start a Business on TikTok? How the Social Network Supports SMEs

TikTok celebrated the third anniversary of Emprende en TikTok, a program that provides training and seed capital access to SMEs in Mexico and Latin America.

## How Does It Work?

According to the social network, 1 out of every 5 SMEs use TikTok to promote products and services. Additionally, they highlight that 6 out of every 10 users who see a recommendation on the social network visit the locations that appeared in their feed.

TikTok's idea is to leverage mobile phones, technology, and creativity so that small and medium-sized enterprises can reach more places and customers to increase their sales. Company data reveals that 7 out of every 10 businesses plan to increase their presence on the platform, making TikTok a key channel for business development.

Emprende en TikTok 2026 is already underway in Mexico, Colombia, and Peru, and for this edition, it will add Argentina. The support program will provide access to training for all participating SMEs, and the three selected businesses will have access to various amounts of seed capital focused on promoting the growth and consolidation of their projects.

- First place: $15,000

- Second place: $10,000

- Third place: $5,000

## How to Participate in the Emprende en TikTok Program?

The call opened on March 26 and closes on July 5, 2026. During this period, SMEs can register and participate in the program.

**Requirements:**

- Be an SME with at least 1 year of operation

- Have verifiable minimum annual billing of $10,000

- Have a business profile on TikTok and a medium level of digital maturity

- Demonstrate leadership, organization, and capacity to participate in the program

- Added value (not mandatory): generate positive environmental and/or social impact through your business model

## Program Structure

The program consists of two phases: free virtual classes and workshops led by industry professionals.

Classes are in podcast format with a maximum duration of 15 minutes. There are 6 open classes for all of Latin America, focused on creativity, TikTok Live, Ads (Step by step and Metrics), and TikTok Shop. The only requirement to participate in this stage is a desire to learn.

For the Emprende en TikTok program, interested participants will take intensive workshops taught by experts with training on key topics such as finances, leadership, legal matters, brand development, digital marketing, public relations, and strategies.

The second part consists of mentorships focused on the best-performing projects, where they receive personalized mentoring, visibility, and the possibility of winning up to seed capital.

By July 5, when the call closes, TikTok will select 10 ventures from each country to proceed to the second stage of classes and workshops.

## Program Impact

TikTok highlights that the program has registered more than 21,850 projects, 50 ventures have received acceleration, and it has awarded more than $550,000 in seed capital to 25 finalists.
