# Fiscal Council President Warns MEF Recognizes 2027 Budget Infeasibility

Alonso Segura, president of the Fiscal Council, warned that the Ministry of Economy and Finance (MEF) already recognizes the infeasibility of preparing the 2027 budget.

In an interview with Canal N, Segura indicated that the MEF acknowledges this problem. "The impact is permanent. In this report the MEF recognizes the problem, begins to raise alerts and make the situation transparent. From where? What the MEF indicates is that it will be very difficult to be able to prepare a budget for 2027," said the president of the Fiscal Council.

He added that the next government will receive a critical situation and will have to evaluate spending cuts and revenue measures that can be implemented. "The next government will have to evaluate the situation, I imagine the MEF will leave proposals to the next government as soon as it takes office, because the budget must be submitted to Congress. It will have to see what it cuts, what revenue measures it can implement and any eventual modification, hopefully not of fiscal trajectory," he emphasized.
