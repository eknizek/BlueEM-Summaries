Fiscal Council President Warns MEF Acknowledges 2027 Budget Inviability

04 May 2026 · 03:49 PM

Alonso Segura, president of the Fiscal Council, warned that the Ministry of Economy and Finance (MEF) already acknowledges the impossibility of preparing the 2027 budget.

In an interview with Canal N, Segura indicated that the MEF recognizes this problem. "The impact is permanent. In this report the MEF recognizes the problem, begins to raise alerts and make the situation transparent. From where? What the MEF points out is that it will be very difficult to be able to prepare a budget for 2027," said the Fiscal Council president.

He added that the incoming government will receive a critical situation and must evaluate spending cuts and revenue measures that can be implemented. "The next government will have to evaluate the situation. I imagine the MEF will leave proposals for the next government as soon as it takes office, because the budget must be submitted to Congress. They will have to see what things they cut, what income measures they can implement and any eventual modification, hopefully not to fiscal trajectory," he stressed.
