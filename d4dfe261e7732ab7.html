Brazil has 50 'ChatGPTs' with Brazilian accent. Will they survive the AI race?

30 April 2026 · 10:58 AM  ·  Neofeed
Following ChatGPT's late-2022 launch, a wave of Brazilian AI startups emerged seeking to build local alternatives to global large language models. Brazil now hosts around 50 generative AI ventures competing in a sector dominated by well-funded international players like OpenAI. The question facing these startups is whether they can secure sufficient capital, develop differentiated products, and achieve scale before being outcompeted by deep-pocketed global incumbents. Success likely depends on localization, vertical specialization, and strategic partnerships or acquisition.
