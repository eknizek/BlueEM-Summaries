Brazilian Government Extends Deadline for Fuel Distributors to Report Profit Margins

22 April 2026 · 11:30 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Brazilian government extended the submission deadline for fuel distributors to provide weekly gross profit margin information to the National Petroleum Agency (ANP) by 20 business days. This regulatory extension reflects ongoing government efforts to monitor and control fuel distributor pricing and margins in the domestic market. The move is part of broader price transparency and inflation management initiatives in Brazil's oil and gas sector.
