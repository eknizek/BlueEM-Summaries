Aceros AZA Opens Scrap Recycling Center in Puerto Montt Processing 18,000 Tons Annually

21 April 2026 · 11:06 PM  ·  Company press release
Aceros AZA, Chile's largest green steel producer, has opened a new scrap metal recycling center in Puerto Montt with a US$1 million investment. The facility is designed to process approximately 1,500 tons of metal waste monthly, totaling 18,000 tons annually, strengthening local metal supply chains in southern Chile. The project supports Aceros AZA's sustainability strategy and circular economy initiatives in the region.
