# CSP Inaugurates Train Section to AIFA; 'We Had Problems'

With 25,800 million pesos and 3 years later... CSP inaugurates Train to AIFA

Mexico City (April 26, 2026) - 23:20 hrs

The extension of the Suburban Train connecting Mexico City with AIFA was inaugurated, whose cost exceeded 25 million pesos; AMLO promised it would be ready in 2023.

The Magistrate, accompanied by her husband, Governors and members of the federal and expanded Cabinet, assured that with this inauguration the commitment to the "people of Mexico" is being fulfilled.

The extension of the Suburban Train that will connect Mexico City with AIFA was finally inaugurated yesterday. The cost exceeded 25 billion pesos. AMLO had promised it would be ready in 2023.
