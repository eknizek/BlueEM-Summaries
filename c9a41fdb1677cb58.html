'The taste of believing': Nutresa rebrands 17 products across 30 million packages in inspirational campaign

27 April 2026 · 09:43 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Nutresa, Colombia's leading food multinational, has launched a major rebranding campaign affecting 17 products across 30 million packages. The campaign, titled 'El sabor de creer' (The taste of believing), repositions key brands including Zenú, Chocolatinas Jet, Galletas Festival, Tosh, Colcafé, and Doria with inspirational messaging. This strategic initiative reflects the company's efforts to strengthen brand positioning and consumer engagement across its diversified portfolio in the Colombian and broader Latin American markets.
