Dharma AI Hires Former AWS Executive as New Chief Commercial Officer

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Dharma AI has appointed a former Amazon Web Services (AWS) executive as its new Chief Commercial Officer to structure and scale the company's sales organization. The hire coincides with the company raising an estimated US$8 million funding round. This executive addition signals preparation for growth and reflects Dharma AI's ambition to build a major Brazilian AI/technology company with global-caliber leadership.
