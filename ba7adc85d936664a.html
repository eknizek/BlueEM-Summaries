"It is not reasonable for the economy to pay for political differences": Andi president on crisis with Ecuador

02 May 2026 · 12:16 AM  ·  Portfolio - Negocios
Bruce Mac Master, president of Colombia's business association (Andi), has condemned the ongoing commercial dispute with Ecuador as harmful to both nations' economies. He characterized the crisis as "tremendously clumsy" and argued that the private sector should not bear the costs of political disagreements between governments. The statement reflects concerns from Colombian business leaders about trade disruption and the need for diplomatic resolution.
