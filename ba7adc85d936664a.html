ANDI president criticizes Ecuador trade crisis as economically damaging

02 May 2026 · 12:16 AM  ·  Portfolio - Empresas

The bilateral trade dispute is creating economic friction between the neighboring countries and threatens to disrupt regional commerce. Mac Master's statement reflects business sector concerns about political tensions undermining cross-border trade relationships.
