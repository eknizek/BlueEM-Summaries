Digital Advertising in Brazil Grows 12.7% to R$42.7 Billion in 2025

30 April 2026 · 10:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Digital advertising investments in Brazil totaled R$42.7 billion in 2025, representing 12.7% growth compared to 2024 according to the IAB Brasil Digital Adspend 2026 report. This growth rate marks the second-highest in the sector's historical record, reflecting strong demand for digital marketing channels among Brazilian advertisers. The data underscores the continued shift of marketing budgets toward digital platforms and the maturation of the Brazilian digital ecosystem. For investors, the result highlights the attractiveness of Brazil's digital media and marketing services sector.
