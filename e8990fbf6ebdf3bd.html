Petrobras Falls Short of 10% of Diesel Orders from Distributors for May, Sources Say

17 April 2026 · 09:51 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
Petrobras has again failed to fulfill the total diesel orders placed by fuel distributors for May delivery, according to market sources. This shortfall represents approximately 10% of requested volumes and reflects ongoing supply challenges in Brazil's downstream fuel market. The supply constraint could impact fuel availability and pricing for distributors and end consumers. This situation underscores operational or refining capacity challenges at Latin America's largest oil producer.
