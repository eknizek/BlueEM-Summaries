Guide to Paloma Valencia's proposal to eliminate motorcycle insurance payments for bikes up to 250cc

04 May 2026 · 04:55 PM  ·  Portfolio - Negocios
Colombian legislator Paloma Valencia has proposed legislation to eliminate SOAT (mandatory motorcycle insurance) requirements for motorcycles with engines up to 250cc. If approved, millions of Colombian motorcycle owners would no longer be required to pay annual insurance premiums directly. The proposal has potential implications for the insurance industry and consumer household finances, though it remains a legislative proposal.
