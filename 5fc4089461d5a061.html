PFalimentos Adds Claudio Melandri, Former Banco Santander President, to Board

30 April 2026 · 10:38 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
PFalimentos, a Chilean food company, has appointed Claudio Melandri—the former president of Banco Santander—to its board of directors, replacing Esperanza Fernández.
