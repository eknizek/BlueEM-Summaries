Over 80% of homes in Lima are purchased before delivery: why do Peruvians prefer to buy this way?

30 April 2026 · 10:40 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Lima's residential real estate market is dominated by early-stage purchases, with over 80% of Social Interest Housing (VIS) bought before project completion across Lima Moderna, Centro, Sur and Norte districts. This purchasing pattern reflects strong demand for affordable housing and efficient living spaces. The trend consolidates early-stage investment as the primary market driver, benefiting from high demand and growing sales in a context of accessible pricing and space efficiency.
