Grimoldi lost almost $1,900 million at the start of the year and bets on new brands to recover

14 May 2026 · 05:30 PM  ·  El Cronista - Negocios
Grimoldi, a major Argentine clothing manufacturer and retailer, reported substantial losses of approximately $1,900 million during the opening months of 2024, reflecting challenging market conditions in Argentina. The company is pursuing a turnaround strategy centered on developing and introducing new brands to its portfolio. This move signals management's confidence in brand diversification as a path to restore financial performance and competitiveness in the competitive apparel market.
