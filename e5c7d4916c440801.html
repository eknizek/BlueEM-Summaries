Banco da Amazônia Plans Revenue Diversification and Target of 1 Million New Customers

20 April 2026 · 12:02 PM  ·  Neofeed
Banco da Amazônia, the federal government-controlled development bank, has outlined a 2026 strategic plan to diversify its revenue streams away from near-total reliance on credit portfolio income. The bank aims to reduce the credit segment's contribution from the current 85% to approximately 55% within two years, while simultaneously expanding its customer base by 1 million accounts. This diversification strategy reflects efforts to strengthen the institution's financial resilience and expand its role in supporting economic development across Brazil's Amazon region.
