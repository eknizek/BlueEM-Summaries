Toll Booth Fares Increase 4.6% for Routes to Acapulco, Puebla, and Querétaro

13 April 2026 · 09:13 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Capufe, Mexico's federal toll road operator, announced fare increases of up to 4.6% for three major routes: Cuernavaca–Acapulco, Mexico–Querétaro, and Mexico–Puebla, compared to 2025 levels. The increases reflect inflation adjustments permitted under concession agreements and impact logistics costs across Mexico's freight and passenger transport sectors. These price adjustments influence operating margins for transportation and logistics companies reliant on these strategic highway corridors.
