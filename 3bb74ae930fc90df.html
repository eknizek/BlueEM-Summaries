The Risky Pivot Inside Australia's Pension System

04 May 2026 · 09:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg - Economics
Growing anxiety about retirement savings in Australia is driving a shift from traditional pension schemes toward self-directed investment vehicles (self-managed superannuation funds), where participants are increasingly allocating to cryptocurrencies and other speculative assets. This trend reflects concerns about adequacy of defined contributions and represents a meaningful shift in retirement investing behavior. The phenomenon raises questions about investor protection and financial stability implications.
