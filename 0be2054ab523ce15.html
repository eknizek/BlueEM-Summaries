Government Seeks Alternatives for Training Continuation After Sence Franchise Elimination

24 April 2026 · 10:28 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Following the elimination of the Sence (National Employment Service) franchise program, the Chilean Labor Ministry is evaluating alternative approaches to workforce training and employment support. Under Minister Tomás Rau's leadership, the ministry is analyzing options to strengthen labor intermediation and job placement programs, with emphasis on leveraging existing resources such as the Solidarity Unemployment Fund. This policy shift reflects changes in Chile's approach to workforce development and skills training infrastructure. The decision impacts vocational training availability and labor market policy across the country.
