Chile's FNE concludes e-commerce report, recommends greater platform transparency

01 May 2026 · 01:10 AM  ·  Diario Financiero
Chile's Fiscalía Nacional Económica (FNE) competition authority completed a preliminary investigation into the e-commerce sector and identified structural characteristics that could create competition risks going forward. The regulator recommended enhanced transparency requirements for digital platforms to mitigate potential anti-competitive behavior. The FNE indicated it will continue monitoring sector evolution to ensure fair market conditions.
