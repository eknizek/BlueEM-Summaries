# Executive Shuffles: Atlas Agro Appoints New CEO and Other Leadership Changes

Many executives changed offices this past week, and there were no shortage of promotions either. Check out who has a new workspace:

Lieven Cooreman takes on the role of global CEO of Atlas Agro, a low-carbon nitrogen fertilizer company. "The price increases caused by disruptions in global supply chains strengthen the conviction that Brazil needs structural measures to promote local nutrient production," the executive told the Column.

Allianz Seguros announces Fabio Maretti as the new director of Property and Loss Prevention. Bruno Haddad is the new general operations director at Oncoclínicas. At V8.Tech, the news is the arrival of Antonio Ximenes for the Partnership Directorate. Meanwhile at Motiva, Douglas Coutinho takes on the role of Financial Planning and Market Intelligence director. With 14 years at the company, Giuliano Argenta Scalabrin was promoted to national Sales director at UPL, a sustainable agricultural solutions company. Henrique Casagrande also moved offices. Since 2018 at Dock, he is stepping down as COO to take over the SaaS (software as a service) division. Lloyal, a loyalty solutions platform, has new CRO and COO appointments: Renato Bracarense and Rubiane Pires, respectively.

James Prestes, former VP of Distance Education at UniCesumar, has a new address. He now works as VP of Operations and Partnerships at Vitru Educação, owner of the UNIASSELVI and UniCesumar brands. Renato Acciarto is the newest member of the Board of Directors at Instituto Baccarelli in Heliópolis. Tiago Fronza Frare is the new president of Cooperativa Vinícola Aurora. Apent, the holding company that brings together Clube FII and Autem Investimentos, announces the arrival of Celson Placido as CIO.

Diego Dzodan is the new General Manager of Roblox for Latin America. Boavista Tecnologia registers the arrival of Marcos Elias Santos as the new CRO. Fabio Sarmento takes on the role of Head of Engineering at Uncover. Bruno Bolivar is the new Chief Revenue Officer (CRO) of Teltec Data. After 11 years as Technology director, Rodrigo Medeiros takes on the role of CTO at Zup, a technology company from the Itaú group. Strada, a freight platform, is under new direction with the arrival of Carlos Araujo as CEO. Grupo Almaviva and Tivit announce the creation of a Global AI Directorate, which will be led by Valeria Sandei (global AI director of Grupo Almaviva/Tivit).

Thiago Escrivão is back at SAS. He will lead the company's work in the banking and insurance sectors in Brazil, as senior Sales manager for FSI.
