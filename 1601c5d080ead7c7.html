Colombia's Labor Ministry clarifies salary and work hour reduction requirements for companies

27 April 2026 · 08:57 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's Ministry of Labor has released clarification on employer obligations regarding salary levels and work hour reductions following legislative changes. The work week has been incrementally reduced: from 48 hours in 2022 to 47 in 2023, 46 in 2024, with a target of 42 hours by July 2026. The ministry's guidance addresses employer implementation requirements and wage adjustment expectations as this significant labor reform takes effect.
