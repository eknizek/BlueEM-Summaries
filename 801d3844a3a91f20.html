Fuel Price Increases Set High Floor for May Inflation as March Carryover Effects Persist

20 April 2026 · 09:53 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's May inflation reading will be significantly influenced by carryover effects from March fuel price increases, which were only partially reflected in the prior month's consumer price index. Unless market conditions shift, petrol station prices are unlikely to decline when a sector-wide fuel pricing agreement expires on May 15, creating an elevated baseline for May inflation. The cascading impact of energy price inflation on broader consumer prices underscores how commodity-driven shocks propagate through the economy. This dynamic complicates the Central Bank's inflation-fighting agenda amid broader monetary policy tightening.
