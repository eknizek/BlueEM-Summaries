CFE labor liabilities grew 24.3% year-on-year in first quarter, Imco reports

30 April 2026 · 09:09 PM

The labor liabilities of the Federal Electricity Commission (CFE) increased 24.3 percent year-on-year in the first quarter, according to an analysis by the Mexican Institute for Competitiveness (Imco).

The institute notes that total debt stood at 489.2 billion pesos, an annual decline of 7.1 percent, equivalent to 1.3 percent of estimated GDP for this year.
