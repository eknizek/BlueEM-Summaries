Sugarcane Productivity in Minas Gerais Expected to Surge 10%, Boosting Harvest

24 April 2026 · 08:59 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo

This growth trajectory reflects positive sector fundamentals and anticipated favorable farming conditions.
