Chile Targets Faster Permits to Unlock $100B Pipeline

20 April 2026 · 04:51 PM  ·  Mining.com
Chile's government announced efforts to streamline mining permitting processes, aiming to revive a $100B investment pipeline that has faced regulatory delays. As the world's largest copper producer, Chile is prioritizing faster approvals to reignite mining investment and create jobs amid global demand for copper and lithium for energy transition. The regulatory push reflects mounting pressure to maintain competitiveness as investors consider alternative jurisdictions for critical mineral development.
