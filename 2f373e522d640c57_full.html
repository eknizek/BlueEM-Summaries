Mendoza firm prepares new residential-commercial project in Reñaca

01 May 2026 · 01:31 AM

The Foresta sector of Reñaca has transformed into one of the most valuable residential and commercial poles on the border between Viña del Mar and Concón. Now, the area will add a new project featuring a residential building and neighborhood shopping center developed by the Mendoza-based company Kristich.

Called Proyecto Infinito, the development will be located at Avenida La Foresta de Reñaca 160 and encompasses a built area of 18,300 square meters, distributed among 79 apartments, 24 commercial spaces, and a gym. With an investment of US$32 million, the project already has its building permit, and the real estate company plans to begin construction during the second half of this year, once it receives environmental authority approval.

The trans-Andean company Kristich has established itself as a relevant player in the real estate sector of Reñaca. With more than 20 years of experience, the firm led by Flavio Kristich expanded its operations from Mendoza into the Chilean market, where it already has several completed projects.
