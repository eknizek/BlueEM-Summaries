Argentina loses US$25 billion annually due to poor infrastructure maintenance, says Construction Chamber

20 April 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's Construction Chamber estimates the country loses US$25 billion per year due to insufficient maintenance of its infrastructure. While the government has focused on toll-road concessions, the Chamber emphasizes that 30,000 kilometers of non-concessioned routes require urgent attention. Additionally, the industry has raised complaints about the national government's outstanding debts to construction companies, which further constrains sector activity.
