Construction Chamber: Argentina loses $25 billion annually due to poor infrastructure maintenance

20 April 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's construction industry chamber has highlighted significant economic losses stemming from poor infrastructure maintenance, estimating annual damages at $25 billion. Beyond toll-road concessions, the sector is calling for urgent attention to 30,000 kilometers of non-concessioned highways in deteriorating condition. The chamber also raised concerns about outstanding government debts owed to construction companies, underlining both the maintenance deficit and payment arrears affecting the sector.
