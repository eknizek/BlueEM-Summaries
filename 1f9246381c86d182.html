Allbirds pivots to AI with new 'NewBird AI' brand; stock surges over 600% on $50M funding

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Allbirds has undergone a significant repositioning, renaming itself to NewBird AI as it pivots away from its core footwear business toward artificial intelligence applications. The company has secured $50 million in funding to support this strategic transformation. The rebrand and new focus have generated substantial investor enthusiasm, with the company's stock surging over 600% following the announcement, signalling strong market confidence in the AI-driven strategy.
