State of Mexico breaks record: adds over 281,000 formal jobs, highest in 27 years

15 May 2026 · 11:04 PM  ·  El Economista
The State of Mexico achieved record formal job creation with 281,366 positions registered with IMSS from September 2023 to April 2026, marking the highest 27-year total for the state. The data was presented by the state's Economic Development Secretariat as part of a regular economic indicators update. This employment surge reflects strong regional economic activity and labor market tightening, with significance for consumer spending, wage dynamics, and regional economic health assessment.
