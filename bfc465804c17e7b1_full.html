# Berkshire Hathaway CEO Says Company Won't Invest in AI for Investment's Sake

The CEO of the Berkshire Hathaway megaconglomerate, Greg Abel, said that he will not invest in artificial intelligence (AI) simply for the sake of investing. At a time when the group—which is made up of quite traditional businesses, such as railroads, insurance, and consumer companies—has been pressured by investors to invest in more innovative businesses, the executive was very clear in stating that he will follow Warren Buffett's traditional investment strategies.

He explained that the group will implement AI with restricted scope and focused on creating value propositions.
