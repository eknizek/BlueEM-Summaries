CVC says it has not received takeover proposal to date; media reports Despegar.com pursuing tender offer

The travel operator and agency CVC said in a statement on Sunday (3) that, although it constantly analyzes opportunities that could benefit CVC Corp and its shareholders, to date it has not received any communication or proposal regarding a possible public offer to acquire CVC Corp shares.

"CVC Corp reaffirms its commitment to transparency and the duty to inform its shareholders and the market in general about any material facts related to its business," the statement said.

Journalist Lauro Jardim published in his column on Sunday (3) in the newspaper "O Globo" that Decolar's controller, Despegar.com, is evaluating a public offer to acquire shares (tender offer) of CVC at R$ 3.30 per share.
