Market signals the new Colombian government awaits for investment, according to AmCham

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
AmCham reports that U.S. foreign direct investment into Colombia declined significantly by 37% in 2025, signaling investor hesitancy about the business environment. Despite this contraction, the United States remains Colombia's dominant investment source, accounting for 29.4% of total foreign direct investment. The investment pullback reflects market concerns about policy direction and business conditions that the incoming government must address to restore confidence and reverse the FDI decline.
