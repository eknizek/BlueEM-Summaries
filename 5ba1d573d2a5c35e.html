New Rural Desenrola Program Offers Discounts on Family Farming Debt

04 May 2026 · 08:42 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
The government has introduced a specialized Desenrola Rural component targeting small-scale and family farming operations with subsidized debt renegotiation and relief on PRONAF (National Program to Strengthen Family Farming) obligations. The program provides discounted settlements and extended payment terms to eligible agricultural producers, supporting sector viability and rural income preservation. This targeted intervention reflects policy commitment to maintaining rural economic participation and preventing agricultural producer default.
