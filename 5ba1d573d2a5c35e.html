Novo Desenrola Rural offers discounts on family farming Pronaf debts

04 May 2026 · 08:42 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Brazil has introduced 'Novo Desenrola Rural,' a companion program to the household debt relief initiative, specifically targeting family farmers enrolled in Pronaf (Programa Nacional de Fortalecimento da Agricultura Familiar). The program offers debt discounts to reduce financial burdens on small-scale agricultural producers. This policy aims to strengthen the rural credit market and support agricultural sustainability through improved farmer liquidity.
