Mexico, second global destination for cyber money laundering

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Mexico has become a primary target for cybercriminals seeking to launder illicit proceeds through the financial system, ranking second globally in cyber money laundering activity. This regulatory and compliance risk reflects weaknesses in anti-money laundering (AML) frameworks and digital banking security. For institutional investors, particularly those in banks, private credit, and fintech, this signals elevated compliance costs, reputational risk, and potential regulatory enforcement actions.
