Former AFP Habitat Chief Expresses Concerns About Pension Reform and Growing State Role

22 April 2026 · 09:12 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Cristián Rodríguez, former president of AFP Habitat and current board chair of AAISA, voiced concerns about Chile's pension reform law, stating it imposes restrictions on individual choice and expands the state's role in the pension system. While acknowledging some positive aspects of the legislation, Rodríguez highlighted the limits on member freedom of selection. This commentary is significant for investors in pension funds and insurance companies operating in Chile, as it reflects ongoing debate about the reform's market implications.
