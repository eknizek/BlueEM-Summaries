Petrobras Fuel Production Grows 6.4% in Q1 Amid Iran War

30 April 2026 · 10:49 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Petrobras increased fuel production by 6.4% in the first quarter of 2026, driven by a strategic shift toward domestic production capacity rather than relying on imports. This growth came during a period of heightened international instability caused by regional conflict in Iran, which impacted global energy markets. The move reflects Petrobras' focus on self-sufficiency and demonstrates the company's operational resilience in volatile geopolitical conditions. For investors, this signals the company's ability to leverage its production assets and reduce exposure to supply chain disruptions.
