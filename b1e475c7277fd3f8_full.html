# Grupo Centenario Announced as New Member of Confederation of Real Estate Developers of Peru

Grupo Centenario was announced as a new member of the Confederation of Real Estate Developers of Peru (CODIP), an organization that brings together leading companies in the real estate sector and promotes best practices in city development.

The incorporation marks a relevant step in the company's participation within the real estate ecosystem, in a context where coordination between actors is key to driving more orderly growth.

"Joining CODIP allows us to contribute from our experience and be part of a collective effort to strengthen the sector with a vision of formal and orderly growth, supported by sustainability and integrity standards. We are convinced that real estate companies must not only continue closing the inequality gap by developing formal property throughout the country, but must do so by firmly confronting corruption and money laundering," said Eduardo Herrera, CEO of Grupo Centenario.

Currently, CODIP represents 85% of formal real estate supply in Metropolitan Lima, consolidating itself as a key space for sector development and coordination among its main actors.

Grupo Centenario highlighted that its arrival at the guild aims to propose new ideas and trends, as well as contribute to building standards that benefit the industry. Likewise, it seeks to continue driving its sustainable growth throughout the country, reinforcing its role within the industry.
