Companies shift from forecasts to strategic bets amid volatility

22 April 2026 · 12:19 PM  ·  O Globo (Brazil)
Corporate leaders in Brazil are moving away from reliance on traditional economic forecasts and instead making strategic bets based on scenario planning and real-time market signals.
