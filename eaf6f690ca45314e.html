Court restores Anatel rule on irregular cell phones sold on Amazon

24 April 2026 · 09:59 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
The Federal Regional Court of the 3rd Region (TRF-3) restored telecommunications regulator Anatel's rule concerning irregular and unlicensed cellular phones sold on Amazon Brazil. The ruling reinforces regulatory enforcement against non-compliant electronics sales on major e-commerce platforms. This decision affects Amazon's operations in Brazil, device manufacturers, and regulatory compliance requirements for online retailers.
