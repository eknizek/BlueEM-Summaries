Catarina Airport, JHSF Property, Posts 28% Revenue Growth in Q1; Attracts Líder Participações to Executive Aviation Fair

08 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Catarina airport, owned by JHSF Participações, achieved 28% revenue growth in the first quarter, generating R$77.8 million in gross revenue. The property hosted its fifth annual executive aviation fair, which for the first time attracted Líder Participações, a major Brazilian capital national airline company interested in electric aircraft from Beta Technologies and the HondaJet. This signals rising corporate interest in sustainable aviation technology and executive travel amenities in Brazil.
