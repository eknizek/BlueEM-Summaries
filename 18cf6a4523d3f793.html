Tax revenue falls for ninth consecutive month: down 3.8% in April

04 May 2026 · 09:57 PM  ·  La Nación
Argentina's tax revenue contracted 3.8% in April, marking the ninth consecutive monthly decline. The deterioration was driven by reduced collections from foreign trade-related levies and taxes tied to overall economic activity levels. This sustained fiscal weakness reflects the ongoing economic slowdown in Argentina and presents challenges for government finances and public spending capacity during a period of macroeconomic stress.
