Family Office Ghia Cultivates R$5B in AUM by Serving Agribusiness Wealth Management

23 April 2026 · 07:55 PM  ·  Neofeed
Ghia Multi Family Office, founded in 2010 in Uberlândia, Minas Gerais, has scaled to approximately R$5 billion under management by serving the expanding wealth management needs of agribusiness entrepreneurs and investors. The family office has capitalized on the maturation of Brazil's agricultural sector, which is generating substantial wealth requiring specialized portfolio and estate planning services outside traditional banking channels. Ghia's growth reflects a structural shift in how high-net-worth agribusiness clients approach asset management and succession planning. The family office model is increasingly attractive to agricultural wealth creators seeking customized, long-term capital stewardship aligned with their operating businesses.
