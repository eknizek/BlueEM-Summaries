Government faces impasse with agribusiness and sees risk to fuel price containment plan

13 May 2026 · 09:09 PM  ·  Neofeed

The competing interests between the government's fiscal objectives and agricultural sector demands create an impasse that threatens the implementation of the fuel price containment plan.
