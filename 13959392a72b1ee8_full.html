# Government bans operation of Polymarket, Kalshi and other prediction market platforms in the country

The Ministry of Finance has blocked the operations of all companies mapped by the government that operate prediction markets in Brazil — 27 in total so far, including Polymarket and Kalshi, the most well-known in the segment.

According to the minister, there is no possibility of correction.
