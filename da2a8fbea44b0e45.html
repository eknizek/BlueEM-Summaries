Exclusive: After raising R$ 31M, BUSUP acquires Spanish IOMOB

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
BUSUP, a Brazilian mobility and transportation platform, has acquired Spain-based IOMOB after closing a R$ 31 million funding round. The capital raise and acquisition represent a significant expansion step for BUSUP as it moves into European markets and diversifies its service offerings through IOMOB's capabilities. This cross-border acquisition demonstrates the growing maturity of VC-backed Latin American startups expanding into developed markets, and the appetite for consolidation in the transportation and logistics technology space.
