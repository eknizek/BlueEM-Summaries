ProExplo 2026: Peru is Latin America's second country using AI to identify new mining exploration zones

04 May 2026 · 07:51 PM  ·  El Comercio - Portada
Peru has become the second Latin American country, after Brazil, to implement artificial intelligence for identifying new areas with mining potential. Alonso Marchena, head of INGEMMET's Metalogeny Program, disclosed this development during the proEXPLO 2026 International Congress of Prospectors and Explorers. The adoption of AI-driven exploration represents a significant modernization of Peru's mining sector strategy and could enhance discovery efficiency and resource identification capabilities.
