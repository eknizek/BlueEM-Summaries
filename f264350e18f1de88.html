Eternit Defies Slowdown, Announces USD 3.5 Million Investment and Doubles Capacity in Colombia

04 May 2026 · 02:34 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
Eternit, a construction materials company, is investing USD 3.5 million to double its production capacity across facilities in Barranquilla and Yumbo, Colombia. The investment includes launching new sustainable roofing products manufactured from recycled plastic, demonstrating a focus on environmental responsibility. The expansion is expected to generate additional employment and strengthen the company's competitive position despite broader economic slowdown.
