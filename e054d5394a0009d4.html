Z.ro Bank Shifts to Global Expansion After Closing Retail Accounts

02 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Z.ro Bank, a Brazilian fintech founded in 2019, has decided to discontinue its B2C (retail) operations to pursue international growth. The company will focus on providing cross-border payment infrastructure and cryptocurrency solutions to markets outside Brazil. This strategic pivot from consumer banking to B2B infrastructure positioning reflects changing market dynamics and investor appetite for specialized fintech solutions in emerging markets.
