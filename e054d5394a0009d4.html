Z.ro Bank shifts focus to global expansion after exiting consumer banking

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Z.ro Bank, founded in 2019, has exited the consumer (B2C) banking segment to concentrate on enterprise solutions. This strategic shift reflects a movement from fintech consumer banking toward B2B infrastructure plays with higher margins and global addressable markets.
