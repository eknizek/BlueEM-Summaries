# Inpasa Ships 45,000 Tons of DDGS to Turkey

Inpasa has shipped 45,000 tons of DDGS (Dried Distilled Grain with Solubles), a byproduct of corn processing, to Turkey. The company expects to produce 3.5 million tons of DDGS this year and export 1.5 million tons of the product.
