Mexico's Public EV Charging Network Expands 25% Annually

16 April 2026 · 06:46 PM

Mexico's public EV charging network expanded 25% annually, according to the Electro Movilidad Asociación (EMA). The national public charging network for electric vehicles grew 24.6% annually, increasing from 3,514 charging positions to 4,378 connection points.

Meanwhile, the private charging network reached 55,224 positions, representing an annual growth rate of 25.7%.
