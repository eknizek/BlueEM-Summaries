Nuevo León agricultural production falls 11% in 2025 amid drought and profitability challenges

18 April 2026 · 11:01 AM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Agricultural production in Nuevo León, Mexico fell 11.1% year-over-year in 2025, reaching 670,616 tonnes. The decline is attributed to severe drought conditions and poor profitability for farmers in the region. This production shortfall in a major Mexican agricultural state signals potential supply constraints for grains and other crops, with implications for food security and export capacity in the broader region.
