Sleeping budget: 1,442 districts have not used a quarter of funds for public works

20 April 2026 · 11:29 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
A critical public finance issue in Peru reveals that 1,442 out of 1,874 municipalities have failed to execute even 25% of their 2026 budgets allocated for public works, with 97 municipalities showing zero execution. This chronic underutilization of capital budgets undermines infrastructure development and public investment efficacy. The problem carries macroeconomic implications for Peru's infrastructure sector and fiscal policy effectiveness. Poor budget execution at the municipal level reflects administrative and institutional constraints.
