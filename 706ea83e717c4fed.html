OFAC Issues New Venezuela-related General Licenses Authorizing Commercial Negotiations of Contingent Contracts with GOV and Financial Services involving Named Venezuelan Banks

24 April 2026 · 03:12 PM  ·  Baker Mckenzie
The U.S. Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) issued new general licenses expanding certain authorizations for commercial negotiations and contingent contracts with the Venezuelan government and specific Venezuelan financial institutions. These licenses permit limited financial services activities with named Venezuelan banks subject to OFAC compliance. This represents a partial relaxation of sanctions pressure on Venezuela, potentially opening commercial and investment opportunities for qualified entities.
