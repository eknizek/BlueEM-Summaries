BTG Pactual Seeks Banking Regulatory Authorization from Peru's SBS

14 April 2026 · 05:01 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
BTG Pactual, one of Latin America's leading investment banks, has submitted an application to Peru's banking regulator (Superintendencia de Banca, Seguros y AFP) for authorization to operate as a commercial bank in Peru. This move signals BTG Pactual's expansion into traditional commercial banking in Peru and represents significant competition in the Peruvian banking market. The authorization request is subject to SBS regulatory review and approval.
