# Mexican Peso Strengthens for Second Consecutive Day; Dollar Exchange Rate on April 9, 2026

The Mexican peso advances for a second consecutive day and extends its gains in a cautious market monitoring a fragile ceasefire in the Middle East agreed between the United States and Iran, with the dollar price today, April 9, 2026, trading at 17.34 pesos; here you can see the exchange rate at Mexican banks.

The Mexican currency registers an appreciation of 0.54% against the reference price from Wednesday, when it recorded its largest daily gain since April 2025, driven by an improved environment for risk assets and a decrease in crude oil prices.

The advance of the Mexican peso defied a technical support level placed by Kapital Grupo Financiero at 17.35 pesos for the session, as investors also assimilated a series of economic data published in Mexico and the United States. Locally, it was announced that general inflation rose in March to its highest level in approximately a year and a half, while the underlying index decelerated. Meanwhile, the minutes from the most recent Bank of Mexico (Banxico) meeting showed a deeply divided board of directors on how to weigh new inflationary risks.
