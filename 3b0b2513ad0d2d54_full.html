# Peso Advances for Second Consecutive Day; Dollar Price Today April 9, 2026

The Mexican peso advances for a second consecutive trading day and extends its gains in a cautious market attentive to a fragile ceasefire in the Middle East agreed upon between the United States and Iran, with the dollar price today, April 9, 2026, trading at $17.34 pesos; here you can find the exchange rate in Mexican banks.

The Mexican currency registers an appreciation of 0.54% compared to Wednesday's reference price, when it recorded its largest daily gain since April 2025, driven by a better environment for risk assets and a decrease in crude oil prices.

The advance of the Mexican peso defied a technical support level placed by Kapital Grupo Financiero at 17.35 pesos for the session, as investors also assimilated a series of economic data published in Mexico and the United States.

On the local level, it was reported that general inflation rose in March to its highest level in approximately a year and a half, while the underlying index decelerated. For its part, the minutes from the most recent meeting of Mexico's central bank (Banxico) showed a deeply divided board of governors on how to weigh the new inflationary risks.
