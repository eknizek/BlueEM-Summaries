Climate Change Mobilizes Coffee Growers' Cooperatives

04 May 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  O Globo
Brazilian coffee cooperatives are mobilizing climate adaptation initiatives in response to rising temperatures, unpredictable rainfall, and pest pressures threatening the country's coffee production. These cooperative efforts focus on sustainable farming practices, water management, and crop diversification. The movement reflects broader industry recognition that climate resilience is critical to long-term profitability and market positioning in specialty coffee segments.
