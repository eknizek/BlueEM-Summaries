Miguel Galuccio prepares two investments in Vaca Muerta: upcoming RIGI projects

30 April 2026 · 08:48 PM  ·  Clarin
Miguel Galuccio's Vista Energy is progressing with documentation to submit RIGI (Régimen de Incentivo a Grandes Inversiones) guarantee requests in June for two Vaca Muerta blocks in Argentina. The company has improved its production and gross margin forecasts, supported by geopolitical tensions in the Middle East that strengthen global energy pricing. RIGI is Argentina's incentive regime for major foreign investments, offering tax and currency guarantees to attract capital to strategic sectors like energy.
