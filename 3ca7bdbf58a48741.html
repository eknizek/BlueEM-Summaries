Eight Brazilian Startups Enter Endeavor's 'Outliers' High-Growth Portfolio List

07 April 2026 · 03:38 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Brazil continues to be Endeavor's most important market and is growing in strategic importance, with 39 portfolio companies entering the 2024 Outliers high-growth list—approximately 17% of the global total. Eight Brazilian startups made their debut on the list this year. This reflects the country's deepening role as a VC hub and the quality of scaling startups across the region. For LatAm venture investors, this demonstrates sustained momentum in Brazil's startup ecosystem and validates the commercial quality of the portfolio.
