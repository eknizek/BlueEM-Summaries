Trupal from Gloria Group readies investment to modernize El Agustino plant

20 April 2026 · 09:35 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Trupal, a subsidiary of Peru's Grupo Gloria, is planning capital investment to modernize its production facility in El Agustino, Lima. The upgrade focuses on three areas: optimizing wastewater treatment systems, enhancing water recirculation efficiency, and ensuring full compliance with environmental regulations. This capex initiative reflects growing corporate focus on sustainable operations and regulatory adherence in Peru's manufacturing sector. Grupo Gloria is a major diversified food and beverage conglomerate.
