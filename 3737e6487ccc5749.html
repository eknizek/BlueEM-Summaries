Supersociedades confirms OMA reorganization and activates stabilization plan for 76 billion peso liabilities

21 April 2026 · 11:52 AM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia's Superintendence of Companies (Supersociedades) has confirmed a reorganization of OMA and activated a stabilization plan targeting 76 billion pesos in liabilities. The approval enables a ten-year payment schedule designed to restore the company's financial viability. This regulatory decision is material for stakeholders and creditors of OMA, signaling formal recognition of the restructuring process and providing a framework for debt resolution.
