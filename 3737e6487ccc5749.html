Supersociedades Confirms OMA Reorganization and Activates Stabilization Plan for $76 Billion Liabilities

21 April 2026 · 11:52 AM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Supersociedades (Colombia's business oversight authority) has confirmed the reorganization of OMA and approved a debt stabilization plan addressing approximately 76 billion pesos in liabilities. The plan establishes a ten-year payment schedule designed to restore the company's financial viability and creditworthiness. This regulatory approval is critical for OMA's recovery and provides clarity for creditors regarding repayment terms and the company's path to operational stability.
