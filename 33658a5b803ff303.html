Seedtag Announces NeuroX, Its AI Platform to Optimize Advertising Campaigns on Web and Television: How It Works

05 May 2026 · 08:14 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
Seedtag has announced NeuroX, an artificial intelligence platform designed to optimize advertising campaigns across digital and television channels. The platform analyzes impressions across Seedtag's global network of over 30,000 publishers and broadcasters, enabling enhanced campaign performance and targeting. This represents a strategic technology advancement in the adtech sector, relevant to investors tracking software innovation and digital marketing platform consolidation in Latin America and globally.
