GPA reaches creditor agreement, halves debt burden to R$2.1 billion

06 May 2026 · 01:03 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
GPA, a major Brazilian retail group, successfully negotiated a debt restructuring agreement with creditors that reduces its total debt from R$4.5 billion to R$2.1 billion—a reduction of over 50%. The company filed for judicial recovery (recuperação extrajudicial) in early March and subsequently reached the accord, which offers creditors three alternative resolution options including R$1.5 billion in debt relief. This restructuring is critical for the company's financial stabilization and return to operational viability.
