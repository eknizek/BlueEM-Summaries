Rohel Sánchez on Megapuerto Corío: 'Arequipa can become the Pacific's great connectivity hub'

08 May 2026 · 05:25 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Arequipa's regional governor Rohel Sánchez has promoted the Megapuerto de las Américas (Corío) mega-port as a transformational infrastructure project with capacity to position southern Peru as an international commerce, connectivity, industrialization, and technology hub. The project is designed to serve as a strategic platform connecting South America and Asia-Pacific markets, potentially reshaping Peru's role in global trade flows.
