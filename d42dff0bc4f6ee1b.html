Federal and São Paulo State formalize R$2.5 billion loan for Santos-Guarujá tunnel

12 April 2026 · 07:03 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
The Brazilian federal government and São Paulo State have formalized a R$2.5 billion loan agreement to finance the construction of the Santos-Guarujá tunnel, a major infrastructure project aimed at improving connectivity between the port of Santos and the city of Guarujá. This project is critical infrastructure for one of South America's busiest ports, with direct impact on logistics efficiency, trade flows, and regional economic development. The financing structure and terms are relevant to understanding Brazil's infrastructure investment capacity and asset quality for infrastructure-focused investors. Successful completion would enhance port throughput and reduce transportation costs for exporters.
