Millicom (Tigo parent) acquires Colombian state stake in Coltel following auction

24 April 2026 · 04:40 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Millicom, the parent company of Tigo, has successfully acquired the Colombian government's shareholding in Coltel through a competitive auction process. This transaction marks a significant privatization milestone, with the Colombian state divesting its long-standing stake in the country's telecommunications sector after more than 70 years of involvement. The sale reflects broader Latin American trends of state asset disposals and consolidation within the telecom industry.
