Millicom acquires Colombian government stake in Coltel following auction

24 April 2026 · 04:40 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Millicom International Cellular, parent company of Tigo, has acquired the Colombian government's remaining shareholding in Coltel following a public auction process. This transaction marks the Colombian state's full exit from direct ownership of the telecommunications operator after more than 70 years of involvement in the telecom sector. The sale reflects broader regional trends of government divestiture from utilities and telecom assets, consolidating ownership among major private operators.
