# Mexican Stock Exchange Falls 1.3%

The Mexican Stock Exchange (BMV) fell 1.3% on Monday, April 6, 2026, as international financial markets closed with mixed performance, conditioned by the conflict in the Middle East and uncertainty surrounding the Strait of Hormuz, through which approximately one-fifth of the world's oil and gas circulates.
