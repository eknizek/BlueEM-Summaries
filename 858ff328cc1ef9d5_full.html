Pemex denies operational collapse, describes situation as restructuring

06 April 2026 · 11:01 AM

# Pemex denies collapse: 'it is restructuring'

In response to claims from its personnel about a possible collapse in its services, Pemex assured that it is undergoing a process of "structural recovery" to reverse accumulated setbacks over decades, particularly in medical infrastructure, maintenance, and operations.

The deterioration at Pemex has turned into a crisis that impacts workers' health, the operation of facilities, and internal union stability.
