Only 8 of 42 Lima districts executed 30% of budget for public works

02 May 2026 · 01:45 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
A review of Lima's 42 districts reveals slow public works spending, with only 8 districts achieving 30% budget execution. Top performers include San Juan de Miraflores, Surco, and Comas, while 13 municipalities have spent less than 10% of allocated budgets. Barranco, La Victoria, and San Bartolo have executed zero percent of their budgets, indicating systemic inefficiencies in municipal capital spending and infrastructure delivery.
