Spirit Airlines bankruptcy impacts connectivity to Cancun from eight U.S. cities

02 May 2026 · 11:23 PM  ·  El Economista
Spirit Airlines' bankruptcy has resulted in reduced flight connectivity between Cancun and eight U.S. cities, affecting tourism and business travel flows to Mexico's premier beach destination. Quintana Roo's state governor Mara Lezama confirmed that the state continues operating normally with alternative air connectivity maintained and visitor safety assured. Authorities are coordinating with federal agencies to manage the transition and minimize disruption to the tourism sector, which is critical to the region's economy.
