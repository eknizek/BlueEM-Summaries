Generac and CPower Expand Access to DER Technologies to Support Resiliency, Energy Costs and Grid Capacity

07 April 2026 · 11:00 AM  ·  PR Newswire
Generac Holdings and CPower, a demand response and distributed energy resources provider, are expanding their partnership to increase market access to DER technologies that support grid resilience, lower energy costs, and improve capacity utilization. This reflects growing North American investment in distributed energy infrastructure and decarbonization. DER technologies including battery storage, microgrids, and demand-side management are critical for energy transition. For LatAm investors, DER expansion in the region remains nascent, though energy security and cost pressures create long-term opportunities, particularly in Brazil, Mexico, and Chile.
