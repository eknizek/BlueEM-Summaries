DIAN launches final opportunity for debtors: tax relief benefits expire April 30

19 April 2026 · 07:16 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia's tax authority (DIAN) announced the final opportunity for taxpayers to benefit from emergency economic relief measures under Decree 240, with benefits expiring April 30. The program includes reductions of up to 85% on penalties for tax debtors. This is material for Colombian corporates managing tax liabilities and represents a policy stimulus measure affecting government revenues and corporate cash flows.
