Exxon sees 'positive' scenario and returns to considering investment in Venezuela, according to CEO

02 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea

The CEO's comments suggest potential softening of sanctions or political conditions that could enable foreign oil companies to re-engage with Venezuelan operations. For energy investors, this signals potential opportunities in Venezuelan oil and gas assets if normalization continues, alongside geopolitical and regulatory risks.
