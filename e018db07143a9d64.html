ISA reports 20% earnings decline in Q1; wealth tax impact totaled $15 billion

09 May 2026 · 12:35 AM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
ISA, Colombia's major infrastructure operator, reported a 20% decline in Q1 2025 earnings, attributable to currency movements and a $15 billion impact from the wealth tax. The company cited the strengthening of the Colombian peso relative to the Brazilian real, US dollar, and Chilean peso as significant headwinds. These results reflect both macroeconomic pressures and the effect of new Colombian fiscal policy on multinational infrastructure companies.
