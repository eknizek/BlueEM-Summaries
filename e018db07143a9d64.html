ISA reports 20% profit decline in first quarter; wealth tax impact of 15bn pesos

07 May 2026 · 02:25 AM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
ISA, a major Colombian infrastructure and energy company, reported a 20% decline in first-quarter profits, impacted by a 15-billion-peso charge from Colombia's new wealth tax. Currency headwinds from the appreciation of the Colombian peso against the Brazilian real, US dollar, and Chilean peso also weighed on results. The company's Latin American operations, particularly in Brazil and Chile, were affected by unfavorable foreign exchange movements.
