From delivery to dining: iFood acquires 100% of reservation startup Get In

29 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
iFood, Brazil's dominant food delivery platform, completed the full acquisition of Get In, a restaurant reservation technology startup, marking a strategic vertical integration into the dining experience. The deal enables iFood to extend its restaurant ecosystem beyond delivery by capturing reservations, table management, and customer data. This acquisition strengthens iFood's competitive moat by creating a more comprehensive platform for restaurant partners and aims to drive higher customer engagement and lifetime value.
