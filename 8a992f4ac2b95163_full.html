# CBSF becomes B100 and debuts unique three-digit ticker on B3

The Brazilian Financial Services Company (CBSF), a fiduciary administration company from Reag listed on the stock exchange and sold to Planner's holding company following the Hidden Carbon Operation, has changed its name to B100 S/A, as approved at an extraordinary general meeting on April 30. The change had been approved by B100's board of directors in March, when the replacement of the old trading code on B3 for the new ticker B1003 was also announced. This is the first code on the Brazilian stock exchange made up of three numerals.

The completion of the change represents another stage in the process initiated with the acquisition of the listed company structure that belonged to Reag/Ciabrasf, the former controlling shareholder. At the same meeting, shareholders approved the amendment to the bylaws to remove provisions that required the launch of a public share acquisition offer in case of reaching relevant participation in the company.

B100 completed the acquisition of 97% of CBSF's capital for a symbolic value of R$ 1,000, in a transaction that shifted the economic value of the business to the future, with variable mechanisms linked to performance or a possible liquidity event. The operation, announced in September, was completed in January, weeks before the Central Bank decreed the extrajudicial liquidation of Reag Trust DTVM.
