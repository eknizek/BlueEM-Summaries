MBRF boosts Uruguay beef capacity in $70m expansion

24 April 2026 · 07:00 PM  ·  O Globo (Brazil)
MBRF, a major Brazilian beef producer, is investing $70 million to expand beef production capacity in Uruguay. This expansion reflects strong regional consolidation within South America's livestock sector and MBRF's strategy to increase production footprint across multiple countries. The investment signals confidence in regional demand and the company's competitive positioning in export-oriented beef markets.
