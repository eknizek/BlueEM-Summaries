Economic Activity Points to GDP Contraction

25 April 2026 · 11:01 AM  ·  Reforma - Portada
Mexican financial groups are aligned in their interpretation that the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (Inegi) data on IGAE (monthly economic activity index) for the first quarter indicates an upcoming contraction in quarterly GDP. This consensus view among analysts reflects concerns about the strength of Mexico's economic growth trajectory. The IGAE, a leading indicator of economic activity, provides an early signal of GDP performance.
