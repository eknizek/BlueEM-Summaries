Peru's Agricultural Sector Grows 2.4% in First Two Months of 2026

20 April 2026 · 06:06 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's Ministry of Agricultural Development (Midagri) reported that the agricultural sector grew 2.4% during January-February 2026 compared to the same period in 2025. The agricultural subsector expanded 2.9% while the livestock subsector grew 1.8%, indicating broad-based strength. This growth reflects improved production levels across Peru's key agribusiness segments and suggests positive momentum for the sector heading into 2026.
