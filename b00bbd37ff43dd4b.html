Brazil set for record wheat imports in 2026

27 April 2026 · 05:00 PM  ·  O Globo
Brazil is projected to import record volumes of wheat in 2026 as domestic wheat production fails to meet national demand. This reflects structural challenges in Brazilian wheat farming, including weather, yields, and competition for arable land with higher-margin crops like soy. Record imports have implications for Brazil's agricultural trade balance, food security, commodity pricing, and agribusiness investment opportunities.
