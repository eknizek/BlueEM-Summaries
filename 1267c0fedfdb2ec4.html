Desenrola 2 Provides Relief but Does Not Resolve Brazil's Structural Debt Crisis

30 April 2026 · 09:15 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazil's government is preparing to launch Desenrola 2, an emergency debt renegotiation program for defaulted individuals, three years after the initial Desenrola Brasil initiative (instituted via provisional measure in June 2023). While the program provides temporary relief to over-indebted households, analysts argue it addresses symptoms rather than structural drivers of Brazil's persistent personal debt crisis. The program reflects ongoing consumer credit stress in the Brazilian economy.
