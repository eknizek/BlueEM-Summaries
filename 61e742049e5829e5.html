Colombia Buys Dollars Ahead of $4 Billion Bond Buyback Plan

15 April 2026 · 08:34 PM  ·  Bloomberg
Colombia is purchasing dollars on the foreign exchange market to build reserves ahead of executing a $4 billion sovereign bond buyback program. This debt management strategy aims to reduce Colombia's outstanding debt stock and improve its debt maturity profile by repurchasing bonds at favorable prices. The buyback operation reflects the government's confidence in its fiscal position and commitment to debt reduction, which supports credit metrics and investor confidence in Colombian sovereign credit quality.
