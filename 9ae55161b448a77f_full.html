# Rise in Measles Cases Prompts PAHO to Call Countries to Strengthen Vaccination

Due to the rise in measles cases, PAHO (Pan-American Health Organization) has called on countries in the Americas to intensify immunization actions. The Vaccination Week in the Americas begins this Saturday (25th) and runs until next Saturday (2nd).
