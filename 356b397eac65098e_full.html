# Supersociedades Confirmed Presto Reorganization Agreement

In a public hearing this Thursday, the reorganization agreement for the company Franquicias y Concesiones SAS, known in the market by the Presto brand, was confirmed. "This agreement constitutes a clear example of how creditor support, reflected in capital reductions and coordinated decisions, can make business rescue viable," said Nini Johanna Castañeda, acting Superintendent of Companies.

The decision was adopted after verifying that the presented agreement obtained a favorable vote of 81.39% of qualified creditors.

With this agreement, Presto will address a total liability of COP$31 billion after a capital reduction of COP$19 billion with fifth-class creditors and COP$211 million with second-class creditors was approved.

The total liability to be reorganized will be addressed over a period of six years.

The company Franquicias y Concesiones SAS was established in November 2002 and is engaged in the sale and distribution of food products. With the execution of the agreement, the company will seek, according to Supersociedades, the preservation of 694 direct jobs as well as the operation of 180 sales points in Colombia.

The acting superintendent added that this is a significant effort on the part of creditors, which demonstrates their confidence in the company's recovery, and an equally relevant commitment on the part of the company to reorganize its operations and meet its obligations on time.
