Eurofarma and Novo Nordisk Partnership in the GLP-1 Weight-Loss Drug Market

20 April 2026 · 06:58 PM  ·  Neofeed
Novo Nordisk's semaglutide patent expired in Brazil on March 20, 2026, opening the market to generic and biosimilar competition for blockbuster weight-loss drugs Ozempic and Wegovy. The Danish pharma company, which unsuccessfully sought legal patent extension, has partnered with Brazilian pharmaceutical company Eurofarma to strengthen its competitive position before generic versions flood the market. This partnership positions both companies to capture market share in the high-growth GLP-1 agonist category as the competitive landscape intensifies.
