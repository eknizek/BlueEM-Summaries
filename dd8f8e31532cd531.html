Eurofarma and Novo Nordisk Team Up in Battle for Weight-Loss Market

20 April 2026 · 06:58 PM  ·  Neofeed
Novo Nordisk's patent for semaglutide, the active ingredient in blockbuster weight-loss injections Ozempic and Wegovy, expired in Brazil on March 20, 2026. After an unsuccessful legal attempt to extend patent protection, Novo Nordisk is partnering with Brazilian pharma company Eurofarma to maintain market position ahead of competing generic and biosimilar versions. The patent expiration opens a significant commercial opportunity in Brazil's weight-loss market.
