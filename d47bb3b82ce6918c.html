Elite Cattle Auctions Hit Record, Reaching R$ 218 Million in Uberaba (MG)

02 May 2026 · 05:32 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Auctions of elite cattle linked to Expozebu in Uberaba, Minas Gerais, set a new record exceeding R$ 218 million, surpassing the previous high of R$ 200 million with three days remaining in the event series. The strong auction results reflect continued demand for premium livestock genetics. This represents significant commercial activity in Brazil's premium beef cattle sector.
