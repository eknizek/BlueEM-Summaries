Milei's 'chainsaw' slashes energy subsidies for three million households

04 May 2026 · 05:08 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times
President Milei's government has significantly reduced energy subsidies to their lowest levels in years, affecting approximately three million households in Argentina. The subsidy elimination and tariff reform program has generated US$5.6 billion in savings according to government figures. Nearly half of all households now pay the full energy tariff rather than subsidized rates, marking a major shift in the government's fiscal consolidation strategy and energy pricing policy.
