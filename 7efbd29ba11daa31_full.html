## What is the MEP Dollar?

The MEP dollar is an exchange rate higher than the official dollar, which appears as the cheapest option for purchasing as a store of value.

## How the MEP Dollar Works

The MEP dollar consists of purchasing a bond in pesos and selling it in dollars. This exchange rate is calculated by dividing the price in pesos by the dollar quotation.

## What Time Does the MEP Dollar Quotation Close?

The MEP dollar quotation operates during the same hours as the stock market: until 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday.

## Why is it Called MEP Dollar?

Its name derives from the acronym 'MEP', which means Electronic Payment Market. It is a variant of the savings dollar, as it presents fewer restrictions as mentioned above.
