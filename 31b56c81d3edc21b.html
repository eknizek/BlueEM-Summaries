Colombian coffee brand bets on Spain as 'springboard' for premium coffee in Europe

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Juan Valdez, Colombia's iconic coffee brand, is leveraging Spain as a gateway to expand its premium segment presence across Europe. The company launched a limited-edition product in partnership with Spanish designer Ágatha Ruiz de la Prada and plans to open up to 140 retail stores in Spain within six years. This European expansion strategy positions Spain as the critical entry point for consolidating Juan Valdez's premium positioning in the broader European market.
