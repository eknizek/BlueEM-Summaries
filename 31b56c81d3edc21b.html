Colombian coffee brand uses Spain as 'springboard' for premium expansion into Europe

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Juan Valdez, a Colombian premium coffee brand, is using Spain as a strategic gateway to establish its presence in Europe's high-end coffee segment. The company has launched limited-edition products in partnership with Spanish designer Ágatha Ruiz de la Prada and projects opening up to 140 retail locations across Spain within six years. This expansion strategy reflects Latin American consumer brands' increasing appetite for direct-to-consumer retail in developed markets, leveraging European entry points to build global premium positioning.
