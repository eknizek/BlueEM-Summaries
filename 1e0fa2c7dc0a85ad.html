Sebrae opens applications for AI acceleration program targeting small and medium enterprises

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Sebrae has opened applications for Scale AI, an acceleration program targeting 30 startups developing AI solutions for micro and small enterprises (MPEs) in Brazil. The program aims to match startup innovation with specific pain points faced by the SME sector. Applications close on April 26.
