Spanish multinational Telefónica advances its exit from Latin America with the sale of its Mexican subsidiary

08 April 2026 · 04:36 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Telefónica is advancing its strategic withdrawal from Latin America through the sale of its Mexican operations, marking a key milestone in the company's broader regional exit strategy. The Spanish telecom giant still needs to complete the disposal of its Venezuelan assets, its last remaining market in the region. This restructuring reflects Telefónica's shift toward focusing on its European core markets, particularly Spain and Europe, and represents a significant strategic repositioning in one of the world's most competitive telecommunications regions.
