Credicorp Ltd. Announces Filing of Form 20-F 2025

27 April 2026 · 08:05 PM  ·  GlobeNewswire
Credicorp Ltd., the parent company of major Latin American financial institutions including Banco Creditista and Banco del Perú, has announced the filing of its Form 20-F for fiscal year 2025 with the U.S. SEC. This annual report provides comprehensive disclosure of the company's financial performance, corporate governance, risk factors, and operational results across its regional banking operations. The Form 20-F filing is a key disclosure event for investors monitoring Credicorp's financial health and strategic direction. The filing includes detailed financial statements and management discussion essential for equity and credit investors evaluating the group.
