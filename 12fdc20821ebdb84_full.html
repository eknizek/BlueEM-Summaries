# Senator Flávio Bolsonaro Cannot Resist 24 Hours of Fiscal Adjustment Criticism

Senator Flávio Bolsonaro (PL) could not withstand 24 hours of criticism about fiscal adjustment on social media. A pre-candidate for the presidency, he called fake news a Folha report stating that he would implement an initial adjustment of around two percentage points of GDP if elected. For this, his plans would involve adjusting pensions and expenses with health and education only by inflation rates.

The senator forgot to align his message with the team responsible for his economic program. In search of support, his advisors have been conveying to Faria Lima and business sectors the message that he would be the minister of spending cuts. The pre-candidate himself has been selling himself as a savior of public accounts in contrast to Lula, portrayed as a spendthrift president.

The adjustment proposals have been detailed behind the scenes, as journalist Fernando Canzian anticipated in the report that caused discomfort, as it stirred up Lula supporters. It is not fake news. His team considers them to include the decoupling of health and education expenses and the separation between real wage increase policy for active workers and pension and BPC adjustments. Deindexing is the most difficult of the measures.

The denial by the son of former president Jair Bolsonaro reveals that fiscal adjustment proposals are a political liability for a candidate. In Congress, Flávio was never a defender of fiscal adjustment during his father's administration. The so-called "DDD" constitutional amendment (Deobligate, Deindex, Decouple), presented in 2019 as a solution for public accounts by former Economy Minister Paulo Guedes, did not receive support from Bolsonarism, beyond empty rhetoric in favor of fiscal responsibility. Jair Bolsonaro rejected ending the Christmas bonus, a notoriously inefficient benefit, and sponsored the Kamikaze amendment, costing billions.

If Bolsonaro's number 01 says it is fake news, he will have to speak openly about what he intends to do to adjust the accounts. Otherwise, he is either selling snake oil to the market or committing fraud against voters.
