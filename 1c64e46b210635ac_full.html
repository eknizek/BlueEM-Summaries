Decathlon to open three new stores in southern Chile to strengthen sports equipment market leadership

04 May 2026 · 09:11 PM

# Decathlon to Open Three New Stores in Southern Chile to Strengthen Sports Equipment Market Leadership

The sporting goods chain Decathlon has given a new boost to its expansion plan in Chile, announcing the opening of new stores this year in the country's southern region. The new locations will be situated in the cities of Temuco, Valdivia, and Osorno, where the French-origin firm seeks to absorb demand from a relevant sports and tourism hub for the country, and respond to the needs of both national and foreign consumers.

For the company, this territorial expansion is fundamental to strengthen its market share and connect with local stakeholders. In this regard, Decathlon's Expansion and Development leader, Jorge González, explained that the decision represents "a key step within our development strategy," since the challenge aims to "bring sports and wellness to more and more people, adapting to the characteristics of each territory, with an unmatched offering."

The executive added that the new facilities "not only expand our omnichannel network and physical contact points, but also strengthen our commitment to local communities, promoting active and healthy lifestyles."

## Upcoming Openings

The first milestone of this growth plan is scheduled for mid-year with the inauguration of the Osorno store. It will be more than 1,000 square meters dedicated to equipment for high-demand disciplines in the region, such as trekking, running, cycling, winter sports, and outdoor activities. The new store will be located in the Cenco Osorno shopping center.

The opening schedule will continue during the second half of the year, when the inauguration of the Valdivia branch at Mall Paseo Valdivia and the Temuco location at Cenco Temuco are projected.

From the perspective of economic and labor impact, this commercial venture will generate an approximate 10% increase in the company's total workforce in the country. In response to this boost to regional employment, González highlighted that Decathlon seeks to position itself as a relevant employer brand, fostering a differentiating environment amid its expansion.

## From Entrepreneurship to the World

Decathlon was born in France in 1976 when a group of seven entrepreneurs decided to implement an unprecedented store that would bring together equipment for multiple sports at the best price. It arrived in Chile in 2018 with the opening of its first branch in La Reina, beginning a continuous growth that included the launch of its e-commerce platform in 2019, the inauguration of its first logistics center in 2020, and the presentation of its mobile application at the end of 2023.

Currently, it operates 12 stores in the country under large-format ("flagship") and neighborhood ("connect") formats, in the cities of La Serena, Viña del Mar, Talca, Concepción, Puerto Montt, as well as various points in the Metropolitan Region, with a staff of over one thousand employees and offerings in 65 sports disciplines.
