Vinci Compass Merges Argentine Asset Management Operations, Doubles Regional Footprint

22 April 2026 · 09:05 PM  ·  Neofeed
Vinci Compass, managing R$354 billion in assets, announced a strategic merger of its Argentine asset management business with BACS, a manager affiliated with Banco Hipotecario. The transaction doubles Vinci Compass's asset management footprint in Argentina and represents a key step in the firm's regional consolidation strategy across Latin America. Led by Alessandro Horta, the combined entity strengthens the group's competitive position in the region's wealth management market.
