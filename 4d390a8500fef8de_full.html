# Edgardo del Rincón Becomes New CEO of Banamex; Manuel Romo Resigns

Banamex announced changes in its top leadership. Edgardo del Rincón has been named as the new Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of the institution, taking on the challenge of replacing Manuel Romo, who has led the bank for the last seven years.

This change represents the return of one of the most experienced figures in the sector to what was his home for more than three decades, with the objective of Banamex recovering absolute leadership of the Mexican market.

According to the institution's statement, Manuel Romo will remain at the head of Banamex until June 1, 2026. His departure marks the end of a cycle that began in April 2019, when he returned to the bank to lead consumer products, before being named Chief Executive Officer of Citibanamex Financial Group in November of that same year.

Romo stated that his resignation responds to personal reasons, specifically to focus on social development projects. In his farewell, he assured that he is delivering a financial group that is "solid, well-positioned and growing," trusting the future of the institution to the vision of Fernando Chico Pardo, reference shareholder.

"After almost seven years at the helm of Banamex, I am closing a professional cycle with deep pride in what we achieved. I leave enormously grateful to its people and its extraordinary management team," Romo declared.

The appointment has the backing of the Board of Directors. Fernando Chico Pardo, President of the Board, highlighted the delivery of a "strong and dynamic" bank by Romo.

"He is delivering a strong and dynamic Banamex ready for the next stage. Thanks to what the team has built with him, we have an excellent foundation to return Banamex to where we all want it to be, in first place," he emphasized.

The goal is also to bring Banamex back to first place in Mexico's financial sector.

## Who is Edgardo del Rincón, Banamex's New CEO?

With 40 years of experience in banking, Edgardo del Rincón is rejoining Banamex, where he worked for 34 years before his time at other institutions. Until recently, he served as Chief Executive Officer of Banco del Bajío (since 2019), where he was recognized for delivering substantial returns to investors. His experience spans from corporate banking to leadership in Consumer Banking, making him a comprehensive profile for the current digitalization challenges.

"I am joining a great management team to give continuity to the strategic plan, driving our technological transformation and digitalization, with the customer at the center of everything we do," del Rincón declared.

In a statement, BanBajío announced that Edgardo del Rincón presented his resignation as Chief Executive Officer and member of the board of directors of the institution. According to BanBajío, Edgardo del Rincón's resignation is effective as of May 1, 2026.
