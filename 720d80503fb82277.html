Canacol contract cancellations would increase gas bills, industry warns

05 May 2026 · 11:47 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombian business associations issued warnings that potential contract terminations by gas producer Canacol would significantly increase consumer gas bills and threaten regional supply stability on the Caribbean coast. The disruption would affect approximately 50% of demand in the region and jeopardize mining operations, including Cerro Matoso. Gas producers and industrial users expressed serious concerns about supply continuity and pricing impacts. The situation reflects tensions between government policy toward domestic energy producers and energy security.
