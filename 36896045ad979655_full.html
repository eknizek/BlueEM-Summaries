Canacar Endorses ANTAC Complaints But Calls for Dialogue

The National Chamber of Freight Transportation (Canacar) stated that its members suffer from the same problems that affect the National Association of Transporters (ANTAC); however, it called for opening dialogue and establishing permanent working tables with authorities.

For Canacar, it is necessary that transporters work with the Secretary of Interior and not be outside blocking highways.
