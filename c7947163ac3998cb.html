Astor raises $5M for AI-powered investment advisory services

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Astor, a Brazilian investment advisory fintech, has raised $5 million in funding to accelerate the development and deployment of artificial intelligence-powered advisory services. The capital will support expansion of the platform's AI capabilities for personalized investment recommendations. This reflects growing investor appetite for AI-enhanced wealth management solutions in Latin America.
