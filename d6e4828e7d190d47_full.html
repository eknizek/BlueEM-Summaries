Inner AI Reaches R$500M Valuation With Round Led by Canary and OneVC

The artificial intelligence platform Inner AI has just raised R$30 million in a funding round co-led by Canary and OneVC, with participation from Norte, Crivo, and British firm Phenomen VC. The investment valued the company at R$500 million and brought total fundraising to R$42 million, including the R$12 million from the pre-seed round.

The resources will be allocated to platform expansion and the launch of Squad.com, a new interface based on autonomous task agents under the concept of a digital team, which will operate continuously (24 hours a day, seven days a week) and will only alert the user when necessary, rather than depending on commands at each step.

The Squad will integrate under an independent brand a single product with several agents, three of which at launch: Maky, for marketing, generates leads by creating and scheduling Instagram posts, in addition to responding to comments and dispatching personalized campaigns for customer recovery. Waz handles service and sales via WhatsApp, with real-time scheduling and follow-ups. Finally, Fin, the financial agent, processes sales by generating payment links, recording expenses, and projecting cash flow.

"Brazil is one of the largest AI markets in the world and we have extremely high-level talent. It doesn't make sense to just import this technology. This round represents the ambition of Brazilian venture capital to bet on something unprecedented," says Pedro Salles, CEO of Inner AI.

"Large companies have marketing, operations, and service teams; successful people have investment advisors, nutritionists, lawyers. But most people don't have access to any of that. Now, for the first time, AI makes it possible to level the playing field. And we're starting with the Brazilian entrepreneur," Salles adds.

The launch includes a temporary store at Conjunto Nacional in São Paulo, where the public will be able to test the product. The company has also opened a waiting list for access to the service.
