Inner AI reaches R$500M valuation with Canary and OneVC-led round

The artificial intelligence platform Inner AI has just raised R$30 million in a round co-led by Canary and OneVC, with participation from Norte, Crivo, and British firm Phenomen VC. The investment valued the company at R$500 million and brought total fundraising to R$42 million, including the R$12 million from the pre-seed round.

The resources will be allocated to expanding the platform and launching Squad.com, a new interface based on autonomous task agents under the concept of a digital team that will operate continuously (24 hours a day, seven days a week) and will only alert the user when necessary, rather than depending on commands at each step.

The Squad will integrate into an independent brand a single product with multiple agents, three of them at launch: Maky, for marketing, generates leads by creating and scheduling Instagram posts, in addition to responding to comments and launching personalized campaigns for customer recovery. Waz handles customer service and sales via WhatsApp, with real-time scheduling and follow-ups. Finally, Fin, the financial agent, processes sales by generating payment links, recording expenses, and projecting cash flow.

"Brazil is one of the world's largest AI markets and we have extremely high-level talent. It doesn't make sense to simply import this technology. This round represents the ambition of Brazilian venture capital in betting on something unprecedented," says Pedro Salles, CEO of Inner AI.

"Large companies have marketing, operations, and customer service teams; successful people have investment advisors, nutritionists, lawyers. But most people don't have access to any of this. Now, for the first time, AI makes it possible to level the playing field. And we're starting with Brazilian entrepreneurs," Salles adds.

The launch includes a temporary store at Conjunto Nacional in São Paulo, where the public can test the product. The company has also opened a waiting list for access to the service.
