Inner AI reaches R$ 500 million valuation with funding round led by Canary and OneVC

The artificial intelligence platform Inner AI has just raised R$ 30 million in a funding round co-led by Canary and OneVC, with participation from Norte, Crivo, and British firm Phenomen VC. The investment values the company at R$ 500 million and brings total funding to R$ 42 million, including the R$ 12 million from the pre-seed round. The funds will be used to expand the platform and launch Squad.com, a new interface based on autonomous task agents, under the concept of a digital team that will operate continuously (24 hours a day, seven days a week) and will contact the user only when necessary, rather than depending on step-by-step commands.

The Squad will integrate into an independent brand a single product with multiple agents, three of which are launching initially: Maky, for marketing, generates leads by creating and scheduling Instagram posts, as well as responding to comments and launching personalized campaigns to recover customers. Waz handles customer service and sales via WhatsApp, with real-time scheduling and follow-ups. Finally, Fin, the financial agent, processes sales by generating payment links, recording expenses, and projecting cash flow.

"Brazil is one of the world's largest AI markets and we have talent of the highest level. It doesn't make sense to just import this technology. This round represents the ambition of Brazilian venture capital to invest in something unprecedented," says Pedro Salles, CEO of Inner AI.

"Large companies have marketing, operations, and customer service teams; successful people have investment advisors, nutritionists, lawyers. But most people don't have access to any of this. Now, for the first time, AI makes it possible to level the playing field. And we're starting with the Brazilian entrepreneur," says Salles.

The launch includes a pop-up store at Conjunto Nacional in São Paulo, where the public can test the product. The company has also opened a waitlist for access to the service.
