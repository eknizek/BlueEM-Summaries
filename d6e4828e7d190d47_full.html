# Inner AI Reaches R$500 Million Valuation in Canary and OneVC-Led Round

The artificial intelligence platform Inner AI has just raised R$30 million in a round co-led by Canary and OneVC, with participation from Norte, Crivo, and British firm Phenomen VC. The investment valued the company at R$500 million and brought total fundraising to R$42 million, including the R$12 million from the pre-seed round.

The funds will be used to expand the platform and launch Squad.com, a new interface based on autonomous task agents under the concept of a digital team, which will operate continuously (24 hours a day, seven days a week) and will only engage the user when necessary, rather than depending on step-by-step commands.

The Squad will integrate into an independent brand a single product with multiple agents, three of them at launch: Maky, for marketing, generates leads by creating and scheduling Instagram posts, in addition to responding to comments and launching personalized campaigns for customer recovery. Waz handles customer service and sales via WhatsApp, with scheduling and real-time follow-ups. Finally, Fin, for finance, processes sales, generating payment links, recording expenses, and projecting cash flow.

"Brazil is one of the largest AI markets in the world and we have top-level talent. It doesn't make sense to only import this technology. This round represents the ambition of Brazilian venture capital in betting on something unprecedented," says Pedro Salles, CEO of Inner AI.

"Large companies have marketing, operations, and customer service teams; successful people have investment advisors, nutritionists, lawyers. But most don't have access to any of this. Now, for the first time, AI allows us to level that playing field. And we're starting with the Brazilian entrepreneur," says Salles.

The launch includes a temporary store at Conjunto Nacional in São Paulo, where the public can test the product. The company has also opened a waitlist for access to the service.
