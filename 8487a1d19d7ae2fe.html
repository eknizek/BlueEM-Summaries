Mendoza real estate development targets mining, energy, fintech and logistics firms

20 April 2026 · 08:55 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Grupo Presidente, a diversified developer with holdings in agro-exports and auto dealerships, has unveiled an office complex in Mendoza aimed at attracting mining, energy, fintech, and logistics sector companies. The development features co-working spaces, networking facilities, streaming studios, and recreational amenities including a paddle tennis court. The project reflects a strategy to position Mendoza as a service hub for Argentina's natural resource and technology-driven industries.
