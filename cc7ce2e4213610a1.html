Americans Close Billion-Dollar Deal for Brazilian Rare Earths

20 April 2026 · 08:12 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
USA Rare Earth announced an acquisition of 100% of Serra Verde, a mining company operating in Goiás state, for $2.8 billion. The deal gives American interests control over Brazil's first discovered rare earth mineral reserve. This transaction occurs as Brazilian policymakers discuss establishing a state-owned rare earth company, highlighting the strategic importance of these critical minerals for technology and defense applications.
