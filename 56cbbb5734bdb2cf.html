New tool streamlines Pix refunds for banks and fintechs

10 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Stark Infra has developed a tool to help banks and fintech companies meet new MED 2.0 regulatory requirements for Pix transaction refunds and reversals. The platform provides real-time processing, integration capabilities, and full transaction traceability to support compliance with evolving central bank standards. This infrastructure solution addresses operational and regulatory challenges in Brazil's rapidly evolving payments ecosystem. The tool enables financial institutions to automate refund processing while maintaining audit trails required by regulators.
