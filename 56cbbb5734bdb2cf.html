New Tool Facilitates Pix Transfer Reversals

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Stark Infra, a fintech infrastructure provider, has released a new tool enabling banks and financial technology companies to handle Pix transfer reversals in compliance with Brazil's updated MED 2.0 regulations. The solution provides real-time processing, integration capabilities, and full transaction traceability—essential for regulatory compliance. This reflects Brazil's evolving payment system regulations and the need for infrastructure providers to support emerging compliance requirements. The tool demonstrates the competitive fintech ecosystem in Brazil and ongoing infrastructure modernization of the Pix instant payment system.
