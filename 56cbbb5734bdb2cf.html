New Tool Simplifies Pix Transfer Reversals for Banks and Fintechs

08 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Stark Infra has launched a new tool designed to help banks and fintechs comply with Brazil's MED 2.0 regulations governing instant payment (Pix) transaction reversals. The solution provides a infrastructure layer with enhanced traceability, integration capabilities, and real-time processing to support seamless Pix refund workflows. This innovation addresses regulatory requirements while improving customer experience in Brazil's instant payments ecosystem, a critical competitive differentiator for financial services providers.
