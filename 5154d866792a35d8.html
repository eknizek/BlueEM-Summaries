Knowledge gap slows regenerative farming adoption in Brazil

27 April 2026 · 07:00 PM  ·  O Globo
Inadequate knowledge and technical understanding among Brazilian farmers is constraining the adoption of regenerative farming practices. Regenerative agriculture—focused on soil health, carbon sequestration, and sustainability—represents a growing investment opportunity, but knowledge gaps hinder farmer uptake. Addressing education gaps is critical for scaling sustainable agriculture as an ESG-focused investment theme in Brazil.
