# Fiscal Reform Weakens Public Spending Controls, Warns México Evalúa

The tax reform contains modifications that weaken mechanisms for controlling spending and the use of public debt, warned México Evalúa.

The reform to the LFPRH modifies the definition of structural current spending to exclude two items. Credit: Bloomberg.

Mexico City (April 9, 2026) — 14:53 hrs

The fiscal reform contains modifications that weaken control mechanisms for spending and the use of public debt, warned México Evalúa. The reform to the LFPRH (Law of Budgetary and Fiscal Responsibility) modifies the definition of structural current spending to exclude two categories, representing what critics describe as a "low blow" to fiscal rules.
