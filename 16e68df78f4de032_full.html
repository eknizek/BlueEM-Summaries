# Former BRB President May Attempt Plea Deal in Master Case

Arrested in the fourth phase of the Federal Police's Compliance Zero operation, Paulo Henrique Costa, the former president of BRB, may now have incentives to pursue and close a plea deal even before Daniel Vorcaro, the owner of Master, who has already signed a confidentiality agreement to begin collaboration.

Accused of passive corruption and money laundering, Costa is suspected of having concealed six properties received as bribes, valued at R$ 146.5 million. Since he was not the head of the criminal organization, as Vorcaro was, his eventual plea deal pointing to high-ranking individuals involved in the scandal could be of great importance to the investigations.

Before his arrest, he was advised to make a plea deal but resisted the idea even knowing that investigations were advancing with robust evidence against him. The reasons remain unknown today. It is suspected that he may have received (or had a promise of receiving in the future) financial or other advantages in exchange for not cooperating. Another possibility, less plausible but cannot be ruled out, is that Costa was not fully aware that Vorcaro kept so many incriminating messages against him on his cell phone.

In prison, the situation changes. PH, as he is called, may have no reason to spare anyone. Since he did not cooperate before, to now obtain a sentence reduction, he would have to turn over everyone. Everyone means including politicians who are staying on the sidelines of the scandal's center. Had he cooperated with the investigation earlier, it is possible he would have had to turn over fewer people and obtained more benefits. Since he delayed too long, it will be necessary to incriminate very important figures and still receive fewer sentence reduction benefits.

Any eventual collaboration would certainly compete with Vorcaro's. The Master owner would certainly have to raise the bar on evidence and return of stolen money to mitigate sentences. The former BRB president could be the candidate to be the bombshell witness in the case.
