Nubank Takes Naming Rights of Palmeiras Stadium

10 April 2026 · 04:44 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)

The deal reflects Nubank's aggressive brand expansion strategy in sports marketing and its ability to deploy capital for brand building.
