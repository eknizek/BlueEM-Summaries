Franchises to Move US$1.6 Billion in 2026 by Expansion of Local Brands and New Formats, Says CCL

07 April 2026 · 10:01 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
According to the Lima Chamber of Commerce (CCL), Peru's franchise sector is on a solid recovery trajectory, with projected revenue of US$1.6 billion in 2026—representing 8% YoY growth and a return to pre-pandemic revenue levels. Growth will be driven by expansion of local franchises and new business format innovation. The recovery reflects improving consumer confidence, expansion of domestic brands regionally, and a maturing franchise ecosystem in Peru. For LatAm PE and growth equity investors, this signals healthy M&A opportunities in franchise consolidation and expansion of proven local brands across the region.
