Peruvian Franchises Expected to Reach $1.6B in 2026 on Local Brand Expansion

07 April 2026 · 10:01 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Peru's franchise sector is experiencing solid recovery with the Peruvian Chamber of Commerce (CCL) projecting revenues of $1.6 billion in 2026, an 8% year-over-year increase that would restore pre-pandemic performance levels. The growth is supported by expansion of local brands and emerging business formats across the domestic market. For LatAm investors, this signals sustained consumer spending resilience and the maturation of Peru's retail franchise ecosystem as a stable asset class.
