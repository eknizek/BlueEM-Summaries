Argentine Fintech Takenos Seeks Payment Market Leadership in Peru with $10M Investment

23 April 2026 · 01:57 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Takenos, an Argentine-origin fintech founded in 2022, announced its entry into the Peruvian market as part of a regional expansion strategy. The company has secured $10 million in funding to support its operations and growth targets in Peru. Takenos aims to acquire 100,000 active users during its first year operating in the country, positioning itself as a payments leader in the Peruvian fintech space. The expansion represents a cross-border LatAm fintech play, with the company leveraging experience from Argentina to capture market share in Peru's growing digital payments ecosystem.
