Itaú appoints new chief economist from central bank

29 April 2026 · 03:32 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
Diogo Guillen, who concluded his tenure as Director of Economic Policy at Brazil's central bank in December, has been appointed chief economist of Itaú Unibanco. He replaces Mário Mesquita, who held the position for nearly a decade and was also a former central bank policy director. Guillen holds a degree in Economics from PUC-Rio and a PhD from Princeton University, bringing strong academic and regulatory credentials to the role.
