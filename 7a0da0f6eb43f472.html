TIM and PicPay Partner with Eye on Credit Services

04 May 2026 · 08:46 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
TIM Brasil and PicPay have announced a strategic partnership to connect their customer bases and offer new services and benefits to users. The partnership links TIM's 60 million telecom customers with PicPay's 67 million digital payment users, creating a significant cross-selling opportunity. While the companies did not disclose customer overlap, the partnership focuses on credit services and financial offerings. The deal does not involve equity exchange and represents a strategic alliance to expand financial services reach in Brazil's digital economy.
