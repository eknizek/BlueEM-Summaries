TIM and PicPay Partner to Target Credit Services

04 May 2026 · 08:46 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
TIM Brasil and fintech platform PicPay announced a strategic partnership integrating their customer bases (60 million and 67 million users respectively) to develop new financial services and credit offerings. The partnership leverages TIM's telecom infrastructure and customer relationships alongside PicPay's fintech capabilities and payments ecosystem. The collaboration targets credit expansion without involving equity stakes, positioning both companies to cross-sell financial products to their combined customer base.
