FGTS use in Desenrola program will impact job creation and housing delivery, says Abrainc

04 May 2026 · 09:13 PM  ·  Valor Economico
Abrainc, Brazil's real estate developers association, commented that the use of FGTS (Severance Indemnity Fund for Employees) resources in the government's Desenrola debt relief program could impact job generation and housing delivery in the construction sector. The program diverts FGTS funds from traditional uses, potentially reducing investment in housing projects. This represents a trade-off between short-term consumer debt relief and long-term housing construction activity.
