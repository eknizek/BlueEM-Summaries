# Diesel S-10 Prices Show Signs of Relief at Brazilian Pumps in Early April, Veloe/FIPE Monitor Indicates

Diesel prices showed signs of relief in the first half of April, after rising 22.1% at pumps since the beginning of the war between the United States, Israel, and Iran at the end of February, according to the Veloe Fuel Price Monitor, produced with technical support from the Foundation Institute of Economic Research (Fipe).

Ethanol and gasoline maintained stability in the first half of the month.
