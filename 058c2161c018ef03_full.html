# S-10 Diesel Prices Show Signs of Reprieve at Pumps in Early April, Veloe/Fipe Monitor Indicates

Diesel prices showed signs of relief in the first half of April, after rising 22.1% at the pumps since the beginning of the war between the United States, Israel, and Iran at the end of February, according to the Fuel Price Monitor from Veloe, produced with technical support from the Foundation for Economic Research Institute (Fipe).

Ethanol and gasoline maintained stability in the first half of the month.
