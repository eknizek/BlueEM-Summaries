# InnovaFest 2026 advances with entrepreneur incentives

The Ministry of Economy continued activities for InnovaFest 2026, a forum designed to connect local technology projects with the business sector. During the event, headed by the ministry's director, Marcelo Ebrard, the progress of the national innovation network was detailed and new incentives were announced for participants in the upcoming regional editions.

Ebrard reported that the innovation ecosystem in the country has reached more than 3,700 inventors and entrepreneurs.
