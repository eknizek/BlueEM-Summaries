Cedro, Lucas Kallas' holding company, exits Biomm lab to focus on mining

29 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea

The divestment reflects a strategic shift in Kallas' portfolio away from healthcare toward natural resources. This move signals changing investment priorities and may create liquidity for alternative mining or commodity-related opportunities in the Brazilian market.
