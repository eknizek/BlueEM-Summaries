proEXPLO 2026: Buenaventura evaluates extending San Gabriel mine life by over a decade

05 May 2026 · 05:42 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Buenaventura's San Gabriel gold project in Moquegua, Peru could see its operational life extended by more than ten years, according to José Luis Aquino, the company's Head of Geology. The extension would be achieved through new exploration work and evaluation of potential surface mining operations. The announcement was made during the company's presentation at proEXPLO 2026, a major mining conference in Peru. This expansion represents a significant opportunity to maximize returns from an established asset and secure long-term production.
