Four Direct Routes from Lima Canceled Due to TUUA Transfer Fee, Says AETAI

06 April 2026 · 09:53 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Four direct international routes from Lima's Jorge Chávez International Airport have been cancelled by Latam Airlines Peru, effective March 29, 2026 (Lima–Orlando, Lima–Curaçao, Lima–Florianópolis, Lima–Tucumán), attributed to the new TUUA transfer fee. These cancellations follow prior route cuts and signal operational challenges for Peru's aviation sector. For aviation and tourism investors, route contraction reduces connectivity, pressures airline revenues, and may dampen tourism and business travel, with knock-on effects for hospitality and regional economic activity.
