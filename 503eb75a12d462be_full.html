# Advent, Natura and a paradox

The private equity fund has set an average price to build a position in the company

By Maria Luíza Filgueiras — São Paulo 04/16/2026 4:07 PM Updated 4 hours ago

Advent reached an agreement with Natura's controlling block on a shareholding position with governance alignments. However, as the stock has risen more than 10% since then, the fund has not yet become a shareholder, according to Pipeline sources linked to the beauty company.

The private equity fund plans to build a position of 8% to 10% within six months, at an average price of R$ 9.75. Currently, Natura's stock is trading at R$ 10.12.

The paradox is that investors became excited about the fund's entry, causing the stock to rise, but the fund has already determined a price threshold for this to happen, and therefore must wait for it to fall.

Upon reaching this stake, Advent will appoint two board members. When contacted, the fund did not comment.
