Steel industry starts year with improved balance, but faces cost and energy pressure

19 April 2026 · 10:52 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombia's steel sector is showing improved financial performance at the start of the year, according to Diaco's country director Mauro de Castro. Import tariffs are helping to shield the domestic industry from foreign competition threats. However, the sector continues to face significant pressure from rising production costs and elevated energy expenses, which are constraining margins and competitiveness.
