From Colina 1 to the World: Q Studio's Copper Cladding

24 April 2026 · 10:22 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Q Studio has developed a business model integrating Chile's position as the world's leading copper exporter with a unique social impact approach. The company produces copper cladding and design products manufactured through a partnership involving Chile's penal system, creating employment for incarcerated individuals. This model positions design not merely as an aesthetic commodity but as a vehicle for social rehabilitation and economic impact. The venture exemplifies intersection of commodity production, manufacturing, and social enterprise within Chile's economy.
