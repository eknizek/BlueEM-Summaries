Peru's ADEX president: country has room to expand U.S. market presence

05 May 2026 · 06:56 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
The president of Peru's Exporters Association (ADEX), César Tello Ramírez, highlighted that Peru captured only 0.31% of U.S. imports in 2025 and ranked as the 37th largest supplier to the United States—indicating substantial headroom for export growth. He emphasized that the U.S. market, while highly competitive and demanding, represents a key opportunity for Peruvian exporters to diversify revenue and consolidate market presence. This reflects structural themes in Peru's trade competitiveness and import-substitution strategies.
