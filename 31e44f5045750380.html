Two waterway concessions return to auction agenda

15 April 2026 · 01:53 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazil's Ministry of Ports and Airports has restarted its General Waterway Concessions Plan after overcoming conflicts with Indigenous communities in the Amazon that had threatened the initiative in February. The ministry is now moving forward with institutional steps to auction two waterway concessions. This represents a resumption of Brazil's infrastructure privatization agenda despite pressure from an election year calendar and ongoing Indigenous land disputes.
