Beef export dollar inflows grew over 50% in first quarter from US quota expansion

21 April 2026 · 05:45 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's beef exports delivered strong first-quarter results, with dollar inflows exceeding $1.07 billion across 164,000 tonnes—a 50%+ increase driven by both improved international prices and expanded access to US markets following quota increases. The expansion reflects improved competitiveness and higher global protein demand, providing welcome foreign exchange relief for Argentina's external accounts. This recovery in agricultural export value supports broader macro stabilization efforts and corporate profitability in the livestock complex.
