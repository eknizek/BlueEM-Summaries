Argentine Beef Exports Surge Over 50% in Q1 as US Quota Expansion Drives Dollar Inflows

21 April 2026 · 05:45 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Argentine beef exports generated over $1.07 billion in the first quarter of 2024, representing a 50%+ increase in dollar inflows compared to the prior year period. Total shipments reached 164,000 tonnes, benefiting from both improved international prices and volume growth. The surge was significantly driven by the expansion of Argentina's export quota to the United States, a key market for high-quality beef, demonstrating the sector's sensitivity to trade policy changes.
