Editora Globo signs agreement to purchase CasaCor

14 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  O Globo
Editora Globo, the publishing and media division of Globo, has signed an agreement to purchase CasaCor, a prominent Brazilian design and architecture exhibition and brand. This acquisition represents strategic expansion of Globo's media and content portfolio. CasaCor is a well-established platform in the design and real estate sectors with significant market presence in Latin America.
