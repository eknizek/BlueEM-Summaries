Only 26% of Professionals See Leadership Aligned for AI Use, Survey Finds

05 May 2026 · 09:00 AM  ·  Valor Economico
A survey reported by Valor Econômico shows that only 26% of Brazilian professionals perceive their organizational leadership as properly aligned for artificial intelligence implementation. This finding suggests widespread misalignment between C-suite strategy and employee perception of AI readiness across Brazilian enterprises. The research highlights potential governance and change management challenges as companies accelerate digital transformation initiatives.
