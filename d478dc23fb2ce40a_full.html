# Dollar Price Today April 16, 2026

The Mexican peso retreats amid a slight strengthening of the U.S. currency, as investors await an agreement between the United States and Iran that could reduce the escalation of the conflict in the Middle East. The dollar price today, April 16, 2026, is trading at $17.28 pesos.

The Mexican currency registered a depreciation of 0.19% compared to Wednesday's reference price. "For today I expect a session of moderate volatility with a lateral bias for the exchange rate, fluctuating between 17.20 and 17.32," said Felipe Mendoza, analyst at EBC Financial Group.

Investors were also digesting U.S. figures showing a decline in weekly unemployment benefit claims, although companies are cautious about hiring new workers, as the Middle East conflict casts a shadow over the economy. John Williams, president of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, stated that the war was already causing an increase in inflationary pressures, in statements in which he noted that the central bank is prepared to respond to any situation that presents itself in the economy.
