Saavi Energía Announces Agreement for Global Infrastructure Partners to Combine Power Generation Assets with Grupo México

27 April 2026 · 03:38 PM  ·  Company press release
Saavi Energía has announced an agreement between Global Infrastructure Partners (GIP) and Grupo México to combine power generation assets, creating a significant consolidated energy platform in Mexico. The transaction represents a strategic consolidation of electricity generation capacity and brings together complementary operational capabilities. This deal reflects growing interest from global infrastructure investors in Mexican renewable and conventional energy assets amid the country's energy transition. The combination aims to create scale and operational synergies in Mexico's competitive power generation market.
