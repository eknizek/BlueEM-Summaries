Match Group Invests $100M in Sniffies, Grindr Rival

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Match Group, owner of Tinder and other dating platforms, is making a significant $100 million investment in Sniffies, a location-based casual dating app for men that has already attracted 3 million users. The move positions Sniffies as a direct competitor to Grindr in the men's dating space. This investment demonstrates Match Group's strategy to diversify its portfolio across different dating verticals and user demographics. The capital injection will likely accelerate Sniffies' product development and user acquisition efforts.
