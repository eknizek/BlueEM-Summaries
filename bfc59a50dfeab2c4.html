16 April 2026 · 11:01 AM  ·  Reforma (Mexico)
Mexico City's Benito Juárez International Airport (AICM) is experiencing multiple infrastructure and operational challenges as the country prepares to host the 2026 FIFA World Cup. Issues include failures in basic services (restrooms), suspension of the aerotrain connection, ongoing construction work, and inadequate parking capacity. These deficiencies raise questions about the airport's readiness for the anticipated surge in international traffic during the World Cup tournament.
