Just Climate and EuReciclo inaugurate plant with R$116 million from BID Invest

15 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Just Climate and EuReciclo have inaugurated a new recycling plant within Veolia's ecopark in Brusque, Brazil, supported by R$116 million in financing from BID Invest. The facility is designed to recover approximately 15,000 tonnes of materials annually, focusing on circular economy principles. This project represents a significant investment in Brazil's waste management and recycling infrastructure, aligned with environmental sustainability and resource recovery objectives.
