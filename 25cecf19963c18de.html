Following breach of covenants, Oncoclínicas seeks court protection against creditors

13 April 2026 · 02:47 PM  ·  Neofeed
Oncoclínicas, a Brazilian oncology and cancer care provider, announced on Monday, April 13, that it will seek a preliminary injunction in São Paulo's courts to suspend early debt maturity clauses and halt creditor obligations. The company is facing deteriorating capital structure and financial difficulties. This legal filing represents a defensive maneuver by the company to buy time while managing its debt obligations during a period of operational and financial stress. The covenant breach signals deeper liquidity and operational challenges within the healthcare services company.
