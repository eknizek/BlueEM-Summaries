Gratification and CTS for CAS workers: Congress now wants to 'force' MEF to prioritize 2026 payments

24 April 2026 · 12:24 AM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Peru's Congress has mandated that workers under the CAS (Contratación Administrativa de Servicios) special contract regime receive gratification and CTS (Compensación de Tiempo de Servicios—severance compensation), and now seeks to compel the Ministry of Finance to prioritize payment in 2026. This policy imposes additional fiscal obligations despite warnings from fiscal authorities about budgetary constraints. The measure affects labor costs for public sector employers and has implications for Peru's public finance management. Implementation may strain government finances in an election-sensitive environment.
