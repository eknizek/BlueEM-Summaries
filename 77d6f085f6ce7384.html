Congress Seeks to Force Finance Ministry to Prioritize CAS Worker Gratuity and CTS Payments in 2026

24 April 2026 · 12:24 AM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Peru's Congress has advanced legislation requiring gratuity and Compensación de Tiempo de Servicios (CTS) severance payments for workers under the special Administrative Services Contract (CAS) regime, despite warnings from the Finance Ministry about fiscal sustainability. The legislative push now seeks to force the government to prioritize these payments in 2026. This represents a significant contingent liability for public finances and reflects political pressure to expand benefits to a large class of government contractors. The measure highlights tension between social spending demands and fiscal consolidation in Peru.
