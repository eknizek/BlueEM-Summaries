Camacol Green Congress to gather construction leaders in Medellín for sustainable building roadmap

23 April 2026 · 11:35 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
The Colombian Construction Chamber (Camacol) will host its Green Congress in Medellín on May 14-15, bringing together national and international experts to discuss sustainable construction practices. The event will cover topics ranging from sustainable housing to smart cities and environmental standards. The gathering reflects the construction sector's commitment to aligning development with climate and sustainability goals in Colombia.
