BBVA Sees Mexico's 3.5% Fiscal Deficit Target for 2027 as "Very Difficult"

Mexico City (April 6, 2026) — To achieve the goal of -3.5 percent of GDP for RFSP in 2027, the federal government will need to achieve a primary surplus of 1.1 percent of GDP in 2027. Credit: AFP

The fiscal consolidation target set by the Government for 2027 will be "very difficult" to achieve, which is why a tax reform will be required in the medium term, warned the Economic Studies area of BBVA México.
