Peru's manufacturing sector grows 2.7% in February 2026, led by non-primary capital goods

20 April 2026 · 07:40 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's manufacturing sector registered 2.7% growth in February 2026, according to the National Industries Society (SNI), with the non-primary manufacturing subsector advancing 3.6% and primary manufacturing rising 0.3%. The primary driver of expansion was the capital goods segment within the non-primary sector. The data indicates continued momentum in Peru's industrial production and investment-related manufacturing.
