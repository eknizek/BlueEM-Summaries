Peru's Manufacturing Sector Grows 2.7% in February 2026, Led by Non-Primary Capital Goods Production

20 April 2026 · 07:40 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's manufacturing sector expanded 2.7% in February 2026, according to Peru's National Society of Industries (SNI) Economic and Social Studies Institute (IEES). The non-primary manufacturing subsector led growth at 3.6%, particularly in capital goods production, while primary industries grew just 0.3%. This expansion reflects recovering industrial activity and investment in machinery and equipment. The data suggests improving business investment and industrial competitiveness in Peru's economy.
