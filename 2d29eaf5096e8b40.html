Prio (PRIO3) increases production to 173k boepd in April with Wahoo advance

04 May 2026 · 10:06 PM  ·  MoneyTimes
Prio (PRIO3) reported total production of 173.4 thousand barrels of oil equivalent per day (boepd) in April 2026, significantly above the Q1 2026 average of 155.3k boepd. The increase was driven by production gains at the Cluster Valente (Frade + Wahoo), which grew from an average of 31.9k barrels/day. This production ramp-up reflects successful field development and operational efficiency improvements at the company's offshore assets.
