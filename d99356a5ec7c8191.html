Mosaic to halt fertilizer plant operations in Brazil after sulfur surge

16 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
The Mosaic Company is halting operations at its fertilizer manufacturing plants in Brazil in response to soaring sulfur prices caused by geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. The decision highlights Brazil's vulnerability in the fertilizer supply chain and exposes the challenges facing the country's agriculture sector's reliance on imported raw materials. This shutdown represents a setback for Brazilian efforts to reduce dependence on foreign agricultural nutrients and underscores how global energy shocks can disrupt domestic agricultural production capacity.
