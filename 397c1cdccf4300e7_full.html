# MBRF and HPDC Complete Formation of Sadia Halal with Share Offering on the Horizon

MBRF, the world's largest halal chicken producer, announced on Sunday (05/03) together with the Halal Products Development Company (HPDC) — a subsidiary of the Saudi sovereign wealth fund Public Investment Fund (PIF) — the completion of the transaction that creates Sadia Halal. In mid-April, antitrust authorities had already approved the operation.

Valued at approximately US$ 2.07 billion, the new company encompasses MBRF factories and distribution centers in Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates, as well as distribution firms in Qatar, Kuwait, and Oman.

Halal food is produced in accordance with the precepts of the Islamic religion.
