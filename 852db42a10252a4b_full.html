Mexico demands explanation from US regarding agent presence

22 April 2026 · 11:01 AM

Mexico has requested an explanation from the United States regarding the presence of its agents in an operation in Mexico, and the Attorney General's Office (FGR) is already investigating a possible violation of the Constitution and the National Security Law.
