Brazil's Supreme Court Decision Could Triple CVM Budget

05 May 2026 · 09:13 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Justice Flávio Dino of Brazil's Supreme Court (STF) granted an injunction that could more than double the CVM's (Securities Commission) budget if confirmed by the full court—a major shift after years of underfunding. The injunction was granted in the context of a Direct Action of Unconstitutionality, suggesting the court may be addressing constitutional violations in the CVM's funding constraints. A tripled budget would significantly expand the capital markets regulator's enforcement and supervisory capacity, with material implications for Brazil's financial sector oversight and investor protection.
