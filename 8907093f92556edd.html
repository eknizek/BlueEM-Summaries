Facing credit scarcity, Argentine SMEs diversify financing sources: options and access paths

02 May 2026 · 03:58 PM  ·  Clarin
Argentine SMEs are increasingly turning to alternative financing sources due to constrained traditional bank credit availability. Digital platforms and non-bank financial entities regulated by the Central Bank are gaining traction as SMEs seek diversified funding options. Investment banking firms (ALyC) and other specialized financial institutions are expanding credit products tailored to small business needs. This shift reflects both the supply-side constraints in the banking sector and the growing sophistication of alternative finance infrastructure in Argentina.
