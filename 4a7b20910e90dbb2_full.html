# Día1 Summit 2026: Leaders to Analyze Economic and Political Scenario Ahead of Second Presidential Round

On May 14, Día1, the business supplement of El Comercio, will hold the third edition of the Día1 Summit, a space that will bring together business leaders, analysts, and authorities to discuss the main issues that will shape the country's economic and political direction in the midst of the pre-electoral context.

The meeting will take place at a key moment, marked by the definition of candidates who will advance to the second round of presidential elections and by the election of the new bicameral Congress. In this context, the event will seek to provide tools to understand the political and economic scenario, identify risks and opportunities, and anticipate how the decisions of the next government will impact the business environment.

During the event, topics such as the economic agenda of the new bicameral Congress and the composition of the Senate and Chamber of Deputies will also be addressed. Likewise, the global geopolitical environment and its impact on Peru's competitiveness will be analyzed. Daniel Zovatto, president of Latam Asesoría Estratégica 360, will also participate, analyzing the Peruvian election in the context of the electoral supercycle in Latin America.

The Día1 Summit is positioned as a space to listen, dialogue, and contribute to informed decision-making in an environment of high political and economic uncertainty.

The Día1 Summit takes place within the framework of the anniversary of the Día1 supplement, which this year 2026 marks 22 years, as part of its initiatives to promote analysis and discussion on the main issues that impact the country's business environment. To participate in the event, you can purchase your tickets here.

The business supplement of El Comercio has published 1,131 editions in its 22 years of existence. It has also received recognition, with the most recent being the Lima Chamber of Commerce President's Award, for its contribution to the dissemination of relevant information and opinions in the country's economic and business activities.
