Colombia's SIC Fines Rappi Over 4 Billion Pesos for Systematic Service Failures and Consumer Rights Violations

08 April 2026 · 03:00 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia's SIC has issued a substantial fine exceeding 4 billion pesos against Rappi for systematic service failures and violations of consumer rights across multiple service lines. The violations encompassed issues with Rappi Turbo (ultrafast delivery) and membership programs including Rappi Prime and Rappi Pro. The penalty underscores regulatory focus on consumer protection in Colombia's fast-growing fintech and delivery services sectors. The enforcement action may pressure Rappi to improve operational standards and could influence how Colombian regulators supervise other digital platform operators and their consumer safeguards.
