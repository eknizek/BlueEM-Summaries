'We Will Not Go Public Until the Market Calms Down': Interview with Adrián Neuhauser, President of Abra, Avianca's Controlling Group

12 April 2026 · 10:18 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Adrián Neuhauser, president of Abra (the holding company controlling Colombian airline Avianca), confirmed in an interview that the SEC registration process for a potential IPO is nearly complete but the company will wait for market conditions to improve before proceeding. Abra simultaneously announced ticket price increases, signaling confidence in demand recovery. This is directly relevant to LatAm capital markets and private equity strategy, as Avianca is a major regional airline and the IPO timing has implications for aviation sector valuation and debt refinancing in Colombia.
