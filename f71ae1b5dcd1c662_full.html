# Tortilla Price Increases Attributed to Gasoline Price Hikes

Following the announcement by the National Tortilla Council (CNT) to increase the price of tortillas by up to 4 pesos per kilogram in some parts of the country, the reason is justified by operational costs, mainly due to increased gasoline prices.

Homero López García, president of the CNT, recalled that in Mexico the price of this food is unregulated.
