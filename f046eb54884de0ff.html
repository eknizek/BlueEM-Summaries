Mexico's debt level pressures sovereign rating, HR Ratings warns

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
HR Ratings maintained Mexico's long-term sovereign credit rating at BBB+ but upgraded the outlook from negative to stable, acknowledging debt pressures as a medium-term concern. The affirmation signals cautious confidence in fiscal stability and debt management despite elevated public debt levels. This development is directly relevant to fixed-income investors, sovereign bond pricing, and corporate borrowing costs in Mexico, as sovereign creditworthiness influences the cost of capital for all Mexican entities.
