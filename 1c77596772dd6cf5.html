Scala Bets on 'Data Center City' to Attract AI Investments to Brazil

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Scala is developing an integrated data center campus in Brazil to position the country as a regional hub for artificial intelligence and cloud computing infrastructure. The initiative targets multinational tech companies and AI developers seeking low-cost, reliable data center capacity in Latin America. This infrastructure play reflects broader trends of technology companies establishing regional data footprints to serve growing computational demand in the region.
