Scala Bets on Data Center 'City' to Attract AI Investments to Brazil

20 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Scala is constructing an expansive data center complex in Brazil positioned as a hub for AI and technology infrastructure investment. The project aims to provide the computational power and connectivity necessary to support Brazil's growing AI and cloud computing ambitions. By creating dedicated infrastructure for data-intensive industries, Scala seeks to position Brazil as a regional technology leader and attract multinational tech companies. This development reflects Brazil's strategic efforts to compete in the global digital economy and reduce reliance on foreign cloud infrastructure providers.
