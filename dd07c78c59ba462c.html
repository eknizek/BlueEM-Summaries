Folha Partners with Google to Improve Gemini

09 April 2026 · 08:49 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Folha de S.Paulo, Brazil's leading newspaper, has entered a partnership with Google to enhance the Gemini AI model by providing editorial content and data. Such partnerships between major media outlets and technology companies raise important questions about content valuation, AI training data licensing, and the evolving relationship between traditional media and Big Tech platforms in Latin America.
