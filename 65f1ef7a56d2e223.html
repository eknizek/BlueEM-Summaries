TGP on pipeline incident: "It is unwise for Osinergmin to suggest it has found non-compliance"

25 April 2026 · 11:51 AM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
TGP has responded to Peru's energy regulator Osinergmin's investigation into an incident at the Camisea gas pipeline, characterizing the regulator's preliminary conclusions as premature. The company argues that the investigation is still ongoing and that it is inappropriate for Osinergmin to suggest findings of non-compliance at this stage. This regulatory dispute involves one of Peru's critical energy infrastructure assets and touches on compliance standards for major hydrocarbon operators.
