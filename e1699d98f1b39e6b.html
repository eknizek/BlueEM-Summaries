Instacart enters Brazil via acquisition of Colombian Instaleap, intensifying online grocery competition

14 April 2026 · 02:47 PM  ·  Neofeed
American online grocery platform Instacart expanded into Brazil through the acquisition of Colombian competitor Instaleap, marking the company's strategic entry into Latin America after years of focusing on North American markets. The transaction intensifies competition in the regional online grocery sector as Instacart leverages Instaleap's existing operations and market knowledge. This cross-border deal reflects growing consolidation in Latin America's digital commerce space and Instacart's push to diversify geographically beyond its core U.S.-Canada operations.
