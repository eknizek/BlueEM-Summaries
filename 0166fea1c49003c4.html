Texas Oil Rises 7.13% After Trump Announces US Blockade of Strait of Hormuz

13 April 2026 · 02:05 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
West Texas Intermediate (WTI) crude oil jumped 7.13% to $103.48 per barrel at the start of the week following US President Donald Trump's announcement that the United States will blockade the Strait of Hormuz, a critical global oil chokepoint through which approximately one-fifth of the world's oil supply passes. The announcement of this geopolitical measure triggered immediate market reaction, reflecting investor concerns about potential supply disruptions and inflationary pressures. This development has material implications for Latin American oil producers and energy-dependent economies in the region.
