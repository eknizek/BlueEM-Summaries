Texas Oil Rises 7.13% Following Trump's Announcement to Block Strait of Hormuz

13 April 2026 · 02:05 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
West Texas Intermediate (WTI) crude oil began the week with a 7.13% gain, reaching $103.48 per barrel, following Donald Trump's announcement that the United States would block the Strait of Hormuz, through which approximately one-fifth of global oil supply passes. The blockade threat represents a significant geopolitical shock with immediate market implications. This price movement directly impacts Latin American energy producers and refineries, particularly Mexico, Brazil and other regional oil exporters.
