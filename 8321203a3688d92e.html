Colombian government launches incentive plan for electric vehicles: here are the benefits

19 April 2026 · 04:30 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
The Colombian government announced a new incentive program for electric vehicles including 0% tariffs on EV machinery and preferential import quotas conditional on local manufacturing investment. The decree aims to accelerate EV adoption and build domestic supply chains in the transportation sector. This policy signals support for energy transition and has implications for automotive manufacturers, suppliers, and the clean energy transition in Colombia.
