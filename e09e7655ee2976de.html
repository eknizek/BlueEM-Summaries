Luxury shopping malls grow with return of foreign brands and gastronomy

02 May 2026 · 03:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The luxury shopping mall segment in Brazil is thriving post-COVID despite overall retail sector weakness, benefiting from the return of international brands and expanded gastronomic offerings targeting classes A and B consumers. While broader retail struggles with declining footfall and sales, the high-end mall format has proven resilient and profitable. This bifurcation reflects Brazil's consumer spending polarization between affluent and mass-market segments.
