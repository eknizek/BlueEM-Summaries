A new geography of wine: northeastern Brazilian terroirs begin to gain ground

25 April 2026 · 12:30 PM  ·  Neofeed
While much attention focuses on the wine boom in Brazil's Southeast region, production in the Northeast is expanding rapidly. Although initial winemaking experiments in the São Francisco Valley began in the 1970s, the region is now experiencing renewed growth with new vineyard development spreading across northeastern states beyond the traditional river valley areas. This geographic diversification reflects changing investment patterns and climate opportunities in Brazilian wine production.
