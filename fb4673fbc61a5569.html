How many dollars did Argentine agribusiness liquidate in April and what lies ahead

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Argentine agricultural sector foreign exchange liquidations showed strong monthly performance in April with growth above 20%, but cumulative results for the first four months of the year remain down 11% compared to the same period last year. The data, reported by sector chambers, reflects export dynamics and currency inflows from the country's key agricultural exports. This metric is closely watched as an indicator of agribusiness health and foreign exchange generation for the Argentine economy.
