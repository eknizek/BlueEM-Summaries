# Peru's Banking Regulator Initiates Deposit Repayment to CRAC del Centro Customers in Liquidation

This Monday, the refund of deposits to customers of the Rural Savings and Credit Fund of the Center (CRAC Del Centro) began, following the liquidation process ordered last week by the Banking Superintendent, Insurance and Private Pension Fund Administrators (SBS).

The payment of the Deposit Insurance Fund (FSD) coverage to customers included in the first list is being processed at the offices of the Municipal Savings and Credit Bank of Arequipa, nationwide. Depositors can consult the list through the SBS website.

Additionally, in the coming days, the payment date will be announced for customers not included in this first list.

This first payment covers savings and fixed-term deposits of individuals (including joint accounts). Natural persons who maintain CTS accounts, debts, or holds with CRAC Del Centro will be included in subsequent lists.

Regarding savings and fixed-term deposits in foreign currency, coverage will be paid in soles, using the accounting exchange rate from the day prior to CRAC Del Centro's intervention (April 13, 2026), which the SBS publishes on its institutional portal (S/ 3.3740).

It is worth noting that deposits from this entity are protected, in accordance with article No. 152 of the General Law of the Financial System, up to a maximum amount of S/ 117,200 per depositor.
