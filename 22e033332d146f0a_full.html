# Peru's Banking Regulator Begins Returning Deposits to CRAC del Centro Customers in Liquidation

On Monday, the return of deposits to customers of the Caja Rural de Ahorro y Crédito Del Centro (CRAC Del Centro) began, following the liquidation process ordered last week by the Superintendencia de Banca, Seguros y Administradoras Privadas de Fondos de Pensiones (SBS).

Payment of the Deposit Insurance Fund (FSD) coverage to customers included in the first list is being processed at the offices of the Caja Municipal de Ahorro y Crédito Arequipa nationwide. Depositors can consult the list through the SBS website. Additionally, payment dates for customers not included in this first list will be announced in the coming days.

This first payment covers savings and fixed-term deposits of individuals (including joint accounts). Individuals holding CTS accounts, debts, or freezes with CRAC Del Centro will be included in subsequent lists.

For savings and fixed-term deposits in foreign currency, coverage will be paid in soles using the accounting exchange rate from the day before the intervention (April 13, 2026) of CRAC Del Centro, as published by the SBS on its institutional portal (S/ 3.3740).

It is worth recalling that deposits at this institution are protected in accordance with Article No. 152 of the General Law of the Financial System, up to a maximum amount of S/ 117,200 per depositor.
