Paraná government chose faster model for Celepar sale

04 May 2026 · 09:13 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Paraná state government has chosen a faster privatization pathway for Celepar, a state-owned information technology company, despite legal advisors at Stocche Forbes recommending a more cautious alternative. The legal opinion identified two potential sale models: one deemed more legally and politically sound, and another characterized as faster and better aligned with the government's timeline. The government prioritized speed and schedule adherence over the most conservative legal approach. This privatization proceeds against the backdrop of regulatory questions regarding public security data handling.
