AmCham Targets 60% Export Growth to US in Agriculture, Manufacturing, and Services by 2030

21 April 2026 · 03:37 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
The American Chamber of Commerce in Colombia (AmCham) has outlined an ambitious export growth plan targeting a 60% increase in shipments to the United States by 2030, focused on agriculture, manufacturing, and services sectors. The organization cautions that any deterioration in US-Colombia bilateral relations could materially impact external sales, foreign investment inflows, and household income. This macroeconomic assessment reflects Colombia's strategic dependence on the US market and the importance of trade stability.
