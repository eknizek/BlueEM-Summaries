Lovable's 'barefoot' strategy: no fixed roadmap, plans change every 15 hours

23 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Lovable, a Brazilian intimate apparel company, operates with an unconventional 'barefoot' strategy that eschews traditional fixed roadmaps and instead shifts tactical plans every 15 hours based on market conditions and performance data. The model demonstrates adaptability but also highlights the volatility of strategy execution in fast-moving consumer markets.
