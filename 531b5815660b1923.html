Aris Mining reaches key milestone at Marmato to double gold production in Colombia by late 2026

20 April 2026 · 08:38 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Aris Mining has reached a significant milestone in its Marmato expansion project in Colombia, one of the country's largest mining development initiatives. The company aims to double its gold production, reaching an annual output target of 500,000 ounces by the end of 2026. This expansion represents a major capital deployment in Colombia's mining sector and reflects the company's confidence in commodity prices and operating conditions, with implications for both equity returns and local supply.
