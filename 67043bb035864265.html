Insecurity Hampers Mexico's Competitiveness, Says Canacar

15 April 2026 · 11:44 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Mexico's National Chamber of Cargo Transportation (Canacar) identifies insecurity as the primary impediment to the country's competitiveness and economic growth. The association highlights that violence and criminal activity directly undermine the transportation and logistics sector, raising costs and reducing operational efficiency. This structural challenge has material implications for supply chains, Foreign Direct Investment decisions, and Mexico's ability to compete in regional and global trade.
