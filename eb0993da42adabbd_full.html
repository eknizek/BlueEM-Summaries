# Mexico and US Add Intellectual Property to USMCA Technical Talks Agenda

During the second technical meeting for the USMCA review, observations on intellectual property were considered. Credit: AP

In the second technical meeting, prior to the start of formal work, Mexico and the United States added to the agenda issues related to tariffs on aluminum, copper and steel, under Section 232, as well as observations on intellectual property, which were outlined in Special Report 301.
