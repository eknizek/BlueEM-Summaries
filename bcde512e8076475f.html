Government Decree Orders Transfer of $25 Billion from AFP to Colpensiones: Which Colombians Does This Amount Belong To?

23 April 2026 · 06:23 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Finanzas
The Colombian government has issued a decree requiring pension fund administrators (AFP) to transfer approximately $25 billion in private pension savings to the state-run Colpensiones system. The transfer must be completed in two phases: 50% within 20 days and the remaining 50% within the following 10 days. This policy affects Colombian pension holders with accounts in the private system and represents a significant shift in the country's pension landscape.
