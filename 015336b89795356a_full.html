# Dollar Price Today Mexico. Exchange Rate Banks April 22, 2026

The Mexican peso registered slight depreciation this Wednesday, dragged down by investor caution in the face of persistent geopolitical uncertainty generated by the conflict in the Middle East.

## Exchange Rate: Dollar vs. Peso

At the opening of trading, the Mexican currency was quoted at 17.31 units per dollar, remaining virtually unchanged compared to the reference price of 17.3210 registered at the close on Tuesday. This lack of clear direction in foreign exchange markets was also reflected in the dollar index—which measures the strength of the U.S. currency against a basket of major currencies—which showed only slight variations.

## What Factors Are Affecting the Mexican Peso Today?

Pressure on emerging markets and the exchange rate responds mainly to three factors in the international landscape:

**Diplomatic Uncertainty:** Following the announcement made the previous day by President Donald Trump about an extension to the ceasefire, it remains unclear whether Israel and its regional counterparts will accept the agreement, leaving peace prospects up in the air.

**Strait of Hormuz Blockade:** This crucial maritime route for global trade remains practically blocked.

**Increased Hostilities:** Tensions rose in recent hours after reports that three commercial vessels were struck by gunfire in the strait area.
