# Personal Pay, Mercado Pago, and Ualá: How to Generate Returns with Minimal Investment

Generating returns with your money is one of the most attractive options offered by virtual wallets like Personal Pay, Mercado Pago, Modo, and Ualá, among others. Through their accounts, users can earn interest automatically, without needing to make complex investments or lock up their money, as is the case with fixed-term deposits. In a context where every peso counts, having money "working" while remaining available becomes a key advantage. It allows you to use your balance at any time while continuing to generate returns.

## How to Activate Returns in Virtual Wallets and How Remunerated Accounts Work

Virtual wallets generate returns through a low-risk investment fund. Once this option is activated, the money starts earning interest automatically, without needing to perform any additional operations. Generally speaking, the estimated annual return—that is, the rates shown by each application—is calculated based on the fund's performance over the last 30 days, so it can change daily.

Personal Pay offers discounts of up to 25% on holiday dinner products. Interest is typically credited only on business days, although this may vary depending on the application. In most cases, no minimum amount is required to start earning profits, although some platforms do set limits. Additionally, this system has no maintenance costs and, unlike a fixed-term deposit, the money is not locked up: it can be used at any time, 24 hours a day, while continuing to generate returns.

The procedure for activating this function depends on each application:

**Personal Pay**

- Deposit money into your account via transfer or cash

- Click on the banner in the Savings section to grow your money

- Accept the Terms and Conditions, and you're done

- Requirement: be over 18 years old

**Ualá**

- Download Ualá

- Deposit money: keep between $10,000 and $1,000,000 on deposit

- Starting from $250,000 or more, you get the Plus rate

- If you have $500,000 or more, you get the highest Plus rate

**Mercado Pago**

- Go to the application's home screen

- Select the "Make Your Money Grow" option

- Click "Start" and then "Invest My Money Now"

- Requirement: be over 13 years old

- Additionally, your account holder must authorize you to invest and activate returns on their own account

## Another Way to Earn Extra Income in Virtual Wallets

Another alternative within applications like Mercado Pago to generate income is the referral program "Invite and Earn." This system allows you to obtain financial benefits by recommending the application to other people. Each time an invited user starts operating, whether by making their first payment, buying a Point device, or collecting an ANSES benefit, the person who recommended them can receive income that, depending on the active promotion, can reach up to $10,000 per person. The program works from the "Invite and Earn" option within the app, located in the "More" menu at the bottom right corner of the screen. There you can check the updated prizes and conditions.
