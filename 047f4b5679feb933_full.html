# Personal Pay, Mercado Pago and Ualá: how to generate returns with minimal investment

Generating returns with your money is one of the most attractive options offered by virtual wallets such as Personal Pay, Mercado Pago, Modo and Ualá, among others. Through their accounts, users can earn interest automatically, without needing to make complex investments or immobilize their money, as happens with fixed-term deposits. In a context where every peso counts, having your money "working" while remaining available becomes a key advantage. It allows you to use the balance at any time and, at the same time, continue generating returns.

## How to activate returns in virtual wallets and how do remunerated accounts work?

Virtual wallets generate returns through a low-risk Investment Fund. Once this option is activated, the money begins to produce interest automatically, without needing to perform new operations. In general, the estimated annual return—that is, the rates shown by each application—is calculated based on the fund's performance over the last 30 days, so it can change daily.

Interest is usually credited only on business days, although this may vary depending on the application. In most cases, no minimum amount is required to start earning profits, although some platforms do set limits. Additionally, this system has no maintenance costs and, unlike a fixed-term deposit, the money is not immobilized: it can be used at any time, 24 hours a day, while continuing to generate returns.

The procedure to activate this function depends on each application:

**Personal Pay**

- Transfer or deposit cash into your account

- Click on the banner in the Savings section to make your money grow

- Accept the Terms and Conditions, and that's it

- Requirement: be over 18 years old

**Ualá**

- Download Ualá

- Deposit money: keep between $10,000 and $1,000,000 deposited

- From $250,000 or more you get the Plus rate

- If you have $500,000 or more you get the highest Plus rate

**Mercado Pago**

- Go to the application's home screen

- Select the "Make your money grow" option

- Click "Start" and then "Invest my money now"

- Requirement: be over 13 years old

- It is also necessary that your account manager authorizes you to invest and activates returns on their own account

## Another way to earn extra income in virtual wallets

Another alternative within applications like Mercado Pago to generate income is the referral program "Invite and Earn." This system allows you to obtain economic benefits by recommending the application to other people. Each time an invited user begins to operate, whether by making their first payment, buying a Point device or collecting an ANSES benefit, the person who recommended them can receive income that, according to the current promotion, can reach up to $10,000 per person. The program works from the "Invite and Earn" option within the app located in the "More" menu, which is found in the lower right corner of the screen. There you can check updated prizes and conditions.
