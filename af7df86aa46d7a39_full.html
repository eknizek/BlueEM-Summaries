Inca Rail Announces Transfer of 80% Stake to RBB Andes Holding Limited

18 April 2026 · 02:34 AM

Inca Rail announced that it is in the process of transferring 80% of its capital stock, belonging to its shareholder Track Investment Holding Limited, whose controller is Carlyle Perú Fund L.P. This was indicated through a material fact presented to the Securities Market Superintendence (SMV) on Friday.

As recalled, in November, Inca Rail indicated that Track Investment Holding Limited was evaluating transferring its stake in the railway company. According to Inca Rail, this is part of an agreement signed on December 4 between its majority shareholder and RBB Andes Holding Limited for the sale of 21,407,814 shares of the company that operates in Cusco. This transaction will be over-the-counter and subject to the fulfillment of prior conditions.

According to the material fact, the process contemplates that this Friday Track Investment Holding Limited will present a sell order and RBB Andes Holding Limited will present a buy order to Credicorp Sociedad Agente de Bolsa, the intermediary for the operation. The transfer will be completed after two business days of the buy order being registered with the corresponding authorities. The operation would be finalized on April 21.

Inca Rail added that it will present a new material fact when the transfer of shares is completed. The company indicated that, once the transfer of shares is finalized, it will issue a new material fact to inform the market about the completion of the process.
