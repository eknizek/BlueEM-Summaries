# Government Approves Regulations for Special Economic Zones to Attract Investment and Generate Employment

The Ministry of Foreign Trade and Tourism (Mincetur) published today in the Legal Standards of the Official Gazette El Peruano the DS 005-2026-Mincetur, which approves the Regulations of Law No. 32449, which defines the regulatory framework for the creation, authorization, and operation of Private Special Economic Zones (ZEEP) in the country.

The regulation, signed by President José María Balcázar, approves the regulations that enable implementation of a strategic instrument aimed at attracting high-impact investments, generating quality formal employment, and revitalizing industrial activity with a decentralized approach.

"The approval of this regulation is a fundamental step toward transforming the country's productive structure. The ZEEP will allow us to advance toward an economy of greater added value, incorporating innovation, technology, and new growth engines that drive Peru's productivity and competitiveness," said the Minister of Commerce and Tourism, José Reyes.

The approved document establishes essential aspects for ZEEP operations, such as the procedure for zone creation, private operator selection, permitted activities, and supervision and control mechanisms. Through this regulatory framework, the goal is to decentralize investment opportunities, allowing new regions to integrate into global value chains.

The first stage for creating a ZEEP will be zone qualification, followed by authorization of the Private Operator, where applicant requests will be evaluated. Applicants must meet requirements such as being a legal entity in Peru and committing to a minimum investment of 1,500 UIT within 2 years, among others.

Additionally, the regulation was developed in coordination with public entities, such as the Ministries of Economy and Finance, and of Production, incorporating input from the private sector gathered through technical meetings and the pre-publication process of the regulation draft.
