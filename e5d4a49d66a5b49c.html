Rebuilding trust becomes major challenge for new Government: Comfama

20 April 2026 · 07:36 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Comfama, a major Colombian social security and benefits organization, plans to increase its investments by 14.5% in the current year, with total investments exceeding $60 billion, according to director David Escobar. The company views rebuilding trust as the primary challenge facing Colombia's new government.
