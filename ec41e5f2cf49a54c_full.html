CFE Infrastructure Unharmed After Earthquake

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM

# CFE Infrastructure Unharmed After Earthquake

The Federal Commission of Electricity (CFE) reported that following this morning's earthquake, no damage was detected in the infrastructure of electrical transmission and distribution networks. It stated that the generation system is operating normally.

"Regarding the earthquake that occurred on May 4 at 09:19 hours, with its epicenter in Pinotepa Nacional, Oaxaca, in accordance with protocols, activities were immediately activated to review the electrical infrastructure, with no damage detected."

The commission assured that continuous monitoring is being maintained at monitoring centers. The company is asking users to report any issues to 071.

## Blackout in Quintana Roo

In a separate incident, CFE stated that on Monday at 04:56 hours (central time), an outage occurred at an element in the Playa del Carmen substation, affecting service to 286,087 users in the municipalities of Solidaridad and Cozumel in the state of Quintana Roo. The company said that with the immediate intervention of CFE personnel, full service was restored at 6:50 in the morning.

"Distribution and Transmission personnel are carrying out work to achieve normal operating conditions in their infrastructure."
