CFE Infrastructure Undamaged After Earthquake

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM

# CFE Infrastructure Undamaged After Earthquake

The Federal Commission of Electricity (CFE) reported that following this morning's earthquake, no damage was detected in the electrical transmission and distribution network infrastructure. It stated that the generation system is operating normally.

"Regarding the earthquake that occurred on May 4th at 09:19 hours, with its epicenter in Pinotepa Nacional, Oaxaca, in accordance with protocols, activities were immediately activated to review the electrical infrastructure, with no damage detected."

The company assured that continuous monitoring continues at monitoring centers. The company asks users to report any situation to 071.

## Power Outage in Quintana Roo

In a separate matter, the CFE reported that on Monday at 04:56 hours central time, an element at the Playa del Carmen substation went out of operation, affecting the power supply to 286,087 users in the municipalities of Solidaridad and Cozumel in the state of Quintana Roo.

It stated that with immediate intervention by CFE personnel, total service restoration was achieved at 6:50 in the morning.

"Distribution and Transmission personnel are carrying out work to achieve normal operating conditions in their infrastructure."
