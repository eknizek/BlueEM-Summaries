Rodobens Exceeds R$1 Billion in Consortium Credits in March

24 April 2026 · 05:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Rodobens, a Brazilian network of vehicle dealerships and financial services company, achieved R$1.067 billion in consortium credit sales in March 2026. Of this total, R$887 million came from the company's own operations while R$180 million was generated through associated companies. Consortium financing (consórcio) is an important alternative credit mechanism in Brazil, and Rodobens' performance reflects strong demand in this segment.
