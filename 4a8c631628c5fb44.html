Entrepreneur Alexandre Liuzzi Launches Wellness Club Kontrast After Remessa Online Sale

23 April 2026 · 08:10 PM  ·  Neofeed
Alexandre Liuzzi, who previously sold fintech Remessa Online for approximately R$1.2 billion, has launched Kontrast, a wellness club in São Paulo's Faria Lima business district combining sauna, ice bath, and self-knowledge practices. The venture represents a diversification from his fintech background into the growing wellness market as Liuzzi pursues a new definition of business success. Kontrast targets affluent professionals seeking premium health and wellness experiences in São Paulo's financial hub. The move signals entrepreneurial capital shifting from digital payments into lifestyle and wellness segments catering to high-income consumers.
