Illegal Mining Mobilized Around US$12 Billion in 2025, Ten Times More Than Drug Trafficking

05 May 2026 · 08:03 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
According to Peru's High Commissioner for Combating Illegal Mining, unlawful mining operations mobilized approximately US$12 billion in 2025—roughly ten times the scale of illegal drug trafficking revenues. Material confiscations related to illegal mining reached S/7.195 billion over the past two years. Additionally, the Peru Mining Institute (IPE) reports that illegal mining activity has delayed 23 mining projects representing at least US$12 billion in potential foreign and domestic investment, significantly impacting Peru's mining sector competitiveness and sovereign revenues.
