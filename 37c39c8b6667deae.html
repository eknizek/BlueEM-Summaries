Illegal mining in Peru generates $12 billion in 2025, dwarfing drug trafficking

05 May 2026 · 08:03 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
According to Peru's High Commissioner for Combating Illegal Mining, illegal mining operations generated around $12 billion in 2025—ten times the scale of illicit drug traffic. Seized materials related to illegal mining totaled S/7.195 billion over the prior two years. The Institute of Peruvian Economics (IPE) reported delays to 23 formal mining projects representing at least $12 billion in value, reflecting the economic and regulatory distortion caused by unregulated extraction. This has significant implications for Peru's mining sector, FDI, and commodity supply.
