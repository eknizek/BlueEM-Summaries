Massive fine imposed on Movistar: SIC penalizes telecom multinational over 4.6 billion pesos for breaching switching rights

30 April 2026 · 03:06 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia's Superintendence of Industry and Commerce (SIC) has imposed a substantial fine exceeding 4.6 billion pesos on Movistar, a multinational telecommunications operator, for antitrust violations related to restricting users' ability to switch service providers. The penalty underscores regulatory enforcement of consumer switching rights and competitive market conduct in Colombia's telecom sector. The case reflects broader regulatory pressure on incumbent telecom operators regarding market access and consumer protection.
