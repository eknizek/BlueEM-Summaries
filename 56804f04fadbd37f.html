SIC imposes 4.6 billion peso fine on Movistar for consumer protection violations

30 April 2026 · 03:06 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
Colombia's Superintendence of Industry and Commerce (SIC) has imposed a significant fine exceeding 4.6 billion pesos on Movistar for anti-competitive practices. The penalty addresses violations of consumer protection rights, specifically the right of users to switch telecom operators freely. The enforcement action highlights regulatory scrutiny of market conduct in Colombia's telecommunications sector.
