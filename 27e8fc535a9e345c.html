Brazil Has Surplus Clean Energy. They Want to Turn It into Bitcoin

16 April 2026 · 10:30 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Brazilian wind and solar facilities are frequently curtailed to prevent grid instability from excess generation capacity, but entrepreneurs are exploring cryptocurrency mining as a solution to monetize this otherwise-wasted renewable energy. Energy curtailment is deterring new renewable investments and represents a drag on clean energy economics. Bitcoin mining powered by surplus renewable energy could offer returns to project operators and grid operators. This approach reflects creative asset optimization but also highlights underlying capacity and infrastructure challenges in Brazil's electrical system.
