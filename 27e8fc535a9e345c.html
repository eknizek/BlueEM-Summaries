Brazil Has 'Surplus' of Clean Energy. They Want to Transform It into Bitcoin

16 April 2026 · 10:30 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Brazil's renewable energy sector is generating more power than the grid can absorb, forcing wind and solar operators to disconnect during peak production hours—a phenomenon known as curtailment. This grid constraint is deterring renewable investors but has sparked interest from cryptocurrency miners seeking cheap power sources. Several projects are exploring the use of surplus clean energy to mine bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies, turning a liability into a revenue opportunity while supporting continued renewable investment.
