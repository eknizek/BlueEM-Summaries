Peru obtains official authorization to export poultry and pork to Singapore market

16 April 2026 · 05:41 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
The Peruvian Ministry of Agrarian Development and Irrigation (Midagri) announced that Peru has secured official authorization to export poultry and pork to Singapore through its National Agricultural Health Service (Senasa). Singapore represents a strategic destination in Asia with high purchasing power and over 6 million consumers. This authorization expands Peru's agricultural export opportunities in the Asian market and strengthens the country's position in high-value protein exports.
