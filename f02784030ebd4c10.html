Peru Obtains Official Authorization to Export Poultry and Pork to Singapore Market

16 April 2026 · 05:41 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's Ministry of Agrarian Development and Irrigation (Midagri) announced that the country has secured official approval to export poultry and pork to Singapore through its agricultural health authority (Senasa). Singapore represents a strategic, high-income export destination in Asia with over 6 million consumers and strong purchasing power. This market access approval opens new revenue streams for Peru's livestock and meat production sectors. The development is strategically important for agribusiness exporters seeking diversification and higher-value market opportunities beyond traditional destinations.
