With Backing from WOW and Ventiur, Logiez Aims to Bring Customs Expertise to Brazilian SMEs

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Logiez, a startup from Rio Grande do Sul, secured funding from WOW and Ventiur to expand its platform simplifying import and export customs procedures for small and medium-sized enterprises. The platform abstracts the complexity of international trade documentation and compliance, enabling SMEs to access foreign markets more easily. The funding and acceleration support will help the company scale its service offerings across Brazil and potentially into other Latin American markets.
