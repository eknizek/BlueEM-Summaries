# #EmprendeEnTikTok 2025: Meet the Winners and How to Participate in the Next Edition

TikTok continues strengthening the entrepreneurial ecosystem in Latin America, particularly in Peru, Mexico, and Colombia, with the announcement of the winning ventures from the first edition of #EmprendeEnTikTok Latam, an initiative that provides SMEs with tools, visibility, and strategic support to boost their development in the digital environment.

During January and February 2026, the 12 finalist ventures (4 from each country) completed a phase of specialized and personalized mentoring in business, marketing, and communications, designed to strengthen their capabilities and accelerate their growth. This process involved a network of more than 32 expert professionals, including business specialists, marketing experts, and leaders from the TikTok team.

## Winners of #EmprendeEnTikTok Latam 2025

Following an evaluation process that considered aspects such as value proposition, impact, strategic use of the platform, and scalability potential, the winning ventures stood out for their ability to transform ideas into solid and relevant projects within their communities. They also distinguished themselves by developing authentic narratives, producing creative content, and effectively using tools like Ads and trends to connect with their audiences and boost their growth.

As recognition of their performance, the projects received seed capital to drive their growth, distributed as follows: $25,000 for first place, $20,000 for second and third place, and $15,000 for fourth place.

After more than 3,200 applications from ventures in Peru alone, the award winners were:

### Peru

**1st Place: @crafters.oficial** – A Lima-based brand of premium backpacks and accessories that values the work of master artisans and seniors. They will invest the prize in specialized machinery and expansion of family workshops.

**2nd Place: @wawki.pe** – A San Martín company that highlights Amazonian vanilla through a sustainable model with local communities. They will finance professional recording equipment and the development of a premium vanilla extract.

**3rd Place: @restaurant.donmanrique** – A family business in Cañete that blends traditional and modern Peruvian gastronomy. They will allocate the prize to implement a sustainable water management system and protect their brand legally.

**4th Place: @cruceromiramar** – A transport and tourism company in Lima led by a woman. The funds will be used to strengthen their fleet and hire female staff in operational roles.

### Mexico

**1st Place: @nucyclesmx** – A family textile company from Tlaxcala with over 115 years of history, specializing in waste recycling under a circular economy model. They will use the capital to expand their productive capacity and boost their TikTok campaigns.

**2nd Place: @materiales.el.mir** – A family business from San Luis Potosí dedicated to selling construction materials. With their educational and humorous content, they dignify the profession. They will invest in modernizing their machinery and offering free training for industry professionals.

**3rd Place: @mybikemx** – A Puebla company that designs and manufactures customized urban bicycles to promote sustainable mobility. The capital will allow them to launch pop-up stores and optimize their production processes.

**4th Place: @mairospachi** – A textile design brand from Mexico City inspired by Mexican biodiversity. The prize will be allocated to acquire machinery to increase production and establish their own store with a focus on environmental education.

### Colombia

**1st Place: @coffeekreis** – From Barranquilla, they recycle coffee waste to transform it into sustainable products.
