Understand how scammers manage to falsify phone numbers

11 April 2026 · 04:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil faces a significant telephone fraud crisis, with residents receiving more than 1 billion abusive calls monthly according to Idec (Brazilian Consumer Defence Institute). In 2025 alone, 161.16 billion short calls lasting up to six seconds were registered, many deployed by automated systems to validate active numbers before commercial solicitation or fraud attempts. The scale and sophistication of these attacks pose reputational and operational risks for financial institutions and telecommunications providers operating in Brazil.
