# 20 Casinos Approved for Bermúdez Family

Mexico City (April 27, 2026) — In the final year of the AMLO government, 20 permits were granted to operate casinos to Operadora Clíe, linked to betting houses of the family of former Tabasco Security Secretary Hernán Bermúdez Requena, who is accused of criminal association, kidnapping, and extortion.

An investigation by Mexicans Against Corruption and Impunity (MCCI) found that Nabor Castrejón, the majority partner of Operadora Clíe, shared the same official license with the Bermúdez family's betting houses before receiving these new permits. Nabor used permit DGJS/DGAAD/DCRCA/P-01/2017 to operate the Epic casino in Michoacán, and with that same permit, the online sites CrownCity and CityBets were operated, belonging to Controladora de Inversiones y Promociones del Sureste, where Humberto Bermúdez Requena—brother of the former Tabasco Security chief—is a partner, and whose incorporation was witnessed as notary by Morena senator Adán Augusto López.

Diamante Casino operated under that same permit, and last week, the Office of Foreign Assets Control of the Treasury Department published sanctions due to its links with the Northeast Cartel.

The General Directorate of Games and Raffles of the Secretary of Interior (Segob) authorized Compañía Operadora Clíe to install and open 20 betting centers on February 1, 2024, eight months before AMLO's administration ended, in compliance with resolutions from an administrative litigation trial.

In February 2024, when the 20 new permits were granted, Segob was headed by outgoing Morena leader Luisa María Alcalde, who will become the new Legal Counsel to the Presidency.

The 20 permits granted to Clíe in the previous administration have a validity of 15 years and will expire in 2039. The authorizations were issued in compliance with a ruling from the Eighth Metropolitan Regional Chamber of the Federal Administrative Justice Court as part of case 1681/19-17-08-5.

The casino permits were signed by Leonardo Manuel Figueroa Martínez, a lawyer who has been a close collaborator of Luisa María Alcalde over the past six years.
