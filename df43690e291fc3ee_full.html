# Dialetto and 8D Hubify Merge to Reach R$23M Revenue in 2026

Two companies that met as partners in the RD Station ecosystem decided that working together made more sense than operating separately. With this in mind, marketing agency 8D Hubify and public relations (PR) agency Dialetto announced the merger of their operations. With the M&A, disclosed this Tuesday (24), the companies create a business that combines marketing, sales, technology and public relations in a single structure of more than 130 professionals, whose goal is to reach R$23 million in revenue by the end of 2026.

Founded in 2023 from the merger between 8D Pro, a CRM consultancy from Minas Gerais, and Hubify, a São Paulo-based agency, 8D Hubify has around 100 employees, more than 40 clients and the largest managed revenue of RD Station clients in the country. Its client portfolio includes names such as BYD, Ajinomoto, Total Express, Consórcio Magalu, Nuvemshop, Remessa Online, TOTVS and RD Station, among others.

Dialetto, based in Florianópolis, is a PR agency that currently has 30 employees and has about 50 active clients in its portfolio, with emphasis on entrepreneurship and innovation players such as Sebrae Startups, Instituto Caldeira, Kamino and Semantix.

According to 8D Hubify founder and CEO Fabio Duran and Dialetto founder and CEO Rodrigo Lóssio, the integration plan between the companies will be phased. Over the next two years, the companies will connect commercial strategies, develop joint projects that include new services resulting from the combination of their operating fronts, and share methodologies between teams. The formation of the holding company that will control the group's companies is scheduled for 2027, after the consolidation of operational synergies.

The operation had M&A advisory from WP Capital and Norder Capital on behalf of 8D Hubify, and from Silva Schutz Advogados, Softcon and Black T-shirt Consulting on behalf of Dialetto.

## Combining Competencies

The two companies had their first contact within the RD Station partner ecosystem, where both were among the top tier agencies. Dialetto was the first agency to join the program, working for ten years with inbound marketing and press relations in parallel. 8D has been a partner since 2018.

The merger aligns with a trend already underway in the communication and marketing market – the so-called RevOps, a methodology that connects areas such as sales, customer success marketing to generate predictability and efficiency in revenue generation. 8D Hubify was already operating within this model, and now with Dialetto's arrival it adds a layer that the model didn't yet have.

According to 8D's CEO, reputation and authority building enter as elements in the growth strategy, not as a parallel communication initiative. "Companies don't grow efficiently and sustainably with marketing, sales and technology operating in silos. What we're building is a structure oriented toward Revenue Operations, where each discipline works connected to a real revenue generation strategy," says Fabio Duran.

For Rodrigo Lóssio, reputation and authority have an important impact on any growth strategy. "When a company builds trust in the market and has clear positioning, marketing, sales and customer relationships work better. By connecting public relations to a RevOps structure, we can further demonstrate the impact of communication on business results," concludes the executive.
