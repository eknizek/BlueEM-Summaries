EY buys Brazilian consulting firm MB Agro

24 April 2026 · 04:54 PM  ·  O Globo (Brazil)
EY has acquired MB Agro, a Brazilian consulting firm specializing in agribusiness advisory. This acquisition allows EY to strengthen its agricultural sector capabilities and deepen its presence in Latin America's largest agribusiness market. The deal reflects continued consolidation among professional services firms seeking to build specialized expertise in high-growth sectors like agriculture.
