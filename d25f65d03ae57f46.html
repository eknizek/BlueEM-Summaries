FT: Global economic outlook deteriorates amid Iran war escalation

12 April 2026 · 10:03 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
The Financial Times reports that global economic outlooks have deteriorated amid escalating military tensions between the US and Iran, with markets pricing in higher geopolitical risk premiums. Central banks and economists are revising growth forecasts downward due to energy price uncertainty, potential supply chain disruptions, and elevated inflation risks stemming from conflict in the Middle East. For Latin America, this translates to headwinds for export competitiveness, higher import costs for energy and industrial inputs, and potential currency volatility as investors retreat from risk assets. LatAm equities and credit markets face pressure in this risk-off environment.
