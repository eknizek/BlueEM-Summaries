InRetail and IFS Announce $130M Acquisition of InFinance XP (Formerly Financiera Oh!)

07 April 2026 · 09:29 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
InRetail Perú Corp. and Intercorp Financial Services Inc. have completed the acquisition of InFinance XP S.A. (formerly Financiera Oh!) for $130 million, acquiring the entity through IXP Holding Corp. from IFH Retail Corp. This deal strengthens IFS's financial services capabilities in Peru and expands InRetail's ecosystem beyond retail into consumer lending and financial technology. For LatAm private equity and public market investors, the transaction reflects continued consolidation in Peru's fintech sector and Intercorp's strategic diversification into higher-margin financial services.
