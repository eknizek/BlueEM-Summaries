Treasury Requests More Information to Analyze Federal District's Guarantee Request for BRB Loan

29 April 2026 · 10:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Brazilian Treasury National Secretary Daniel Leal confirmed that the Federal District government submitted a request for federal guarantee backing a capitalization loan to BRB (Brasília Bank), but stated that insufficient information was provided for analysis. The bank faces a serious crisis stemming from fraudulent operations involving Banco Master, associated with Daniel Vorcaro. The guarantee request is critical to resolving the bank's capitalization needs as it addresses structural weaknesses exposed by fraud.
