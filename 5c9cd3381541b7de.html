Savegnago Supermarket Chain Reports 120% Jump in Job Applications with 5x2 Work Schedule

09 May 2026 · 03:30 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Savegnago, a Brazilian supermarket chain, hired Jaciani Rizziolli, a former financial market executive, as HR director in 2022 to address high employee turnover in the retail sector. The company implemented a 5x2 work schedule (five days on, two days off) to improve work-life balance and talent retention. This scheduling change resulted in a 120% increase in job applications, demonstrating strong employee interest in the improved working conditions. The initiative highlights how retail companies are adapting HR strategies to compete for talent in a sector traditionally characterized by high staff turnover.
