MIP Plans to Invest Over US$12 Billion in Energy and Roads in Mexico

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
MIP, a significant infrastructure investor, is executing a capital raise program to fund a five-year project portfolio in Mexico spanning energy and highway infrastructure. The firm is targeting approximately US$6 billion in equity capital and US$6 billion in debt financing to support over US$12 billion in total investments. This large-scale infrastructure deployment reflects confidence in Mexico's economic outlook and the availability of capital for long-cycle infrastructure projects. The initiative demonstrates the continued appetite from large institutional investors for infrastructure exposure in Latin America, particularly in Mexico's energy transition and transportation connectivity needs.
