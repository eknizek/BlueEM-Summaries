Connectivity of 893 Colombian Municipalities at Risk Due to Indecision on Azteca Comunicaciones Sale

22 April 2026 · 02:45 AM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Tech
Azteca Comunicaciones, which operates Colombia's largest fiber optic backbone network spanning 32,000 kilometers, faces a critical juncture as financial difficulties threaten service to 893 municipalities across the country. The operator's inability to resolve its financial distress creates uncertainty for rural and regional connectivity infrastructure across Colombia. The situation highlights critical infrastructure dependency on a single operator and broader challenges in telecom sector consolidation and financing.
