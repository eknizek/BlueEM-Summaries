Asenav Shipyard Strengthens Los Ríos Naval Industry with First Sustainable Cruise Ship for $90 Million

20 April 2026 · 10:18 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Asenav shipyard in Los Ríos, Chile has launched the Magellan Discoverer, a $90 million sustainable cruise ship marking a significant milestone for Chilean naval construction. The vessel's maiden navigation on the Calle Calle River required raising the Cau Cau Bridge to an unprecedented 30-degree angle. The project employed 500 workers and generated substantial supply chain activity, opening new opportunities for diversifying Chile's shipbuilding industry beyond traditional vessel types toward sustainable tourism-oriented vessels.
