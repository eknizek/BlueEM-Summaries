Asenav Shipyard Strengthens Los Ríos Naval Industry with First Sustainable Cruise Ship Worth $90 Million

20 April 2026 · 10:18 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Astillero Asenav completed construction and launch of the Magellan Discoverer, a $90 million sustainable cruise ship, marking a significant expansion of Chile's naval industry in Los Ríos. The vessel's maiden navigation required the historic Cau Cau Bridge to be raised at an unprecedented 30-degree angle. The project employed 500 workers and generated multiple productive linkages, opening opportunities for diversification into tourism-focused vessel construction and supporting regional economic development.
