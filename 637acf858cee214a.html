24 April 2026 · 11:04 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
The Chilean government, through a circular from the finance ministry, has instructed various government offices to establish their financial plans for 2027-2031, marking a shift from the traditional annual budgeting approach used in previous years. The decision comes amid controversy and criticism over proposed cuts to social programs. The new multi-year planning framework signals stricter budget discipline and represents a significant change in how Chile will approach its government spending.
