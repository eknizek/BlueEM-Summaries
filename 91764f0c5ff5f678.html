SulAmérica Advances into Brazil's Interior Markets to Reach R$35 Billion Revenue Target

24 April 2026 · 10:57 AM  ·  Neofeed
SulAmérica, the health insurance arm responsible for nearly 60% of Rede D'Or's 2025 revenues, is pursuing geographic expansion into Brazil's interior markets to achieve R$35 billion in total revenue by 2026. The growth strategy involves launching regionalized health plans with lower costs and lower margins than those offered in traditional high-density markets like São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro. This strategy allows SulAmérica to tap underserved markets while managing profitability expectations in lower-density regions.
