SulAmérica expands into interior Brazil targeting R$35 billion revenue by 2026

24 April 2026 · 10:57 AM  ·  Neofeed
SulAmérica, which generates nearly 60% of Rede D'Or's revenue, is pursuing geographic expansion into Brazil's interior regions outside the traditional São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro markets. The growth strategy focuses on lower-cost regional plans to reach R$35 billion in revenue by 2026. While these expansion areas offer growth potential, they may carry lower margins compared to established metropolitan markets.
