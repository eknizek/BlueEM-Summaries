Peru Auto Market Expected to Grow 10–15% in 2026 Absent 'Black Swan' Events

06 April 2026 · 08:27 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Neoauto CEO Mariano Nejamkis projected Peru's vehicle market (light and heavy duty) will grow 10–15% in 2026 assuming no major economic shocks, despite ongoing political uncertainty and geopolitical tensions. The forecast reflects resilient domestic demand and consumer confidence in vehicle purchases. For LatAm investors in automotive retail, equipment, and consumer goods, this indicates continued expansion in one of the region's key auto markets, though execution depends on macro stability and financing availability.
