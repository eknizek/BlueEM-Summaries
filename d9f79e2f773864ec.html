Quequén Port Dispute Resolved After 15+ Days of Strikes

24 April 2026 · 08:02 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
A prolonged labor dispute at Argentina's Quequén port concluded after over 15 days of strikes and access blockades initiated by transport workers demanding tariff increases. The disruption stranded approximately 17 vessels and delayed export of 347,600 tonnes of grain, significantly impacting agricultural export logistics. Following agreement on tariff rate increases, normal truck operations are expected to resume within hours, alleviating the export bottleneck for Argentine grain producers.
