Port of Quequén labor dispute resolved after 15+ days of strike

24 April 2026 · 08:02 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
A labor dispute at Argentina's Port of Quequén has been resolved after more than 15 days of strikes by transporters, which had blocked port access and delayed cargo operations. The standoff had left approximately 17 vessels waiting to load and 347,600 tons of grain stranded. Following an agreement on tariff increases for transport operators, truck traffic is expected to normalize in the coming hours, alleviating a significant supply chain bottleneck for Argentine agricultural exports.
