Quequén Port conflict resolved after more than 15 days of strike

24 April 2026 · 08:02 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's Quequén Port has resolved a labor dispute that blocked access for over 15 days, with transporters demanding higher tariffs. The strike had caused significant congestion, with 17 vessels waiting to load and approximately 347,600 tonnes of grain shipments delayed. Following a negotiated agreement on tariff increases, truck traffic is expected to resume normalization in the coming hours, relieving agricultural export bottlenecks.
