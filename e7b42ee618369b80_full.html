Yucatán Positioned as Growth Hub for Industrial Real Estate Investment

20 April 2026 · 11:15 PM

The pressure on energy, water, and costs in the north and Bajío regions has opened space for new areas, and Yucatán is already appearing in real investment decisions for the development of industrial warehouses, anticipated the firm Spot2.mx.

The goal is for Yucatán to stop being perceived as an emerging or alternative market and establish itself as the primary industrial hub.
