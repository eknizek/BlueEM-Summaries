Enel receives government decision not to revoke concession 'with satisfaction' and states Chile is strategic

01 May 2026 · 10:49 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Chile's government announced its decision not to proceed with revoking Enel's electricity distribution concession after nearly two years of regulatory scrutiny. Energy Minister Ximena Rincón stated that the government lacks legal grounds to terminate the concession, even if courts rule in Enel's favor on force majeure claims related to service failures. Enel expressed satisfaction with the decision and reaffirmed Chile's strategic importance to the company, removing a major regulatory risk for the Italian utility's Chilean operations.
