US Trade Representative Accuses Mexico of Commercial Barriers in T-MEC Review

20 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
The US Office of the Trade Representative has formally accused Mexico of maintaining commercial and business barriers as part of discussions surrounding the review of the USMCA (T-MEC). This allegation is significant for the trilateral trade agreement's scheduled review, which could impact tariffs, investment protections, and market access across North America. The complaint reflects ongoing tensions in US-Mexico bilateral trade relations and may signal a harder US negotiating stance in the upcoming review process.
