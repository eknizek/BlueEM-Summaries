Real Plaza Announces New Shopping Center in Trujillo with S/500 Million Investment

22 April 2026 · 05:11 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Real Plaza, a leading Peruvian shopping center operator, announced plans to develop a new commercial center in Trujillo with an investment of 500 million soles (approximately $135 million USD). This expansion represents the company's continued growth strategy in Peru and signals retail confidence in regional markets, coming over a year after the previous facility was impacted by a natural disaster.
