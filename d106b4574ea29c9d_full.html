# OdontoPrev Net Profit Falls 10.3% in Q1 to R$ 152 Million

OdontoPrev recorded a net profit of R$ 152.1 million in the first quarter, which represents a 10.3% decline compared to the same period last year. The company's trading code on B3 changes this Tuesday (5th) from ODPV3 to SAUD3.

Revenue increased 4% to R$ 595.4 million. However, costs increased at a larger proportion, rising 12.7% to R$ 377.8 million.

The dental plan operator is part of Bradsaúde, a company created in March that brings together all health assets of the Bradesco group. Bradsaúde has annual revenue of approximately R$ 52 billion.

On Friday (1st), Bradesco announced that it increased its stake in Bradsaúde from 53.61% to 91.35% of the total social capital and voting power of the new health company.
