Ecopetrol Acquires 26% of Brava Energy for R$2.77 Billion and Launches Control Bid

23 April 2026 · 10:01 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Ecopetrol, the Colombian state-controlled oil company, has signed a contract to acquire a 26% stake in Brava Energia, a Brazilian independent oil and gas producer, at R$23 per share for an initial investment of R$2.77 billion. Ecopetrol has simultaneously announced a public tender offer (OPA) to acquire an additional stake to achieve 51% control of the company. This transaction marks a significant strategic expansion by Ecopetrol into Brazil's oil and gas sector and represents major M&A activity in Latin American energy.
