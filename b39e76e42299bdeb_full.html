# Peso Falls on Middle East Concerns; Dollar Price April 13, 2026

The Mexican peso begins the week with losses amid renewed investor concerns about the duration of the Middle East conflict, following a weekend with negative signals in negotiations between the United States and Iran, with the dollar price today April 13, 2026 trading at $17.32 pesos; find out here the exchange rate in Mexican banks.

The Mexican currency registers a depreciation of 0.21% compared to 17.29 pesos, the reference price from Friday. "The opening of the Mexican peso occurs near 17.37 per dollar and marks the beginning of a high-tension day, where the national currency is caught between notable internal economic resilience and a drastic deterioration of the global geopolitical outlook," said Felipe Mendoza, market analyst at EBC Financial Group, in a note to clients.

U.S. President Donald Trump said over the weekend that the U.S. Army would collaborate with other nations to block all maritime traffic in the Strait of Hormuz, after negotiations failed to reach an agreement to end the six-week conflict with Iran.
