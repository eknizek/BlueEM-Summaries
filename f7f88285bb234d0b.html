Latin America to Double Demand for Care Workers by 2050 Amid Aging Population: ILO

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
The International Labour Organization (ILO) has projected that Latin America will need to double its care worker workforce by 2050 to meet demand driven by population aging and demographic shifts. This forecast reflects significant structural labor market changes across the region. The trend has implications for healthcare spending, social policy, pension systems, and employment in the care and healthcare sectors across Latin America.
