Inflationary pressures will rise in second half, Banamex projects

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Banamex, one of Mexico's largest commercial banks, has issued a forward guidance warning that inflationary pressures are likely to intensify in the second half of the year, cautioning against premature optimism. The bank cites ongoing external economic shocks and structural factors as persistent headwinds to price stability. This assessment carries implications for monetary policy expectations, fixed-income valuations, and equity forecasts tied to consumer spending and corporate margins.
