Holcim Plans Possible NYSE Delisting of Cementos Pacasmayo ADS

06 April 2026 · 11:45 PM  ·  Stock Titan
Following Holcim's acquisition of a controlling stake in Cementos Pacasmayo, the parent company is evaluating a potential delisting of Cementos Pacasmayo's American Depositary Shares (ADS) from the NYSE. Delisting would consolidate operational and reporting structure under Holcim but remove liquidity for minority shareholders and public equity investors. This decision affects portfolio positioning for LatAm-focused equity and ADR investors in the building materials sector.
