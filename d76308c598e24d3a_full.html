# Manitoba Brand Expands Portfolio with Launch of Almond and Cashew Nut Cream

The Manitoba brand announced the launch of its new nut cream made with almond and cashew, a proposal that seeks to provide new experiences to its consumers, drive growth in the category, and respond to the growing demand from consumers for nutritious foods, practical options, with natural ingredients and rich in protein.

The company presented this product that combines almonds and cashews, two ingredients with high nutritional value and growing preference among consumers.

## A Growing Market Driven by Healthy Consumption

The launch occurs in a context of growth for the tree nut market in Colombia. Within this segment, nut-based creams have gained prominence thanks to their nutritional contribution and versatility in daily consumption. In this scenario, ingredients such as almonds and cashews show remarkable dynamism. According to Nielsen data, almonds are growing by 30%, while cashews show significant potential with growth close to 980%, reflecting the growing interest of consumers in protein-rich, fiber-rich snacks and healthy fats that can be easily integrated into their daily routine. Additionally, this combination of flavors creates an experience where two delicious trending nuts are combined.

In response to this trend, Manitoba's new nut cream offers an excellent source of protein and fiber, plus it is free of warning labels, reflecting its balanced profile and its commitment to offering alternatives aligned with current eating trends. Beyond its flavor, the combination of almonds and cashews provides important nutritional benefits. Both ingredients are recognized for their content of healthy fats, fiber, antioxidants, and plant-based protein, making them an ideal option for those seeking balanced nutrition.

For the brand, this launch also responds to the leadership that Manitoba has maintained for many years within the tree nut cream category. Currently, the brand is one of the most relevant in the peanut cream segment in Colombia and during 2025 registered growth of 23%.

This vision of constant innovation has also been reflected recently in the launch of a 100% natural cream for dogs in alliance with Ringo, an initiative that expands the brand's portfolio and strengthens its value proposition within the healthy food segment for these important family members.

"This launch represents a natural step in the evolution of our portfolio. We want to continue leading innovation in the tree nut segment and offer consumers nutritious products, differentiated experiences, flavor and quality," said Gustavo Llano Domínguez, founder and Commercial Manager of Manitoba.

## An Accessible Premium Product

The new nut cream seeks to democratize access to premium products, offering an alternative with high nutritional value ingredients at a competitive price. With Manitoba, many people will be able to access this product as it is within reach of different budgets. The launch strategy will include sampling, promotion at points of sale and brand activations, with the objective of bringing the product closer to consumers and strengthening category growth.
