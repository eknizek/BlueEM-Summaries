Why does GameStop want to take on debt to buy eBay?

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
GameStop has announced plans to acquire eBay for $56 billion, a transaction representing nearly five times the video game retailer's current market capitalization. The proposed all-debt financed acquisition would be one of the largest leveraged buyouts in history. The move reflects GameStop's strategy to transform from a brick-and-mortar gaming retailer into a broader e-commerce platform, though the deal's feasibility and financing structure raise significant questions about execution and financial leverage.
