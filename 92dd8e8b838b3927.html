Congress Makes Final Push to Revive Tax Exemption Bill for Data Centers

08 April 2026 · 10:15 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazil's Special Taxation Regime for Data Center Services (Redata), a government fiscal incentive program designed to attract foreign investment to the data center sector, has stalled in the Senate after earlier delays. Parliamentary coalitions representing the productive sector are organizing political pressure campaigns to unblock the vote and advance the legislation. The program aims to improve Brazil's competitiveness in attracting digital infrastructure investment and cloud computing operations. Success would signal government commitment to digital economy development and could catalyze data center investment across Latin America.
