Buenos Aires issues US$500m debt at record-low 7.37% yield

06 May 2026 · 08:29 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Buenos Aires City Government successfully issued USD 500 million of 10-year bonds at a coupon of 7.37%, marking the lowest yield in the municipality's history. The operation involved major Wall Street banks and was structured to improve the city's debt maturity profile. The successful pricing reflects improved market sentiment toward Argentine credit despite broader macroeconomic constraints.
