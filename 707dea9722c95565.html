Fintech company Addi closes US$150 million financing round and accelerates regional expansion

20 April 2026 · 03:46 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Addi, a Colombian fintech platform specializing in buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) solutions, has closed a US$150 million financing round led by J.P. Morgan, which contributed US$130 million of the total raise. The funding will accelerate the company's regional expansion across Latin America, strengthening its position in the high-growth digital payments and consumer credit segment. This capital injection reflects strong investor confidence in the BNPL and financial inclusion opportunity in emerging markets.
