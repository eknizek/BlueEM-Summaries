Aviation Fuel Price Surge Has 'Serious Impacts', Says Airlines Association

02 May 2026 · 06:26 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Brazil's airline industry association has flagged the sharp increase in aviation fuel (kerosene) prices as having 'serious impacts' on carriers' operations and financial viability. The price surge directly affects operating margins for airlines serving the Brazilian market. This cost pressure comes amid broader economic pressures and regional competition. The association's statement underscores the vulnerability of transportation operators to commodity price fluctuations.
