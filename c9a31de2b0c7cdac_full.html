# Markets sour on government fuel pricing plan

The mechanism presented by the government to enable possible new reductions in fuel taxes, given the price impacts that the Middle East war has generated, does not seem to be a cause for great fiscal concern. However, the confusion in the announcement of the measure, the lack of transparency, and doubts about its execution have awakened apprehension in the market.

Ministers of Planning Bruno Moretti, Finance Dario Durigan, and Institutional Relations José Guimarães held a press conference on measures related to PIS/Cofins.
