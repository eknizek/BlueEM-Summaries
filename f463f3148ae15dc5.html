"Extreme Gravity": Sugar Industry Warns of Government Natural Gas Supply Regulation During Harvest Season

05 May 2026 · 06:53 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Argentina's sugar industry and Tucumán's industrial sector have raised serious concerns about government resolution SE N° 66/2026 modifying natural gas transport and supply systems. The new regulation reduces transport capacity and prioritizes residential consumption, threatening to cut industrial gas supplies during winter months—a critical period for sugar processing during harvest season. The Centro Azucarero Argentino and Unión Industrial de Tucumán warn the changes pose extreme risks to production continuity.
