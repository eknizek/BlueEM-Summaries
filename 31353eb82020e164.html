Inner AI Reaches R$500M Valuation in Canary, OneVC-Led Round

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Inner AI, a Brazilian artificial intelligence company, has achieved a R$500 million valuation following a capital raise led by Canary and OneVC, with new funding of R$30 million earmarked for developing unified autonomous agents capable of executing complex business tasks. The investment reflects strong VC appetite for enterprise AI solutions in Brazil and broader LatAm markets. The funding round underscores the region's growing position in AI application development.
