Brazil's Central Bank Imposes Eight-Year Secrecy on Documents Related to Banco Master Liquidation

06 April 2026 · 08:28 PM  ·  Valor Economico
The Brazilian Central Bank has imposed an eight-year information embargo on documents related to the liquidation proceedings of Banco Master, a failed financial institution. This decision limits public and investor transparency regarding the failure and resolution process. For LatAm investors, this raises concerns about regulatory transparency, deposit protection mechanisms, and the precedent for information disclosure in Brazilian banking crises, affecting confidence in the Central Bank's crisis management and stakeholder communication.
