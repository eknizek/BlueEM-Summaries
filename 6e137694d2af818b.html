Brazil's Petrobras decides not to exercise waiver rights over Novonor's stake sale in Braskem

23 April 2026 · 11:35 PM  ·  Reuters
Petrobras has decided not to exercise pre-emptive waiver rights on Novonor's planned stake sale in Braskem, a major petrochemical company. This decision removes a regulatory obstacle in the transaction and signals the state-controlled oil giant's acceptance of the deal structure. The move is significant for capital markets activity in Brazil's chemicals sector and reflects Petrobras's strategic priorities regarding its portfolio.
