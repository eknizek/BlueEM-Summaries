Restricting Mexico's cultural promotion would deter investment, CNET warns

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Mexico's tourism authority (CNET) cautions that restricting the country's cultural and tourism promotion would undermine foreign direct investment and Mexico's position as a leading global tourism destination. Responsible cultural promotion is credited as the foundation for Mexico's tourism competitiveness and international standing. Tourism sector performance directly impacts foreign exchange earnings and economic activity in Mexico.
