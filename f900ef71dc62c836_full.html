Lufthansa Seeks Additional Landing Slots at Mexico City Airport

The German airline Lufthansa Group is seeking to obtain more takeoff and landing schedules, better known as slots, at Mexico City International Airport (AICM) to expand its connectivity in Mexico.

For now, Lufthansa has no interest in operating passenger flights at Felipe Ángeles International Airport (AIFA).
