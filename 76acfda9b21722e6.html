Stone Navigates Payment Hardware Pressure Amid New Business Diversification

07 April 2026 · 03:41 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Brazilian fintech Stone&Co has seen its stock decline 20% following disappointing fourth-quarter earnings and weak forward guidance, as the company grapples with margin pressure in its core payment processing business. While the payments segment maintains high margins, management recognizes the need to diversify beyond hardware-dependent maquininha (POS terminal) revenue to drive growth. The challenge highlights competitive pressures in Brazil's fintech payments market and investor concerns about Stone's ability to transition to higher-margin, scalable revenue streams.
