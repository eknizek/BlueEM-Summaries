An opinion article argues that e-commerce platforms are inappropriately treating pharmaceuticals like consumer goods while avoiding regulatory and safety responsibilities. The piece emphasizes that medications differ fundamentally from typical retail items due to active ingredients, dosing, contraindications, drug interactions, storage requirements, traceability, and need for professional pharmaceutical guidance. The article highlights that medication errors can result in serious health harm or death, distinguishing pharmaceutical liability from standard consumer dissatisfaction.
