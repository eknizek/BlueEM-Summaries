ISA Appoints Olga Patricia Castaño as Acting President and Announces Other Changes

05 May 2026 · 09:32 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombian utility company ISA has appointed Olga Patricia Castaño as acting president, marking the second interim leadership appointment following the annulment of Jorge Carrillo's election in February. The company also announced additional organizational changes. This succession event is significant for investors monitoring ISA's governance and strategic direction during a period of leadership transition and potential regulatory scrutiny in Colombia's infrastructure sector.
