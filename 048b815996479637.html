EntrePay crisis reaches credit market as FIDCs close for redemptions

17 April 2026 · 06:30 PM  ·  Neofeed
The financial crisis at Grupo Entre, a Brazilian payment processor that left clients unable to receive sales proceeds from card transactions, is now threatening Credit Receivables Investment Funds (FIDCs) with exposure to EntrePay's receivables. The Central Bank decreed extraordinary liquidation of EntrePay in late March, forcing FIDCs holding its assets to suspend redemptions. This represents systemic stress in Brazil's fintech-backed credit market and non-bank lending ecosystem.
