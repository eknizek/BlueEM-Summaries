Hidrovia Concession Bidding Begins Today with US Attention Focused on Argentina

21 April 2026 · 10:11 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
The Argentine government is opening technical bids today from two remaining bidders for the strategic Hidrovia (Paraná River) concession, a critical infrastructure project. The Hidrovia handles approximately 80% of Argentina's external trade, making the concession award significant for exports and economic activity. The US government is closely monitoring the bidding process, reflecting the geopolitical and economic importance of the concession.
