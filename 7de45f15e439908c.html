Nuvini executes largest M&A in company history to advance AI capabilities

06 April 2026 · 05:59 PM  ·  Neofeed
Nuvini, the internet holding founded by Pierre Schurmann in 2020 that consolidates SaaS companies, announced on April 6 its largest acquisition and first transaction outside Brazil, targeting artificial intelligence advancement. The deal reflects Nuvini's strategic pivot toward AI-enabled software solutions and geographic diversification beyond the Brazilian market. This acquisition is material for tracking Brazilian tech/SaaS consolidation trends and PE-backed growth strategies in the region.
