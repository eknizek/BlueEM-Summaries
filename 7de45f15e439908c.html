Nuvini, Owned by Pierre Schurmann, Makes Its Largest M&A Deal in History to Advance in AI

06 April 2026 · 05:59 PM  ·  Neofeed
Nuvini, a software-as-a-service (SaaS) holding company founded by Pierre Schurmann in 2020, announced on April 6 its largest acquisition to date and its first cross-border deal, focused on expanding AI capabilities. The move reflects the Brazilian tech sector's aggressive pivot toward artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance its service offerings. The acquisition signals investor appetite for consolidation in LatAm SaaS and highlights growth ambitions in a competitive global AI landscape. For LatAm-focused PE and tech investors, this deal exemplifies the shift toward AI-driven software platforms as core value drivers.
