Gilead Acquires Tubulis to Strengthen Oncology Pipeline with Antibody-Drug Conjugate Platform

This strategic acquisition allows Gilead to add potentially best-in-class cancer therapeutics and next-generation platform capabilities. While a significant pharma M&A event, Gilead's operations are global but this deal has limited direct impact on Latin American operations or investment markets.
