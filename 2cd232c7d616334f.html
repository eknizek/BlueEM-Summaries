Peru's Manufacturing Industry Grows 2.7% in February 2026, Driven by Capital Goods

20 April 2026 · 07:40 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's manufacturing sector grew 2.7% in February 2026, with the non-primary manufacturing subsector advancing 3.6% and the primary subsector growing 0.3%, according to the National Society of Industries (SNI) economic institute. Capital goods production drove non-primary sector growth, signaling business investment activity and industrial capacity expansion. The data reflects Peru's industrial recovery and suggests ongoing economic momentum in manufacturing. Manufacturing growth is a key indicator of domestic investment, employment, and economic health relevant to regional investors.
