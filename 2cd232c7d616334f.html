Peru's Manufacturing Industry Grows 2.7% in February 2026, Driven by Non-Primary Capital Goods

20 April 2026 · 07:40 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's manufacturing industry recorded 2.7% growth in February 2026, driven by robust performance in the non-primary (secondary) manufacturing subsector, which expanded 3.6%. Capital goods production was a standout performer within the non-primary segment. Primary manufacturing (resource-based) grew more modestly at 0.3%, reflecting typical commodity sector dynamics. The data from the Institute of Economic and Social Studies (IEES) indicates strengthening industrial activity in Peru's economy.
