Economists Welcome Desenrola 2 Relief but Warn of Limited Duration

04 May 2026 · 11:20 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Economic analysts have assessed the government's Desenrola 2 debt relief initiative as providing immediate household income relief but question its long-term structural effectiveness. Experts highlight that while the program offers temporary support for struggling families, the measures lack sufficient scale and duration to address Brazil's underlying household debt dynamics. The assessment points to a need for complementary structural reforms to achieve lasting financial stability.
