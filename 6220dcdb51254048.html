Colombian foreign trade in crisis due to persistent DIAN IT system failures

20 April 2026 · 08:45 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombia's foreign trade sector is facing significant disruption due to ongoing technical failures in the DIAN (Dirección de Impuestos y Aduanas Nacionales) computer systems. According to Fitac, a business federation, these IT irregularities are obstructing normal commerce activities and creating bottlenecks in customs processing. The crisis affects exporters, importers, and logistics operators who depend on DIAN systems for regulatory compliance and shipment clearance. This represents a critical infrastructure and regulatory challenge impacting economic activity and foreign exchange flows.
