Mexico tourism receipts hit lowest level in 28 months

10 April 2026 · 06:36 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Mexico's tourism sector continues to struggle, with February revenues posting only a 0.4% year-over-year increase and hitting the lowest level in 28 months. This stagnation reflects softening international travel demand and reduced domestic tourism spending, with implications for hospitality REITs, hotel operators, and ancillary service providers across the sector. The weak performance may pressure government tax revenues and consumer-related businesses dependent on tourism spending, while signalling broader economic hesitation among both Mexican and foreign travellers.
