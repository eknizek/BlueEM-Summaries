Ecopetrol Secures US$1.25 Billion Refinancing Loan

This refinancing improves the company's liquidity position and extends its debt maturity profile. The deal is material for LatAm energy investors monitoring Ecopetrol's financial health, leverage ratios, and ability to fund operations and capital expenditure in a volatile commodity environment.
