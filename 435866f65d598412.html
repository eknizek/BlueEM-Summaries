Veriff, Estonian Unicorn, Enters Brazil Using AI to Combat AI Fraud

10 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  InvestNews Startups
Veriff, an Estonia-based identity verification and fraud detection startup valued at over $1 billion, has entered the Brazilian market with AI-driven solutions designed to combat increasingly sophisticated AI-enabled fraud schemes. The company leverages advanced machine learning to authenticate users and detect fraudulent activities in real time. This expansion reflects growing demand in Latin America for robust digital identity and fraud prevention solutions as fintech adoption accelerates across the region.
