SEC to Settle Lawsuit Against Elon Musk Over Twitter Share Purchases

04 May 2026 · 09:42 PM  ·  Wall Street Journal - Tech
The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission is settling a lawsuit against Elon Musk regarding his acquisition of Twitter (now X). The case alleged that Musk failed to make timely public disclosure of his growing ownership stake in the social media platform before he ultimately purchased and took it private. The settlement concludes litigation over securities law compliance related to one of the most high-profile acquisitions in tech history.
