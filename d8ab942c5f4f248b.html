After Acquiring Empírica, SRM Enters Debt Market and Targets R$500 Million

07 April 2026 · 09:26 PM  ·  Neofeed
SRM, a Brazilian asset manager specializing in credit rights funds (FIDCs), is diversifying into debt capital markets (DCM) following its acquisition of Empírica. The firm currently manages approximately R$2 billion in assets under management and aims to generate R$500 million in its first year of DCM operations. This strategic expansion represents a significant diversification move for the firm, moving beyond its core FIDC business into broader debt markets. The acquisition and new division launch signal growth ambitions and operational scale-up in Brazil's credit and capital markets.
