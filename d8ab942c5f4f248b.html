After acquiring Empírica, SRM enters debt market and targets R$ 500 million

07 April 2026 · 09:26 PM  ·  Neofeed
SRM, a Brazilian alternative asset manager, is launching a debt capital markets (DCM) division to diversify beyond its core FIDC (multi-cedent, multi-obligor credit fund) business, which manages approximately R$2 billion in assets. Following the acquisition of Empírica, the manager aims to originate R$500 million in debt capital market transactions in the first year. This expansion reflects growing institutional demand for alternative credit solutions in Brazil and positions SRM as a multi-platform credit platform serving institutional investors across structured credit and direct lending.
