2025 Report: Crowdfunding financing surges

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Crowdfunding platforms in Mexico are experiencing accelerated adoption as both a credit source for individuals and businesses and as an investment vehicle for capital-seekers. The sector is maturing as a legitimate alternative to traditional banking and venture capital, offering both lower-income borrowers and retail investors access to previously unavailable opportunities. Growth in 2025 reflects increased financial inclusion and diversification of Mexico's credit ecosystem beyond conventional banking channels. This trend aligns with broader fintech expansion across Latin America and growing demand for alternative finance solutions.
