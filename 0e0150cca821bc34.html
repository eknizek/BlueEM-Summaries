Peru concentrates over 28% of copper projects in the Americas: surpassing Canada, Chile and the United States

30 April 2026 · 07:33 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru dominates the Americas copper project landscape with 19 of 67 identified projects (28.4% of the regional total), surpassing major mining jurisdictions including Canada, Chile, and the United States. This concentration reflects Peru's location within one of the world's most prolific polymetallic belts. The insight was shared by Roque Benavides Ganoza, chairman of Compañía de Minas Buenaventura, at ExpoCobre 2026, highlighting Peru's strategic importance for global copper supply amid rising demand for electrification and energy transition.
