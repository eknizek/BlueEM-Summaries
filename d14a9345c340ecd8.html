Indecopi Launches Antitrust Investigation Against Five Poultry Companies for Alleged Price-Fixing in Live Chicken Sales

06 April 2026 · 11:18 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Peru's competition authority (Indecopi) has initiated a formal sanction proceeding against five poultry companies and three individuals for alleged anticompetitive price-fixing in live chicken sales across five Peruvian regions (Lambayeque, La Libertad, Piura, Áncash, and Cajamarca) during February 2020–March 2021. This regulatory enforcement action reflects ongoing scrutiny of agricultural commodity markets and potential supply-chain discipline. For investors in Peruvian agribusiness and food production, the case signals regulatory risk and potential penalties for affected producers, with implications for sector profitability and market consolidation dynamics.
