Indecopi Launches Antitrust Proceedings Against 5 Poultry Producers for Alleged Price-Fixing

06 April 2026 · 11:18 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Peru's National Competition Authority (Indecopi) has launched administrative sanctions proceedings against five poultry producers and three individuals for alleged concerted price-fixing in the live chicken market across Lambayeque, La Libertad, Piura, Áncash, and Cajamarca regions. The investigation covers conduct between February 2020 and March 2021 and represents a significant enforcement action in Peru's food supply chain. This case is material for investors in agribusiness and consumer-facing companies relying on poultry inputs, as successful sanctions could reshape competitive dynamics and pricing in Peru's protein sector.
