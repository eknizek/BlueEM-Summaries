Banking strike at BCRA: what will happen with ATMs, transfers and payments on Monday April 27

24 April 2026 · 03:13 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentine bank workers are planning a strike for Monday April 27 in protest of the Central Bank's decision to close 12 provincial treasury offices. During the strike, banks will operate with weekend-level service, with limited access to ATMs, transfers, and payment processing. The labor action represents resistance from banking sector workers to the Central Bank's restructuring of its regional infrastructure.
