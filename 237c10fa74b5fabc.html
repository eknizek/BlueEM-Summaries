Court condemns 99Food in iFood campaign dispute; orders damages

10 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
A Brazilian court has condemned 99Food, a Chinese-backed food delivery platform, for running defamatory marketing campaigns against competitor iFood. The company has been ordered to pay R$50,000 in moral damages and is prohibited from continuing similar advertising campaigns. This legal decision highlights competitive disputes in Brazil's food delivery market and regulatory scrutiny of aggressive marketing tactics.
