Federal Audit Court Orders INSS to Suspend New Payroll Deduction Loan Contracts

29 April 2026 · 09:41 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's Federal Audit Court (TCU) issued an order requiring the National Social Security Institute (INSS) to immediately suspend new consigned loan contracts in credit card and benefit card modalities. This regulatory intervention targets the payroll deduction lending market, addressing concerns around consumer protection and financial stability. The suspension impacts a significant retail credit segment in Brazil's financial market.
