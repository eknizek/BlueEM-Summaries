Federal Audit Court orders INSS to suspend new payroll deduction credit contracts

29 April 2026 · 09:41 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Federal Audit Court (TCU) issued a directive requiring Brazil's Social Security Institute (INSS) to immediately halt new payroll-deducted credit (consignado) contracts in both credit card and benefit card modalities. This regulatory intervention addresses concerns over consumer debt accumulation and lending practices in the INSS portfolio. The suspension impacts a significant private credit segment serving Brazilian retirees and pensioners, affecting financial institutions dependent on consignado origination channels.
