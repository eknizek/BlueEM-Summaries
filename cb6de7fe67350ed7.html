Government authorizes mining exploitation start in La Tapada, first phase of Tía María project

18 April 2026 · 09:39 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's Ministry of Energy and Mines (Minem) issued a directorial resolution formally authorizing the start of mining exploitation at La Tapada, representing the first phase of the Tía María copper project located in Arequipa region. This regulatory approval is significant for the long-delayed copper development project and represents progress on a strategically important mining investment for Peru. The project's advancement is material for copper supply dynamics and Peru's mining sector development.
