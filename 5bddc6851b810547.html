"One of Largest Oil Market Shocks Hits Aviation Fuel, Puts Airlines on Alert," Warn S&P and IATA

08 April 2026 · 07:12 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
S&P Global and the International Air Transport Association (IATA) have issued alerts regarding unprecedented disruptions to aviation fuel supply and logistics, driven by Middle East geopolitical tensions. Jet fuel prices are rising sharply, creating material cost pressures on airlines globally and regionally. For LatAm investors, this affects airlines (LATAM, Aeromexico, Avianca), tourism-dependent economies, and broader transportation logistics costs. Airlines operating in the region face margin compression unless they can pass costs to consumers. This is a critical sector-specific risk affecting aviation and tourism-dependent equity valuations across Latin America.
