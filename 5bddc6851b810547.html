One of the largest oil market shocks hits aviation fuel and puts aviation on alert, warn S&P and IATA

08 April 2026 · 07:12 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
S&P Global and the International Air Transport Association (IATA) have jointly warned of an unprecedented supply and logistics disruption in aviation fuel markets stemming from recent geopolitical shocks. IATA notes that fuel price surges are already pressuring airline operating costs and margins. For LatAm investors, this affects airlines (LATAM, GOL, Avianca), transportation logistics, and consumer discretionary spending; the higher fuel costs cascade through the region's connectivity and tourism sectors.
