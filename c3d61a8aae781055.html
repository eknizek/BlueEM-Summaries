Colombia to build new multipurpose port terminal in Ciénaga with rail connection for 30-year concession

11 April 2026 · 08:44 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia's National Infrastructure Agency (ANI) has approved a concession for a new multipurpose maritime terminal in Ciénaga with $82.8 million in investment and a 30-year operating agreement. The terminal will prioritize rail connectivity, improving intermodal logistics efficiency. This infrastructure project enhances Colombia's export capacity for agricultural and mining products, attracting private investors and supporting LatAm trade and logistics expansion through a major Caribbean hub.
