The R$37 Billion "Blackout" in the FIDC Market

20 April 2026 · 12:10 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazil's FIDC (Fundo de Investimento em Direitos Creditórios, credit receivables fund) market, which has experienced rapid growth in recent years, faces a disclosure crisis with repeated delays in monthly reporting. This "blackout" affects approximately R$37 billion in assets and undermines investor transparency across a fund category known for complex structures involving various types of receivables. The delays raise regulatory and investor protection concerns in a sector that has attracted significant capital flows and is critical to Brazil's non-bank lending ecosystem.
