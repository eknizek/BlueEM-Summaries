Eliminating export taxes would "balance fiscal impact in 4 years," says Rosario exchange

24 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
The Rosario Stock Exchange (Bolsa de Comercio de Rosario) released analysis arguing that elimination of Argentina's export taxes (retenciones) would achieve fiscal balance within four years by stimulating economic activity and provincial tax bases. Provinces would see immediate positive effects from higher agricultural and commodity activity. The projection was made during the auction of the first soybean lot for the 2025/26 season, reflecting ongoing debate over tax policy affecting Argentina's agricultural sector.
