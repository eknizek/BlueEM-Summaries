Eliminating export tariffs would balance fiscal impact within 4 years, analysts project

24 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
The Rosario Stock Exchange projects that eliminating Argentina's export tariffs (retenciones) would produce immediate positive effects for provinces through increased agricultural activity, with the overall fiscal impact fully offset within four years. The analysis was presented during the 2025/26 soy auction (Remate de Soja), reflecting ongoing debate over tariff policy's impact on agribusiness profitability and government revenues.
