Mexican Grain Producers Anticipate Price Decline Despite Planting Increases

08 April 2026 · 11:00 AM

Mexican grain producers anticipate increased planting this year even though the value of their production would record a decline.

Mexican grain producers foresee increasing their planting this year although the value of their production would experience a drop.

Mexico City (April 8, 2026) - 05:00 hrs

Mexican grain producers are projecting an increase in planting this year, even as the value of their production is expected to decline. The expansion in planted area comes amid concerns about falling commodity prices that could offset gains from higher production volumes.
