# Flora & Fauna Returns to Avenida Dos de Mayo After 80 Years

Flora & Fauna announced the opening of its thirteenth store on Avenida Dos de Mayo in San Martín, a location the brand is returning to after 80 years.

The inauguration will take place on April 23 and will feature preliminary activities including nutritional talks, children's activities, alcohol-free wine tasting, healthy snacks sampling, and a conscious after-office event.

"Beyond commercial expansion, this opening represents a strategic and symbolic milestone for Flora & Fauna. Ten years ago we emerged as part of a global trend that we have driven in the local market. Today, the Peruvian consumer understands that it's not just about healthier products, but cleaner ones. Ours exclude more than 200 harmful ingredients and additives for health. Caring for the well-being of our customers remains our purpose, just as it was for our grandfathers," said Erasmo Wong, CEO of Flora & Fauna.

Part of the brand's expansion in these areas is to continue strengthening the bonds created between customers and collaborators. "We have customers who visit our stores several times a week, even daily; they are neighbors and people who work in those areas. Returning to the neighborhood with our store number 13 reaffirms values such as closeness, origin, and consciousness. This new location renews the family legacy: bringing well-being through our products to new generations," said Eileen Wong, co-founder of Flora & Fauna.
