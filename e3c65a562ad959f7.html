Mother's Day: SMEs project sales of up to S/3.65 billion despite political uncertainty

27 April 2026 · 09:33 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in Peru are maintaining positive sales expectations for the Mother's Day campaign, despite current political uncertainty and electoral context. The Lima Chamber of Commerce (CCL) estimates sales will reach between S/3.55 billion and S/3.65 billion, representing growth of 6% to 9% compared to the prior year. This projection suggests consumer spending resilience in Peru's retail sector despite macroeconomic headwinds.
