Retailers Seek to Boost 65-inch TV and Premium Smartphone Sales During World Cup, but Fear Bad Debt

26 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazilian consumer electronics retailers are targeting increased sales of 65-inch and larger televisions and premium smartphones during the 2026 World Cup, traditionally a major consumption event comparable to Black Friday. However, retailers are concerned about credit risk and consumer payment defaults amid economic pressure. The sector expects strong demand for entertainment and technology products tied to World Cup viewership but faces uncertainty around consumer creditworthiness.
