Desenrola 2.0 introduces new phase for borrowers meeting payment obligations amid budget constraints

05 May 2026 · 01:34 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Brazil's Desenrola 2.0 debt renegotiation program is introducing a new phase targeting borrowers who are current on payments but facing budget constraints. This additional tier recognizes a segment of consumers who maintain payment discipline despite financial pressure and offers relief mechanisms to prevent future defaults. The expansion reflects government recognition of middle-income household financial stress and aims to prevent deterioration in credit quality.
