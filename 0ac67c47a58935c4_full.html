Camila Escobar Steps Down as Procafecol President After Eight Years Leading Juan Valdez Expansion

15 April 2026 · 09:46 PM

Procafecol announced that Camila Escobar will step down as president and chief executive officer (CEO), a position she has held since 2018. According to the company, the decision reflects a personal determination by the executive after completing a period focused on transforming the organization and strengthening the international positioning of the Juan Valdez brand.

Escobar presented her resignation to the Board of Directors on April 15, 2026, and it will take effect as of April 30. According to the company, the executive will take on a new professional challenge in an organization focused on promoting and developing talent in the country.

During her tenure, the company highlighted advances in consolidating its global presence and the strategic evolution of the business. These results, according to Procafecol, marked a key stage in the brand's development and its positioning in international markets.

The Board of Directors accepted the resignation and expressed recognition for the work performed during the years Escobar led the organization. In its statement, the company highlighted her contribution to both the company and the coffee sector.

As part of the transition process, the company appointed Carlos Arturo Azuero Perdomo as acting president and chief executive officer. Azuero currently serves as president and member of Procafecol's Board of Directors, which will allow for continuity of management while the leadership succession is defined. The appointment seeks to ensure the company's operational stability during this period while strategic decisions related to the organization's future leadership are made.

In parallel, Procafecol announced that it will initiate a structured process for selecting the new president and chief executive officer. This process will be supported by specialized advisors and will be developed under criteria of transparency, according to the company. The goal is to identify the profile that will assume leadership in a new stage for the company, in which it is expected to give continuity to the implemented strategies and face market challenges. The company indicated that updates and results from this process will be communicated in a timely manner.
