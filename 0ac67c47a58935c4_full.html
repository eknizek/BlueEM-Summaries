# Camila Escobar Steps Down as President of Procafecol After Eight Years Leading Juan Valdez Expansion

Procafecol announced that Camila Escobar will step down from her position as Chief Executive Officer (CEO), a role she has held since 2018. The decision, according to the company, responds to a personal determination by the executive after completing a stage focused on the transformation of the organization and strengthening the international positioning of the Juan Valdez brand.

The departure was presented by Escobar to the Board of Directors on April 15, 2026, and will take effect starting April 30. According to the company, the executive will assume a new professional challenge in an organization focused on the promotion and training of talent in the country.

During her tenure, the company highlighted advances in consolidating its global presence as well as the strategic evolution of the business. These results, according to Procafecol, marked a key stage in the brand's development and its positioning in international markets.

The Board of Directors accepted the resignation and expressed recognition for the work performed during the years Escobar led the organization. In a statement, the company highlighted her contribution to both the company and the coffee sector.

As part of the transition process, the company appointed Carlos Arturo Azuero Perdomo as interim chief executive officer. Azuero currently serves as president and member of the Board of Directors of Procafecol, which will allow continuity in management while the leadership transition is defined. This appointment aims to ensure the company's operational stability during this period while strategic decisions regarding future leadership of the organization are made.

In parallel, Procafecol announced that it will initiate a structured process for selecting the new chief executive officer. This process will be accompanied by specialized advisors and will be developed under criteria of transparency, according to the company. The objective is to identify the profile that will assume leadership in a new stage for the company, in which it is expected to continue with implemented strategies and face market challenges. The company indicated that advances and results of this process will be communicated in a timely manner.

Camila Escobar is an industrial engineer with a master's degree in administration.
