Court Grants Oncoclínicas Request to Suspend Bank Charges for 60 Days

16 April 2026 · 10:25 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's São Paulo Court of Justice approved Oncoclínicas' emergency request to suspend bank charges for 60 days following the company's disclosure of R$3.3 billion in debt, exceeding the limits established with its creditors. The oncology company faces significant debt distress and sought judicial relief to manage liquidity pressures. This financial distress represents a critical moment for the healthcare provider and its creditors.
