# Exchange rate: dollar closes at S/3.427 on Tuesday, April 7, 2026

The price of the dollar closed at S/3.427 for Tuesday, April 7, 2026, according to the Central Reserve Bank (BCR). The currency retreated 10 basis points compared to Monday's close, which ended at S/3.428.

It should be noted that as hours pass, the dollar can vary due to various factors such as supply and demand in the market, decisions by the U.S. Federal Reserve, commodity prices, inflation, the performance of the global economy, and the political situation.

During the trading day, supply came from non-resident companies. In the market, US$122 million was traded at an average price of S/3.4353. The dollar had a minimum level of S/3.423 and a maximum level of S/3.440.

Additionally, the ISM non-manufacturing PMI stood at 54.0 in March, below expectations and the previous figure, showing a moderation in the services sector growth, although it still remains in the expansion zone. Meanwhile, the ISM non-manufacturing price index rose to 70.7, exceeding forecasts and the previous data, which shows an increase in costs within the sector and possible inflationary pressures.
