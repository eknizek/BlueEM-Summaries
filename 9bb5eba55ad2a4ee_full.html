# Bradesco (BBDC3;BBDC4), Itaú (ITUB3;ITUB4) and 7 Other Companies Pay Dividends This Week; See Calendar

By Anna Scabello

May 3, 2026, 1:00 PM - Updated April 30, 2026, 1:30 PM

During the week of May 4-8, nine companies listed on the Brazilian stock exchange will pay dividends and interest on equity (JCP) to their shareholders.

On Monday (4), Bradesco will pay JCP of R$ 0.017 for BBDC3 and R$ 0.019 for BBDC4, both with a cutoff date of April 1 of this year. Also on Monday, Itaú Unibanco will pay JCP of R$ 0.018 for its ordinary shares (ITUB3) and preferred shares (ITUB4), based on shareholders positioned on March 31, 2026.
