Spanish Prima developers attract Bradesco in R$545 million project

16 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Prima, a Spanish real estate developer, has attracted investment from Bradesco (Brazil's largest private bank) in a R$545 million project in northern Bahia state. Prima has already announced R$5.6 billion committed to the Bahia development and holds an additional R$1.2 billion in projects under development and retrofit in Rio de Janeiro. This partnership demonstrates significant foreign direct investment and institutional banking support flowing into northeastern Brazilian real estate and infrastructure, highlighting the region's attractiveness to international developers and domestic institutional capital.
