Spanish Prima Developers Attract Bradesco in R$545 Million Project

16 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Spanish real estate developers Prima have attracted Bradesco (Brazil's second-largest bank) as an investor in a R$545 million commercial or residential development project in Bahia state. The project has already attracted R$5.6 billion in investor commitments, with an additional R$1.2 billion earmarked for property development and retrofit activities in Rio de Janeiro. This partnership underscores significant capital flows into Brazilian real estate development.
