18 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Cimed, a Brazilian pharmaceutical company, is entering e-commerce with Urso, a digitally-native supplement brand launching with 32 SKUs (stock keeping units) targeted for mid-year release. The move reflects Cimed's expansion beyond traditional pharmaceutical products into the growing consumer health segment. This launch aligns with the company's broader portfolio diversification strategy in the consumer goods space.
