Sucre: 50 years the La Mojana community waited for a bridge benefiting 15,000 people and reactivating rural economy

04 May 2026 · 09:04 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
A long-awaited infrastructure bridge project in La Mojana, Sucre department, has been completed after 50 years of community expectation. The bridge benefits approximately 15,000 people and is expected to significantly reactivate the local agricultural and rural economy by improving connectivity. The project represents a major win for rural infrastructure development in Colombia. Economic activity in the region should improve with enhanced road connectivity to larger markets.
