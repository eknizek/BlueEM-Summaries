Caja Cusco Shareholder Assembly Unanimously Approves BID Invest as Strategic Partner

04 May 2026 · 05:37 PM  ·  Company press release
Caja Cusco, a Peruvian microfinance institution, received unanimous shareholder approval for BID Invest (the private arm of the Inter-American Development Bank) to acquire a 7.5% minority stake. The partnership aims to strengthen Caja Cusco's equity base and expand its financing capabilities in underserved markets. This strategic investment by a multilateral development institution signals confidence in the institution's business model and growth prospects, while providing Caja Cusco with enhanced capital and expertise.
