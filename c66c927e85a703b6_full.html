# Integratel Registers 156% EBITDA Increase in First Quarter

Integratel Perú, which this month marks one year since acquiring Telefónica del Perú, reported that it registered EBITDA (earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization) of S/215 million in the first quarter of 2026, in the presentation of its quarterly financial statements. This represents growth of 156% compared to the same period in 2025.

According to the company, the results are due to the new management model implemented following the purchase of Telefónica del Perú.

In these first three months, efficiencies of S/98 million have been achieved and a year-over-year expense reduction of 24%. It is worth noting that during 2025, the company had achieved efficiencies of S/229 million. This year the company seeks to ensure growth is accompanied by profitability, as well as improving its customer service experience.

On the other hand, Integratel Perú's net profit registered a negative result of S/154 million due to the impact of depreciation and amortization that the company faced. For the company, this result represents an improvement of S/153 million versus the first quarter of 2025.

Additionally, it reported that the profit margin on revenues was 18%, compared to 7.5% at the close of 2024, prior to the company's acquisition.

## Paid TV

Within Integratel's operations, the paid TV service registered a 15% increase in sales to new customers compared to the fourth quarter of 2025. This is explained by the incorporation of Liga 1 broadcasting through the Movistar brand. Added to this are the benefits granted to its customers through sponsorships of clubs.

Thus, it aims to provide a better experience and benefits, and promote events such as Multiteatro Movistar and concerts. This is also reflected in 16% growth in customer retention in the company. Club Movistar benefits include invitations to six concerts with top-level artists.
