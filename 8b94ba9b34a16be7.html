Nubank to Invest R$ 45 Billion in Brazil in 2026

27 April 2026 · 01:59 PM  ·  Business Wire
Nubank, Latin America's leading fintech bank, announced plans to invest R$ 45 billion (approximately USD 8.5 billion) in Brazil during 2026. This substantial capital commitment underscores the company's strategy to deepen its market presence and expand digital banking services across Brazil. The investment is expected to support product development, customer acquisition, and infrastructure improvements. This move reflects Nubank's confidence in the Brazilian market and its long-term growth trajectory as a digital-first financial institution.
