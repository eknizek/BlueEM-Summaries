Powerpay seeks to consolidate 'Buy Now Pay Later' model in Peru: "We were born to meet a need that banking didn't cover"

01 May 2026 · 10:18 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Powerpay, a fintech subsidiary within BBVA's group, is positioning itself as a leader in Peru's Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) market, targeting consumer segments—particularly health, technology, and education—that traditional banking has underserved. CEO Rafael Hospina emphasized that 2026 is a consolidation year, with the company expanding across both credit and debit card user bases and penetrating new merchant verticals. This growth reflects the rising adoption of flexible payment solutions in Peru's middle-market consumer base.
