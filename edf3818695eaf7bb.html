Colombia's April inflation resists decline, expected near 5.7% as central bank faces pressure

07 May 2026 · 11:58 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's inflation is projected to accelerate in April to approximately 5.7%, defying expectations for a continued downward trend. The acceleration is attributed to rising food prices, fuel costs, and service inflation. This development places pressure on the Banco de la República as it navigates monetary policy amid persistent price pressures, complicating efforts to return inflation to target levels.
