Banks see Uruguay as financial hub and 'Singapore of Latin America'

18 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Banks are actively promoting Uruguay as a financial services hub and regional technology center, drawing comparisons to Singapore's role in Asia. The strategy capitalizes on Uruguay's political stability, favorable regulatory environment, and geographic position in Latin America. Financial institutions are investing in fintech infrastructure and wealth management capabilities to attract regional and international capital. This positioning reflects broader trends of financial decentralization within Latin America and growing competition between regional financial centers.
