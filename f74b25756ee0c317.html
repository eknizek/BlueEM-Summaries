Eurofarma and Novo Nordisk partnership in the weight-loss drug market battle

20 April 2026 · 06:58 PM  ·  Neofeed
Novo Nordisk's semaglutide patent expired in Brazil on March 20, removing exclusivity for Ozempic and Wegovy weight-loss injectables. Despite failed legal efforts to extend protection, Novo Nordisk partnered with Brazilian pharma Eurofarma to compete in the imminent generic/biosimilar market. The partnership aims to establish market position ahead of competitive entries, reflecting the high-value opportunity in Brazil's obesity drug market.
