Trafigura Prepares Entry into Energy Trading in Brazil

23 April 2026 · 06:42 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Trafigura, one of the world's largest commodity trading houses based in Singapore, is building an electricity trading operation in Brazil. The move brings a major new player into Brazil's energy trading market at a time of significant volatility and market disruption, with many independent traders exiting or consolidating. Trafigura's entry signals confidence in Brazil's liberalized power market and represents a strategic diversification for the global commodities trader. The operation is expected to compete in physical and financial energy derivatives trading across Brazil's interconnected system.
