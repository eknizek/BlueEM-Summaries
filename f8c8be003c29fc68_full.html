# Indecopi Sanctions Rimac Seguros and Orders Indemnity Payment for Disability

In second administrative instance, the Commission of the Indecopi Regional Office in Lambayeque sanctioned Rimac Seguros y Reaseguros for failing to make timely payment of the mandatory traffic accident insurance (SOAT) indemnity for permanent disability to a consumer, in violation of article 19 of the Consumer Protection and Defense Code.

As a result, Indecopi imposed a fine of 3.49 tax units (UIT), equivalent to S/19,195.

The case originated following a traffic accident that occurred on January 9, 2023, involving the insured vehicle in which the affected party was traveling. The consumer requested the indemnity on January 8, 2025; however, the company failed to make payment within the legal period of 10 days following the request.

During the proceedings, the insurance company argued that on January 17, 2025, it communicated via email the referral of the case to the National Rehabilitation Institute (INR) for evaluation.

However, the Commission concluded that this communication was invalid, since the company's virtual system conditions the processing of the request to the mandatory registration of an email address and does not allow consumers free choice of the means by which they wish to be notified.

As a corrective measure, Indecopi ordered the company to pay the complainant, within a maximum period of 15 business days, the sum of S/10,700 for indemnity for 50% permanent disability. Additionally, it must certify compliance with this obligation before the corresponding authority, under penalty of imposing a coercive fine of 3 UIT in case of non-compliance.
