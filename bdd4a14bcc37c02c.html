Grupo Aeroportuario del Pacifico reports 1% passenger traffic growth in September

04 April 2026 · 07:36 AM  ·  msn.com
Grupo Aeroportuario del Pacifico (GAP), Mexico's second-largest airport operator, reported a modest 1% increase in passenger traffic for September compared to the prior year. The figure reflects ongoing travel demand in Mexico's Pacific region but suggests a slowdown from earlier 2024 momentum. As a key infrastructure play and dividend-paying REIT, GAP's traffic trends are closely watched by LatAm income and infrastructure investors as bellwethers of economic activity and tourism in Mexico.
