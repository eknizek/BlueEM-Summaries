# 20% of Gas Stations Breach Diesel Price Agreement

One in five gas stations in the country is failing to comply with the voluntary agreement to sell diesel below 28 pesos per liter, according to monitoring by the Federal Consumer Protection Agency (Profeco).

Prosecutor Escalante spoke at a press conference Monday at the National Palace, noting that compliance with the agreed price has not been universal across all participating establishments.
