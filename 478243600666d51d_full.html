# Imagen Insurance Summit 2026: Urgent Need to Boost Insurance Culture in Mexico

The 2026 edition of the Imagen Insurance Summit was held at Ciudad Imagen facilities, where industry specialists recognized the need to promote greater insurance culture so that customers view insurance as an investment tool for life and not as an expense. Additionally, they discussed the need to find new ways to communicate benefits and leverage artificial intelligence as a marketing ally.

During the first panel titled "Communication and Marketing Tools for Insurance Access," Fabiola Dosal Via, Head of Product and Customer Proposition at HSBC Life, and Juan José Anduaga, Director of Innovation and Strategic Alliances at Dentalia, spoke with Crystal Mendivil about the value that society should place on protecting health and material assets through insurance.

## "The Word Insurance Frightens People"

Fabiola Dosal Via, Head of Product and Customer Proposition at HSBC Life, expressed that "the word insurance frightens people; it is seen as an expense or mere transaction. What we should seek is for financial advisors to truly achieve wealth planning with clients and provide support during all key moments in the lives of our clients and their families."

Juan José Anduaga, Director of Innovation and Strategic Alliances at Dentalia, commented that "undoubtedly, the strategy is to turn something invisible into something very visible. This strategy is divided into three axes: generating tangibility—when you have products and benefits that can have frequent use, that gives tangibility to different products; another is making it simple—access must be quite simple; and the other is that usage is focused on something that generates added value, such as prevention."

Gerardo Rebollar Fernández, CEO of Hylant México, also participated in this first panel, speaking about the advantages that artificial intelligence offers for developing communication tools to spread insurance culture.
