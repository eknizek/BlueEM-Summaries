# Imagen Insurance Summit 2026: Urgent Need to Boost Insurance Culture in Mexico

The 2026 edition of the Imagen Insurance Summit took place at Ciudad Imagen facilities, where industry specialists recognized the need to promote a greater insurance culture so that customers view insurance as an investment tool in their lives rather than an expense. Additionally, they discussed achieving new ways to communicate benefits and the use of artificial intelligence as a marketing ally.

During the first panel on Communication and Marketing Tools for Insurance Access, Fabiola Dosal Via, Head of Product and Customer Proposition at HSBC Life, and Juan José Anduaga, Director of Innovation and Strategic Alliances at Dentalia, spoke with Crystal Mendivil about the value that society should place on protecting health or material goods through insurance.

## "The Word Insurance Frightens"

Fabiola Dosal Via, head of Product and Customer Proposition at HSBC Life, expressed that "the word insurance frightens people; it is seen as an expense or mere transaction. What we should seek is that through financial advisors we truly achieve wealth planning with clients and provide support during all the key moments in the lives of our clients and their families."

Juan José Anduaga, Director of Innovation and Strategic Alliances at Dentalia, commented that "undoubtedly, the strategy is to turn something invisible into something very visible. This strategy is divided into three axes: generating tangibility—when you have products and benefits that can have frequent use, that gives tangibility to different products; another is making it simple, access must be quite simple; and the other is that the use is focused on something that generates added value for you, such as prevention."

Gerardo Rebollar Fernández, CEO of Hylant México, also participated in this first panel, discussing the advantages that artificial intelligence offers for developing communication tools to disseminate insurance culture.
