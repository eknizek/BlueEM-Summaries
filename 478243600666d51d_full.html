# Imagen Insurance Summit 2026: Urgent Need to Drive Insurance Culture in Mexico

The 2026 edition of the Imagen Insurance Summit was held at Ciudad Imagen facilities, where industry specialists acknowledged the need to promote a stronger insurance culture so that customers view insurance as a life investment tool rather than an expense. They also discussed new ways to communicate benefits and the use of artificial intelligence as a marketing ally.

During the first panel titled "Communication and Marketing Tools for Insurance Access," Fabiola Dosal Via, Head of Product and Customer Proposition at HSBC Life, and Juan José Anduaga, Director of Innovation and Strategic Alliances at Dentalia, discussed with Crystal Mendivil the value that society should place on protecting health and material goods through insurance.

## "The Word Insurance Frightens"

Fabiola Dosal Via, Head of Product and Customer Proposition at HSBC Life, expressed that "the word insurance frightens people; it's seen as an expense or mere transaction. What we should seek is for financial advisors to truly achieve wealth planning with clients and provide support during all key moments in the lives of our clients and their families."

Juan José Anduaga, Director of Innovation and Strategic Alliances at Dentalia, commented that "without a doubt, the strategy is to turn something invisible into something very visible. This strategy is divided into three axes: creating tangibility—when you have products and benefits that can be used frequently, that gives tangibility to the different products; another is making it simple—access must be quite simple; and the other is that use is focused on something that generates added value for you, such as prevention."

The first panel also included Gerardo Rebollar Fernández, CEO of Hylant México, who discussed the advantages offered by artificial intelligence in developing communication tools to promote insurance culture.
