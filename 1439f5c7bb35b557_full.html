Australia Purchases Crude Oil from Mexico and the United States

06 April 2026 · 09:49 PM

Australia is turning to the United States and Mexico to obtain fuel and compensate for supply interruptions from its traditional Asian partners, according to Australian Energy Minister Chris Bowen, as the country works to mitigate shortages caused by the war in Iran.

The Australian Energy Minister said that the fuel is already on its way, orders are confirmed, and that is positive.
