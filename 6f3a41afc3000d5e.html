Oncoclínicas advances in restructuring as Porto and Fleury exit, eyes capital raise

14 April 2026 · 06:08 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazilian oncology services provider Oncoclínicas ended its exclusive M&A negotiations with Porto and Fleury and filed for judicial restructuring on April 14, signaling a shift in turnaround strategy. The company now plans to pursue independent capital raising to address financial distress rather than proceed with a sale to healthcare peers. This restructuring marks a critical juncture for the company as it navigates its financial challenges through court-supervised recovery rather than acquisition.
