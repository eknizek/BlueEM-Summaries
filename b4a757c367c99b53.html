Civil Defense Will Have Eight New Weather Radar Systems to Act on Prevention of Extreme Climate Events

14 April 2026 · 05:21 PM  ·  O GLOBO
Brazil's Civil Defense agency will deploy eight additional meteorological radar systems to enhance monitoring and prevention capabilities for extreme weather events. This infrastructure investment supports climate risk management and disaster preparedness at the national level. The initiative has implications for public safety, insurance risk assessment, and potentially for infrastructure and real estate investment strategies in climate-vulnerable regions. The investment reflects growing focus on climate adaptation in Brazil.
