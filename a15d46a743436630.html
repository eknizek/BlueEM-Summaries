Michoacan Avocado and Tabasco Cocoa Granted Protected Designation of Origin

04 May 2026 · 11:36 PM  ·  Reforma - Portada
Mexico's National Institute of Industrial Property (IMPI) has granted Protected Geographic Indication (PGI) status to 'Aguacate Franja Michoacán' (Michoacan Franja Avocado) and 'Cacao de la Región Chontalpa de Tabasco' (Chontalpa Tabasco Cocoa). These designations protect the geographical and quality standards associated with these regionally-specific agricultural products, enhancing their market value and supporting premium positioning. The move strengthens intellectual property protections for Mexican agricultural exports and helps producers command higher prices for certified origin products.
