# Kallpa Begins Commercial Operation of Sunny Expansion

Kallpa Generación announced that the Economic Operation Committee of the National Interconnected System, COES, authorized the start of commercial operation of Sunny Expansion as of 00:00 on April 3, 2026.

In October of last year, the Sunny Photovoltaic Solar Plant had entered commercial operation, located in La Joya, Arequipa, which, as of this date, together with Sunny Expansion, increases its capacity to 345 MW.

The consolidation of this project will allow Kallpa to continue generating safe, efficient and competitive energy, in accordance with the country's needs and the signals provided by the market.

It should be noted that Kallpa Generación is an electrical generation company that operates a diversified portfolio in Peru. The company has 2.6 GW of installed capacity, which supplies 22% of the demand in the Peruvian electrical market.
