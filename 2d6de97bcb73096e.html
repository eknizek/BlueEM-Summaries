Investors react negatively to Ecopetrol's offer for Brava Energia, citing value destruction risk

24 April 2026 · 03:31 PM  ·  Neofeed
Ecopetrol announced an agreement to acquire 26% of independent Brazilian oil company Brava Energia for R$2.8 billion and launch a public offer to reach 51% control. However, investors reacted negatively to the deal, with Brava's stock falling 3.87% on concerns about potential value destruction. The market skepticism reflects investor doubts about the strategic and financial rationale for Ecopetrol's takeover bid.
