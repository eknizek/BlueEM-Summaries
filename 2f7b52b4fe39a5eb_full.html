# Ecopetrol in Final Stages to Enter Brava; Prepares Partial Tender Offer

After months of negotiations, Colombian company Ecopetrol is finalizing an agreement to purchase a stake in Brava Energia, according to three sources with knowledge of the matter. The conversations are directly with selling shareholders and do not involve the company's management. The negotiations were first revealed by Pipeline in December. During this period, in addition to a price dispute per share, the outbreak of war in Iran affected oil prices, slowing down the negotiation — but conversations resumed with momentum in recent weeks.

The Colombian company had initially proposed R$ 21 per share, which shareholders with appetite for selling found too low, and raised the offer to R$ 23 at the beginning of the month. The shareholders were trying to push for R$ 24 in the private block.

Once the block purchase is completed, the company is expected to proceed with a partial tender offer, targeting 50% plus one share, Pipeline found out.
