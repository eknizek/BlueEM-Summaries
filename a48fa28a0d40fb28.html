Aneel Announces Yellow Flag for May, and Electricity Bills Will Have Extra Charges for the First Time in the Year

24 April 2026 · 10:07 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's National Electric Energy Agency (Aneel) announced a yellow tariff flag for May 2026, marking the first additional charge to electricity bills in the year. The flag was triggered by reduced rainfall during the transition from the wet to dry season, affecting hydroelectric generation capacity. Consumers will pay an additional R$1.885 per 100 kWh consumed, impacting utility revenues and operating costs across the Brazilian economy.
