Brazil announces yellow tariff flag for May, electricity bills to charge extra fees for first time this year

24 April 2026 · 10:07 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's National Electric Energy Agency (Aneel) announced the yellow tariff flag for May 2026, marking the first additional charge on electricity bills in 2026. The flag was triggered by reduced rainfall during the transition from rainy to dry season, straining hydroelectric generation capacity. This adds R$1.89 to consumers' bills per 100 kWh consumed, indicating tightening electricity supply conditions and inflationary pressure on utility costs.
