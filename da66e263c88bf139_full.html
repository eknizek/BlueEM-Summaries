Fires at Businesses, Shootouts and Narco Blockades Reported in Nayarit Following Arrest

Business fires, shootouts and narco blockades are being reported in different areas of Nayarit following the arrest of Audias Flores Silva, "El Jardinero," one of the leaders of the Jalisco New Generation Cartel (CJNG) after the death of Nemesio Oseguera, "El Mencho."

One of the zones that has registered the most attacks is Tecuala, in northern Nayarit, near Mazatlán, Sinaloa.
