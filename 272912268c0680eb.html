Rio Negotiating with Turkish and Chinese Partners for Direct International Flights at Galeão

26 April 2026 · 03:10 AM  ·  BrazilJournal
Rio de Janeiro's municipal investment agency (Invest.Rio) is negotiating with Turkish and Chinese partners to establish direct international flights at Galeão International Airport, with Istanbul and China identified as the highest-priority routes. The expansion is framed as a strategic priority to boost the airport's international connectivity and attract investment to the city. These negotiations signal growing infrastructure investment in Brazil's aviation sector and potential private concession opportunities.
