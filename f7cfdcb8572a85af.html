Peru Launches New Equities Platform as Stock Exchange Integration with Chile and Colombia Advances

27 April 2026 · 11:58 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru has inaugurated a new equities trading platform as part of its broader initiative to integrate its stock exchange with those of Chile and Colombia. The platform, operated by Nuam, a holding company unifying the regional exchanges, recorded a 19.7% increase in trading volume on its first day of operations compared to the previous Friday. The trilateral integration aims to create a more liquid and efficient capital market across the Andean region, enhancing cross-border investment opportunities. This represents a significant infrastructure upgrade for Peru's capital markets and regional financial integration.
