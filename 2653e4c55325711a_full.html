# Energy Crisis Catches Pemex Off Guard

Pemex has been unable to take advantage of high oil prices resulting from the Middle East conflict because it is "caught off guard" due to declining production and exports, according to a panel of economic analysts from Banamex.

The directors of Economic Studies at Banamex, Iván Arias and Sergio Kurczyn, warned yesterday that the damage from the global energy crisis will be greater due to Pemex's vulnerability.
