One by one, the national routes authorized to collect tolls following the Government decree

17 April 2026 · 09:38 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Argentina's government has authorized toll collection on national routes across nine provinces (Córdoba, Santa Fe, Río Negro, Neuquén, Santa Cruz, Corrientes, Mendoza, San Luis, and San Juan) following bilateral agreements between national authorities and provincial governors. This policy represents a shift toward private or concessionary infrastructure financing and maintenance models. The measure aims to improve road maintenance and fund transportation infrastructure while distributing implementation across the country's federal structure.
