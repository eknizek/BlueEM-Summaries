Petro-Perú Purchases Nearly 500,000 Barrels from US Strategic Petroleum Reserve

07 April 2026 · 07:37 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Petro-Perú, Peru's state-owned refinery, has closed a major contract to purchase approximately 500,000 barrels of 'Bayou Choctaw' crude oil from the US government's Strategic Petroleum Reserve in Louisiana, according to Bloomberg reporting. This purchase enhances Peru's crude sourcing optionality and reflects strategic engagement with non-traditional suppliers to secure refinery feedstock. For LatAm energy investors and portfolio companies dependent on domestic fuel supply, this signals positive signs for Peru's refining sector and energy security amid global commodity price volatility.
