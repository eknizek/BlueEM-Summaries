Petro-Perú purchases nearly half a million barrels of crude from US emergency reserve

07 April 2026 · 07:37 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Petro-Perú, Peru's state-owned refiner, has closed a contract to purchase approximately 500,000 barrels of Bayou Choctaw crude oil, typically held as part of the US Strategic Petroleum Reserve in Louisiana. This procurement demonstrates Petro-Perú's ability to access strategic US oil reserves and reflects supply chain diversification efforts for Peru's refining operations. The transaction has implications for Peru's energy security, refinery operations, and downstream petroleum supply management in an increasingly volatile global oil market.
