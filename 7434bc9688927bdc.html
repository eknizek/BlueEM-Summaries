Construction Chamber: Argentina loses $25 billion annually due to infrastructure maintenance failures

20 April 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Argentina's Construction Chamber, via its Federal Council, stated that inadequate infrastructure maintenance costs the nation $25 billion annually. The organization called for expanded concessions covering 30,000 km of non-toll roads currently neglected, and demanded the national government settle outstanding debts to construction companies. Infrastructure underinvestment has become a structural drag on economic competitiveness. The appeal underscores the financing gap between politically-viable infrastructure spending and actual maintenance needs.
