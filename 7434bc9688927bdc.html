Construction Chamber: Argentina loses $25 billion annually due to infrastructure maintenance failures

20 April 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's Construction Chamber warned that inadequate infrastructure maintenance costs the country $25 billion annually. The Federal Council called for investment attention beyond concession highways to include 30,000 kilometers of non-concessioned routes in poor condition. The chamber also pressed the government to settle outstanding debts owed to construction companies. This assessment highlights Argentina's infrastructure deficit and its drag on economic productivity.
