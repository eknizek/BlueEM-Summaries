Lima Chamber of Commerce Projects Up to 7% Growth in Peruvian Entertainment Sector in 2026 and Record Events

21 April 2026 · 09:31 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
The Lima Chamber of Commerce (CCL) forecasts robust growth in Peru's entertainment and events sector for 2026, with expansion reaching up to 7%. The projection is underpinned by a record calendar of concerts and live performances, combined with increasing consumer spending on entertainment experiences. This growth reflects broader consumer confidence and discretionary spending trends in Peru's economy. The sector expansion could benefit hospitality, venue operators, and ancillary service providers.
