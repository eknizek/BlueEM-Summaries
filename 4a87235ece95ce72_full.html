Violence in Southern Mexico Impacts Construction and Tourism Growth

07 April 2026 · 11:00 AM

Violence in Southern Mexico

Construction and tourism in the southern part of the country fell 42.9 and 2.3 percent annually in the fourth quarter of 2025 due to high levels of insecurity in the region and budget underspending, according to the latest data from the Bank of Mexico (Banxico).

In recent months, the Municipality of El Salto has registered various acts of violence.
