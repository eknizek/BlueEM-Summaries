Madero invests in 'empanada factory' and reinforces delivery strategy

29 April 2026 · 09:21 PM  ·  Neofeed
Madero, a Brazilian restaurant chain led by Junior Durski, is expanding its empanada production capacity with dedicated manufacturing facilities. The group currently produces approximately 280,000 empanadas monthly but faces demand exceeding supply. Empanadas have emerged as a profitable menu item contributing significantly to Q1 2026 results, prompting the chain to allocate capital toward scaling production and reinforcing its delivery-focused business model.
