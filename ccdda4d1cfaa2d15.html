Mercado Libre Announces Strategic Investment in Gradient Capital Fund to Accelerate AI Innovation Across Latin America

20 April 2026 · 07:04 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Mercado Libre announced a strategic commitment as a Limited Partner in Gradient Capital Fund 5, a Silicon Valley-based early-stage venture capital fund specializing in applied artificial intelligence. The investment reflects Mercado Libre's focus on accelerating AI-driven innovation across Latin America and positioning the region at the forefront of technology adoption. This strategic partnership enables Mercado Libre to access promising AI startups and emerging technologies while supporting ecosystem development. The move demonstrates Mercado Libre's commitment to long-term regional technology advancement beyond its core e-commerce operations.
