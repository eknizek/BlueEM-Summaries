# Annuity Sales Hit Record in Q1 as Penta Leads Expansion

Everything points to the record numbers registered in the annuity business during last year continuing with this trend in 2026. The sale of this pension insurance offered by life insurance companies increased 45% between January and March of this fiscal year, reaching 18,377 policies—a figure never before seen for the same period, according to figures from the Financial Market Commission (CMF).

In this way, in just the first three months of this year, 31% of all annuities sold in 2025 were already commercialized.

"Annuities have had an extremely dynamic start to the year, consolidating the 2025 trend. The tiered annuity modality has allowed insurers to structure a more complete and attractive commercial offer, making this pension modality competitive against programmed withdrawals, allowing them to capture a growing volume of business," explained José Ampuero, partner at Actuarial Consulting, to DF.

To this, the expert added, was coupled the reduction in the minimum pension threshold for contracting an annuity, which democratized access to it. It should be recalled that the 2025 pension reform flexibilized the minimum requirements for contracting an annuity, setting as a requirement financing a pension of at least UF 2 ($ 79,868). Previously, the amount was UF 3.

## Délano-Lavín Duo Leads Growth

The insurance company that has benefited most from the high tide in annuity sales is Penta Vida, linked to businessmen Carlos Alberto Délano and Carlos Eugenio Lavín. Between January and March 2026, the life insurance company completed 4,123 sales, which added to the 13,019 it commercialized in 2025, leading the industry in that period, ahead of other companies that dominate the business, such as Confuturo, Consorcio, and MetLife.

"During 2025, the company maintained the strengthening of its main lines of business through efficient management focused on service. This approach allowed for solid performance in annuities, with 13,019 new retirees, along with important advances in digitalization and customer experience," the company stated in its institutional report for last year.

Considering the figures from the Association of Insurers regarding people who opted for annuities, Penta Vida closed last year with a 12.7% market share, while in 2024 the figure was 10.5%.
