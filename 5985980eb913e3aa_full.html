# Annuity Sales Hit Record in First Quarter with Penta Leading Expansion

All signs point to the record numbers registered in the annuity business last year continuing this trend in 2026. Sales of this pension insurance offered by life insurance companies increased 45% between January and March of the current fiscal year, reaching 18,377 policies—a figure never before seen for the same period, according to data from the Financial Market Commission (CMF).

In this way, in just the first three months of this year, 31% of all policies commercialized in 2025 were already sold.

"Annuities have had an extremely dynamic start to the year, consolidating the 2025 trend. The tiered annuities modality has allowed insurers to structure a more complete and attractive commercial offering, making this pension modality competitive against programmed withdrawals, allowing them to capture increasing business volumes," José Ampuero, partner at Actuarial Consulting, explained to DF.

The expert also noted that this was complemented by a reduction in the minimum pension threshold required to contract an annuity, which expanded access. It should be recalled that the 2025 pension reform relaxed the minimum requirements for contracting an annuity by setting the requirement of financing a pension of at least UF 2 (approximately $79,868). Previously, the amount was UF 3.

## Délano-Lavín Duo Leads Growth

The insurance company that has benefited most from the surge in annuity sales is Penta Vida, linked to businessmen Carlos Alberto Délano and Carlos Eugenio Lavín. Between January and March 2026, the life insurance company completed 4,123 sales, which added to the 13,019 it commercialized in 2025, leading the industry during that period, ahead of other companies that dominate the business, such as Confuturo, Consorcio, and MetLife.

"During 2025, the company maintained the strengthening of its main business lines through efficient management focused on service. This approach allowed us to achieve solid performance in annuities, with 13,019 new retirees, along with significant advances in digitalization and customer experience," the company stated in its institutional report for last year.

Considering figures from the Insurance Association regarding people who opted for annuities, Penta Vida closed last year with a 12.7% market share, while in 2024 the figure was 10.5%.
