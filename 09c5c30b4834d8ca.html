Log Gains Momentum for Expansion Plan After Sale of 11 Warehouses to Itaú

04 May 2026 · 09:10 PM  ·  Neofeed
Log, the logistics warehouse company controlled by the Menin family, announced the sale of 11 warehouses to Itaú, generating proceeds that will support the company's aggressive expansion plan. The transaction, announced alongside the company's financial results, demonstrates Log's ability to monetize assets while maintaining operational control through long-term leaseback arrangements. The capital raised from the real estate sale provides financial firepower for the company to pursue growth opportunities in Brazil's booming logistics and e-commerce sectors. This asset-light strategy allows Log to expand its warehouse footprint without requiring additional debt or equity capital.
