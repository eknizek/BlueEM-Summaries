Sequoia sells mega sorter to Mercado Libre and exits B2C market

22 April 2026 · 10:29 AM  ·  Neofeed
Sequoia Logística announced the sale of its mega sorter facility to Mercado Libre for R$40 million, marking its exit from business-to-consumer (B2C) e-commerce logistics. The facility, located in São Bernardo, São Paulo, will continue operating under the Mercado Libre platform. This transaction reflects Sequoia's strategic repositioning away from the competitive B2C logistics market. The sale includes related warehouse lease agreements and represents a significant asset divestment as the company refocuses its operations.
