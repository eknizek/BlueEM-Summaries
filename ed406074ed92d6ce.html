AI will reshape corporate hierarchy and eliminate 'middle management'

07 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Block Inc. chairman and Square co-founder Jack Dorsey contends that artificial intelligence represents a paradigm shift in corporate organization, not merely incremental productivity gains. Most enterprises currently deploy AI as a productivity tool within existing hierarchies; Dorsey argues the technology will eventually dismantle centuries-old middle-management structures. This structural transformation has profound implications for operational cost models, talent strategy, and organizational efficiency in enterprise software and fintech—sectors critical to Latin American digital transformation and operating leverage in tech-driven businesses.
