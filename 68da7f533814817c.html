Government and Private Sector Agree to Cap Diesel Price at $28.30

20 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Mexico's government and the private sector (IP) have reached an agreement to cap diesel prices at 28.30 pesos per liter as part of broader inflation mitigation efforts. The measure complements existing price controls maintaining regular gasoline below 24 pesos per liter. These price ceilings aim to cushion consumers and businesses from global energy price volatility while supporting macroeconomic stability and consumption. The policy carries fiscal costs and signals continued government intervention in energy markets.
