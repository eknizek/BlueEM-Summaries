# Colombia's SIC Launches Investigation Into D1 for Payment Failures, Defective Products, and Electronic Channel Irregularities Following Thousands of Consumer Complaints

The Superintendency of Industry and Commerce (SIC) announced on Thursday, April 9, the opening of an investigation against Tiendas D1 for alleged violation of consumer rights in its e-commerce channels. This follows the receipt of approximately 13,000 customer complaints against the well-known retail store.

According to the Superintendency, alleged irregularities were identified in the "commercialization of products and provision of services" in digital channels. First, the SIC detected failures in electronic payment systems, as there were allegedly double charges and problems with the proper completion of transactions on the shopping portal.

The entity also reported the delivery of defective or expired products, having received at least 700 complaints for "possible failures to meet quality and suitability standards." There were also failures in order delivery in at least 1,200 cases. According to the SIC, customers reported incomplete, late, undelivered, or canceled deliveries.

Additionally, the Superintendency pointed to the existence of allegedly misleading advertising by D1, as products were offered at prices or discounts that did not correspond to the charges subsequently applied to customers. According to the SIC, this could mislead consumers.

Finally, the entity denounced the omission of essential information in promotions for "not completely informing relevant conditions such as offer restrictions or unit availability."

The SIC adopted the decision to open the investigation against D1 after analyzing approximately 13,000 complaints filed during the second half of 2024 and following information requests and preliminary documentation review provided by the stores in response to authorities' requirements. The Superintendency stated that opening the investigation and formulating charges against D1 does not constitute a final decision on its responsibility and assured that the right to defense of the renowned company will be guaranteed during the process.
