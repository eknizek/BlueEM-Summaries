# Who is Carlos Mario Giraldo, the Outgoing Manager of Grupo Éxito?

Carlos Mario Giraldo Moreno, current general manager of Grupo Éxito Colombia, will effectively leave the organization on June 30, 2026.

Giraldo Moreno is a lawyer graduated from the University of Medellín and holds a Master's degree in Law from Tulane University in the United States. His academic training includes studies in Strategic Management and Retail Planning at Babson College, as well as update programs in management and marketing at Kellogg and Stanford universities.

Before entering the retail sector, he consolidated a 13-year trajectory at Grupo Nutresa (formerly Noel and Zenú), where he served as president of Noel Food Industries and Noel Cookie Company for nearly a decade. In total, he accumulates more than 25 years of professional experience in the mass consumption sector.

## Leadership at Grupo Éxito

His connection with Grupo Éxito began in 2007, when he assumed the position of operational president of Retail, a position he held until 2013. From March of that year, he was appointed president and general director of the company, a responsibility he maintained until March 21, 2024. Since March 22, 2024, he has served as general manager for Colombia.

## Participation in Boards and Business Groups

Throughout his career, he has held leadership positions in various organizations and companies:

- He was president of the Board of Directors of ANDI between 2003 and 2004

- He served on the Board of Directors of ISA S.A. until 2021

- Currently, he is a member of the boards of directors of the Éxito Foundation, Copa Holdings, Copa Airlines Colombia, and the National Federation of Merchants and Businesspeople (Fenalco)

## End of an Era

This marks the end of a trajectory of more than 18 years leading one of the most representative companies in commerce in the country. The decision, which had been previously communicated to the Board of Directors, marks the close of a stage characterized by profound strategic transformations, commercial innovation, and brand consolidation in the national market.

During his leadership, Grupo Éxito experienced an evolution process that positioned it as one of Colombia's most solid companies and as the number one brand in the Top of Mind of the retail sector. During this period, the company promoted an ambitious strategy to modernize formats, highlighting the strengthening of Éxito Wow and Carulla Fresh Market, focused on offering differentiated experiences and greater value to consumers.

One of the most relevant milestones of his management was the development of digital and omnichannel sales business, which today represents approximately 14% of sales in Colombia operations, reflecting the group's adaptation to new consumption dynamics and technological changes in the retail sector. Added to this is the consolidation of Grupo Éxito as one of the country's largest clothing retailers, supported by iconic proprietary brands such as Arkitect, Bronzini, and People.

Giraldo also led the expansion and consolidation of the shopping centers business, where Viva Envigado became a national reference. Likewise, he promoted key strategic alliances, such as the one with Bancolombia for the creation and strengthening of Puntos Colombia, today considered the country's leading loyalty coalition and a "second currency" that reaches approximately one in every three Colombian households.
