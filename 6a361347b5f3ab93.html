With Strengthened Tigo Operator, Fixed Internet Becomes New Battleground in Colombian Telecom Sector

26 April 2026 · 04:51 PM  ·  Portfolio - Negocios
Millicom (operating as Tigo) has consolidated its market position in Colombia, shifting competitive intensity to fixed internet services as the primary battleground in the telecom sector. With Tigo strengthened, pressure is mounting on Claro and smaller competitors to compete aggressively in the high-margin fixed broadband segment. This competitive shift reflects the sector's transition from mobile-centric growth to converged fixed-mobile services. For telecom investors, this market dynamic suggests consolidation opportunities, pricing pressure on incumbent fixed operators, and potential margin compression as competitors vie for fixed-line customers.
