Green Fertilizer Project Challenges Industry Dependence on Natural Gas

24 April 2026 · 05:48 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
A UK-listed green fertilizer company is developing a pilot project in Paraguay to test whether low-cost renewable energy can sustain ammonia production without reliance on natural gas. The initiative responds to fertilizer market disruptions caused by Middle East instability and represents an opportunity to decarbonize fertilizer production while reducing cost volatility. Success could reshape the global agribusiness supply chain and create new investment opportunities in renewable-based agriculture inputs.
