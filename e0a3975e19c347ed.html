Investment Fund Administration Industry Grew 23.5% Last Year with Record Assets of US$43.85 Billion

20 April 2026 · 11:49 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Chile's investment fund administration industry (ACAFI) experienced robust growth of 23.5% last year, with managed assets reaching a record US$43.85 billion. The new ACAFI president, María José Montero, identified system design as a key challenge for expanding institutional participation in the sector. This growth reflects strong investor demand and confidence in Chile's capital markets, positioning the country as a regional hub for asset management in Latin America.
