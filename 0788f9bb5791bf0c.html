Crypto Sector Fears Central Bank Recommendation to Restrict Stablecoins and Enable IOF Tax

30 April 2026 · 10:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's cryptocurrency sector expressed concern over a Central Bank technical note submitted to Congress recommending the prohibition of stablecoins issued by unregulated companies and the imposition of IOF (Financial Operations Tax) on crypto transactions. The regulatory proposal marks a significant tightening of Brazil's crypto oversight and could materially impact digital asset businesses operating in the country. Industry associations and companies warned of potential competitive disadvantages and economic impacts from such restrictions. The development reflects ongoing tension between financial regulators and the crypto industry over supervision and taxation.
