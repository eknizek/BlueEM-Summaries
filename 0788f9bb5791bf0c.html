Crypto Sector Opposes Central Bank Recommendation to Restrict Stablecoins and Impose IOF Tax

30 April 2026 · 10:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's cryptocurrency sector voiced strong opposition on April 30, 2026, to a Central Bank technical note recommending the prohibition of stablecoins issued by unregulated companies within Brazil. The recommendation also flags potential imposition of IOF (financial transaction tax) on crypto transfers. The proposed restrictions aim to strengthen financial system stability and regulatory control but face industry resistance from crypto platforms and associations. This regulatory move could significantly impact the operating model and competitiveness of crypto service providers in Brazil's growing digital asset market.
