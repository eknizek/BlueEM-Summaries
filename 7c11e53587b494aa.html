Peru's tax authority proposes digital wallets be recognized as electronic payment receipts

21 April 2026 · 03:13 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's National Tax Authority (SUNAT) has proposed that transaction confirmations generated by digital wallets (billeteras digitales) be formally recognized and accepted as electronic payment receipts. This regulatory step would legitimize and standardize digital payment documentation, supporting the growth of fintech and digital payment platforms in Peru's formal economy. The proposal could accelerate adoption of cashless transactions and improve tax compliance tracking.
