Enel Brasil Raises R$10 Billion One Day Before Credit Rating Downgrade

27 April 2026 · 10:13 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Enel's Brazilian subsidiary obtained R$10 billion (US$2 billion) in credit facility from Enel Américas, timed one day before a ratings downgrade from credit agencies. The financing, with an 18-month term, supports operations across São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Ceará. The rapid capital deployment reflects liquidity management amid credit pressure, with timing raising questions about refinancing urgency and credit stress on the utility.
