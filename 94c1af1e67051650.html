Petrobras accelerates Búzios 8 production start on P-79 platform

01 May 2026 · 09:01 PM  ·  MoneyTimes
Petrobras announced that the P-79 platform, located in the pre-sal Santos Basin, commenced production on schedule ahead of the company's 2026–2030 business plan timeline. The eighth platform at the Búzios field adds 180,000 barrels of oil per day production capacity and 7.2 million tonnes per day of gas compression capability. This acceleration demonstrates operational progress in Petrobras' flagship offshore development and supports production guidance.
