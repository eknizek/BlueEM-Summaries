Allos and Kinea to become partners and create a shopping center REIT giant

10 April 2026 · 10:37 AM  ·  Neofeed
Allos, operating 45 shopping centers with 1.93 million square meters of leasable area and R$2.7 billion in 2025 revenue, is partnering with Kinea Investimentos, a major fund manager backed by Itaú, to create a leading REIT platform in Brazil's retail real estate sector. The merger combines Allos's large portfolio with Kinea's institutional investor relationships and capital access, consolidating market position in a segment exposed to consumer spending trends. This deal reflects growing institutional appetite for consolidated Brazilian real estate platforms and signals confidence in structured retail real estate despite broader consumer caution.
