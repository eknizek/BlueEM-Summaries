Heat Bonus, Pickup Trucks, Union Offices, and Million-Dollar Subsidies: Unusual Demands from USO to Ecopetrol

21 April 2026 · 10:50 AM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
The USO (Unión Sindical Obrera), Colombia's largest trade union, has presented an unusual set of demands to state-owned oil company Ecopetrol that extend beyond traditional wage negotiations. Demands include heat bonuses, provision of pickup trucks, union office facilities, and substantial cash subsidies, alongside requests for changes to employment contracting practices and direct equity participation in the company. These negotiations reflect labor's assertiveness in Colombia's energy sector and potential cost implications for Ecopetrol's operations.
