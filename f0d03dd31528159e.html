Bill proposes fiscal law exception to fund fuel tax reduction

23 April 2026 · 08:52 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
A legislative proposal in Brazil seeks an exception to the Fiscal Responsibility Law (LRF) to enable the government to reduce fuel-related taxes without the usual fiscal constraints. The measure addresses pressure to lower fuel costs while managing the fiscal impact. This reflects ongoing tension between social demands for lower fuel prices and Brazil's fiscal consolidation objectives.
