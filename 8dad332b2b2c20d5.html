Metallurgical sector rebounds in March despite persistent weakness; Q1 shows cumulative decline

20 April 2026 · 02:26 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina's metallurgical sector, a critical input for broader manufacturing, showed a modest 1.5% improvement in March production according to industry chamber Adimra. However, the sector continues to operate at depressed levels, with cumulative first-quarter output down nearly 7% year-over-year. The modest March rebound suggests stabilization may be underway, but broader industrial recovery remains constrained amid Argentina's ongoing economic adjustment.
