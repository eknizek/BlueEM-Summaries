Bankers Denounce Cartel Scheme to Evade Debt Payment to the Financial System

21 April 2026 · 04:47 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Finanzas
Asobancaria, Colombia's banking association, has raised concerns about a purported cartel exploiting insolvency mechanisms to avoid debt repayment to financial institutions. The association reports a marked uptick in irregular insolvency claims over the past two years, suggesting potential coordination among debtors. This trend threatens credit quality and bank profitability while undermining the integrity of Colombia's insolvency framework. The issue has implications for credit risk assessment, loan loss provisions, and broader financial system stability.
