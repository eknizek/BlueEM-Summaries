Airline Industry Groups Initiate Administrative Litigation Over TUUA Passenger Transfer Levy

07 May 2026 · 04:42 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
International and regional airline associations—IATA, ALTA (Latin American and Caribbean Air Transport Association), and AETAI—have commenced administrative litigation against Peru's Ministry of Transportation and Communications regarding a proposed passenger transfer levy (TUUA) as part of Amendment No. 9 to the concession agreement with Lima Airport Partners (LAP). The amendment is scheduled for signature in May 2026 and represents a significant operational and financial change for airlines operating Peru's primary international gateway. The dispute reflects tensions between airport concessionaires' revenue optimization and airline cost management.
