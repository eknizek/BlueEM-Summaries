DRINKS Survey Identifies $40B Commerce Gap Between Consumer Discovery and Purchase of Alcohol; Demand for AI and Embedded Commerce Grows

07 April 2026 · 10:00 AM  ·  Business Wire
A DRINKS industry survey has identified a $40 billion gap between how consumers discover alcoholic beverages and how they can purchase them, highlighting an emerging opportunity in embedded commerce and AI-powered recommendations. This research underscores the digital transformation of the beverage and alcohol sector, where traditional retail constraints (physical inventory, location-based sales, regulatory complexity) are being disrupted by technology platforms. For Latin American investors, this signals rising fintech and e-commerce activity in the alcohol and beverages space—an increasingly relevant theme as regional consumers and suppliers digitize purchasing.
