German giant Merck is optimistic about Brazil and will have a 'bioprinting' hub in Rio

04 May 2026 · 09:01 AM  ·  O Globo
The German pharmaceutical and chemical company Merck announced plans to establish a bioprinting technology hub in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, reflecting optimism about the country's innovation ecosystem and life sciences potential. Bioprinting represents an advanced field combining 3D printing with biological materials, positioning Brazil as a regional center for next-generation medical device and pharmaceutical research.
