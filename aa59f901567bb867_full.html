# Grupo Falabella Brings Renato Fernández to Its Ranks

One of the first visible changes at Falabella will occur this Monday, following Fernando de Peña's arrival as president of the company. This involves the arrival of Renato Fernández, who will assume the position of Manager of Corporate Affairs and Sustainability at the group, a position that did not previously exist as such.

Fernández is an executive with extensive experience in the area and in the last six months had held a similar position at Mallplaza, a firm he joined in October 2025 through a process led by headhunter Korn Ferry. Previously, Fernández was part of the team at Vinson Consulting and had earlier worked at Codelco as Vice President of Corporate Affairs and Sustainability. Before the state company, he worked at the Cencosud group between 2011 and 2018 as Manager of Corporate Affairs.

When contacted, Renato Fernández was not available to provide a statement.
