# Grupo Falabella adds Renato Fernández to its ranks

One of the first changes, at least visibly, will occur this Monday at Falabella, following Fernando de Peña's arrival as president of the company. It involves the arrival of Renato Fernández, who will assume the position of manager of Corporate Affairs and Sustainability in the group, a position that did not previously exist as such.

Fernández is an executive with extensive experience in the area and has spent the last six months in a similar role at Mallplaza, a company he joined in October 2025 through a process led by headhunter Korn Ferry. Previously, Fernández was part of the team at consulting firm Vinson Consulting and before that had worked at Codelco as vice president of Corporate Affairs and Sustainability.

Prior to the state company, he worked at the Cencosud group between 2011 and 2018 as manager of Corporate Affairs.

When contacted, Renato Fernández was unavailable for comment.
