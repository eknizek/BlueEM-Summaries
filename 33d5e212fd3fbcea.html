Kokoriko changes ownership and passes to KKO fund: company enters new stage after founder's death

21 April 2026 · 11:43 AM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Kokoriko, a major Colombian quick-service restaurant chain specializing in fried chicken, has changed ownership as it passes from IGA group control to the KKO investment fund. The transaction was announced following the death of the company's founder, marking a significant transition in leadership and ownership structure. This acquisition represents a potential private equity-backed recapitalization and growth phase for the restaurant operator.
