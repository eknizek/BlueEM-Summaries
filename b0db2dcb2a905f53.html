Coto Hypermarket Construction Approved by Court After 13-Year Freeze; La Matanza Municipality to Appeal

07 May 2026 · 11:12 PM  ·  Clarin
A Buenos Aires court has ruled in favor of allowing Coto retail to proceed with a hypermarket and cinema complex in Isidro Casanova, La Matanza district, after a 13-year construction freeze. Coto argues the project will create 1,000 jobs and generate economic activity; the municipality contests the approval citing that the 1998-era permits have expired and claiming negative retail and commercial impacts. The ruling represents a significant resolution to a long-stalled real estate development, though municipal appeal remains pending. For real estate and retail investors, this signals potential unlocking of constrained projects and clarification of permit expiration standards.
