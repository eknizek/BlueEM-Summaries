Bradesco Acquires 100% of Tivio Capital Asset Management Joint Venture

20 April 2026 · 04:26 PM  ·  Neofeed
Bradesco has completed the acquisition of 100% ownership of Tivio Capital, the asset management joint venture it launched with Banco BV in August 2022. The move reflects Bradesco's strategy to build a proprietary wealth management and asset management platform comparable to competitors like Kinea. The full acquisition demonstrates the bank's commitment to controlling and scaling its asset management capabilities rather than maintaining shared ownership.
