Why Bradesco Acquired 100% of Tivio Capital

20 April 2026 · 04:26 PM  ·  Neofeed
Bradesco completed its acquisition of 100% stake in Tivio Capital, the asset management joint venture it formed with Banco BV in August 2022. The move allows Bradesco to build a proprietary wealth management platform comparable to Kinea, the successful asset management subsidiary of rival Itaú. By taking full control, Bradesco gains complete operational and strategic flexibility in the growing Brazilian asset management market, aligning with its broader strategy to diversify revenue beyond traditional banking.
