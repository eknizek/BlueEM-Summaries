# Peso Rebounds! Dollar Price Today March 30, 2026

The Mexican peso shakes off the losing streak from the previous three trading days and advances at the start of the week ahead of Federal Reserve Chairman Jerome Powell's speech, with the dollar price today March 30, 2026 trading at 18.05 pesos; here you can see the exchange rate at Mexico's banks.

The Mexican currency registers a gain of 0.28%.

Sunday afternoon in its foreign exchange trading weakened to 18.1595 pesos, a level not seen since mid-December.

Powell is expected to speak shortly at Harvard University. Markets will be watching closely for any indication about the trajectory of interest rates amid concerns about the impact of the Middle East war on global inflation.
