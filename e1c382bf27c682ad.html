Platanitos expects World Cup 2026 boost and plans store expansion in Peru and Chile

07 May 2026 · 02:08 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Platanitos, a Peruvian footwear and retail chain with a marketplace platform, is expanding its physical footprint ahead of the 2026 FIFA World Cup, expecting significant uplift in football-related product sales. The company plans to open 11 new stores in Peru and 3 additional locations in Chile, capitalizing on anticipated sports merchandise demand. The expansion reflects confidence in regional consumer spending dynamics and positions the company to benefit from World Cup-driven merchandise cycles and market growth.
