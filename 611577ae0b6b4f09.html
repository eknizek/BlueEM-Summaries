Desenrola 2 offers debt renegotiation for income up to R$8,105 and debt forgiveness up to R$100k

04 May 2026 · 09:45 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
The Brazilian government launched Desenrola 2, an expanded debt relief initiative offering renegotiation terms for households earning up to R$8,105 monthly and debt forgiveness for obligations reaching R$100,000. The program targets vulnerable consumer segments burdened by accumulated debt and aims to restore credit access and household financial health. Desenrola 2 represents a policy intervention into household debt dynamics and consumer credit markets, with implications for financial institutions' loan portfolios and provisions. The initiative reflects broader government concern over household indebtedness and its drag on consumption and economic growth.
