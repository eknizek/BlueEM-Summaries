Desenrola 2 Offers Debt Renegotiation and Deletion for Low-Income Households

04 May 2026 · 09:45 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
The Desenrola 2 program establishes eligibility criteria targeting low-income Brazilian households, offering debt consolidation, renegotiation, and partial or full deletion of outstanding obligations. The program targets individuals with monthly incomes up to R$8,105 and eligible debt balances up to R$100,000. This initiative aims to restore financial capacity for vulnerable consumers and reduce household leverage in Brazil's economy.
