Juan Lucas Vega appointed as new general manager of Grupo Éxito

24 April 2026 · 11:55 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Juan Lucas Vega has been appointed as the new general manager of Grupo Éxito, Colombia's leading retail conglomerate, replacing Carlos Mario Giraldo who announced his departure effective June 30. The appointment marks a leadership transition at the company as it navigates the Colombian retail market. Vega's appointment comes as Grupo Éxito continues to operate its portfolio of retail banners across the region.
