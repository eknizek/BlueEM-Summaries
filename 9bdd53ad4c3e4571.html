The Bottlenecks Suffocating Brazil's Critical Minerals Potential

30 April 2026 · 12:13 PM  ·  BrazilJournal
Brazil possesses significant reserves of critical minerals required for energy transition and high-tech manufacturing, yet faces regulatory, infrastructure, and policy bottlenecks that limit exploitation. Global competition for critical mineral supplies—from bilateral agreements to strategic state investment—is unprecedented, rivaling historical oil booms. However, Brazil's institutional capacity and permitting frameworks lag peers, risking lost investment opportunities and market share to competitors.
