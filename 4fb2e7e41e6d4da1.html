Failed With a Dotcom in 1996 and Sold Paperless in 2017: The Story of Pablo Vicuña

02 May 2026 · 07:21 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Pablo Vicuña's entrepreneurial career spans multiple ventures: in 1996 he attempted to build a VoIP-like service before Skype existed; he founded Paperless in 2001 working without salary for three years and raised US$3 million, moving his family to São Paulo for seven years; in 2017 he sold 100% of Paperless. Currently, Vicuña operates Beagle Ventures, a US$8 million venture fund that invested in Rankmi before SoftBank and reports a DPI of 5x.
