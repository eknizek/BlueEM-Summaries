Nuvini, Owned by Pierre Schurmann, Makes Its Largest M&A Deal in History to Advance in AI

06 April 2026 · 05:59 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazilian SaaS holding Nuvini, founded by entrepreneur Pierre Schurmann in 2020, completed its largest acquisition and first international deal to expand artificial intelligence competencies. The move underscores accelerating consolidation in LatAm's software sector and strategic focus on AI-driven product enhancement. The deal signals that LatAm SaaS founders and investors view AI integration as critical to competitive positioning in global markets. For LatAm PE and growth equity investors, this transaction exemplifies the ongoing build-out of AI-enhanced technology platforms and M&A activity in the region's high-growth software segment.
