Argentina to Use Revenue from Privatizations to 'Modernize' Armed Forces

04 May 2026 · 11:38 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times
The Argentine government intends to direct revenues generated from privatization programs toward modernizing the country's Armed Forces, signaling a reallocation of state resources. This represents a fiscal policy decision under the Milei administration to balance deficit reduction with strategic national priorities. The strategy underscores the government's use of asset sales as a funding mechanism for targeted expenditures rather than direct deficit reduction.
