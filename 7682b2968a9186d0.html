Shopee Opens Three New Distribution Centers in ES, PR, and CE

04 May 2026 · 08:39 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Shopee announced the opening of three distribution centers in Vitória (Espírito Santo), Curitiba (Paraná), and Fortaleza (Ceará) as part of its strategy to expand logistics infrastructure in Brazil. The new facilities are expected to generate approximately 900 jobs and have combined capacity to process up to 700,000 orders per day. This expansion reflects Shopee's continued investment in last-mile delivery capabilities and infrastructure to support e-commerce growth in key Brazilian markets.
