Volcan Mining Company completes Romina integration tunnel and advances toward new mining operation launch

08 May 2026 · 06:19 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Volcan Compañía Minera announced completion of the Romina Project's integration tunnel at 100%, achieving physical connection between the ore body and the Alpamarca concentrator plant. This milestone marks the final phase prior to mine operations commencement. The project represents a significant expansion for Volcan, Peru's largest zinc producer, and signals investment momentum in Peru's mining sector.
