Real estate fund index hits record high amid Selic rate cut projections

19 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's primary real estate investment fund index, IFIX, renewed its historical record on Friday, reaching 3,930 points, despite prevailing elevated interest rate levels. The rally reflects investor optimism about expectations for interest rate reductions by the central bank (Selic). Lower rates are expected to improve REIT valuations and increase investor demand for yield-bearing real estate assets.
