Prologis and Fibra Mty Intensify Competition for Macquarie Acquisition

24 April 2026 · 04:12 PM

Fibra Mty informed on Friday that it has launched a public acquisition and reciprocal subscription offer (OPA) directed at holders of real estate trust certificates (CBFIs) of Fibra Macquarie valued at 37 thousand 462 million pesos, compared to the previous potential offer of 34 thousand 427 million.
