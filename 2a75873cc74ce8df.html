Sabesp sees Copasa privatization timeline as 'tight' with 'somewhat higher risk'

07 April 2026 · 03:56 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Sabesp, São Paulo's state water and sanitation utility, is evaluating a potential acquisition of Copasa (Minas Gerais' water company) but has signaled that any participation remains contingent on detailed analysis of the execution risk and return profile. CEO Carlos Augusto Piani characterized the privatization timeline as 'tight' and noted 'somewhat higher risk,' suggesting concerns about feasibility and complexity. In parallel, Sabesp is awaiting bids for concessions under its Universaliza São Paulo program, expected in H2, which management views as lower-risk infrastructure investment opportunities. This is material for LatAm infrastructure and utilities investors tracking consolidation and privatization in Brazilian water assets.
