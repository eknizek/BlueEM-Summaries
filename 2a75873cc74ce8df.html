Sabesp Views Copasa Privatization Timeline as 'Tight' and Higher Risk

07 April 2026 · 03:56 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Sabesp, Brazil's largest water utility, is studying potential participation in the Copasa (Minas Gerais water company) privatization but CEO Carlos Augusto Piani has flagged concerns about the accelerated timeline and increased execution risk. The company is simultaneously awaiting infrastructure bidding opportunities in the second half of the year under São Paulo's Universaliza program, which offers potentially more favorable risk-return profiles. This signals Sabesp's strategic focus on infrastructure concessions and selective M&A while maintaining discipline on deal risk assessment.
