'Art of the Deal': Americans Close Billion-Dollar Deal for Brazil's Rare Earths

20 April 2026 · 08:12 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
USA Rare Earth announced an agreement to acquire 100% of Serra Verde, a Brazilian rare earth mining company operating in Goiás, for $2.8 billion. The transaction represents a strategic move by U.S. interests to secure control over Brazil's first discovered rare earth mineral reserve, a critical resource for advanced technology and defense applications. The deal comes as Brazilian policymakers discuss establishing a state-owned rare earths company, but American investors have moved to consolidate control of this strategic asset ahead of potential government intervention.
