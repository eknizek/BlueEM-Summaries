Sugarcane production expected to grow 5.3% in Brazil

29 April 2026 · 08:00 PM  ·  O Globo
Brazilian sugarcane production is projected to grow 5.3%, driven by strong domestic demand and export opportunities in both sugar and ethanol markets. This growth indicates increased capacity utilization at mills and higher revenues for sugarcane producers and processors. The expansion also supports downstream industries including biofuels and reflects Brazil's competitive advantage in renewable energy and agricultural commodities.
