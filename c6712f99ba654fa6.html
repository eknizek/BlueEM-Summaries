Tork Capital closes funds amid hostile environment for stock managers

22 April 2026 · 10:36 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Tork Capital has announced the closure of its asset management operations following deteriorating market conditions for Brazilian equity fund managers. The firm managed approximately R$600 million across long-only, equity hedge, and pension funds—a scale insufficient to operate profitably in the current environment. The shutdown reflects broader pressure on smaller Brazilian asset managers facing fee compression and competition. This signals continued consolidation in Brazil's asset management industry.
