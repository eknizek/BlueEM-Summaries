Colombian Exports Grew 20.9% in March 2026 Driven by Higher Gold and Coal Sales

05 May 2026 · 08:39 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia reported strong export growth of 20.9% in March 2026, with total external sales reaching $5.315 billion according to national statistics agency DANE. Gold and coal sales provided primary growth drivers, reflecting strong commodity prices and continued demand. This macroeconomic indicator is relevant for investors assessing Colombia's export-dependent economy and the performance of its mining and energy sectors, particularly as geopolitical factors influence global commodity markets.
