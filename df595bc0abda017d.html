Brazil to begin testing higher biodiesel content in diesel by May

24 April 2026 · 08:00 PM  ·  O Globo (Brazil)
Brazil is initiating testing of diesel blends with higher biodiesel content by May, advancing its energy transition and domestic biofuel policy. The move reflects Brazil's strategy to increase the use of renewable energy sources and reduce reliance on petroleum-based fuels. This policy shift has implications for energy companies, agricultural producers (particularly soy and sugarcane growers), and the broader oil and gas sector, as higher biodiesel mandates could reshape fuel markets and create new opportunities in the biofuels supply chain.
