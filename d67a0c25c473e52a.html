Medical centers, convenience stores and cafes: the new map of closed bank branches in Santiago

26 April 2026 · 11:48 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Over the past 18 months, Chilean banks have aggressively closed branches, with Banco de Chile leading with 15 closures and Bci following with 10. Rather than remaining vacant, many former bank branches are being repurposed as medical centers, convenience stores, and cafes. BancoEstado remains the only major bank expanding its physical footprint. The trend reflects digital banking adoption and changing branch economics in the Chilean market.
