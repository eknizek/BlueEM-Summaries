# Elida Project: New Drilling Activities Position It as Key Exploration Focus

During the proEXPLO 2026 event, Manuel Montoya, Vice President of South American Exploration for Globe Trotters Resources Inc, reported that the Elida mining exploration project, located 86 km from Barranca (north of Lima), has achieved over 28,000 meters of drilling.

This has made it possible to identify mineralization points with a grade of 0.58% copper equivalent, which includes copper (0.49%), molybdenum (0.04%), and silver (4.65 grams per ton).

In this regard, Montoya explained that additional studies suggest the presence of a mineralized zone with higher purity, which has motivated extending the porphyry cluster 6 km northward toward an as-yet unexplored area.

He also recalled that the potential of the Elida project was discovered in 2013 and that its location opens opportunities to leverage its productive potential.

"It is a project located near the Antamina port and the recently inaugurated Port of Chancay, which provides logistical facilities and availability of human resources," the specialist stated.

Montoya provided this project update during proEXPLO 2026, where he gave updates on the potential of the area and detailed the scope of the latest drilling operations conducted in the zone.
