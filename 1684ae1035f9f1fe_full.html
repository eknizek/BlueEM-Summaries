# Integration allows us to compete at greater scale in increasingly demanding market, says Millicom (Tigo) CEO

Marcelo Benítez assured that this acquisition reflects the company's long-term commitment to the country.

Millicom International Cellular S.A. officially announced the acquisition of the remaining 32.5% stake in Colombia Telecomunicaciones S.A. E.S.P. (Coltel), which was held by La Nación, thereby completing 100% ownership of the company. This step follows the conclusion of the public offer for Telefónica's controlling stake, completed in February 2026, and marks the closure of the company's strategic consolidation process in Colombia.

Marcelo Benítez, CEO of Millicom, assured that this acquisition reflects the company's long-term commitment to the country and its conviction about the potential of the Colombian market. "By unifying these operations, we strengthen our ability to compete in a rapidly evolving industry, accelerate the deployment of next-generation infrastructure, and contribute to Colombia's digital and economic development," he stated.

According to the company, the operation represents a milestone for the telecommunications sector, as it will allow Coltel to be completely integrated into its structure, resulting in a larger-scale operator with financial strength and greater investment capacity.

In this regard, the executive highlighted that consolidation will facilitate the deployment of 5G networks nationwide, improve service quality through the integration of technical capabilities, and contribute to the country's digital inclusion objectives, with faster, more reliable connectivity and a more robust digital experience for millions of users.

He also emphasized that this transaction occurs in an increasingly competitive environment, in which traditional operators face pressure from global digital platforms, satellite providers, and new players in the connectivity ecosystem. In this context, scale, operational efficiency, and investment capacity are consolidated as key factors for maintaining competitiveness in the market.
