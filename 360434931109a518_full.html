French Saint-Gobain Sells Telhanorte to Tauá Partners

French multinational Saint-Gobain announced on Monday (4) that it has signed a definitive agreement with Tauá Partners for the sale of Telhanorte, a building materials retail network, according to a statement released by the company. The transaction, which should be completed by the end of the first half of this year, involves the sale of all Saint-Gobain's distribution units in the country. The last operation was in December with the sale of Tumelero.

Telhanorte currently operates 27 stores and a logistics center with a strong presence in the state of São Paulo, employing approximately 1,650 people. In 2025, it generated sales of around 180 million euros, according to Saint-Gobain. The value of the deal was not disclosed.
