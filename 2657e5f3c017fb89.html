Petro government to convene extraordinary Ecopetrol shareholder assembly to make adjustments to board of directors

30 April 2026 · 07:47 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
Colombia's Finance Minister has announced that the Petro government will convene an extraordinary shareholder assembly for Ecopetrol, the state-owned oil company, to make adjustments to the board of directors. This governance action reflects the administration's intent to reshape the company's leadership and strategic direction. For investors in Ecopetrol and Colombian energy markets, board changes at a major state-controlled enterprise signal potential shifts in capital allocation, dividend policy, and operational strategy.
