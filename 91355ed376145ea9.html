Aro raises $2.5M for AI-powered credit guidance platform

13 April 2026 · 07:19 PM  ·  Neofeed
Aro, a Brazilian fintech developing an artificial intelligence agent to guide consumers through credit options, announced a $2.5 million pre-seed round led by venture capital firms ONEVC and 17Sigma. The platform uses AI to help users navigate the credit landscape and make informed borrowing decisions. The funding enables Aro to accelerate development and expand its reach in the Brazilian credit market, addressing a gap in consumer credit guidance and financial inclusion.
