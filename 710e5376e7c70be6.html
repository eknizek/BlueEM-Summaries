65% of Peruvians Will Allocate 20% of Monthly Budget to Cyber WoW Shopping

23 April 2026 · 06:42 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Consumer behavior research from Kantar and Google reveals that 65% of Peruvian shoppers intend to allocate approximately 20% of their monthly budget to purchases during the upcoming Cyber WoW promotional event (April 20–23). The data reflects a maturation of Peru's e-commerce market, where planned, budget-conscious purchasing is replacing impulse buying. This trend indicates growing consumer financial discipline and the increasing importance of major e-commerce events to Peruvian households.
