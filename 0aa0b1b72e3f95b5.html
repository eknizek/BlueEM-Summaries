Aegea delays IPO plans, delivers full-year results

13 April 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Aegea, a major Brazilian water and sanitation company, has abandoned plans for an IPO in 2025, disappointing market expectations. CEO Radamés Casseb announced the postponement during a teleconference presenting full-year results. The delay had been partially telegraphed by Aegea's extended delay in releasing fourth-quarter results, which created market uncertainty. The decision reflects broader conditions in Brazil's capital markets and may impact infrastructure financing strategies in the utility sector.
