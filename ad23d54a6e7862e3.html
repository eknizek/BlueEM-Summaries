Auctions and R$45B in debentures: how sanitation became fixed income star

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Brazil's sanitation sector has become a prominent fixed-income market player, raising R$45 billion through debentures and public auctions. The sector's prominence reflects infrastructure investment needs, regulatory mandates for service expansion, and investor appetite for stable, long-duration assets. This trend highlights the financialization of essential services and the capital-intensive nature of Brazil's water and sanitation infrastructure upgrades.
