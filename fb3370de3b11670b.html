Request to integrate Water Treaty with Mexico into T-MEC

24 April 2026 · 11:38 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico)
Republican members of Congress proposed incorporating Mexico's international water treaty obligations into the USMCA (T-MEC) framework, creating enforcement mechanisms to compel Mexico to meet its Rio Bravo water delivery commitments to the United States. This proposal links trade policy with water rights disputes, raising potential tariff or trade consequences for non-compliance. The move could materially impact US-Mexico trade relations, agricultural sectors dependent on water resources, and bilateral investment. This represents a significant policy development with direct implications for companies operating across the border.
