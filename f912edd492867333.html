Budget Execution Crisis: 1,442 Peruvian Districts Have Used Less Than 25% of 2026 Budgets

20 April 2026 · 11:29 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Peru faces a severe budget execution crisis across local government, with more than 1,400 municipalities failing to execute more than 25% of their allocated 2026 budgets, and 97 districts showing zero execution. This indicates systemic dysfunction in public investment deployment and capacity constraints across municipal administrations. The underutilization of public spending represents a significant drag on infrastructure investment and local economic development, with potential implications for project-dependent sectors and infrastructure concessionaires.
