Comfenalco Quindío Contests SIC Sanction and Denies Overpricing in Ferinject Medication

21 April 2026 · 06:32 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Comfenalco Quindío, a Colombian healthcare entity, is contesting a sanction imposed by the SIC (Superintendencia de Industria y Comercio), Colombia's competition authority, regarding alleged overpricing of the medication Ferinject. The organization disputes the finding of excess costs, instead attributing the discrepancy to an accounting or billing error that allegedly distorted the regulatory analysis. This case highlights ongoing regulatory scrutiny of pharmaceutical pricing practices in Colombia's healthcare sector.
