Strongest Latin American Currencies Lead Emerging Markets, but Credicorp Warns of Risks

05 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Latin American currencies have emerged as among the strongest performers in emerging markets as dollar strength accelerates driven by geopolitical tensions and increased global risk aversion. However, the regional rally masks divergent impacts across countries, with some economies more exposed to dollar appreciation risks than others. Credicorp, the major Peruvian financial holding, has highlighted potential vulnerabilities arising from the 2026 dollar strength outlook, warning that currency appreciation could create headwinds for regional exporters and complicate economic policy management. The analyst perspective emphasizes that macro divergence across Latin America will determine which markets benefit versus suffer from the currency dynamics.
