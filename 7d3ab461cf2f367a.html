President of Confiep on Elections: "We Cannot Assert There Was Fraud"

04 May 2026 · 03:11 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Jorge Zapata Ríos, president of Peru's Confederación Nacional de Instituciones Empresariales Privadas (Confiep, the national confederation of private business institutions), commented on the integrity of Peru's April 12 general elections. While acknowledging that irregularities occurred during the electoral process, he explicitly stated that these irregularities do not constitute fraud. His statement reflects the business community's cautious assessment of the electoral process and has implications for political stability and the investment climate in Peru.
