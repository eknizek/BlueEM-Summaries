Camila Escobar steps down as Juan Valdez president, moves toward Colfuturo leadership

16 April 2026 · 12:04 AM

Procafecol S.A., the parent company of the Juan Valdez brand, announced this Wednesday that, by her own decision, Camila Escobar will step down as president and chief executive officer (CEO), a position she has held since 2018.

"Her departure comes after having fulfilled a strategic mission in the transformation of the company and in strengthening the positioning of the Juan Valdez brand globally," the company highlighted in a statement.

The decision, presented to the Board of Directors and effective as of April 30, 2026, responds to the beginning of a new professional challenge in a recognized organization with high impact in the promotion and development of national talent.

The Board of Directors accepted the resignation and expressed its gratitude and recognition to Camila Escobar for her years of service to Procafecol and the coffee sector.

In the context of this transition, the Board of Directors appointed engineer Carlos Arturo Azuero Perdomo, current president and member of the company's Board of Directors, as acting chief executive officer.

Likewise, a structured, transparent process will be initiated with the accompaniment of specialized advisors for the selection of the new CEO who will lead this new moment for the organization. The results of this process will be communicated to the market in due course.

Finally, it should be noted that Camila Escobar's departure from Juan Valdez does not imply a withdrawal from public life, since according to information obtained by Portafolio, the executive would assume the position of executive director of Colfuturo. This move, if confirmed, would mark her transition from the business sector to the field of talent development and would connect two key fronts for the country: the strengthening of human capital and the international projection of Colombian professionals.
