Mexican Pilots Association launches aviation degree program

17 April 2026 · 11:10 PM

The Mexican Pilots Association (CPAM) officially presented the Bachelor's Degree in Commercial Pilot, a joint effort between educational authorities, the aeronautical sector, and legislative representatives. Ángel Domínguez Catzín, president of CPAM, highlighted that this advancement represents a necessary evolution for one of the most demanding and strategic professions.
