First Argentine Product Exported to EU Without Tariffs: Family SME Ships Dark Honey from Entre Ríos

06 May 2026 · 04:40 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
An Entre Ríos-based family honey producer from Concordia achieved the first tariff-free export of an Argentine product to the European Union. The shipment consisted of 22 tonnes of dark honey and was enabled by a newly issued certificate facilitating duty-free trade under the EU-Argentina framework. This development signals improved market access for Argentine agricultural exports and demonstrates competitive positioning for small and medium enterprises in international markets.
