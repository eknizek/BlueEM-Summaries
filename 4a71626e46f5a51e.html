Sogem May Attract Investors to Finance R$500 Million Legal Battle Against Totvs

10 April 2026 · 09:47 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Sogem is exploring litigation financing from Brazilian and international funds to support a legal dispute with software company Totvs valued at R$500 million (approximately USD 100 million). The lawsuit has been ongoing for 17 years, and third-party litigation funding offers are being structured with minimum investments starting at USD 5 million by the EvidJuri expert firm. This represents an emerging private market opportunity in Brazilian legal-risk financing and reflects both the scale of long-running corporate disputes and the growth of alternative financing mechanisms for companies unable to self-fund protracted litigation.
