Peru Advances OECD Accession Process After Passing Consumer Protection Evaluation

07 May 2026 · 08:11 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru's National Integrated Consumer Protection System, led by the competition and consumer authority Indecopi, has successfully completed evaluation by member countries of the OECD's Consumer Policy Committee. This achievement strengthens Peru's candidacy for full OECD membership and signals compliance with global standards for consumer safeguards and regulatory frameworks. The evaluation covers institutional arrangements, legal protections, and enforcement mechanisms affecting market conduct and investor confidence.
