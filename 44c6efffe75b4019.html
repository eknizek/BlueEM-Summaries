"Is the Land Ours?" The Supreme Court Is Close to Ending a Historic Debate

06 April 2026 · 09:30 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazil's Supreme Court (STF) is approaching a definitive ruling on foreign acquisition of agricultural land, resolving a contentious policy question that has divided Congress and the agribusiness sector for over ten years. The issue has generated differing views even within agriculture, with some advocating liberalization to attract capital and others opposing foreign control of land. An STF decision will establish clear legal boundaries for international agribusiness investment and land acquisitions. For LatAm institutional investors and PE firms evaluating farmland and agribusiness opportunities in Brazil, the ruling will be a critical determinant of foreign capital access and investment viability.
