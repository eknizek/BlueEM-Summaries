More lightweight electric vehicles in Colombia would reduce cars and motorcycles on roads, following Shanghai and Netherlands models

05 May 2026 · 08:52 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombia's Law 2486 establishes a regulatory framework for lightweight electric scooters (under 60kg) by exempting them from vehicle registration (SOAT) and insurance requirements. The law draws inspiration from urban mobility models implemented in Shanghai and the Netherlands, which have successfully reduced vehicle congestion through lightweight electric vehicle adoption. This regulatory change represents a shift toward alternative transportation in Colombian cities and reflects growing investment in urban micromobility infrastructure. The policy signals government support for the emerging e-scooter and lightweight EV segment in LatAm's transportation sector.
