SAS launches AI agents for supply chain management

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
SAS, an enterprise analytics and software vendor, has launched AI-based agents designed to automate management of complex, multi-thousand supply chain workflows and processes. Supply chain optimization is a key area of enterprise AI investment, addressing long-standing operational challenges in inventory management, demand forecasting, and logistics efficiency. This product launch is relevant for investors tracking enterprise software adoption trends, AI software monetization, and the competitive positioning of major analytics platforms in capturing supply chain software spending.
