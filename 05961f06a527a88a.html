Kaiser alerts Chile on intellectual property protection issues with EU and US

04 May 2026 · 10:02 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
The leader of Chile's National Libertarian Party (PNL) has raised concerns about intellectual property protection provisions in the government's economic reactivation bill, arguing they are identical to a similar article that was voted down under former President Gabriel Boric less than a year ago. The issue reflects ongoing tensions between Chile and major trading partners including the European Union and United States over IP safeguards. This regulatory concern could impact foreign investment in Chile's technology and innovation sectors.
