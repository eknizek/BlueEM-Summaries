OFAC Continues Relaxation of Sanctions On Venezuela's Mineral Sector and Issues New Guidance About Secondary Sanctions Risks

08 April 2026 · 01:14 PM  ·  Baker Mckenzie
The U.S. Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) has further relaxed sanctions targeting Venezuela's mineral sector and issued new guidance on secondary sanctions exposure for non-US persons engaged in Venezuela-related transactions. This regulatory shift has direct material implications for mining companies, commodity traders, and investors with exposure to Venezuelan minerals and oil. The guidance reduces legal uncertainty and compliance costs for international players seeking to access Venezuelan mineral resources, particularly relevant to mining and energy investors across Latin America and globally.
