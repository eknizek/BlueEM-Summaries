Bradesco BBI Sees Brazil IPO Market Reopening After Compass Listing

08 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Bradesco BBI, the investment banking arm of Banco Bradesco, has expressed optimism that Brazil's IPO market is recovering after a significant slowdown. The bank's assessment is based on the successful listing of Compass Gás e Energia, a major energy distribution company, which demonstrated sufficient investor demand to break through market inertia. This signal suggests improved conditions for corporate listings and reflects broader market sentiment that institutional and retail appetite for new Brazilian equities is returning.
