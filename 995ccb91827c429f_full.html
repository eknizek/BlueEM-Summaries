# GHG Neutralization: EcoPower Certified at Agrishow

EcoPower Energy Efficiency received recognition from Gaia Environmental Certifier as a voluntary company in the neutralization of Greenhouse Gases (GHG), resulting from its participation in Agrishow 2026. The green Carbon Neutral seal guarantees the neutralization of 13 tons of CO2e (carbon equivalent) through carbon credits.

The emission of CO2 in the atmosphere affects the greenhouse effect, which is vital for the viability of life on planet Earth. In other words, all forms of life on the planet necessarily depend on the balance of the greenhouse effect. Our sun sends us energy in the form of electromagnetic radiation (short waves). This energy is reflected and repelled by the Earth's surface, causing the planet to send that amount of energy back into space. However, in the form of infrared radiation (long waves). Nevertheless, our planet has a substantial (thick) atmosphere that absorbs part of this radiation before it reaches outer space. As a consequence, infrared radiation is re-emitted in all directions, causing the lower atmosphere to warm. This effect is known as the greenhouse effect and is essential for controlling our planet's temperature. Without it, it is believed that Earth's average surface temperature would be 30°C lower, around -18°C. This would make life as we know it impossible, and thick layers of ice would cover our continents.

The main gases that aggravate the greenhouse effect are carbon dioxide (CO2), methane (CH4), and nitrous oxide (N2O). CO2 has the least aggressive power, but due to the amount emitted, it is what most aggravates the greenhouse effect, potentially lasting up to 100 years and impacting climate over centuries.

In 2025, in Brazil, which is the fifth largest GHG emitter, solar energy led the growth of the electricity matrix according to the Monitoring Panel of the Expansion of Electricity Generation Supply Report (RALIE), maintained by the National Electric Energy Agency (ANEEL).

Founded in 2013, EcoPower offers solar energy solutions in residential, commercial, industrial, and rural sectors. Thus, it directly contributes to the environment by reducing GHG emissions by generating clean and renewable energy.

According to Érika Carvalho, Administrative Director of the EcoPower Energy Efficiency Group, the company uses its own solar plant, which already acts in reducing GHG, and internally uses electric forklifts, as well as an electric fleet with cars and motorcycles for urban mobility. Furthermore, there is constant awareness among its leaders and other employees about environmental preservation, especially through conscious use of electrical energy and water resources.

"Awareness combined with practice is how EcoPower works on the interaction between people and the environment. We seek to train our employees, as well as franchisees, so they work with full awareness of entrepreneurial issues in the socio-environmental world. Thus, our customers don't just buy a photovoltaic energy system, but rather acquire solutions aimed at financial savings, but also active participation in sustainable care for the environment," said Érika.

The recognition from the Gaia Ambiental group was received with joy, especially within the agribusiness environment of Agrishow: "We are very satisfied to be at the largest agribusiness fair without emitting GHG, proactively collaborating for the environment. This seal validates sustainable practices, concrete commitment to the future, and contribution to combating climate change," said Érika.
