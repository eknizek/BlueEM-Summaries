Key tips for opening a CDT in Colombia after Central Bank rate hike

11 April 2026 · 06:49 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
The Banco de la República of Colombia increased its key interest rate by 100 basis points to 11.25%, creating a more attractive environment for fixed-income savings products such as CDTs (term deposit certificates). This monetary tightening reflects inflation management efforts and signals the central bank's commitment to price stability. For investors, higher rates improve returns on short-term fixed-income allocations and reduce the relative appeal of equity investments in the near term. The move has direct implications for corporate borrowing costs and investment returns across LatAm fixed-income portfolios.
