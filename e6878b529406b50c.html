Presto seeks to avoid bankruptcy: SIC approves restructuring plan for Colombia's fast-food chain

30 April 2026 · 08:38 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Presto, a prominent Colombian quick-service restaurant chain, has received approval from the Superintendencia de Industria y Comercio (SIC) for a reorganization plan aimed at avoiding bankruptcy. The approval follows legal compliance verification, similar to restructuring processes undertaken by competitor Oma. This regulatory clearance allows Presto to implement debt restructuring and operational changes necessary for financial recovery. The case highlights ongoing stress in Colombia's retail food sector and importance of formal reorganization processes for distressed businesses.
