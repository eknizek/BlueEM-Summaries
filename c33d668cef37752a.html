Castlelake, from Brookfield, takes over Grupo Leste platform in the US

10 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor

The transaction preserves the Brazilian manager's minority stake, indicating a strategic expansion of Brookfield's alternative asset platform while maintaining operational continuity through the incumbent stakeholder.
