Real estate funds adopt FIDC and debenture structures to compete with fixed income

27 April 2026 · 10:57 AM  ·  Neofeed
Facing high interest rates and illiquidity, Brazilian real estate fund (FII) managers are adopting structures similar to credit funds (FIDCs) and debentures to remain competitive with fixed income assets. The move addresses liquidity challenges and a closed market for new emissions by combining real estate exposure with fixed-income-like characteristics. This structural innovation reflects portfolio managers' efforts to attract capital in a challenging macroeconomic environment where traditional fixed income dominates.
