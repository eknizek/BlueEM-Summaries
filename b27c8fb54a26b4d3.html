After acquiring Adecoagro and Profértil, stablecoin giant Tether invests in Argentine app

29 April 2026 · 03:46 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
Tether, the issuer of USDT (the world's largest stablecoin pegged to the US dollar), led a $14 million investment round in Belo, an Argentine fintech application. The investment underscores Tether's strategic expansion into Latin America, following previous acquisitions of agribusiness company Adecoagro and fertilizer producer Profértil. Tether plans to deploy capital to scale Belo's operations across the region, marking a convergence of cryptocurrency infrastructure with traditional LatAm asset and payment solutions.
