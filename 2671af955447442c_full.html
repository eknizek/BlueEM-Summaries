US Supplier Normalizes Operations in Pemex Wells Following Payment Receipt

22 April 2026 · 08:54 PM

Weatherford International announced today that activity in Mexico's crude oil fields has normalized and it has begun receiving payments thanks to a new financial mechanism that has helped Pemex obtain funds to pay its suppliers.

The U.S. oil services company said it received a significant payment in the fourth quarter and another in the first quarter.
