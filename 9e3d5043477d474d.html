YPF investors say they'll arbitrate $16-billion Argentina case

23 April 2026 · 02:58 AM  ·  Buenos Aires Times (Argentina)
Former investors in Argentina's YPF oil company have announced their intention to seek international treaty-based arbitration following last month's appellate decision that overturned a US$16.1-billion judgment against the Argentine state. The investors plan to continue pursuing legal remedies through multiple channels, including further appeals and investor-state dispute settlement mechanisms. This case reflects ongoing tensions over Argentina's 2012 expropriation of YPF and represents significant contingent liability for the Argentine government.
