Australian company acquires rare earth and niobium project in Minas Gerais

29 April 2026 · 10:49 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Australian mining company Oceana has completed an acquisition of the Serra Negra rare earth and niobium project in Minas Gerais, Brazil. This transaction follows another high-profile M&A in the sector with USA Rare Earth's acquisition of Serra Verde. The deal reflects growing foreign investor appetite for Brazil's rare earth resources, driven by global demand for critical minerals used in clean energy and technology applications.
