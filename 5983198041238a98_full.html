# Grupo Energía Bogotá evaluates US stock listing and SEC registration

Grupo Energía Bogotá is evaluating alternatives to strengthen its financial structure, execute growth plans, and enable its access to international capital markets. In this regard, the company has initiated exploratory activities to consider a possible listing of American Depositary Shares (ADS) in the United States of America, as well as the corresponding registration with the SEC.

This strategic initiative by GEB would seek to expand its global shareholder base and increase the visibility of GEB's ordinary shares among its institutional investors. Additionally, the company aims to increase the liquidity of its shares and continue developing GEB's long-term growth strategy.

A possible listing of ADS in the United States of America and registration with the SEC would be subject to the fulfillment of certain conditions, including the obtaining of pertinent corporate approvals and authorization of the registration by the SEC and the applicable stock exchange.

GEB assured that this announcement does not constitute an offer to sell securities, nor a request for an offer to buy. Likewise, it does not constitute an offer, request, or sale of securities in any jurisdiction in which such offer, request, or sale would be illegal before its registration or qualification according to the securities laws of that jurisdiction.

The company also clarified that any offer, request, or sale of securities will be carried out in accordance with applicable law in the Republic of Colombia and/or the United States of America, in accordance with Rule 135 of the Securities Act of 1933.
