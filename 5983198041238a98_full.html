# Grupo Energía Bogotá Evaluates Listing Shares on US Market and Registering with SEC

Grupo Energía Bogotá is evaluating alternatives to strengthen its financial structure, execute growth plans, and enable access to international capital markets. To this end, the company has initiated exploratory activities to consider a potential listing of American Depositary Shares (ADS) in the United States, as well as the corresponding registration with the SEC.

This strategic initiative by GEB seeks to expand its global shareholder base and increase the visibility of GEB's ordinary shares among its institutional investors.

Additionally, the company aims to increase the liquidity of its shares and continue developing GEB's long-term growth strategy. A potential listing of ADS in the United States and registration with the SEC would be subject to compliance with certain conditions, including obtaining relevant corporate approvals and authorization of registration by the SEC and the applicable stock exchange.

GEB assured that this announcement does not constitute an offer to sell securities, nor a solicitation of an offer to buy. It also does not constitute an offer, solicitation, or sale of securities in any jurisdiction in which such offer, solicitation, or sale would be illegal before registration or qualification under the securities laws of that jurisdiction.

The company also clarified that any offer, solicitation, or sale of securities will be conducted in accordance with applicable law in the Republic of Colombia and/or the United States of America, in accordance with Rule 135 of the Securities Act of 1933.
