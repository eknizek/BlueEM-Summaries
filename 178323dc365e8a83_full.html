# Nearly one-third of population reports no emergency savings, Anbima/Datafolha survey shows

By the end of last year, nearly one-third (31%) of Brazilians said they had no financial reserves, while another 32% reported having less than the minimum emergency fund recommended by experts — enough to cover three to five months of expenses.

The survey interviewed 5,832 people, all aged 16 or older, about their relationship with the investment world in 2025.
