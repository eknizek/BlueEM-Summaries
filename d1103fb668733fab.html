Despite being prioritized, MOP has not set a date to tender Talcahuano cable car project

17 April 2026 · 11:37 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
A US$133 million cable car infrastructure project in Talcahuano, Chile, continues in technical development phase without a defined tender schedule. Although Mayor Eduardo Saavedra has expressed optimism about the project's advancement, the Ministry of Public Works (MOP) has not yet announced concrete bidding timelines. The project, listed as a priority investment, faces ongoing delays in moving from design to execution phase.
