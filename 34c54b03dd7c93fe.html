Former Transport Minister Ángela María Orozco Joins Avianca as Senior Vice President

17 April 2026 · 04:05 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Ángela María Orozco, a Colombian attorney and former government official who served as Minister of Transport and Minister of Foreign Trade, has been appointed to a senior leadership position at Avianca airlines. Her appointment brings government and trade experience to the airline's corporate leadership. Orozco previously headed Proexport (now ProColombia), the Colombian export promotion agency, adding international commerce expertise to her background.
