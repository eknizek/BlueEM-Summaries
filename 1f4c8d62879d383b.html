US$2bn Solar Projects in Mexico Reach Power Purchase Deal with CFE

06 April 2026 · 04:50 PM  ·  BNamericas
Large-scale solar projects totaling $2 billion in Mexico have concluded negotiations and signed a power purchase agreement (PPA) with Comisión Federal de Electricidad (CFE), the state-owned utility. This deal validates the economic viability of utility-scale solar in Mexico and demonstrates CFE's commitment to renewable energy procurement despite prior regulatory uncertainty. The PPA provides long-term revenue certainty to project developers and investors. For LatAm PE and infrastructure investors, PPAs in Mexico represent stable, off-take secured returns and signal the government's openness to renewable energy infrastructure partnerships.
