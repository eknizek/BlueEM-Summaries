Sankhya acquires Magis5; plans R$200M M&A spending in 2026

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br

The deal emphasizes native integration capabilities for e-commerce platforms. Sankhya has signalled ambitious capital deployment plans, budgeting R$200 million for additional M&A activity in 2026, underscoring its consolidation-driven growth strategy in the Brazilian software sector.
