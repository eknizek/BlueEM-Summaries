The Legal Arsenal That Private Pension Funds Are Preparing to Defend $25 Billion in Retirement Savings

26 April 2026 · 12:52 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Finanzas
Colombia's private pension fund administrators (AFPs) are preparing a coordinated legal strategy to challenge a government decree mandating the transfer of billions in retirement savings to the state-run Colpensiones system. The move represents a major test of pension fund independence and fiduciary obligations in Colombia. The dispute centers on protecting accumulated retirement assets totaling approximately $25 billion on behalf of millions of beneficiaries.
