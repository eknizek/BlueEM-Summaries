06 May 2026 · 04:40 PM

# Fixed-income investment fund launched to capitalize on high rates in Colombia this year

Acciones & Valores announced the launch of the FIC Renta Fija Cerrado 12M Plus, a new investment fund focused on local fixed-income securities with AAA rating and designed to capitalize on the current interest rate environment in Colombia. The vehicle was developed together with trii and MPF Invest, and will be available between May 6 and 26. The strategy contemplates a closed 12-month term and projects estimated net returns between 12.5% and 13.5% effective annual rate at maturity.

According to the firm, the objective is to take advantage of the current fixed-income market conditions following recent inflation peaks. In this context, investors have returned to look at conservative instruments as an alternative to protect capital and obtain competitive returns in short-term horizons.

"The current moment offers a relevant opportunity to structure fixed-income portfolios. Rates have reached attractive levels, which allows obtaining competitive conditions for investors who want to take advantage of short-term opportunities," said Santiago Quintero, Asset Management manager at Acciones & Valores.

The company indicated that the fund will invest in local instruments of high credit quality, seeking to combine stability with return potential. The return projections will depend on market conditions when the investment window closes, although the firm highlighted that the environment continues to favor this type of assets.

## Fixed-income funds regain space in Colombia

The launch of the new FIC occurs at a time when the Colombian financial market is beginning to register greater appetite for fixed-income products. Following a period marked by high inflation and volatility in different assets, many investors are prioritizing alternatives with lower risk exposure.

In this scenario, current rates have become one of the main drivers for the recovery of interest in this type of instruments. Fixed income is repositioning itself as an attractive option for both traditional investors and new participants interested in diversifying their portfolios.

The FIC Renta Fija Cerrado 12M Plus will have a minimum investment of one million pesos and will distribute returns monthly. According to Acciones & Valores, the vehicle was designed for both current clients and individuals and legal entities seeking professional management and stability in the short term.

The firm explained that the 12-month closed structure allows capturing specific market conditions and building strategies aligned with projected return expectations. Additionally, the focus on AAA securities seeks to reduce risks associated with credit deterioration within the portfolio.

The product also reflects how financial entities are adjusting their offerings in response to recent changes in investor behavior. After several years in which variable income assets captured much of the attention, fixed income is returning to consolidate itself as a relevant alternative within local portfolios.

## Investors seek stability and lower volatility

According to information provided by the brokerage firm, interest in this type of vehicle does not respond solely to rates. The firm assured that factors such as specialized management and institutional strength are also gaining weight in investment decisions.

"Beyond the rate, institutional strength and specialized management become key factors when investing," the company highlighted in the product presentation.

This approach targets investors seeking technical support amid a financial environment still marked by caution. The new fund also appears in a context where many investors continue to prioritize liquidity, stability, and generation of periodic cash flow. The monthly distribution of returns seeks precisely to respond to profiles interested in obtaining recurring income while maintaining exposure to conservative instruments.

Although the fund will only be available until May 26, the strategy evidences a broader trend within the Colombian market: the return of interest in fixed-income financial products with professional management. Current rate conditions are allowing firms to structure alternatives with returns that were less frequent some years ago. The bet by Acciones & Valores, trii, and MPF Invest thus reflects the moment the local market is experiencing, where investors are reevaluating lower-volatility instruments as a mechanism to diversify and take advantage of current financial conditions in Colombia.
