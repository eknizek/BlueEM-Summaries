Brazil Legislative Agenda Stalled: Gasoline Tax, Debt Relief, and 6x1 Work Schedule in Limbo

30 April 2026 · 10:55 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Several important Brazilian legislative initiatives remain on hold following recent political developments. The government's planned reduction in federal gasoline tax, the Desenrola 2 debt relief program for individuals, and proposed reforms to the 6x1 work schedule are all pending discussion and approval. The political environment has created uncertainty around timing and passage of these measures, each with potential implications for consumer spending, employment policy, and fiscal management. These delays could impact household consumption and business operating costs across multiple sectors.
