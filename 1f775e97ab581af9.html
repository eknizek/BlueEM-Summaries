Kallpa begins commercial operation of Sunny Expansion

06 April 2026 · 08:46 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Kallpa Generación announced that Peru's National Interconnected System Economic Operating Committee (COES) has approved the commercial launch of its Sunny Expansion solar project starting April 3, 2026. This represents a significant milestone in Peru's renewable energy infrastructure expansion and reflects the country's continued investment in energy transition. The project will add capacity to Peru's grid and supports the government's decarbonization objectives.
