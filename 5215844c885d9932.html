Enter becomes Latin America's first AI unicorn, valued at R$6.4 billion

06 May 2026 · 11:24 AM  ·  O Globo (Brazil)
Enter has achieved unicorn status with a valuation of R$6.4 billion, becoming the first AI-focused company in Latin America to reach this milestone. This represents significant recognition of the region's growing AI innovation ecosystem and investor appetite for technology solutions. The valuation reflects strong demand for AI capabilities across Latin American businesses and positions Enter as a key player in the region's tech sector development.
