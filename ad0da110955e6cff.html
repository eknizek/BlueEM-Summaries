Aneel activates yellow tariff flag, electricity bills rise in May

24 April 2026 · 09:30 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
The National Electric Energy Agency (Aneel) has activated the yellow tariff flag for May, resulting in higher electricity bills for Brazilian consumers. This mechanism automatically increases costs when generation conditions are less favorable. The move signals tightening supply conditions or rising input costs in Brazil's power sector.
