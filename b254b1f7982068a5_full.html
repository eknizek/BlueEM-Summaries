# Jumex Aims to Expand in US and Launch Tequila Brand

Salvi Folch, general director of Jumex, revealed that the company is pursuing significant expansion in the United States and the launch of new product categories. Folch indicated that the plan is to establish itself as a relevant competitor in energy drinks, functional beverages, and alcoholic drinks, which is why they are about to launch Jumex with Tequila.

"Entering the alcohol category is a new avenue for growth. We started with the United States. We launched a base malt product to enable very broad distribution, but soon we will launch the Jumex Hard product with tequila. Mexican tequila with Mexican fruit. We believe it has a lot of strength as a product," he stated.

He advanced that in Mexico, tequila is more attractive, which is why "by June at the latest, Jumex with tequila will be on the Mexican market."

In an interview, Folch explained that one of the most ambitious pillars for the next five years is growth in the United States. Currently, exports represent less than 20% of its sales, but the company has deployed a team in U.S. territory to increase this figure.

"The United States must grow faster than Mexico simply because of the size of the market and the strength of our brand. The idea is to increase our market participation in America," he declared.

To achieve this, Jumex has relied on strategic alliances. Through its partner Arizona, the company already distributes products in the U.S., while in Mexico, Jumex produces for the American brand. Additionally, the company has relaunched the Odwalla brand to compete in the premium smoothies and juice segment.

The company has also strengthened its presence in energy drinks following the acquisition of the Boost brand from Tequila Cuervo last year. However, the most disruptive move is the entry into the alcoholic ready-to-drink beverage segment.

Folch also highlighted the exploration of categories with plant and animal proteins, as well as functional beverages with prebiotics and probiotics, following global health trends. Additionally, because the company works with fruit, it recently launched a line of gummies.
