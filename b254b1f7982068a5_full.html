# Beyond Juices: Jumex Plans US Expansion and Tequila Launch

Salvi Folch, general director of Jumex, revealed that the company is pursuing significant expansion in the United States and launching new product categories. Folch indicated that the plan is to establish itself as a relevant competitor in energy drinks, functional beverages, and alcoholic drinks, which is why they are about to launch Jumex with Tequila.

"Entering the alcohol market is a new avenue for growth. We started with the United States. We launched a malt-based product so it could be distributed very widely, but soon we will launch the Jumex Hard product with tequila. The tequila that is Mexican with Mexican fruit. We believe it has a lot of strength as such," he stated.

He advanced that in Mexico, tequila is more attractive, and therefore "by June at the latest, Jumex with tequila will be on the Mexican market."

In an interview, Folch explained that one of the most ambitious pillars for the next five years is growth in the United States. Currently, exports represent less than 20% of their sales, but the company has deployed a team in US territory to increase this figure.

"The United States must grow faster than Mexico simply because of the size of the market it has and the strength of our brand; the idea is to increase our participation in the American market," he stated in the interview.

To achieve this, Jumex has resorted to strategic alliances. Through its partner Arizona, the company already distributes products in the US, while in Mexico, Jumex produces for the American brand. Additionally, the company has relaunched the Odwalla brand to compete in the premium smoothies and juices segment.

**From the Gym to the Bar**

Diversification is the new rule of the game. Following the acquisition of the Boost brand from Tequila Cuervo last year, Jumex has strengthened its presence in energy drinks. However, the most disruptive bet is the foray into the alcoholic beverage segment (ready to drink).

Additionally, Folch highlighted the exploration of categories with plant and animal proteins, as well as functional beverages with prebiotics and probiotics, following global health trends. Also, because the company works with fruit, it recently launched a line of gummies.
