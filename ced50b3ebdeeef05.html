'No one will think about technology if they're hungry,' says Rio Grande do Sul innovation secretary

30 April 2026 · 10:35 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Rio Grande do Sul's secretary of innovation emphasized that technology adoption and development cannot be a priority when citizens face food insecurity, underscoring the state's economic challenges post-flood. The comment reflects the tension between long-term tech investment and immediate social needs in a crisis recovery context. Rio Grande do Sul has been working on technology sector development, but humanitarian concerns take precedence in the current environment.
