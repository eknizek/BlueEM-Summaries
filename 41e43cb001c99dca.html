Biomethane trucks now move ethanol waste at Cocal

06 May 2026 · 08:00 PM  ·  O Globo
Cocal, a major Brazilian ethanol producer, has begun using biomethane-fueled trucks to move ethanol waste streams, demonstrating circular economy integration in the renewable fuels sector. This operational shift reduces fossil fuel consumption in internal logistics while monetizing previously underutilized byproducts. The initiative aligns with Brazil's energy transition goals and signals growing adoption of alternative fuels across agribusiness supply chains.
