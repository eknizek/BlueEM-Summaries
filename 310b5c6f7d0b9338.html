General Fusion agrees to $1 billion SPAC merger, positioning it as first publicly traded fusion tech developer

22 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Twitter

The transaction reflects growing investor interest in nuclear fusion as a long-term energy transition solution. The capital raise positions General Fusion to accelerate commercialization of its fusion energy technology amid intensifying competition in the advanced energy sector.
