Atos drives Alsea's SAP modernization with a strategic migration to SAP RISE on AWS

08 April 2026 · 10:00 AM  ·  GlobeNewswire
Atos, a major IT services provider, is supporting Alsea, Mexico's largest restaurant operator, in a strategic modernization of its enterprise resource planning systems by migrating to SAP RISE on AWS cloud infrastructure. This represents a significant capital investment in digital transformation and operational efficiency for Alsea, which operates multiple restaurant brands across Latin America. The migration to cloud-based enterprise systems enables improved scalability, cost efficiency, and operational flexibility for the food service operator. Such technology upgrades are critical for retail and hospitality operators to maintain competitive advantage and integrate data across distributed operations.
