Atos drives Alsea's SAP modernization with a strategic migration to SAP RISE on AWS

08 April 2026 · 10:00 AM  ·  GlobeNewswire
Alsea, one of Latin America's largest restaurant and food service operators based in Mexico, is undergoing a strategic digital transformation with IT services provider Atos. The modernization involves migrating Alsea's enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems to SAP RISE, a cloud-native platform hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS). This strategic IT infrastructure upgrade enables Alsea to improve operational efficiency, scalability, and digital capabilities across its restaurant portfolio. The investment reflects growing adoption of cloud-based enterprise systems among major LatAm consumer/hospitality operators.
