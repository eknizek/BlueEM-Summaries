Brazil's agribusiness GDP rises 12.2% in 2025

29 April 2026 · 09:00 PM  ·  O Globo
Brazil's agribusiness gross domestic product grew 12.2% in 2025, reflecting robust performance across the agricultural, commodity, and food export sectors. This growth indicates strong demand for Brazilian agricultural products, improved commodity prices, and positive momentum in a sector critical to the country's exports and economy. The expansion supports investments in agricultural inputs, logistics, and food processing.
