Ultragaz Exits Distributed Energy Generation Market with Asset Sale

04 May 2026 · 03:22 PM  ·  Neofeed
Ultragaz, the energy company within Grupo Ultra, announced the sale of its distributed generation (DG) division to Veo Energia, exiting a market it entered four years ago during peak enthusiasm for the segment. The divestiture includes two photovoltaic solar generation facilities. The move reflects portfolio rationalization by the company and shifting market dynamics in Brazil's distributed energy sector.
