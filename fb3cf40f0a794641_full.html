Mexican Court approves UIF authority to freeze bank accounts

06 April 2026 · 09:10 PM

The Supreme Court of Justice approved today the authority of the Financial Intelligence Unit (UIF) to freeze bank accounts without a court order in any case in which it has "reasonable indicators" that there are funds of illicit origin.

The Court abandoned jurisprudence established in 2018 by its previous composition.
