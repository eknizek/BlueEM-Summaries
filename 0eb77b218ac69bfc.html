Fintech UY3 seeks R$ 3 billion to accelerate private payroll-deduction lending growth

05 May 2026 · 02:07 PM  ·  Neofeed
UY3, a Brazilian fintech specializing in payroll-deduction lending (consignado), is seeking R$ 3 billion in capital to accelerate growth in a segment that reached R$ 101.6 billion in total outstanding credit as of March 2025. The private consignado market expanded 142.4% year-over-year, driven largely by the government's Crédito do Trabalhador program launched a year prior. The capital raise underscores explosive growth in non-bank consumer credit and reflects strong investor appetite for specialty finance platforms in Brazil. UY3's expansion plans position it to capture significant market share in one of Brazil's fastest-growing credit segments.
