Fintech UY3 seeks R$ 3 billion to accelerate growth in private payroll loan market

05 May 2026 · 02:07 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazilian fintech UY3 is seeking R$ 3 billion in funding to accelerate its presence in the private payroll loan (consignado) market. Brazil's Central Bank data shows private payroll lending reached R$ 101.6 billion in March with 142.4% year-on-year growth, driven largely by the government's Crédito do Trabalhador program launched one year prior. The capital raise reflects intense competition and rising demand in this high-growth credit segment among non-bank lenders.
