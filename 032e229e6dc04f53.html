Fami acquires brokerage firm and targets corporate insurance

13 April 2026 · 08:40 PM  ·  Neofeed
Fami Capital, a Brazilian investment firm with over R$75 billion in assets under custody, announced the acquisition of Made4Life Corretora de Seguros e Consultoria Empresarial to expand its corporate insurance vertical. The move is part of Fami's broader strategy to transform its operations into a comprehensive financial ecosystem serving entrepreneurial families and companies. This acquisition strengthens Fami's position in the corporate insurance segment and demonstrates its commitment to building an integrated financial services platform.
