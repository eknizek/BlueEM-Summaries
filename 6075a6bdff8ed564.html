Peru's SBS and BCR sign cooperation agreements on payments and open finance

05 May 2026 · 08:12 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
The Superintendence of Banking, Insurance and AFP (SBS) and the Central Reserve Bank (BCR) of Peru signed two cooperation agreements aimed at strengthening interagency coordination and advancing the country's payments system and open finance (fintech) infrastructure. These regulatory moves reflect Peru's commitment to modernizing its financial system and supporting digital financial inclusion. The initiatives are material for financial services firms and fintech companies operating in Peru.
