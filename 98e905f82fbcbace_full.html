# More than 4,500 commerce entrepreneurs to meet next week in Cali

More than 4,500 entrepreneurs and leaders from the commerce sector will meet on April 21, 22, and 23 in Cali at the 'Retail del Futuro 2026' event, organized by Fenalco Valle del Cauca.

This edition, which will take place at the Valle del Pacífico Events Center, will feature guests from more than 11 countries who will address macro trends in commerce, as well as 250 supplier brands that will present innovative solutions in technology, analytics, logistics, artificial intelligence, and payment methods, among others.

"In a context where consumers change faster than organizations, Retail del Futuro 2026 consolidates itself as a key space for companies to anticipate trends, understand the new consumer, and design competitive strategies in an increasingly digital and dynamic environment," the industry group noted.

In partnership with the Valle del Cauca Governorate, the E-commerce HUB Hall will be present, featuring an exclusive Academic Agenda on topics around e-commerce, dropshipping, and other subjects of interest to digital entrepreneurs and startups.

Additionally, there will be a business networking session designed to connect supply and demand of the business ecosystem through more than 2,500 business meetings, generating opportunities for strategic alliances, new suppliers, and business expansion.

Among the speakers who will be present are figures such as Argentine consumption trends and brand strategy expert Ximena Díaz Alarcón, artificial intelligence specialist applied to commerce Simón Contreras, and technology communicator Joan Cwaik. They are joined by other experts from global companies such as HubSpot, Oracle, TikTok, Snowflake, XBREIN, and BlackSip.
