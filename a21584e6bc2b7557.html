Opinion: Why the 1% of ETF Assets Matters—a Lot

07 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Brazil's exchange-traded fund (ETF) market has reached R$100 billion in assets under management, representing just 1% of the nation's R$10 trillion fund industry but marking a significant 22-year journey from the 2004 launch of PIBB11, the first Brazilian ETF. While still a small slice of the broader asset management landscape, this milestone signals growing retail and institutional adoption of passive and index-tracking strategies. For LatAm asset managers and investors, the ETF milestone reflects structural shifts toward lower-cost, transparent investing vehicles and the potential for continued industry consolidation and product innovation.
