Why the 1% ETF Milestone Matters Significantly for Brazilian Fund Markets

07 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Brazilian ETFs have reached R$100 billion in assets under management, equivalent to 1% of the R$10 trillion Brazilian fund industry, achieving a milestone 22 years after the launch of PIBB11 in 2004. While numerically small, this represents accelerating adoption of passive investment strategies and a structural shift in how Brazilians access capital markets. For LatAm asset managers and investors, this signals growing institutional adoption of low-cost index strategies and potential displacement of higher-fee active funds.
