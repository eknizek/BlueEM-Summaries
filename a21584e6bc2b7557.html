Why the 1% of ETFs matters significantly for Brazil's investment market

07 April 2026 · 09:50 AM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Brazil's exchange-traded fund (ETF) market has achieved a milestone of R$100 billion in assets under management, representing 1% of Brazil's total R$10 trillion asset management industry. This growth took 22 years since the launch of the first Brazilian ETF (PIBB11) in 2004. While seemingly modest in percentage terms, this milestone reflects significant maturation of Brazil's capital markets infrastructure and growing investor appetite for passive investment vehicles, with implications for portfolio construction and asset allocation strategies in Brazil's investment industry.
