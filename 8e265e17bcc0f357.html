Only 8% of Colombia's mining executives satisfied with Petro government support

16 April 2026 · 10:08 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
According to the latest Brújula Minera (Mining Compass) report, only 8% of Colombian mining executives express satisfaction with government support under the Petro administration. Perceived conflictivity in mining reached 66%, reflecting significant tension between industry and policy. This sentiment shift indicates regulatory challenges, policy uncertainty, and strained public-private relations in Colombia's mining sector, a critical concern for investors in metals, minerals, and extractive industries.
