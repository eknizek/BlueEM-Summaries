Goodbye, intermediaries: Nuevi will process card payments with its own technology

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Nuevi, a Mexican fintech company, has obtained regulatory authorization to process card payments directly using its own proprietary infrastructure and technology. This development eliminates the need for traditional payment processing intermediaries, positioning Nuevi as a direct participant in Mexico's payment ecosystem. The company's move reflects broader fintech disruption of legacy payment networks and represents a competitive challenge to traditional card processors and acquiring banks in Mexico.
