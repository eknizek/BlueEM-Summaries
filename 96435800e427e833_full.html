# Bitcoin leads investment rankings for second consecutive month in Brazil

For the second consecutive month, bitcoin has topped the ranking of most profitable investments in the month of April, with an appreciation of 7.98%. However, in the year-to-date accumulated results, the world's most famous cryptocurrency ranks at the bottom of investments, accumulating a devaluation of 21.67%.

Right behind the digital currency, the group of stocks that make up the Ifix, B3's real estate investment fund index, positioned itself best in April's ranking among variable income modalities, advancing 1.53%.

In last place among investments in the fourth month of the year was the group of stocks that are part of the Icon, B3's consumption index, which fell 5.27% during the period.

After leading the ranking of most profitable investments in March, bitcoin returned to the top of the podium for April investments with a 7.98% appreciation. However, in year-to-date accumulated performance, the world's most famous cryptocurrency ranks last among investments with a 21.67% devaluation.
