Chile Targets Faster Permits to Unlock $100B Investment Pipeline

20 April 2026 · 04:51 PM  ·  Mining.com
The Chilean government is implementing measures to streamline environmental and operational permitting processes for mining projects, aiming to unlock approximately $100 billion in stalled investment. The initiative targets faster approval cycles to restore investor confidence and job growth in the copper mining sector, where Chile holds the world's largest reserves. Accelerated permitting is viewed as critical to competing with other mining jurisdictions and maintaining Chile's position as the global leader in copper production.
