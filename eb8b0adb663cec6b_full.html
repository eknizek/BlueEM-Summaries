# GPA announces three new executive board members

GPA – Grupo Pão de Açúcar announced on Thursday (16) the arrival of three new members to the company's executive board.

José Rafael Vasquez will be the new executive director of commercial, digital and marketing. Throughout his career, Vasquez has worked at groups such as Carrefour Brasil, where he held the position of CEO of Sam's Club Brasil and Carrefour Retail division, as well as Walmart, Cencosud and Pague Menos.

Jonas Laurindvicius will be executive director of supply chain. He was previously CEO of Grupo DPSP.

Jorge Jubilato assumes the executive directorship of human resources and sustainability.
