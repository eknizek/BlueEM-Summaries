After rebrand, BOOM targets international markets with "Brazilian Peloton"

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
BOOM, a Brazilian online fitness platform previously known as Academia Foguete, has completed a rebranding effort and is now targeting international expansion. The platform offers a localized alternative to Peloton, with backing from notable influencers and celebrities. The international expansion strategy aims to export the Brazilian fitness-tech model globally while building on domestic success.
