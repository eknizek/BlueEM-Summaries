Oscar Lería, Maratué Founder: 'This Is a Project for the Middle Class, the Great Forgotten'

03 May 2026 · 01:02 AM  ·  Diario Financiero
Oscar Lería, founder of Maratué, discussed Chile's largest residential development initiative currently under development, emphasizing its focus on middle-class and underserved populations. The son of an immigrant who began as a shoe factory worker, Lería detailed his background while explaining the urgency and mission behind launching social housing units at the Puchuncaví-based project. The initiative represents a significant real estate undertaking addressing housing demand in Chile's residential market.
