SAG declares bird flu alert in Los Lagos Region following infected swan discovery

24 April 2026 · 10:19 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Chile's agricultural regulator (SAG) has declared an avian influenza alert in the Los Lagos Region following the discovery of an infected swan in the Piedra Azul sector of Puerto Montt. The agency has activated epidemiological protocols to contain potential spread of the virus. Poultry farm operators are being urged to strengthen biosecurity measures to prevent transmission to commercial flocks, which could impact Chile's poultry and egg production sectors.
