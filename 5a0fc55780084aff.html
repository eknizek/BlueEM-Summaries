Banorte relaunches youth strategy after 1.3 billion peso loss from Bnext exit

13 May 2026 · 12:57 AM  ·  Expansión - Economía
Banorte, Mexico's second-largest bank, is refocusing efforts on the youth market through a new digital application following a significant 1.3 billion peso loss from its exit of the Bnext fintech venture. The bank aims to recapture younger demographics by offering integrated financial products including credit and debit cards alongside investment services through the app. This strategic pivot reflects Banorte's commitment to digital banking and generational customer acquisition despite previous setbacks in fintech ventures.
