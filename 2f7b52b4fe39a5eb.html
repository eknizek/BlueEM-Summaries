Ecopetrol in final stages to enter Brava, preparing partial tender offer

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Colombia's state oil company Ecopetrol is completing preparations to enter the capital structure of Brava, a Brazilian oil producer, and is planning a partial tender offer. The transaction would give Ecopetrol a material stake in the Brazilian upstream asset. This cross-border oil and gas investment represents strategic portfolio diversification for Ecopetrol in Latin American hydrocarbon production.
