Venezuela Launches First Technology Week in Caracas: Seeks to Integrate Tech Ecosystem with Latin America and Attract Investment

22 April 2026 · 08:16 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Tech
Venezuela is organizing its inaugural Technology Week event scheduled for May 11-13, 2026 in Caracas, designed to convene investors, technology companies, and public sector stakeholders. The initiative seeks to integrate Venezuela's technology ecosystem with broader Latin American innovation networks and position the country as an attractive destination for tech investment. The event reflects efforts to rebuild Venezuela's participation in regional technology and innovation dialogue.
