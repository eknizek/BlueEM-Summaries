# Oncoclínicas Creditors Hire Houlihan as Financial Adviser

Oncoclínicas creditors hire Houlihan as financial adviser. The group is formed mainly by debenture holders, with approximately R$ 1 billion in credits.

By Maria Luíza Filgueiras — São Paulo

The financial instability and governance issues at Oncoclínicas have led a group of creditors to hire a financial adviser to prepare for a potential renegotiation, according to reporting by Pipeline. The bank Houlihan Lokey will advise this group, composed mainly of debenture holders, with approximately R$ 1 billion in credits. The group also hired Padis Sociedade de Advogados as legal adviser.

As Valor reported yesterday, Oncoclínicas currently has cash reserves for only 15 days of operations and has already delayed patient treatments due to lack of medications. Amid a clash between shareholders and divisions on the board, creditor concerns are growing about a potential extrajudicial or judicial recovery.
