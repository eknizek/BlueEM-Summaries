# Oncoclínicas Creditors Hire Houlihan as Financial Adviser

The financial instability and governance issues at Oncoclínicas have prompted a group of creditors to hire a financial adviser to prepare for potential negotiations, according to reporting by Pipeline. Investment bank Houlihan Lokey will advise this group, which is primarily composed of debenture holders with approximately R$ 1 billion in outstanding credits. The group has also retained Padis Sociedade de Advogados as legal adviser.

As reported by Valor yesterday, Oncoclínicas currently has sufficient cash for only 15 days of operations and has already delayed patient treatments due to medication shortages. Amid disputes between shareholders and rifts within the board of directors, creditors' concerns are growing about a potential out-of-court or judicial restructuring.
