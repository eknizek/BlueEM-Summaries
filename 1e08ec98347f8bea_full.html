# Trump Administration Poses 80 Questions to Brazil to Decide on Tariffs

By Assis Moreira, Valor — Geneva

The Donald Trump government has posed 80 questions to Brazil regarding alleged commercial practices of the country, in a recent bilateral consultation within the framework of an investigation opened by the USTR, the American trade representation agency.

The investigation by USTR into the country's alleged commercial practices is expected to be concluded by July.
