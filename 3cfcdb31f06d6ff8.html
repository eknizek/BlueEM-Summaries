Mexican authorities sue 53 LPG distributors for 13 billion pesos in damages

24 April 2026 · 11:00 AM  ·  Reforma (Mexico)
Mexico's National Water Commission (CNA) initiated a class-action lawsuit against 53 liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) distribution companies, seeking damages exceeding 13 billion Mexican pesos accumulated over a 10-year period. The legal action targets widespread harm to consumers caused by the gas companies' practices. This represents a significant regulatory enforcement action in the Mexican energy distribution sector.
