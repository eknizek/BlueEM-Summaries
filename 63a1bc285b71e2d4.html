BNDES backs another corn ethanol project

16 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  LatinFinance
Brazil's BNDES (National Development Bank) has approved financing for ALD Bioenergia Deciolândia to expand its corn ethanol production capacity by more than 100%. The project strengthens Brazil's renewable fuel sector and supports the country's energy transition goals. Corn ethanol is a key alternative to sugarcane ethanol and offers diversification in biofuel production. This financing demonstrates continued government support for bioenergy infrastructure development in Brazil.
