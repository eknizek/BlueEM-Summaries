# New Vehicle Registrations Grew 54% in April and Exceeded 100,000 Units in 2026

The automotive market in Colombia maintained its recovery dynamics during April 2026. According to the latest report presented by Fenalco and ANDI, 26,787 vehicle registrations were recorded that month, representing a 54% growth compared to the same period last year.

With this result, the cumulative total between January and April reached 100,446 units, equivalent to a 49.3% increase compared to 2025, confirming a sustained recovery trend in the automotive sector.

## Boost from Electric Vehicles, Hybrids, and SUVs

Market performance was marked by growth in alternative technologies. In April, electric vehicle registrations reached 5,192 units, with a 304% increase compared to the same month last year. Meanwhile, hybrid vehicles reached 7,476 units, with a 76% growth.

In the year-to-date accumulation, electric vehicles totaled 14,541 registrations, with a 207% increase, while hybrids reached 27,238 units, representing a 73.4% increase.

By segments, SUV-type vehicles led market expansion, with a 73.2% growth compared to April 2025. Passenger commercial vehicles also stood out, growing 71%, and cargo commercial vehicles increased 40.5%.

In terms of accumulated volume, SUVs concentrated 59,716 units between January and April, followed by automobiles with 22,338 and pickup trucks with 5,539 units.

## Top Brands and Models

During April, the five brands with the highest number of registrations were Kia, Renault, Tesla, Mazda, and Chevrolet, which together represented 47.7% of the month's market. Kia led the list with 12.3% market share, followed by Renault with 10.5% and Tesla with 9.9%. Mazda and Chevrolet completed the top five with 7.6% and 7.5% shares, respectively.

In the year-to-date accumulation, leadership remains with Kia, Renault, Mazda, Chevrolet, and Toyota, which concentrate 47.1% of registrations in 2026.

By model lines, the Tesla Model Y had the highest participation in April with 8.5% of the total, followed by Renault Duster (4.0%), Kia K3 (3.4%), Mazda CX-30 (2.9%), and Mazda 2 (2.5%). These five models represented 21.2% of monthly market.

In the year-to-date accumulation, the Tesla Model Y also occupies first place with 4.0% participation, followed by Renault Duster, Kia K3, Kia Picanto, and Mazda CX-30, which together account for 17.1% of total registrations in 2026.

## Growth by Cities and Regions

Market dynamism was reflected in several cities across the country. Bogotá led growth in April with a 108% increase in registrations, followed by Medellín (Valle de Aburrá) with 75.9% and Cali with 40.7%. Other cities like Villavicencio and Armenia registered increases of 67.8% and 68.2%, respectively, while Rionegro grew 54.2%.

In the year-to-date accumulation, Bogotá totals 28,922 registrations, representing a 104.8% growth, while Antioquia reaches 19,463 units with a 58.6% increase.

By departments, besides Bogotá and Antioquia, Valle del Cauca stands out with a 48.8% growth, Atlántico with 51.8%, and Meta with 57.6%.

In contrast, some territories showed negative variations in the accumulated period, such as Cundinamarca with a 3.3% decline and Caldas with a 2.3% decrease.

The report also shows that within metropolitan areas, Funza, Bucaramanga, and Barranquilla maintain growth above 19%, while municipalities like Chía and Mosquera recorded declines in the analyzed period.
