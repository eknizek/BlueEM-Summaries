Ibovespa falls as Petrobras drops 4.9% after Strait of Hormuz reopens

17 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
The Ibovespa declined following a 4.9% drop in Petrobras shares, triggered by the reopening of the Strait of Hormuz, a critical global oil shipping route. The reopening typically signals easing of geopolitical tensions and potential stabilization or reduction in oil prices, which negatively impacts oil exporters like Petrobras. This move reflects the sensitivity of Brazilian equity markets and energy stocks to global crude oil dynamics and geopolitical developments.
