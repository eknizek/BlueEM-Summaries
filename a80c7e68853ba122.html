Colombian Veronorte seeks $35M from Latin American investors to fund US startups

04 May 2026 · 09:34 PM  ·  Portfolio - Negocios
Veronorte, a Colombian venture capital firm, is raising $35 million to invest in early-stage US startups, according to cofounder Camilo Botero. The firm has secured four commitments out of 18 targeted from Latin American investors. This fundraising effort reflects growing appetite among LatAm institutional investors to gain exposure to US tech innovation through regional managers.
