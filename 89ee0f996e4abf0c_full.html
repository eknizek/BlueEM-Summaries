# Argentina: Blue Dollar Exchange Rate Today Monday May 4

Find out what the blue dollar is trading at on May 4.

The blue dollar exchange rate today, Monday, May 4 in Argentina is $1385 for purchase and $1405 for sale.

## What is the official dollar rate in Argentina?

The official dollar is trading at $1375 for purchase and $1425 for sale according to Argentina's Banco Nación.

The official dollar, also known as the retail dollar, represents the reference exchange rate in Argentina. This value corresponds to the US dollar exchange rate without additional markups or taxes, and is used as the basis for legal foreign exchange operations in the country. The official dollar is the price at which most exports are settled, except those included in special regimes established by the National Government.

## What is the credit card dollar rate?

In Argentina, the credit card dollar is trading at $1787.5 for purchase and $1852.5 for sale.

The credit card dollar applies to purchases made with credit or debit cards in foreign currency, either abroad or when paying international services from Argentina. Currently, following the elimination of the PAIS tax, its exchange rate is calculated by adding a 30% withholding on account of Income Tax to the value of the official retail dollar, as established by current regulations.

## What time does the dollar open?

The official dollar in Argentina through the Central Bank (BCRA) releases the value and exchange rate of the currency starting at 10 AM. Therefore, the blue dollar reference value is given a few hours later between 11 and 11:30 AM.

## What time does the official dollar close and how does the blue dollar operate?

The official dollar exchange rate, regulated by the Central Bank of the Argentine Republic (BCRA), is updated during banking hours and closes each business day at 3:00 PM.

In contrast, the blue dollar, operating in the informal or parallel market, has no official opening or closing time, and its value can fluctuate freely throughout the day according to supply and demand.

## What is the blue dollar?

The Blue Dollar is the exchange rate of the US currency that is bought and sold outside the formal circuit. It is traded in unofficial places and is also known as the parallel dollar or informal dollar. The exchange rate and price of the Blue Dollar can vary and is not regulated by the government.
