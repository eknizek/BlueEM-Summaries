VOX Invests R$10.8M in Agrorobotics Using Laser and AI for Soil Analysis

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
VOX, a venture capital manager, has led a seed round of R$10.8 million (approximately $2.1 million USD) in Agrorobotics, an agricultural technology startup that merges laser technology with artificial intelligence to analyze soil conditions. The investment marks the first commitment from VOX's fourth fund, which is being managed in partnership with CVC BB Impacto I, a corporate venture vehicle backed by Banco do Brasil. Agrorobotics addresses precision agriculture demand by providing farmers with advanced soil data and insights. The backing from both a dedicated VC fund and a strategic corporate investor signals strong market validation for agtech solutions in Brazil.
