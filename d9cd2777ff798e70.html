'Money Under the Mattress': Argentines Resist Milei's Appeal, Keep $170 Billion at Home

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Despite President Javier Milei's efforts to encourage Argentines to deposit cash into the formal banking system, households continue to hold an estimated $170 billion in physical currency outside regulated financial institutions. This hoarding behavior reflects deep-seated mistrust of the banking system rooted in Argentina's history of financial crises and currency instability. The situation constrains the government's monetary policy effectiveness, limits banking system liquidity for lending, and signals weak confidence in economic stabilization efforts, presenting challenges for credit availability and investment financing across the economy.
