"Money Under the Mattress": Argentines Resist Milei's Appeal, Keep $170B Outside Financial System

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Despite President Milei's 'Amnesty Program' designed to bring unofficial dollar holdings back into Argentina's financial system, deposits have grown by less than $1 billion since February 2024. The persistent reluctance reflects deep-rooted distrust stemming from Argentina's 2001 economic crisis and subsequent financial collapses. This large shadow economy ($170 billion) represents both a drag on monetary policy effectiveness and potential untapped tax revenue.
