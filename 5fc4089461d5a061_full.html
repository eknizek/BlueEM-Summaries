# PFalimentos adds Claudio Melandri, former Banco Santander president, to board

With adjustments to its board of directors, Productos Fernández held its ordinary shareholders' meeting on Wednesday, highlighting the appointment of Claudio Melandri as a new director of the company, replacing Esperanza Fernández, granddaughter of Manuel Arturo Fernández Godoy (son of PFalimentos' founder), who is leaving the board.

Melandri, who joined Banco Santander in 1990 and had an ascending career at the institution, led a key stage as general manager of Santander Chile and later as country head of the Santander Group in the country. He served as president of the board of directors of Banco Santander Chile until March of this year, leaving the entity after 37 years with the Spanish capital group.

"On behalf of the entire board and PFalimentos, we welcome Claudio Melandri. His trajectory and vision will contribute to corporate governance, the company's sustainable growth strategy, and the development of new businesses," the board said, according to a statement from the national multifood company.

In addition to Melandri, the shareholders' meeting approved the incorporation of Max Besser to the board, who served as general manager of the company until October 2025, replacing María Carolina Chacón, who is leaving the board.

The board also thanked Esperanza Fernández and María Carolina Chacón, who are leaving the board, "for their commitment and valuable contribution during the time they were part of this body."

Fernández commented: "My work on the board has been an honor and a responsibility that I assumed committed to the family legacy. Today the company integrates new perspectives that, I am sure, will contribute to its growth."

Thus, the PFalimentos board is now composed of Claudio Fernández, Manuel Fernández, Sergio Barrientos, Max Besser, and Claudio Melandri.
