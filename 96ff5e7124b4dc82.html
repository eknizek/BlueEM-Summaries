From Pérgola to Luxury: Brazil's Best-Selling Wine Finances Campestre's Premium Bet

18 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Campestre, Brazil's most-sold wine brand, is using cash flows from its successful Pérgola line to finance entry into the luxury wine segment. This strategy reflects a classic portfolio approach: leveraging scale and distribution from mass-market products to fund upmarket expansion. The move addresses growing premiumization trends in Brazilian wine consumption. It positions Campestre to capture higher-margin opportunities while maintaining its core market leadership.
