Elevify raises $1.35M seed funding to become the Spotify of education

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Elevify, an education technology startup, has successfully raised $1.35 million in seed-stage funding, supported by Malta government backing. The company positions itself as a music streaming-style platform for education (the 'Spotify of education'), offering innovative learning solutions. The capital will accelerate Elevify's global expansion ambitions and support product development as it scales internationally, tapping into growing investor appetite for edtech solutions.
