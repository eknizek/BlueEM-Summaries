New vehicle registrations grew 54% in April and exceeded 100,000 units in 2026

02 May 2026 · 07:50 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's new vehicle registrations jumped 54% in April 2026, exceeding 100,000 units year-to-date, according to industry reports from Fenalco and ANDI. This recovery signals economic resilience in Colombia and creates opportunities for automotive dealers, manufacturers, and related supply chain participants.
