Michael Clark's Statement to CMF: 'I Do Not Participate in the Ownership of Any Companies in the Sartor Group'

10 May 2026 · 11:30 AM  ·  La Tercera - Pulso
Michael Clark, former president of Azul Azul (a Chilean sports club), submitted a declaration to Chile's Financial Market Commission (CMF) on December 10, 2024, in connection with the Sartor financial group investigation. According to the article, Clark stated he does not participate in ownership of any Sartor group companies. The timing is notable: this declaration was made only three days before Clark acquired control of Azul Azul. Clark was interrogated for approximately two hours but was apparently not questioned about the Azul Azul acquisition during that session.
