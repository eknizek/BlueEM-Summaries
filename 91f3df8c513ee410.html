OpenAI Wants to Go Public. First Sarah Friar Needs to Get It to Grow Up.

02 May 2026 · 01:00 AM  ·  Wall Street Journal - Tech
OpenAI is preparing for a potential initial public offering, one of the most anticipated tech IPOs in years. CFO Sarah Friar faces the challenge of maturing the company's financial and operational infrastructure while managing founder and CEO Sam Altman's expansive strategic ambitions. The article highlights the tension between growth investments, profitability milestones, and governance requirements for public company status. Friar's role involves not only financial management but also institutional development—building the systems and discipline a public company requires while maintaining the entrepreneurial culture that drives OpenAI's innovation.
