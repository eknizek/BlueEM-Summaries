Artificial intelligence and productivity: Brazil's SME adoption challenge ahead

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
At an AI economy forum in Brazil, a senior executive from iFood emphasized that artificial intelligence technology can address long-standing productivity constraints that have historically limited Brazilian small and medium-sized enterprises. The commentary highlights the potential for AI adoption to accelerate efficiency gains and competitiveness among Brazil's SME sector. The discussion underscores broader interest in how emerging technologies can level the playing field for smaller businesses in emerging markets.
