French Giant Air Liquide Acquires Brazil Operations in Respiratory Health and Home Care

08 April 2026 · 08:37 PM  ·  O Globo
Air Liquide, the French industrial gases and healthcare conglomerate, has completed an acquisition in Brazil focused on respiratory health and home care services. This M&A move strengthens Air Liquide's healthcare and medical gases presence in Latin America's largest economy, positioning the company to capitalize on growing demand for at-home care solutions and respiratory products in Brazil's aging population.
