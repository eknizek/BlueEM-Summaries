QXO Announces Investor Presentation Regarding Acquisition of TopBuild

20 April 2026 · 10:00 AM  ·  Company press release
QXO, a blank-check company, announced an investor presentation regarding its planned acquisition of TopBuild Corp, a leading distributor of building materials and insulation products in North America. The deal represents a significant SPAC merger combining QXO with an established building products platform. TopBuild serves residential and commercial construction markets, generating substantial revenue from the U.S. housing and renovation sectors.
