Colombian aviation aligns with regional trend: modern fleet reaches 38% with potential 11% emissions reduction

16 April 2026 · 10:28 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
According to an ALTA (Latin American and Caribbean Air Transport Association) report, Colombia's aviation sector is modernising its fleet, with 38% now classified as modern aircraft, aligning with regional decarbonisation trends. Fleet modernisation could reduce emissions by up to 11%. However, the sector continues to face challenges related to sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) availability and costs. This reflects broader energy transition dynamics and environmental regulatory pressures affecting the aviation and transportation sectors across Latin America.
