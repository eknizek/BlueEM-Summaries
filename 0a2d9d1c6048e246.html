Arequipa Projects 20% Tourism Growth in 2026: Which Sectors Does It Drive and How Does It Aim to Be a Southern Hub?

21 April 2026 · 05:31 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Peru's Arequipa region is positioned for significant tourism expansion, projecting 20% year-on-year growth in 2026 following 300,000 tourist arrivals in 2025. The growth momentum benefits hospitality, restaurants, and related service sectors, supporting Arequipa's ambition to become a leading tourism hub for southern Peru. However, infrastructure constraints remain a challenge to sustaining this growth trajectory. This represents opportunities for real estate, hotel operators, and hospitality-related investments in the region.
