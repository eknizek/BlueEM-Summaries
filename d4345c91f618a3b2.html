Fepasa cargo train carrying cellulose derailed near Chiguayante in Biobío

09 May 2026 · 06:24 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
A cargo train operated by Fepasa, Chile's freight rail operator, derailed near Chiguayante while transporting cellulose destined for a Biobío port. The incident occurred at 05:00 hours with no reported injuries. The accident disrupted both rail and vehicular traffic in the surrounding area, affecting regional logistics and transportation networks.
