# Why the Freedom of Choice Matters for Investors and How XP Translates It Into Different Service Models

As access to investments expands in Brazil, the investor's journey becomes more diverse and complex. Entering the market is only the first step. Over time, objectives change, the level of autonomy shifts, and the need for financial planning evolves, making the discussion about which service model makes sense at each stage of financial life increasingly relevant.

At XP, this diversity is treated as a starting point. The company offers different service and compensation models so that clients can choose the relationship format that best suits their moment, without needing to fit into rigid or standardized structures. Based on a complete ecosystem of financial solutions and an operation guided by excellence in service and consistency of delivery, XP reinforces the conviction that giving investors the power of choice is fundamental to building long-term relationships.

## Service Models That Follow the Investor's Journey

Currently, clients can choose between three service formats at XP: the transactional model, recommended for those who want to build a portfolio with the support of an advisor; the fixed fee model, which works as a monthly subscription that allows for progress in organizing financial life and medium and long-term planning; and consulting, which offers an integrated view of assets, even when distributed across multiple institutions.

This variety of formats allows each client to choose how they want to be served, how they want to compensate for the service, and to vary between the level of autonomy and depth they desire. All of this respects each investor's profile, who can transition between these formats depending on their life stage.

Because just as in life, there are those who prefer to decide alone most of the time, those who seek constant monitoring, and those who move between these extremes as life changes. And life changes quickly: family formation, new goals, moments of greater financial complexity. The models exist precisely to accompany these transitions.

The one thing that doesn't change is excellence in serving the investor, which for us at XP is the starting point. Governance processes, supervision, and excellence in allocation guide this journey and ensure consistency.

The investment professional plays a strategic role today. As a true financial planner for the client, they translate complexities and support life decisions. This role is strengthened by continuous training, investment in AI, and tools that expand analytical capacity.

It is precisely this vision of plurality that inspires the campaign launched by XP and starring tennis player João Fonseca, one of the main names of the new generation of Brazilian sports and an athlete sponsored by the company. In a light manner, the piece uses different types of courts to show that there is no single path. Each surface requires a different strategy. Each investor does too.

Having access to different service models is recognizing that priorities change, challenges arise, and important decisions may require different levels of support. As objectives evolve, the model can evolve alongside them. It is a natural development of the mission that has accompanied XP since the beginning: improving people's lives and helping Brazilians invest better.
