USA Rare Earth Boosts Western Supply with $2.8B Brazil Deal

20 April 2026 · 03:52 PM  ·  Mining.com
USA Rare Earth has announced a $2.8 billion transaction in Brazil aimed at strengthening rare earth supply chains for the Western world. The deal includes a 15-year offtake agreement with a special purpose entity financed by US government agencies and private investors, reflecting strategic government interest in securing critical mineral supplies outside China. This investment signals growing priority placed on developing rare earth production capacity in the Americas as part of broader supply chain resilience efforts.
