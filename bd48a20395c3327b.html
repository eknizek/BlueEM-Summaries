Mexican Banks Distributed Millions of Unwanted Credit Cards

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Mexican banks have distributed millions of credit cards that lack market demand, highlighting the persistent strength of cash-based payments in Mexico. Despite hundreds of millions of dollars in fintech investment and promotional efforts, the country's deeply entrenched cash culture continues to resist the shift toward digital and card-based payments. This dynamic reflects broader structural challenges in financial inclusion and digital payment adoption across Mexico's banking and fintech sectors.
