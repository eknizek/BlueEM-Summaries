Compass Launches IPO on B3 Ending Drought and Offering Up to R$ 5 Billion

08 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Compass, a natural gas company spun off from Cosan, launched its initial public offering on B3 with a potential valuation of up to R$ 5 billion. Trading under ticker PASS3 on the Novo Mercado segment, the deal marks a significant return of large-cap IPO activity to the Brazilian market after a prolonged drought. The offering prices shares at approximately R$ 35 per share with debut on the 11th.
