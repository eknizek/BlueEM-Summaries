# Mexico's Construction Sector Projected to Rebound This Year

In 2026, Mexico's construction sector is expected to experience a rebound driven by increased public spending on public works, which will grow 10.9 percent in real terms and could exceed 650 billion pesos, according to BBVA México.

In 2026, the housing sector is expected to have moderate growth, driven by housing projects and inventory that developers have on hand. The increased public spending is expected to boost the construction industry overall during the year.
