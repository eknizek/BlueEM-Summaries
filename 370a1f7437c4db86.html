CSN Announces Its Annual Report on Form 20-F for the Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2025

30 April 2026 · 10:01 PM  ·  Company press release
Companhia Siderúrgica Nacional (CSN), Brazil's major steel producer, has announced the filing of its annual report on Form 20-F with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2025. This regulatory disclosure provides comprehensive financial and operational results for CSN's iron ore, steel, and related businesses across the period. The filing is mandatory for foreign private issuers with U.S. securities listings and enables U.S. investors to assess the company's financial health and strategic direction.
