Can AI Replace Stock Managers? Most Models Lose Money in Competitions

09 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
A Bloomberg Línea analysis examines whether artificial intelligence can replace human equity portfolio managers by reviewing performance data from AI-driven investment models competing in industry benchmarks.
