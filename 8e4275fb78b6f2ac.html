Uber expands operations to all 32 departments and 1,100 municipalities of Colombia

15 April 2026 · 12:46 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Uber has expanded its mobility operations to cover all 32 Colombian departments and 1,100 municipalities, implementing an integrated model that combines taxis, private vehicles, and motorcycles. This nationwide expansion represents a significant scaling of Uber's presence in Colombia and demonstrates the company's commitment to serving both urban and underserved rural markets. The multi-modal approach addresses diverse transportation needs across the country's varied geography and population density.
