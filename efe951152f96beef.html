GPA Obtains Injunction to Block Casino Share Sale

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
GPA, a major Brazilian supermarket operator, has obtained a court injunction preventing Casino (the French retail conglomerate) from completing the sale of its stake in the company. This legal action reflects ongoing disputes over control and strategic direction of GPA. The injunction could materially impact the ownership structure and strategic options for the Brazilian retail business.
