# Bosch Finances R$ 29.7 Million with BNDES for Ethanol Engine Adaptation and Digitalization

The Bosch group has had financing approved in the amount of R$ 29.7 million by the National Bank for Economic and Social Development (BNDES) to invest in research and technological development, according to a statement released on Wednesday (5/6) by the bank.

One of the fronts is focused on decarbonization and aims to adapt heavy vehicle engines to operate with a mixture of diesel and ethanol without compromising their performance. Additionally, the resources will be allocated to the creation of personalized digital solutions.

The expectation is that the decarbonization technology will replace on average 35% of diesel consumption with ethanol in heavy vehicles.
