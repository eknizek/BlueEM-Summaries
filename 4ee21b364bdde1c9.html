Mid-Sized Companies Emerge From Shadows, Drive 22.5% Increase in Debt Issuance

09 April 2026 · 06:45 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazil's debt capital markets started 2026 at an accelerated pace compared to 2025, which closed with a record R$838.8 billion in public offerings. Mid-sized companies significantly increased their participation in debt issuance, growing 22.5% and emerging as major contributors to capital markets activity. According to Bloxs, an investment banking fintech platform, this trend reflects greater access to capital markets for companies previously reliant on bank credit. For LatAm investors, this indicates a structural shift toward capital markets financing in Brazil and potential opportunities in secondary market trading and covenant structures for mid-market issuers.
