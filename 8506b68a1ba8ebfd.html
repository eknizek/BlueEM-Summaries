Congress approves glacier law in win for Milei and copper miners

09 April 2026 · 02:42 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times
Argentina's Congress has approved a controversial glacier law that reduces environmental protections, a significant win for the Milei administration and the copper mining industry. This regulatory change removes barriers to mining in glaciated regions and could unlock new copper development projects. For LatAm investors, this represents a major policy shift enabling mining expansion in Argentina and potentially signaling regulatory openness to resource extraction.
