# Antonia Pellegrino Chosen to Lead EBC

Screenwriter Antonia Pellegrino was appointed by the government to serve as president of Empresa Brasil de Comunicação (EBC). The announcement was made on Friday evening (April 10). She will replace journalist André Basbaum, who held the position for approximately eight months.

According to the Minister of the Social Communication Secretariat, Sidônio Palmeira, Antonia brings together "management experience, editorial sensitivity, and deep knowledge of Brazilian audiovisual."

"Her trajectory leading EBC's content demonstrates the ability to innovate, expand reach, and reaffirm the strategic role of public communication for democracy, which is the core of our management at Secom," he stated.

With a background in audiovisual work and public management, she assumes command of EBC after serving since 2023 as Director of Content and Programming at the company. She holds a degree in Social Sciences and master's degrees in Literature and Public Administration.

The new EBC president also collaborated on the screenplay for the documentary "Democracia em Vertigem" (Democracy in Vertigo), which was nominated for an Oscar, and developed projects for various platforms, including the series "Amar É Para os Fortes" (Love Is for the Strong) in partnership with Marcelo D2, and the film "Manas" (2024).

Antonia is the wife of former president of the Brazilian International Tourism Promotion Agency (Embratur), Marcelo Freixo. He left his position in March to run for office. He is likely to run for federal deputy with the PT party in Rio de Janeiro.
