Only four provinces created jobs in January as 100,000 positions lost nationwide

18 April 2026 · 04:57 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Argentina experienced significant employment losses in January, with only four provinces reporting job creation while the rest of the country shed approximately 100,000 positions. The provinces that managed employment gains are primarily linked to sectors actively promoted by the government. However, these government-favored sectors have lower labor intensity compared to traditional industrial employment, suggesting that sector reallocation is occurring without offsetting job creation. This divergence highlights structural challenges in Argentina's labor market transition.
