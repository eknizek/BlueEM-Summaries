Cangallo is Peru's largest copper discovery in 20 years, according to AusQuest

05 May 2026 · 05:15 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
The Cangallo copper project in Arequipa, Peru is positioning itself as one of the most relevant mining discoveries in the country in recent decades. Michael Sherington, AusQuest's Chief Operating Officer for Peru, highlighted the project's appeal during proEXPLO 2026, emphasizing its large scale, strategic location, and promising initial drill results. The discovery has attracted significant industry attention as Peru seeks to maintain its position as a major copper producer. The project's development could have substantial implications for Peru's mining sector and AusQuest's portfolio.
