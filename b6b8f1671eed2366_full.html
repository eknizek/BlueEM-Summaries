Juan Lucas Vega appointed as new General Manager of Grupo Éxito

23 April 2026 · 02:33 PM

# Juan Lucas Vega Appointed as New General Manager of Grupo Éxito

Grupo Éxito announced that Juan Lucas Vega has been named the new general manager of the company in Colombia, replacing Carlos Mario Giraldo, who announced his retirement effective June 30.

"Thank you to Francisco and Carlos Calleja for their confidence in me. I assume this appointment with great gratitude and commitment. It is an honor to accompany from this new position the transformation process of Grupo Éxito in Colombia and the leadership of real estate in the region," said the new manager.

Vega is a civil engineer from the School of Engineering of Antioquia, with supplementary studies in Finance from NYU Stern School of Business in New York, and an MBA from Isead Business School in Spain. Since 2013, he has served as vice president of real estate for the company, a position in which he developed the business, converted the Viva brand into one of the most important shopping center brands in Colombia, and led expansion and retail transformation projects in the country.

"We are convinced that his appointment is a wise decision. We know him well and know that with his professionalism and capabilities he will lead a team of more than 30,000 people so that the company continues to grow and win the preference of our customers," said Francisco Calleja, president of the Board of Directors of Grupo Éxito.

The newly appointed manager will be responsible for directing real estate projects in Latin America, in the countries where Grupo Éxito has a presence: Colombia, Uruguay, and Argentina. According to the company, Vega will contribute his experience and new ideas and bring a new mark to the company.

"We know he is the right leader and it is a source of pride to know that he comes from our own company. We undoubtedly have a great team. His high competencies, his leadership, capacity, and exceptional qualities will allow us to continue advancing in the transformation and evolution of the company and in the strengthening of the real estate business in the region. This is a recognition of internal talent," affirmed Carlos Calleja, executive president of the Group.
