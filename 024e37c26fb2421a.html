New Desenrola Program Allows Up to 99% Discount on Fies Debt

03 May 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  Valor Economico
The Brazilian government has launched a new Desenrola program that allows borrowers with overdue Fies (federal student loan) debt to access substantial relief through discounts of up to 99%. This initiative aims to reduce the burden on households carrying education debt and improve financial inclusion. The program has broader implications for consumer credit dynamics and banking sector asset quality in Brazil.
