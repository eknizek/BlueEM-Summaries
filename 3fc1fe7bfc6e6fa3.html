Trade war with Ecuador: Colombia to raise tariffs on coffee, shrimp, rice and plastics from May

18 April 2026 · 03:31 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia's government is preparing a decree to impose tariffs of up to 75% on coffee, shrimp, rice, and plastics, escalating a trade dispute with Ecuador. This trade conflict will directly impact Colombian exporters in agriculture and agribusiness sectors and could affect regional supply chains and commodity prices.
