AES Colombia invests over $10 billion to bring electricity to 800 families in La Guajira

29 April 2026 · 10:19 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
AES Colombia announced investment of over 10 billion Colombian pesos to provide electrical access to 800 Wayuu households in La Guajira department. The initiative is linked to the company's planned wind energy projects in the region, addressing energy access gaps in underserved communities. This investment combines infrastructure development with renewable energy expansion, aligning with both social responsibility objectives and the company's long-term energy transition strategy.
