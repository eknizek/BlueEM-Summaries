Colgas Sets Pace in Energy Sector with Early Payments to SME Suppliers

20 April 2026 · 02:55 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colgas, a Colombian energy company, is differentiating itself by paying suppliers within 7 days, significantly shorter than the typical 60-90 day payment terms prevalent across the industry. This practice benefits small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) that rely on the company as a client, improving their working capital and liquidity. The initiative demonstrates a shift toward more supplier-friendly payment practices in Colombia's energy sector and reflects broader discussions around supply chain financing and corporate social responsibility in emerging markets.
