Federal Government Cuts Pemex Spending 56% But Damages Physical Investment

13 April 2026 · 09:17 PM

In the first two months of 2026, the federal government applied a 56 percent annual cut in spending to Pemex, but it affected physical investment, according to a new analysis by México Evalúa using data from the Treasury Department.

The difference between revenues and budgetary spending resulted in a deficit of 70 billion pesos, which represents a reduction of 37.5 percent in real terms compared to the previous year.
