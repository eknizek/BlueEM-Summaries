# Court Authorizes Oncoclínicas' Precautionary Measure Request

The court has approved Oncoclínicas' request for a precautionary measure against creditor collection for 60 days. The company ended last year with a debt of R$ 3.3 billion and exceeded its debt limit agreement, which could have accelerated the maturity of its liabilities.

The decision suspends executions by creditors who are in negotiations with Caixa, Itaú, Banco do Brasil, and Bradesco.

The approval is partial and does not cover all creditors requested by Oncoclínicas.
