Grupo Gloria's Trupal Plans Modernization Investment at El Agustino Plant

20 April 2026 · 09:35 PM

# Trupal de Gloria Prepares Investment to Modernize Its Plant in El Agustino

Trupal S.A., a company of Grupo Gloria dedicated to the manufacture of paper and cardboard, closed 2025 with a sales growth of 10% above projected figures and reached record production volumes at its plants. Given this, 2026 marks the starting point for new projects, which is why the Grupo Gloria company is preparing an investment to improve its processes.

Thus, the company presented a project aimed at improving its industrial wastewater treatment system at its plant located in El Agustino, Lima. The initiative seeks to optimize the environmental performance of its operations through the modernization of its treatment plant, in line with its operational efficiency and sustainability strategy, while also allowing increased treatment capacity and water resource reuse.

Through the Supporting Technical Report (ITS) presented to the Ministry of Production (Produce), the company announced the improvement and optimization project of the PTAR, which includes the implementation of new technological components such as an IFAS biological reactor, a lamella clarifier, and complementary treatment systems.

In detail, the project contemplates an approximate treatment capacity of 1,200 m³ daily, incorporating advanced processes that will allow treated water recirculation. This optimization seeks not only to increase system efficiency but also to strengthen sustainable water resource management within industrial operations.

Additionally, the initiative incorporates new technological components, such as an IFAS biological reactor, a lamella clarifier, and control and dosing systems, aimed at improving the quality of treated effluent. The project will be executed within the plant in El Agustino and, according to the ITS, will not generate significant environmental impacts nor substantially alter the current production process.

## Project Details

Within the framework of this project, whose objective is to optimize effluent quality and improve water resource management in its operations, the company contemplates the incorporation of key equipment such as an IFAS biological reactor, a lamella clarifier, and dosing and control systems, which will improve the removal of pollutant loads. Thus, the plant will have an approximate capacity of 1,200 m³/day.

"The project proposes a technological improvement of the treatment system that will allow optimization of water use in the production process," states the ITS presented by the company.

The project considers the optimization of the treatment system as part of a technological improvement of the plant's auxiliary system. In this regard, recirculation of treated water between 15% and 25% of total volume is anticipated. These improvements will allow reducing the pollutant load of effluents and ensure compliance with current environmental regulations, without generating significant environmental impacts.

## Timeline and Budget of Trupal's Project

In the plan presented to Produce, Trupal S.A. detailed the activity schedule for implementing the improvement of its Industrial Wastewater Treatment Plant (PTAR) at its El Agustino plant. From the bidding stage and logistical processes to stabilization and commissioning, the project comprises several phases, including environmental assessment, relocation of current equipment, equipment purchase, civil works, and installation of the new reactor.

Thus, the implementation process would take approximately 16 months.

The execution of the proposal involves an investment of US$ 1.64 million, while the annual cost of operation and maintenance was estimated at approximately US$ 721,000 and US$ 38,000, respectively. The PTAR will have a useful life of 15 years.
