Minera Poderosa Announces Appointments of New General Manager, Operations Manager, and Corporate Affairs Manager

Minera Poderosa has appointed Daniel Torres Espinoza as the new General Manager of the company, replacing Marcelo Santillana. Torres has a track record in the mining sector with more than 25 years of experience leading high-complexity operations. He has been part of Minera Poderosa for more than 9 years, where he has served as Operations Manager, a position from which he has driven operational efficiency by implementing continuous improvements for the benefit of operations.

Minera Poderosa emphasized that his appointment, which becomes effective on May 1, responds to an internal succession line that guarantees continuity in management, deep knowledge of operations, and alignment with the company's culture and strategic objectives. It should be noted that Santillana, after a successful career of more than 40 years in the organization, will continue serving his role as a member of the Board of Directors of Minera Poderosa. The company highlighted his legacy in operational strengthening, improved efficiency in mining units, and the consolidation of relationships with communities and stakeholders. "His management has been key to positioning Minera Poderosa as a relevant player in the sector," they stated.

Meanwhile, Pio Carrasco will take on the position of Operations Manager replacing Torres. Carrasco has a career spanning more than 20 years in the sector. Finally, the company also welcomes Luis Vásquez Madueño, who will assume the position of Corporate Affairs Manager effective May 1. Vásquez has more than 25 years of experience in the hydrocarbon sector, bringing an important perspective to the organization.
