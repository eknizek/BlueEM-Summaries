# Minera Poderosa Announces Appointment of New General Manager of Operations and Corporate Affairs

Minera Poderosa has appointed Daniel Torres Espinoza as the new General Manager of the company, replacing Marcelo Santillana.

Torres has an extensive track record in the mining sector with more than 25 years of experience leading high-complexity operations. He has been part of Minera Poderosa for more than 9 years, where he has served as Operations Manager, a position from which he has driven operational efficiency by implementing continuous improvements for the benefit of operations.

Minera Poderosa emphasized that his appointment, which will take effect on May 1, responds to an internal succession plan that ensures continuity in management, deep knowledge of operations, and alignment with the company's culture and strategic objectives.

It should be noted that Santillana, after a successful career of more than 40 years with the organization, will continue in his role as a member of the Board of Directors of Minera Poderosa. The company highlighted his legacy in operational strengthening, efficiency improvements in mining units, and consolidation of relationships with communities and stakeholders. "His management has been key to positioning Minera Poderosa as a relevant player in the sector," they stated.

For his part, Pio Carrasco will take on the position of Operations Manager replacing Torres. Carrasco has a track record of more than 20 years in the sector.

Finally, the company also welcomes Luis Vásquez Madueño, who will take on the role of Corporate Affairs Manager effective May 1. Vásquez has more than 25 years of experience in the hydrocarbon sector, bringing important vision to the organization.
