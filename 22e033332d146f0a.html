Peru's banking regulator begins returning deposits to CRAC del Centro customers in liquidation

20 April 2026 · 08:13 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
The Peruvian Superintendency of Banking, Insurance and Private Pension Administrators (SBS) has initiated the return of deposits to customers of the Rural Credit and Savings Cooperative of the Center (CRAC del Centro), which was ordered into liquidation the previous week. The deposit reimbursement process is proceeding as part of the SBS's supervisory and consumer protection mandate. This action underscores ongoing oversight of Peru's financial system stability.
