Peru's Banking Regulator Initiates Deposit Repayment to CRAC del Centro Customers in Liquidation

20 April 2026 · 08:13 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
The Peruvian Superintendency of Banking, Insurance and Private Pension Fund Administrators (SBS) initiated deposit repayment to customers of Caja Rural de Ahorro y Crédito del Centro (CRAC del Centro), following the institution's recent liquidation order. The repayment process marks the formal resolution of the failed rural savings and credit cooperative. This regulatory action demonstrates Peru's deposit protection mechanisms and the SBS's oversight of the financial system. The liquidation reflects broader credit risk management and institutional supervision in Peru's financial sector.
