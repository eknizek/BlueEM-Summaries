BTG Pactual: Ecopetrol acquisition in Brazil strengthens portfolio but faces key challenges

25 April 2026 · 06:22 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Ecopetrol, Colombia's state-controlled oil company, is pursuing the acquisition of a 51% stake in Brava Energia, a Brazilian oil and gas company, for approximately $1.1 billion. The transaction represents a significant geographic diversification move for the Colombian upstream operator into the Brazilian market. The deal aims to strengthen Ecopetrol's portfolio and production base while presenting execution challenges typical of cross-border energy M&A in Latin America.
