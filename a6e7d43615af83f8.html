BTG Pactual: Ecopetrol acquisition in Brazil strengthens company but presents key challenges

25 April 2026 · 06:22 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Empresas
Ecopetrol, Colombia's state-controlled oil company, is acquiring a 51% stake in Brava Energia for approximately $1.1 billion USD. The deal represents a strategic expansion of Ecopetrol's geographic footprint into Brazil, enhancing portfolio diversification beyond Colombia. BTG Pactual analysis highlights that while the acquisition strengthens Ecopetrol's position, it carries execution risks and operational challenges. This M&A transaction is directly relevant to investors monitoring regional oil and gas consolidation, capital allocation by LatAm NOCs, and Ecopetrol's strategic direction.
