Mexico Extends Fuel Tax Stimulus for Diesel and Magna, Premium Excluded

25 April 2026 · 01:36 AM  ·  Reforma - Portada
Mexico's Finance Ministry (SHCP) has extended fiscal stimulus measures on diesel and Magna-grade gasoline for the third consecutive week, keeping prices at controlled levels. Premium-grade gasoline was excluded from the stimulus program. These measures reflect government efforts to manage inflation and consumer fuel costs amid global energy price pressures and geopolitical disruptions affecting energy markets.
