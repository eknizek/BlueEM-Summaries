Rio Grande do Norte Advances with R$3.8 Billion Sanitation PPP

17 April 2026 · 07:06 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Rio Grande do Norte is progressing with a significant public-private partnership (PPP) for sanitation infrastructure, with investments totaling R$3.8 billion. The tender for Caern (Rio Grande do Norte Water and Sewage Company) is expected in the second half of the year. This initiative follows a period of failed auctions earlier in the year, suggesting renewed momentum in Brazil's sanitation sector and investor appetite for infrastructure concessions.
