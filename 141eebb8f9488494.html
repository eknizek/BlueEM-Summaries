New open finance model set to revolutionize credit rules in Colombia with data-sharing requirements

13 April 2026 · 09:27 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia has issued Decree 0368 of 2026, establishing a new open finance framework that requires banks and financial entities to share customer data—subject to user authorization—under open technical standards. This regulatory shift aims to democratize access to credit and financial services by enabling fintech and alternative lenders to evaluate customer creditworthiness based on comprehensive financial data. The decree represents a significant modernization of Colombia's financial infrastructure, similar to open banking initiatives elsewhere in Latin America, and will reshape competitive dynamics in the lending and fintech sectors.
