New open finance model in Colombia: the silent revolution changing credit rules

13 April 2026 · 09:27 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia has enacted Decree 0368 of 2026, requiring banks and financial institutions to share customer data under open technical standards, subject to user authorisation. This open finance regulation aims to revolutionise credit markets by enabling alternative lenders and fintechs to access borrower information previously siloed within traditional banking. The framework is expected to increase competition, lower borrowing costs, and improve credit access for underserved segments of the Colombian market.
