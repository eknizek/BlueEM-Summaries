How the new open finance model will work: the silent revolution changing credit rules in Colombia

13 April 2026 · 09:27 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombian Decree 0368 of 2026 establishes mandatory open finance standards, requiring traditional banks and financial institutions to share customer transaction and credit data via standardized APIs with authorized third-party providers. This regulatory shift aims to democratize credit access, reduce information asymmetries, and enable fintech and alternative lenders to offer targeted products. The reform mirrors global open banking trends and has material implications for traditional bank margins, competitive dynamics, and credit market participation in Colombia.
