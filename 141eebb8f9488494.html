New Open Banking Model Revolutionizes Credit Rules in Colombia

13 April 2026 · 09:27 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia's Decree 0368 of 2026 mandates that banks and financial entities share customer financial data (with prior authorization) under standardized open banking protocols. This regulatory shift aims to democratize credit access by enabling alternative lenders and fintech firms to assess creditworthiness beyond traditional banking channels. The measure represents a material change to Colombian financial market infrastructure, reducing data monopolies held by incumbent banks and potentially expanding credit availability to underserved populations.
