American PayPal Now Offers Pix to Small and Medium Enterprises in Brazil

13 April 2026 · 04:28 PM  ·  O Globo
PayPal has expanded its Brazilian payment services to offer Pix, the central bank-operated instant payment system, directly to small and medium enterprises. This expansion allows Brazilian SMEs to accept Pix payments through PayPal's platform, improving payment options and reducing settlement times. The move reflects PayPal's strategy to deepen market penetration in Brazil's digital payments ecosystem and compete with local fintech providers.
