Energisa (ENGI11) announces R$18 billion in investments after signing distributor renewal

08 May 2026 · 09:55 PM  ·  MoneyTimes
Energisa announced R$18 billion in planned investments over the next five years following the Federal Government's approval of 30-year concession renewals for its electricity distribution subsidiaries in Mato Grosso, Mato Grosso do Sul, Paraíba, and Sergipe. The investment will prioritize grid expansion and modernization across the four states. This capital commitment reflects confidence in the long-term outlook for Brazil's energy distribution sector and Energisa's strategic growth plans.
