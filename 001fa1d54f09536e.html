Brazil's Petrobras decides not to exercise waiver rights over Novonor's stake sale in Braskem

23 April 2026 · 11:35 PM  ·  Reuters
Brazil's state-controlled oil and gas company Petrobras has decided not to exercise its contractual waiver rights regarding Novonor's sale of its stake in Braskem, a major petrochemical producer. This decision removes a potential hurdle to the transaction and signals acceptance of the change in ownership structure of the chemical company. The move has implications for Braskem's strategic direction and corporate governance in Brazil's chemicals sector.
