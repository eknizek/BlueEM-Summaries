Latin America Data Center Construction Market to Exceed USD 6.93 Billion by 2031, Supported by Google's $500M Digital Port Investment

23 April 2026 · 02:40 PM  ·  PR Newswire
The Latin American data center construction market is forecast to exceed USD 6.93 billion by 2031, driven by accelerating cloud adoption, AI infrastructure buildout, and hyperscaler investment. Google's USD 500 million Digital Port investment in the region is a significant catalyst, supporting regional digital transformation and positioning Latin America as a key node in global cloud infrastructure. This growth reflects structural demand from enterprises, fintech companies, and streaming services across the region. Telecom Argentina and other regional telecom operators benefit from increased infrastructure and interconnection opportunities.
