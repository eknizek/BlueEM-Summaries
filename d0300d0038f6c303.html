Brazilian Corn Ethanol Advances in International Maritime Fuel Regulations

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Brazilian corn ethanol has achieved a significant regulatory milestone at the International Maritime Organization (IMO), clearing an important approval stage for use as a marine fuel. This development positions Brazil favorably in the emerging biofuel supply market for oceangoing vessels, providing competitive advantage over the United States and other producers. The advancement removes a major regulatory barrier and enhances Brazil's prospects for expanding biofuel exports to the shipping sector.
