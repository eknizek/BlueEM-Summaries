US Appeals Court Voids $16.1 Billion Judgment Against Argentina Over YPF Seizure

06 April 2026 · 10:46 AM  ·  MSN
A US appeals court has voided a $16.1 billion judgment against Argentina stemming from the 2012 expropriation of oil company YPF by the Argentine government. This decision eliminates a major contingent liability for Argentina and reduces sovereign risk, improving the country's credit profile. The ruling also removes a long-standing source of bilateral tension with the US. For investors in Argentine sovereign debt and equities, this judgment reversal is a material positive development reducing enforcement risk and potential asset seizures.
