AI Levels Advertising Agencies but Does Not Replace Human Experience, Expertise, and Creativity

08 April 2026 · 10:58 AM  ·  Neofeed
This article profiles Euphoria Creative, an independent Brazilian advertising agency, which demonstrates how artificial intelligence is democratizing competitive capabilities in the creative services industry. The agency has executed campaigns for global brands including Netflix, Havaianas, and Uber while competing directly with much larger multinational groups. According to Euphoria's CCO and cofounder Marcelo Rizério, AI is fundamentally leveling the playing field, but human experience, repertoire, and creative judgment remain irreplaceable and differentiated. The trend illustrates broader technology disruption in creative and services sectors across Latin America, where AI adoption enables smaller firms to challenge established players.
