AI levels advertising agencies but does not eliminate human experience, repertoire and creativity

08 April 2026 · 10:58 AM  ·  Neofeed
Euphoria Creative, an independent Brazilian advertising agency without the financial muscle of global advertising conglomerates, has built a competitive track record creating campaigns for major brands (Netflix, Havaianas, Uber) and is gaining further competitive advantage through artificial intelligence deployment. Co-founder and Chief Creative Officer Marcelo Rizério discusses how AI is leveling the competitive playing field in advertising, allowing smaller, nimble agencies to punch above their weight in campaign quality and scope, though human experience, creative repertoire, and strategic thinking remain irreplaceable. The trend reflects broader AI-driven disruption and democratization of professional services in LatAm, offering growth opportunities for well-positioned tech-enabled service companies while creating cost pressure on legacy agency business models.
