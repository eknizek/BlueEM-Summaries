French Group Invests R$17 Million to Store Elite Artworks in São Paulo

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Horus Finance, a French art storage and logistics firm, is expanding its Brazilian footprint with a second state-of-the-art facility in Barueri, São Paulo, representing a R$17 million investment. The facility targets the ultra-high-net-worth and institutional art collector segment, with management forecasting 80% revenue growth over five years on current annual turnover of R$25 million. This expansion reflects surging demand among Latin American collectors for secure, climate-controlled art storage and professional asset management services.
