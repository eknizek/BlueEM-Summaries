Court blocks funds linked to Beto Louco and Primo under Genial Investimentos administration

04 May 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
São Paulo's judiciary has ruled to freeze assets of fuel distribution companies Aster and Copape, as well as personal assets of executives Roberto Augusto Leme da Silva (Beto Louco) and Mohamad Hussein Mourad (Primo). The action was initiated by São Paulo State's Attorney General as part of Operation Carbon Oculto, a criminal investigation into alleged fuel distribution irregularities. Assets held under Genial Investimentos' administration have been subject to the freeze. The case proceeds under judicial confidentiality orders.
