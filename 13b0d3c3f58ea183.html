Milei Incentives to Accelerate US$12-Billion Vaca Muerta Oil Development

25 April 2026 · 03:10 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times (Argentina)
Private driller Pluspetrol has announced plans to accelerate production from the Bajo del Choique-La Invernada project within Argentina's Vaca Muerta shale basin, leveraging President Milei's RIGI (Régimen de Incentivo a Grandes Inversiones) incentive framework. The US$12-billion Vaca Muerta field represents a critical asset for Argentina's energy sector and energy exports. RIGI offers fiscal and regulatory incentives designed to attract major investment in strategic projects. This acceleration reflects improved investor confidence in Argentina's energy sector under Milei's pro-business policies.
