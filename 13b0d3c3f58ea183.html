Milei Incentives Set to Boost US$12-Billion Vaca Muerta Oil Field

25 April 2026 · 03:10 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times
Pluspetrol, a private oil drilling company, announced plans to accelerate production at the Bajo del Choique-La Invernada field in Argentina's Vaca Muerta shale basin under President Milei's RIGI (Régimen de Incentivo a las Grandes Inversiones) incentive initiative. The $12-billion Vaca Muerta field represents a major growth opportunity in Argentina's energy sector, with fiscal incentives designed to attract capital investment. This reflects Argentina's strategy to increase hydrocarbon production and energy exports.
