Bradesco and Vibra invest R$ 15 million in corporate AI platform PX Data

15 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Brazilian banking giants Bradesco and Vibra have invested R$ 15 million in PX Data, a corporate AI platform offering intelligent agents through its Luria product. The platform has grown using internal resources and is now scaling to serve large enterprises seeking AI-powered automation and workflow solutions. The investment from major financial institutions validates the market opportunity and provides growth capital for the Brazilian AI startup to expand its enterprise customer base.
