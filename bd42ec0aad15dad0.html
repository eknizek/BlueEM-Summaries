A Big Bang for finance: Government seeks to deregulate capital markets with fewer procedures and faster issuances

30 April 2026 · 04:08 PM  ·  Clarin
Argentina's National Securities Commission (CNV) published a series of regulatory changes aimed at expanding capital market activity by streamlining procedures and accelerating issuance timelines. Key measures include automatic approval for certain operations to eliminate processing delays. The initiative, dubbed a "financial Big Bang," seeks to increase the volume of bond, equity, and mutual fund offerings and attract more issuers to Argentina's capital markets.
