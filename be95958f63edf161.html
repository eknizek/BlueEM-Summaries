GloboPop is new vertical video app for short stories and content creators

13 April 2026 · 11:17 AM  ·  O Globo
Globo has introduced GloboPop, a new application for vertical video content tailored to short-form narratives (novelinhas) and independent content creators. This app launch reflects Globo's strategic diversification into digital platforms and the creator economy. The initiative positions the media company to engage younger demographics and compete with other short-form video platforms in Brazil's growing digital content market.
