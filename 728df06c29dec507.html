Luciano Laspina: "Argentina needs basic political agreements more than structural reforms to build confidence"

18 April 2026 · 07:56 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Luciano Laspina, recently appointed executive director of CIPPEC (a major Argentine public policy think tank), argues that Argentina's primary challenge is building political consensus rather than pursuing structural reforms in isolation. He emphasizes the need to 'find common ground between the two Argentinas' that have historically opposed each other, and stresses that 'changes must be built from the foundations, not from the ruins.' His position reflects broader debate in Argentina about the sequencing of economic policy reforms and the necessity of political coalition-building for sustainable change.
