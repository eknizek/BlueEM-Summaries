Parliamentary group pushes to replace payroll tax with revenue-based contribution; explained

04 May 2026 · 07:03 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
A parliamentary coalition is advancing a proposal to fundamentally restructure Brazil's social contribution system by replacing employer payroll taxes with a broad-based revenue levy. The shift aims to reduce labor costs, enhance business competitiveness, and potentially improve employment incentives across the economy. The change represents a significant fiscal and structural reform with implications for corporate profitability, employment patterns, and government revenue. The proposal reflects ongoing debate over optimal tax design to balance social funding with economic growth and competitiveness objectives.
