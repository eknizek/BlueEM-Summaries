Alicorp pursues acquisitions in Colombia as part of mass consumption growth strategy, including Rama deal

24 April 2026 · 01:29 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Alicorp, Peru's leading consumer goods company, has filed a pre-evaluation request with Colombia's Superintendencia de Industria y Comercio (SIC) for the acquisition of Flora Food's food business division. This move is part of Alicorp's broader expansion strategy in Colombia's mass consumption market, following previous acquisitions including Rama. The transaction reflects Alicorp's aggressive regional consolidation strategy to strengthen its presence across key Latin American consumer markets.
