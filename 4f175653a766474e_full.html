J&F Announces Early Results of Exchange Offer

25 April 2026 · 12:59 AM

J&F S.A. announced early results of an exchange offer for Eldorado's 8.500% Senior Notes Due 2032. As of the early tender date of April 24, 2026 at 5:00 p.m. New York City time, US$464,722,000 in principal amount of the outstanding US$500.0 million in Existing Eldorado Notes have been validly tendered and not validly withdrawn, representing 92.94% of the notes outstanding. Related consents required to adopt proposed amendments to the indenture have been received.

A supplemental indenture giving effect to the proposed amendments has been executed and will become operative upon payment of the total exchange consideration, expected on or shortly after April 30, 2026 (the Early Settlement Date).

Eligible holders who validly tendered their notes by the early tender date will receive US$1,000 principal amount of new exchange notes and US$2.50 in cash per US$1,000 principal amount of existing notes, including an early exchange premium of US$50 in exchange notes and US$2.50 in cash.

The Company amended the exchange offer terms for holders tendering after the early tender date but before the May 11, 2026 expiration date. These holders will now receive US$1,000 principal amount of exchange notes (but not the cash portion of the early exchange premium) for each US$1,000 principal amount of existing notes tendered.

The new senior exchange notes will be issued by J&F Luxembourg Finance S.à r.l. and guaranteed by J&F S.A., Eldorado Brasil Celulose S.A., LHG Mining Ltda., and Flora Produtos de Higiene e Limpeza S.A.

The exchange offer and consent solicitation expire at 5:00 p.m. New York City time on May 11, 2026, unless extended. The company reserves the right to terminate, withdraw, amend or extend the offer in its sole discretion.

Documents are available through D.F. King & Co., Inc., the exchange agent, or the designated dealer managers: Banco Bradesco BBI S.A., Banco BTG Pactual S.A., and Citigroup Global Markets Inc.
