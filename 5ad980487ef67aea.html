The new dividend chess game: family offices recalculate strategy amid 10% minimum tax

04 May 2026 · 10:58 AM  ·  Neofeed
Brazilian entrepreneurial families and wealth managers are recalculating dividend distribution strategies ahead of 2025 tax changes that introduce a new dividend income tax regime. Under the new law, monthly dividend distributions exceeding R$50,000 will face a 10% withholding tax at source. Family offices are now optimizing their capital allocation and tax efficiency strategies to adapt to this minimum tax requirement, with wealth managers restructuring dividend timing and distribution methods to maximize after-tax returns for high-net-worth clients.
