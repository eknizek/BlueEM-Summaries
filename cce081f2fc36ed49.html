Zofri free trade zone appoints new board with three former CEOs and 43% female representation

24 April 2026 · 09:02 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
The Zofri free trade zone (Zona Franca de Iquique), a critical logistics and commerce hub in northern Chile, held its ordinary assembly and elected a seven-member board of directors for the 2026–2028 period. The new board includes three former general managers and achieves 43% female representation, bringing expertise in logistics, foreign trade, and mining sectors. Board presidency will be formally determined in coming weeks.
