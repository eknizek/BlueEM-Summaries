Zofri renews board with three former general managers and 43% female participation

24 April 2026 · 09:02 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
The Zona Franca de Iquique (Zofri), Chile's major free trade zone, held its annual shareholder meeting to elect seven board members for the 2026-2028 period. The new directors bring experience in logistics, foreign trade, and mining sectors. The board includes 43% female participation, reflecting growing diversity. The board presidency will be determined in coming weeks, positioning Zofri for continued leadership in facilitating regional trade and commerce.
