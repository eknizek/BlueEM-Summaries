Rede Party Files with Supreme Court to Suspend Sale of Brazilian Rare Earth Miner to USA

25 April 2026 · 02:08 PM  ·  O Globo
The Brazilian Green Party (Rede) has filed a petition with Brazil's Supreme Federal Court (STF) seeking to suspend the sale of a Brazilian rare earth minerals company to a US buyer. The action raises concerns about national sovereignty and control over critical mineral resources essential for technology and defense applications. Rare earth elements are strategically important for battery production, renewable energy, and semiconductor manufacturing, making this transaction politically sensitive in Brazil.
