Mexico Would Lose 220 Billion Pesos in Tax Revenue if Strait of Hormuz Blockade Extends Through 2026

14 April 2026 · 10:00 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Mexico's business federation (IMEF) warned that if the Strait of Hormuz blockade continues throughout 2026, the federal government would lose approximately 220 billion pesos in IEPS (fuel excise tax) revenue. This scenario underscores Mexico's vulnerability to global energy supply disruptions and their direct fiscal impact. The analysis highlights how Middle Eastern geopolitical risks translate into material revenue losses for Latin American governments dependent on petroleum tax collections.
