Peru: Domestic Cement Consumption Rose 17.86% in March 2026, Highest Growth This Year

01 May 2026 · 07:44 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru's National Institute of Statistics and Informatics (INEI) reported that domestic cement consumption grew 17.86% in March 2026 versus March 2025, the strongest month-on-month growth recorded so far in 2026. First-quarter cumulative cement consumption grew 14.36% year-on-year. The surge in cement demand reflects robust construction activity and infrastructure development, signalling strength in the real estate and capital projects sector in Peru.
