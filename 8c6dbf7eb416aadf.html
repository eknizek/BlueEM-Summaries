Colombia: government orders pension funds to shift assets from private to state system

24 April 2026 · 04:49 AM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Colombia's government has ordered private pension funds (AFP) to transfer resources to Colpensiones, the state pension system. This directive represents a significant structural reform of Colombia's pension architecture. The policy shift affects capital markets flows, pension fund asset management, and investor exposure to the Latin American pension sector, with potential implications for regional financial services.
