Micro-pensions: Colombia's strategy to close the pension coverage gap amid pension crisis

20 April 2026 · 09:53 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia is leveraging micro-pension schemes (BEPS) as a policy solution to address its ongoing pension crisis and expand social security coverage to underserved populations. The approach combines flexible contribution structures, government subsidies, and financial technology to reach informal workers and lower-income groups previously excluded from traditional pension systems. This represents a material shift in Colombia's retirement income architecture and reflects broader LatAm trends in pension system modernization. The initiative has direct implications for financial services companies, insurers, and technology platforms involved in pension administration.
