Entertainment industry projects record year in 2026 with expansion of up to 7%

21 April 2026 · 09:42 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's entertainment and cultural sector is projected to achieve record economic performance in 2026, with estimated growth of 5–7% in both economic activity and audience attendance. The forecast comes from Doris Espinoza, president of the Arts, Culture and Related Events Sector at the Lima Chamber of Commerce (CCL), despite ongoing infrastructure constraints and political headwinds. Strong demand for live events is driving the expansion across the entertainment ecosystem.
