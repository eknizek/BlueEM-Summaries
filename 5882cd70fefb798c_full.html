# Dreams Expands Board with Three New Directors

Three new board members were appointed to Dreams during its 2026 shareholders' meeting. They join the existing board composed of Enrique Cibié, Francisco Lepeley, and Claudio Fischer, who serves as president. The new directors are Janet Awad Pérez, Roberta Valenca, and Gonzalo Somoza.

The three new executives arrive at Dreams during a period of improved financial results compared to 2024. In 2025, the operator reported the second-largest profit in its history, reaching $24,888.4 million, a 20.7% increase over 2024.

However, the company is currently involved in a legal proceeding for alleged collusion with Marina del Sol and Enjoy. According to a ruling by the Constitutional Court, the trial could be reactivated in the Free Competition Defense Tribunal (TDLC).

The three new board members bring combined experience in the retail, consumer, and business sectors. Janet Awad is a commercial engineer from the University of Chile with over two decades of executive experience, notably leading operations at Sodexo in Chile and subsequently managing regional strategy in Latin America. She is also currently a candidate for the board of Camanchaca.

Roberta Valenca studied at the Federal University of Pernambuco, Brazil, and Harvard University. She has built a solid career in the mass consumption industry, holding leadership positions at Unilever (eight years) and The Coca-Cola Company (17 years), where she was the first woman to serve as general manager for Chile, Bolivia, and Paraguay. She currently serves as president of the Chilean-American Chamber of Commerce (AmChamChile).

Gonzalo Somoza, former CEO of Falabella, is an industrial engineer from the Buenos Aires Institute of Technology with an MBA from MIT Sloan School of Management. His professional experience focuses on managing complex operations, international expansion, and more recently, creating digital businesses. According to his LinkedIn profile, he also serves on the boards of Castaño, Starken, Wenco, and Almacenes Siman.
