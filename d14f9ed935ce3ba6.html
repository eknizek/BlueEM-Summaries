Colombiamoda Strengthens US Presence with Miami Edition Featuring 35 Local Brands

22 April 2026 · 04:01 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombiamoda, Colombia's flagship fashion trade show, is expanding its international footprint with a dedicated Miami edition showcasing 35 Colombian brands to North American buyers and retailers. The previous edition attracted over 1,200 attendees and generated $140,000 in direct sales, demonstrating market demand for Colombian fashion exports. The US market expansion represents a strategic growth initiative for Colombian fashion and apparel manufacturers seeking to diversify sales beyond regional markets. This platform provides branded Colombian companies access to high-value North American retail and wholesale channels.
