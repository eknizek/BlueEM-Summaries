Alloy Therapeutics Enters Multi-Target Collaboration and License Agreement with Biogen for AntiClastic ASO Platform

07 April 2026 · 10:00 AM  ·  Business Wire
Alloy Therapeutics entered into a multi-target collaboration and license agreement with Biogen for development of antisense oligonucleotide (ASO) therapeutics using Alloy's AntiClastic platform. This partnership validates Alloy's technology platform and provides significant revenue potential through milestone payments and royalties. The deal reflects Biogen's strategy to expand its pipeline, but involves US biotech companies with no stated LatAm focus.
