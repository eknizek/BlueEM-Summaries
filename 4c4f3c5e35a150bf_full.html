Prosegur Prioritizes Efficiency and Profitability in Colombian Market as Global Revenue Hits Record

08 April 2026 · 09:26 PM

# Efficiency and Profitability, Prosegur's Priority in the Colombian Market

Spanish multinational celebrates half a century and achieves record global revenue figures.

Residential line leads Prosegur Alarms growth, with 15% increase in clients in the country.

In the country, Prosegur operates three main lines within the security sector: Cash, Security and Alarms. Photo: Courtesy/Prosegur

The Spanish multinational Prosegur highlights Colombia as a key market within its global operation, marking the commemoration of its 50 years of trajectory and having achieved a record business figure worldwide in 2025.

According to the company, Colombia is consolidating itself as one of the largest economies in South America, with sustained growth and increasing demand for security services, both in physical custody and electronic solutions. The company noted that it accesses strategic sectors such as banking, retail, infrastructure, industry, transport, as well as services oriented to homes and businesses. In this regard, the firm emphasized that Colombia contributes to the stability and diversification of its income in the region. "It is not only a relevant market in terms of size, but also in terms of human talent, business quality and generation of sustainable value," it stated.

In the country, the group has been active for approximately 20 years and operates through three main lines within the security sector: Cash, Security and Alarms. The Cash business, which integrates solutions such as value logistics, cash management, digital asset custody, Cash Today machines, banking correspondent services and ATM administration, is usually the largest within the operation. The company explained that this responds to the volume of transactions and the need for specialized services in the financial and commercial sectors.

For its part, the Security unit—which includes physical and electronic security—represents a significant part of the business, especially in medium and large companies. This line has maintained relevant participation in recent years.

Regarding Alarms, the company stated that it is a leading business in its category in Colombia, with sustained and profitable growth. Although its participation is lower compared to the other lines, it stands out for its expansion potential, driven by increased demand in the residential segment and in commerce, through monitored alarm solutions. The company indicated that this portfolio allows it to combine high-scale businesses with others of greater profitability, which contributes to improving the risk-return profile of the operation in the country.

In terms of results, Prosegur reported income close to one trillion pesos in Colombia. According to the company, this performance was aligned with its objectives of maintaining a profitable operation, with controlled growth and focus on cash generation, in a macroeconomic environment that required discipline and prudence. Looking toward 2026, the company stated that its expectations focus on sustainable growth, prioritizing profitability and efficiency. "Rather than a volume objective, our focus is on close support of our clients, which guarantees the security of their operations, homes and people, as well as comprehensive development of all our employees in the country," it stated.

The global operation that celebrates 50 years

At the global level, the company achieved a record business figure of 4.930 billion euros in 2025, and this result in the context of its 50th anniversary reflects the evolution of its model toward solutions with greater technological component. Currently, more than 30% of its total billing is driven by technological innovation, according to the company.

In this sense, the company has been expanding its portfolio toward higher value-added services. Among the activities it develops are cybersecurity, intelligent operations centers (ISOCs), drone systems, applied robotics, cryptocurrency management, currency exchange, international value transport, ATM administration, cash automation, gold investment, comprehensive home security and solutions against money laundering.

Prosegur has four business areas globally: Security, Cash, Alarms and AVOS Tech, and has presence in 36 countries across five continents. Likewise, the multinational has a workforce of over 180,000 employees, making it the second Spanish company with the largest number of workers in the world. According to the company, this positions it as a relevant player in job creation globally.

Prosegur has been deepening the integration of its different business lines to offer increasingly complete solutions to its clients. The company highlights that the complementarity between its units allows it to comprehensively address security needs, combining operational, technological and management capabilities in different environments. Additionally, the firm has oriented its global strategy toward specialization by sectors, with specific developments for industries such as finance, commerce, logistics and infrastructure. This approach has allowed it to adapt its services to the particularities of each client, strengthening long-term relationships and consolidating its positioning in highly competitive markets.

The company has also been strengthening its innovation capacity through the incorporation of new technologies and the development of proprietary solutions, with the objective of anticipating changes in threats and security needs. This process has involved a progressive transformation of its portfolio toward services with greater technological content.
