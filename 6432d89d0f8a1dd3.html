Mexico Hits Historic Remittance Quarter with $5 Billion in March

06 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Mexico achieved a historic quarterly milestone in remittance inflows, exceeding $5 billion in March and marking a 4.91% year-over-year increase. This strong result reversed a prior trend of weakness in remittance flows, signaling improved labour market conditions for Mexican workers abroad and sustained household income support. Remittances remain a critical source of foreign currency and household consumption support for Mexico's economy.
