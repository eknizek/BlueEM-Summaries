'There Is No Going Back, Only Forward': How AI Is Transforming Everything and What Comes Next, According to Ray Collins of Liberty Latinoamérica

24 April 2026 · 03:25 AM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Tech
Ray Collins from Liberty Latinoamérica spoke at the Links 2026 conference about artificial intelligence's transformative impact across industries and regions. The event, organized by Liberty Networks, focused on both the challenges and opportunities presented by rapid AI advancement in the technology sector. Collins emphasized the irreversible nature of technological progress and the need for stakeholders to adapt to emerging developments. The conference brought together industry leaders to discuss strategic implications for Latin American tech markets.
