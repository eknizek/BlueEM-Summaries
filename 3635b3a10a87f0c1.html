Mercosur-EU: European Cheeses, Chocolates and Wines to Become Cheaper in Brazil Over Next Decade

01 May 2026 · 03:30 PM  ·  Estado de São Paulo - Economia
Under the finalized EU-Mercosur trade agreement, European cheeses, chocolates, and wines will face reduced tariffs upon entry into Brazil, making these products more affordable for consumers and affecting local producers. The agreement is expected to reshape competitive dynamics in these sectors across Brazil and other Mercosur members over the coming decade.
