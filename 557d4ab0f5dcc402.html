Population Living Alone More than Doubles in Brazil Since 2012

20 April 2026 · 03:43 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil has experienced a dramatic demographic shift with the population living alone more than doubling between 2012 and 2026. This trend reflects changing social patterns, delayed marriage rates, and increased economic independence, particularly among women and urban dwellers. The shift has significant implications for consumer spending, housing demand, real estate development, and retail patterns across Brazil.
