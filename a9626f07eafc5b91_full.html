Hydrogen Provides Certainty in Energy Transition Strategy - AMH2

Mexico City (May 4, 2026) - The recognition of renewable hydrogen within Mexico's legal framework represents a clear signal for investment and technological development, according to AMH2.

The approval to incorporate renewable hydrogen into Mexico's strategy to strengthen mitigation policy, aligning it with the Planning Law and Energy Transition Law, generates certainty in this industry, assured the Mexican Association of Hydrogen and Energy Transformation (AMH2).
