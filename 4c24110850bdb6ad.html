JiveMauá Debuts in Agribusiness with R$ 500 Million Fiagro Fund

30 April 2026 · 12:10 PM  ·  Neofeed
JiveMauá entered Brazil's agricultural financing market with the launch of JiveMauá Agronegócio Fiagro (JMAG), a R$ 500 million fund traded on B3. The entry occurs as 16 of the market's Fiagro funds (Agricultural Production Chain Investment Funds) carry problematic assets, and seven of the nine most liquid B3-listed funds face delays or guarantee shortfalls. JiveMauá's market debut reflects investor appetite for structured agricultural credit despite sector-wide stress.
