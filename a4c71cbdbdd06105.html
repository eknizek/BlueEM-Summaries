Before receiving Karina Milei, mining companies promise 50% export increase and record dollar inflows

05 May 2026 · 08:54 PM  ·  Clarin
Argentine mining companies have announced ambitious growth commitments ahead of a high-level government delegation visit led by Karina Milei (the president's sister) and Economy Minister Diego Santilli to San Juan Province. The sector achieved record export sales of US$6 billion in 2025 and has pledged to reach US$9 billion in 2026—a 50% increase—demonstrating confidence in policy continuity and international market conditions. This engagement reflects the government's focus on mining as a driver of foreign exchange earnings and economic growth.
