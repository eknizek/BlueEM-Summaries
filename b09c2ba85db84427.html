BofA downgrades Suzano to neutral on structural pulp price decline; stock falls 6%

07 April 2026 · 04:44 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Bank of America has downgraded Brazilian pulp giant Suzano from 'buy' to 'neutral' and slashed its price target from R$82 to R$57, citing the global pulp market's entry into a structural cycle of lower pricing. The analyst expects the weaker price environment to materially compress EBITDA and net income for the Feffer family company, one of Brazil's largest forestry and pulp exporters. The stock fell 6% on the downgrade, reflecting market concern about the durability of pulp margins. This is a critical negative catalyst for LatAm fixed-income and equity investors holding Suzano or exposed to Brazilian forestry assets.
