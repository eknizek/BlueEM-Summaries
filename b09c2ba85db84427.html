Bank of America Downgrades Suzano as Pulp Market Enters Structural Low-Price Cycle

07 April 2026 · 04:44 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Bank of America has downgraded Suzano from buy to neutral and slashed its price target from R$82 to R$57, arguing that the global pulp market has entered a structural cycle of lower prices that will materially impact the company's EBITDA and profitability. This reassessment reflects oversupply concerns and weaker demand dynamics in global cellulose markets. For LatAm investors, this suggests headwinds for Brazilian pulp producers and signals analyst caution on the sector's near-term earnings trajectory despite Brazil's cost advantages.
