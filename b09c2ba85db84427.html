Bank of America Downgrades Suzano to Neutral, Cuts Price Target to R$57 on Structural Pulp Oversupply

07 April 2026 · 04:44 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Bank of America has downgraded Suzano (Brazil's largest pulp & paper producer) from buy to neutral with a price target cut from R$82 to R$57, citing a structural shift toward lower global pulp prices that will compress EBITDA and earnings. This reassessment reflects broad weakness in commodity markets and poses downside risk for one of Brazil's most significant forestry/industrial exporters. The downgrade signals caution on LatAm commodity-linked equities amid softer demand and supply dynamics.
