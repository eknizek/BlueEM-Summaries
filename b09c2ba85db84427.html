BofA Downgrades Suzano, Cuts Cellulose Price Target; Stock Falls 6%

07 April 2026 · 04:44 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (https://braziljournal.com/)
Bank of America has downgraded Suzano from buy to neutral and slashed its price target from R$82 to R$57, citing a structural shift in the global cellulose market toward persistently lower prices. This downgrade implies reduced EBITDA and earnings potential for the Feffer family-controlled pulp and paper company. The call reflects broader headwinds in global commodity pricing affecting Brazilian exporters in the sector. For LatAm investors, this signals cautious sentiment on Brazilian cellulose producers and potential margin compression in coming periods.
