Fiemg Anjos Announces Second Investment in Hyperus Startup

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Fiemg Anjos, an angel investment group, has announced its second investment in Hyperus, an industrial tech startup that has developed a hardware-software solution for identifying operational losses and improving manufacturing efficiency. The investment reflects growing interest from corporate-backed angel groups in industrial IoT and optimization technologies. Hyperus's dual hardware-software approach addresses real operational challenges faced by manufacturers seeking to reduce waste and enhance productivity. This represents a strategic move by Fiemg to support deep-tech solutions serving traditional industry sectors.
