Brazil top court upholds curbs on foreign-linked land ownership

24 April 2026 · 01:30 PM  ·  O Globo (Brazil)
Brazil's supreme court has upheld legal restrictions on foreign-linked land ownership, strengthening regulations designed to maintain domestic control over agricultural assets. This ruling reflects Brazil's ongoing policy focus on protecting domestic agribusiness interests and controlling foreign capital flows into land acquisition. The decision has significant implications for foreign investors and multinational corporations seeking to acquire Brazilian farmland and agricultural operations.
