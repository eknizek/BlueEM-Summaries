Carlos Mario Giraldo announces retirement from Grupo Éxito after 18 years as CEO

20 April 2026 · 01:43 PM

Carlos Mario Giraldo Moreno, general manager of Grupo Éxito in Colombia, announced his retirement from the company effective June 30, 2026. The decision was communicated to the Board of Directors after more than 18 years with the organization. The transition process is already underway and the appointment of the new general manager will be announced in the coming days, once the corresponding approval is obtained.

The company confirmed that Giraldo Moreno's departure comes at a time when the organization maintains active operations and advances in defining its leadership succession. The decision follows an extended management cycle in which the executive led different stages of business transformation.

Francisco Calleja, president of Grupo Éxito's Board of Directors, and Carlos Calleja, executive president of the company, expressed their recognition and appreciation for the work performed over these years. "For Carlos Mario, our recognition and appreciation. We want to thank him for all his effort, commitment, discipline and affection with which he has worked for the growth of Grupo Éxito. Carlos will always be a friend of the company and of ours," said Francisco Calleja.

Carlos Calleja highlighted the relationship built during their time together in the organization: "In Carlos Mario Giraldo we have had a great colleague and friend. I know of the love he has for his colleagues, for the company and that his dream is for it to continue growing and strengthening, generating opportunities for everyone and building the country. We share that dream of yours and we will continue to make it a reality. Grupo Éxito will always be your home."

During his time with the company, Giraldo Moreno led transformation processes across different business lines. In his assessment, he highlighted the development of new commercial formats and the consolidation of customer experience-oriented strategies. "I leave an organization that as a team has achieved lasting things. The Éxito brand with its Wow format and Carulla with Fresh Market, creating differentiated value and experiences. A 'Digital Sales and Omnichannel' business that already participates with approximately 14% of sales in Colombia's operations," he stated.

The executive also referred to the growth of other units within the group. "The company has become one of the largest clothing retailers, with iconic private labels such as Arkitect, Bronzini and People. We achieved leadership in the shopping center business, with Viva Envigado as a national reference," he noted.

In the same message, he mentioned the development of strategic alliances, including one with Bancolombia. "In alliance with Bancolombia we consolidated Puntos Colombia, the country's leading loyalty coalition, a true 'second currency' that now reaches nearly one in three households," Giraldo Moreno indicated.

The company also highlighted that, under his leadership, Grupo Éxito strengthened its positioning in the retail sector, maintaining presence in different segments and sales channels.

In a message addressed to employees, Giraldo Moreno thanked the support received during his tenure and referred to the team that will continue to lead operations. "A young and experienced management team remains at the helm, emerged from internal succession that guarantees solidity, innovation, but equally a culture of service and integrity. I have only gratitude for my colleagues. Thank you for your inexhaustible energy, permanent joy and unlimited commitment to Grupo Éxito and to Colombia," he expressed.
