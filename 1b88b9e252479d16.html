President Sheinbaum launches updated Mexico Plan to accelerate investment

04 May 2026 · 09:18 PM  ·  El Economista
President Sheinbaum signed executive decrees to accelerate investment flows under an updated Mexico Plan framework, focusing on the ATDT (Authority for Development of Territorial Trust) and expedited authorization processes for investments in designated wellbeing centers. The initiative is designed to remove bottlenecks and attract both domestic and foreign capital to strategic development zones. This reflects the administration's focus on regulatory modernization and infrastructure investment.
