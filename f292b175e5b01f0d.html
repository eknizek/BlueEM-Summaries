Fewer deals but larger: M&A activity in Colombia drops 38% in Q1 2026

10 April 2026 · 03:20 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
According to TTR Data, Colombia's M&A market experienced a significant slowdown in Q1 2026 with transaction volumes falling 38.46% year-over-year to just 48 completed operations. Despite the volume decline, the data suggests a shift toward larger, more strategic transactions as average deal sizes increased. This trend reflects tightening credit conditions, higher interest rates, and selective investment appetite among both Colombian and international acquirers. The pattern indicates a flight to quality where only well-capitalized buyers and best-positioned targets are completing transactions.
