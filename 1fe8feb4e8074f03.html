Sale of Movistar shares in Colombia would generate over $856 billion for National Treasury

24 April 2026 · 11:47 AM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
As part of the merger between Movistar and Tigo in Colombia, the government's sale of its remaining stake in Movistar is projected to generate over 856 billion Colombian pesos for the National Treasury. Colombia's Comptroller's Office will oversee monitoring of these proceeds as part of fiscal governance oversight. The transaction represents a significant divestiture by the Colombian state from its telecommunications assets and reflects the ongoing consolidation in the country's telecom sector.
