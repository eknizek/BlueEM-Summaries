Companies plan 24.5% wage increases for the year, below estimated inflation

04 May 2026 · 09:25 PM  ·  Clarin
Argentine companies are planning wage increases of 24.5% for 2026, distributed across three separate raises throughout the year. Private sector economists are forecasting annual inflation of 30%, creating a real wage erosion gap of approximately 5.5 percentage points. This planning reflects corporate expectations for moderating but persistent inflation and reflects strategies to preserve competitiveness while managing labour costs.
