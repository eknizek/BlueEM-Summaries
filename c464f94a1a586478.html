US Lithium Developer to Go Public via Nasdaq SPAC at $573 Million Valuation

15 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Twitter
A US-based lithium developer has announced plans to go public through a merger with a blank-check company, with the transaction valuing the firm at roughly $573 million on the Nasdaq. This SPAC deal provides the lithium company with capital to fund exploration, development, and production activities in the energy transition sector. Lithium producers are strategically important for battery manufacturing and the global electric vehicle supply chain.
