Self-Employed Workers Will Not Be Required to Contribute to Retirement: Does Pension System Reform Lose Steam?

18 April 2026 · 12:05 AM  ·  El Comercio (Peru) - Economia
Peru's Ministry of Economy and Finance issued Supreme Decree No. 061-2026-EF, exempting self-employed workers from mandatory pension contribution requirements. This action reverses a key pillar of the government's pension system modernization reform, as Congress voted to exclude independent workers from the new contribution regime. The exemption undermines the fiscal sustainability and funding base of Peru's reformed social security system. For investors in financial services and insurance, this signals structural pressure on pension fund growth and affects the long-term viability of private pension management operators.
