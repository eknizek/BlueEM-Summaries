Alckmin announces R$10 billion credit line for agricultural machinery at sector fair

26 April 2026 · 05:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's Vice President Geraldo Alckmin announced a special credit line of R$10 billion with single-digit interest rates for rural producers to purchase agricultural machinery and equipment. The announcement was made at a major agribusiness sector event, targeting one of Brazil's key industries. This represents a significant government-backed financing initiative to support agricultural mechanization and capital investment in the sector.
