Lula Advocates EU-Mercosul Agreement and Proposes Portugal as Gateway for Brazilian Companies

21 April 2026 · 08:24 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
During a five-hour visit to Lisbon, Brazilian President Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva expressed support for the EU-Mercosul trade agreement in a joint statement with Portuguese Prime Minister Luís Montenegro. Lula advocated globalization and proposed Portugal as a strategic entry portal for Brazilian business interests into European markets. This diplomatic initiative signals Brazil's commitment to regional trade integration and identifies Portugal as a key market access point for Brazilian companies seeking European expansion.
