Lula Defends EU-Mercosur Agreement and Proposes Portugal as Gateway for Brazilian Companies

21 April 2026 · 08:24 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
During a five-hour visit to Lisbon, Brazilian President Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva issued a joint statement with Portuguese Prime Minister Luís Montenegro endorsing the Mercosur-EU trade agreement and praising globalization. Lula proposed that Portugal could serve as a major gateway for Brazilian business interests seeking to enter European markets. The statement reflects Brazil's strategic focus on deepening trade ties with Europe and expanding commercial opportunities for its companies.
