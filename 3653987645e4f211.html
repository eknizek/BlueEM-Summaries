Victor Lazarte Allocates Half of New Fund in Seven Months: 'Maximum AI Exposure'

18 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Victor Lazarte, a veteran venture investor formerly with Wildlife and Benchmark, has deployed more than half of his new VL Fund's capital into American startups within seven months, with a stated strategy of 'maximum exposure to AI.' The fund has over $100 million remaining for deployment. This positioning reflects the capital and strategic focus flowing into the AI sector from Latin American investors.
