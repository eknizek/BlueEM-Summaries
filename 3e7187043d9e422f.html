Andi alerts on sale of construction materials for housing failing technical standards in Colombia

20 April 2026 · 11:14 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombia's National Manufacturers Association (Andi) has issued a warning about the sale of construction materials that fail to meet technical standards required by Colombian building codes. The association emphasizes that such non-compliance poses significant risks to structural stability, particularly in seismically active regions and under structural loads. This issue affects the residential construction sector and raises concerns about long-term asset quality and safety liabilities for builders and property investors.
