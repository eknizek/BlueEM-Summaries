Mexican Manufacturing Employment Falls to Worst Level in 17 Years

Total employed personnel in the country's manufacturing industry not only decreased for the third consecutive time in the first two months of the year, but also suffered its most significant setback in 17 years in comparable periods, while the sector's production volume and wages increased.

Only three components that make up the indicator increased their employed personnel in the first two months of the year.
