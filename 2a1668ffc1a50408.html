Billy Escobar resigns from Colombian Superintendencia de Sociedades after four years

24 April 2026 · 09:07 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Billy Escobar has resigned from his position as superintendent of the Superintendencia de Sociedades (Colombia's corporate and business regulator) after four years leading the agency. He cited the need to pursue other professional projects as the reason for his departure. The change in regulatory leadership may influence future enforcement priorities, corporate governance standards, and oversight approaches for Colombian companies, relevant to investors monitoring regulatory risk and compliance environments.
