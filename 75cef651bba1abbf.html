SNMPE Questions Petro-Perú: No Clear Signs of Progress in Restructuring

30 April 2026 · 11:42 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru's National Mining and Petroleum Society (SNMPE) criticized a new financial rescue package for state-owned Petro-Perú, cautioning that additional government support could increase fiscal risks and crowd out private investment. The group expressed concern that the restructuring shows no clear operational improvements despite multiple bailouts. This reflects growing investor anxiety over Peru's state oil company viability and fiscal sustainability amid persistent operational failures.
