# US Dollar Closes at S/3.4390 on April 21 Amid Political Uncertainty

The price of the dollar at the close of today is S/3.4390, a figure registered due to the country's political situation, according to the Central Reserve Bank (BCR). The currency advanced 40 points compared to Monday's close, which stood at S/3.4350.

Jesús Flores Ríos, commercial manager of currency intermediation at Renta4 SAB, explained that during the trading session the dollar remained relatively stable, bringing the currency to a maximum price of S/3.4440 and a minimum of S/3.4340. Additionally, US$205 million were traded in the market at an average price of S/3.4380. Likewise, there were no maturity settlements in Currency Swap Sales today. Meanwhile, the DXY index fell to 98.36 (+0.31%).

## How did the dollar close on Monday, April 20?

The exchange rate closed at S/3.4350, according to the Central Reserve Bank (BCR). The currency fell 20 points compared to Friday's close, which stood at S/3.4370, according to Renta4 SAB. During the trading session, the dollar remained stable, bringing the currency to a maximum price of S/3.4390 and a minimum of S/3.4320.
