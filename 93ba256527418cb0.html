CMN approves working capital credit line for airline sector

23 April 2026 · 10:29 PM  ·  Valor Economico (Brazil)
Brazil's National Monetary Council (CMN) approved a working capital credit line for the aviation sector funded through the National Civil Aviation Fund (FNAC). The measure provides liquidity support to airlines facing operational challenges. This represents targeted credit stimulus for a capital-intensive transport sector dealing with cost pressures.
