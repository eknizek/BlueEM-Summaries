Bradesco Seguros Uses 'Creative Engineering' in Pension Plans Without Focus on Retirement or Succession

16 April 2026 · 07:28 PM  ·  Neofeed
Following regulatory changes to VGBL (Variable Geometry Bonds for Life), Brazil's pension insurance sector experienced significant contraction, with gross fundraising declining R$157 billion over 12 months through January according to Fenaprevi data. Bradesco Seguros is responding by developing innovative pension products through 'creative engineering' that move beyond traditional retirement and succession planning focuses. This strategic shift reflects the broader industry challenge of adapting to new regulatory constraints while identifying alternative growth channels in the private pension market.
