Squirrel Helicopter Surprises Avantto with Demand in Agribusiness Capital, Says CEO

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Avantto, a shared aircraft operator, identified unexpected market opportunity in Goiânia (Brazil's agribusiness hub) during its first expansion outside the Rio-São Paulo axis. The CEO revealed a $1 billion investment in Epic Aircraft to support expansion and announced an integrated strategy combining fixed-wing aircraft and helicopters to serve the agricultural sector. This move reflects growing demand for premium aviation services among agribusiness players in interior Brazil.
