Squirrel helicopter surprises Avantto with demand in 'agribusiness capital', CEO says

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Avantto, a fractional aircraft ownership and shared jet operator, has identified significant untapped helicopter demand in Goiânia, Brazil's agricultural center, during its first expansion outside the Rio-São Paulo axis. The company plans to invest R$1 billion in Epic aircraft and pursue a unique integrated strategy combining fixed-wing and rotorcraft services for the agribusiness sector. This discovery reveals a new market opportunity in serving Brazil's rural and agricultural economy through premium aviation services.
