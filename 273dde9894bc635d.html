PEC on 6×1 Work Schedule Opens New Round Between Congress and Private Sector

22 April 2026 · 10:09 PM  ·  Neofeed
Brazil's Constitutional and Justice Commission (CCJ) approved a proposed constitutional amendment (PEC) on April 22 to eliminate the 6×1 work schedule (six days work, one day rest) in the country. With government backing, the amendment moves forward despite organized business sector opposition, which is preparing for debate in a special commission. The measure represents a significant labor policy shift with broad implications for Brazilian employers and workforce regulations.
