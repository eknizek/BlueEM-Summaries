Electric and hybrid vehicle sales accelerate in Colombia, surpass 41,000 registrations in 2026

04 May 2026 · 08:59 PM

# Electric and hybrid vehicle sales accelerate in Colombia, surpass 41,000 registrations in 2026

The market for electric and hybrid vehicles in Colombia maintained strong growth during the first four months of 2026, after reaching more than 41,700 new registrations between both technologies, according to figures from the Runt consolidated by Fenalco and Andi. The data shows that the electric segment was the one with the greatest expansion in the country, with accumulated growth of 207% compared to the same period in 2025, while hybrids advanced 73.4% during the first four months of the year.

Between January and April, 14,541 new electric vehicles were registered in Colombia, compared to 4,732 units registered a year earlier, consolidating one of the largest historical increases for this market. In parallel, hybrid vehicles reached 27,238 accumulated registrations between January and April, far exceeding the 15,709 units registered during the same period in 2025 and maintaining leadership within electrified technologies.

The Fenalco and Andi report also highlighted that the growth of both segments is accompanied by the arrival of new models, the strengthening of Asian brands, and greater demand for electrified SUVs within the Colombian market.

## Tesla, BYD, and Suzuki lead the electrified vehicle market

Within the electric segment, Tesla established itself as the leading brand in Colombia during April, after reaching 2,639 registrations and a market share of 50.8%, followed by BYD with 1,222 units and 23.5% of the national total. Chery, Chevrolet, and GAC completed the group of most important brands in the electric segment in April, within a market where the five leading companies concentrated 84.8% of registrations during the month.

In the year-to-date accumulation, Tesla maintained leadership with 5,406 electric registrations and a 37.2% share, while BYD reached 3,899 units and Chery reached 980 vehicles registered between January and April.

The hybrid segment showed different dynamics, with a wider distribution among traditional manufacturers and new brands that began to gain participation within the Colombian automotive industry. Suzuki led hybrid registrations in April with 1,338 units and a 17.9% share, followed by Kia with 942 vehicles and Toyota with 927 new registrations during the fourth month of the year. Mazda and Renault completed the group of the five most important hybrid brands in April, while companies like Deepal, Great Wall, Cupra, and Jeep showed some of the largest percentage growth within the segment.

In the year-to-date accumulation, Suzuki reached 4,359 hybrid registrations, Toyota reached 3,593 units, and Kia added 3,299 registrations, consolidating strong competition between Japanese manufacturers, Korean brands, and new Asian companies.

## Tesla Model Y and Corolla Cross dominate electric and hybrid sales

By specific models, the Tesla Model Y became the best-selling electric vehicle in the country both in April and in the year-to-date accumulation, after reaching 2,264 monthly registrations and 4,055 registrations between January and April. The second place among electrics went to BYD Yuan Up with 534 registrations in April and 2,153 units accumulated during the year, followed by Tesla Model 3 and Chery Icar within the national market.

Fenalco and Andi noted that "these five models represented 63% of total electric vehicles registered" during the first four months of 2026, reflecting high concentration around certain specific references.

In hybrids, the Mazda CX-30 led April registrations with 627 units and an 8.4% share, followed by Kia Sportage with 544 registrations and Toyota Corolla Cross with 535 registered vehicles. However, in the year-to-date accumulation, the Toyota Corolla Cross recovered leadership of the hybrid segment with 2,327 registrations, slightly exceeding the Mazda CX-30, which reached 2,291 registrations between January and April.

Models such as Suzuki Swift, Ford Territory, Kia Stonic, and Hyundai Kona also showed strong commercial expansion, especially driving the growth of SUVs and electrified urban vehicles within the Colombian market.

## Bogotá and Medellín concentrate growth of electric and hybrid vehicles

At the regional level, Bogotá continued to establish itself as the country's main electrified mobility market in both electric and hybrid vehicles, concentrating the largest number of registrations during April and the year-to-date accumulation. In electric vehicles, Bogotá reached 2,692 registrations in April and 8,057 units accumulated between January and April, followed by Medellín with 1,543 monthly registrations and 3,661 vehicles registered in the year.

Cali, Chía, Bucaramanga, and Rionegro also showed significant increases within the electric segment, with Rionegro standing out especially with a monthly growth of 1,000% compared to April of last year.

In hybrids, Bogotá registered 3,115 registrations in April and more than 12,000 units accumulated in the year, while Medellín reached 4,497 accumulated registrations and Cali reached 2,663 registered vehicles. The report also noted that Pereira recorded one of the largest growth increases in hybrids with an increase of 755.6%, while Cúcuta and Chía ranked among the cities with the most significant expansion.
