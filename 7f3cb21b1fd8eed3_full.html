# Cencosud Renews Board Leadership: Manfred Paulmann Assumes Directorship Role

Cencosud announced a change in the presidency of its board of directors. Manfred Paulmann Koepfer will assume the position in replacement of Julio Moura Neto, who has led the board since December 2023.

The transition will be formalized at the next ordinary board session, scheduled for April 24, following the Ordinary Shareholders' Meeting, in line with what was agreed by the company in an extraordinary session. The leadership change occurs in a context where the retail sector faces greater competitive pressures and changes in consumer habits, factors that have required companies to strengthen their long-term strategies and corporate governance.

## Continuity in Strategy

The company emphasized that the change in presidency seeks to ensure continuity of the roadmap established in recent years, maintaining focus on sustainable growth and adaptation to an increasingly demanding environment. Moura, who has been a board member since 2011, will continue to participate as a director, contributing his experience during a critical stage for the company.

During his tenure, he led strategic decisions during a period marked by industry challenges, including inflationary pressures, changes in consumption, and the need to strengthen the group's regional operations.

Paulmann brings to the presidency a consolidated trajectory within the holding. He has been a company director since 2021 and possesses extensive knowledge of the business and markets where Cencosud operates. Upon assuming office, he emphasized that his management will be oriented toward maintaining a long-term vision, with focus on innovation, efficiency, and strengthening capabilities.

## Corporate Governance in Transition

The change also reflects the importance the group places on continuity and stability in its corporate governance, in a scenario where strategic discipline becomes key to facing competition in the region. The board valued Moura's management and highlighted that his continued participation as director will allow sustaining a strategic perspective in future decision-making.

With this leadership transition, Cencosud begins a new stage in its leadership structure, at a moment when the retail sector faces structural transformations and greater demands for adaptation.
