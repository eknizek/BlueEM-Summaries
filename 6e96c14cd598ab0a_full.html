# Oil Crisis Highlights Urgency of Energy Transition in Brazil

The creation of a Roadmap for the end of fossil fuels in Brazil is a strategic opportunity to align the country's energy policy with the 21st-century economy. With decisions based on evidence, transparency, and long-term vision, it is possible to carry out a just and planned transition, leveraging national vocations and competitive advantages in renewable fuels, generating jobs and paving the way for sustainable growth in its three pillars (social, environmental, and economic), while also fulfilling climate commitments.

The Roadmap for exiting fossil fuels needs to include demand for renewables.
