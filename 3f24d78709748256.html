H&M Sees Brazil as Key Engine for Global Growth, Says CEO

02 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
H&M's leadership has identified Brazil as a strategic priority market for the Swedish fashion retailer's global growth plans. Brazil's size, consumer base, and retail market dynamics make it central to H&M's international expansion strategy. This reflects the retailer's commitment to strengthening its presence in Latin America's largest economy and capturing market share in the region's fast-growing consumer segment. The statement signals H&M's confidence in Brazil's retail sector and consumer spending potential despite macroeconomic headwinds.
