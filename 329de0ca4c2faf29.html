Caixa Cards Director Says Bus Travelers Deserve Better Service

25 April 2026 · 05:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The director-president of Caixa's card division announced the company is re-entering the credit card market after a period of withdrawal, citing early results from recent strategic measures. The company is leveraging its UAU loyalty points program to capture market share in Brazil's competitive credit card sector. Management sees customer acquisition opportunities across different income segments, including bus travelers traditionally underserved by premium card offerings.
