# Cuenta DNI: All Promotions and Benefits for May 2026

Cuenta DNI is the virtual wallet of Banco Provincia. It offers cashback with limits in supermarkets, stores, and more.

In its advertising slogan, Banco Provincia defines its digital wallet Cuenta DNI as "the best friend of Buenos Aires residents," due to the promotions and benefits it offers. What does it have for May 2026?

Its benefits vary according to the category, day, and cashback limit, which is why many users search for which ones remain valid month by month, as several can change. It is recommended to confirm in the Cuenta DNI App or website at the time of purchase.

## What is Cuenta DNI: Its Promotions and Benefits for May

Cuenta DNI, launched in April 2020, is defined as a "digital solution" that allows you to perform various operations instantly such as paying with QR, DNI code, or Cuenta DNI payment link in various stores. Like other digital wallets, it also allows you to send and receive money instantly by entering the recipient's document number or searching for them in your contact list.

It should be noted that having Cuenta DNI has no costs, neither the application nor the savings account. On its frequently asked questions section, the app page assures that it is not necessary to be a bank customer to have it. "When you download Cuenta DNI if you are not yet a customer, we open a free savings account at the Banco Provincia branch you choose and it is enabled within 48 hours," they clarify.

Cuenta DNI has no costs of any kind, neither the app nor the savings account. It also indicates that you can choose not to print the Debit Card when you first enroll. "If later you want to request the printing of your Debit Card, you can do so through Cuenta DNI," they detail.

As its name indicates, you need to have the latest physical version of the National Identity Document to register, and young people from age 13 can have it. It allows you to perform operations 24 hours a day, any day of the week.

Regarding promotions and benefits, these can be filtered by categories such as "All," "Food," "Supermarkets," and "Various."
