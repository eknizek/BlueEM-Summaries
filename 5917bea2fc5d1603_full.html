# Lenovo Partners with Inteli on Postgraduate AI and Data Science Training in Brazil

The Chinese multinational Lenovo has decided to enter the classroom to help create a base of technology specialists in the country. The company has structured a postgraduate program in artificial intelligence and data science with the Institute of Technology and Leadership (Inteli).

The year-long course uses project-based learning methodology, which places students in practical cases, with AI opportunity diagnoses, business plans, artificial intelligence agent prototypes, and predictive models.

"Training in current topics like artificial intelligence and data science equips high-level professionals for the market and increases strategic competitiveness of the company," says Marcelo Parada, Lenovo's Research and Development Manager.

The company is present in more than 160 countries. Half of the available positions are reserved for scholarship recipients.

"The signals coming from the job market are very clear: there is relevant demand for professionals who understand the different dimensions of AI and who also have a business-focused perspective," says Ana Garcia, Growth Director at Inteli. "This postgraduate program in partnership with Lenovo offers exactly this qualification."
