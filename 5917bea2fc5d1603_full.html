# Lenovo's Postgraduate Program with Inteli

The Chinese multinational technology company Lenovo has decided to enter the classroom to help create a base of technology specialists in Brazil. The company has structured a postgraduate program in artificial intelligence and data science with the Institute of Technology and Leadership (Inteli).

The one-year course uses project-based learning methodology, which places students in practical cases, including diagnostics of AI opportunities, business plans, artificial intelligence agent prototypes, and predictive models.

"Training in current topics like artificial intelligence and data science qualifies high-level professionals for the market and increases the strategic competitiveness of the company," says Marcelo Parada, Lenovo's Research and Development manager.

The company is present in more than 160 countries. Half of the program's spots are reserved for scholarship recipients.

"The signals coming from the job market are very clear: there is significant demand for professionals who understand the different dimensions of AI and who also have a business solutions perspective," says Ana Garcia, Growth director at Inteli. "This postgraduate program in partnership with Lenovo offers exactly this qualification."
