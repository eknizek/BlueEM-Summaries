Telefónica Mexico Sold to Melisa Acquisition Consortium for USD 450 Million

20 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Excelsior
Telefónica has confirmed the sale of its Mexican telecommunications operations to Melisa Acquisition Consortium for USD 450 million. The transaction represents a significant M&A activity in Mexico's telecom sector, marking Telefónica's exit from the Mexican market after years of operations. This deal highlights ongoing consolidation and strategic repositioning in Latin American telecom, with implications for competitive dynamics and spectrum ownership. The sale provides investors with clarity on major player realignment in one of Latin America's largest telecom markets.
