Labor reform: two months on, Argentine companies still measuring impact amid implementation delays

01 May 2026 · 04:53 PM  ·  Clarin
Two months after Argentina's labor reform approval, many companies have yet to fully assess its practical impact, with private surveys indicating widespread uncertainty about material effects. Implementation has been slowed by ongoing legal challenges and the absence of complete regulatory guidance in key areas, preventing uniform application across sectors. This implementation lag reflects the reform's complexity and suggests phased business adaptation over coming quarters.
