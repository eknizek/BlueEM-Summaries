J&F Prices Offering of US$400,000,000 Senior Notes Due 2033

16 April 2026 · 08:58 PM  ·  GlobeNewswire

This debt capital raise supports the company's financial strategy and liquidity management. JBS is one of the world's largest protein producers with significant operations across Latin America, North America, and Europe.
