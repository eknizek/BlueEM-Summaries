Argentina's YPF swings to profit on higher prices, more shale output

07 May 2026 · 09:35 PM  ·  Reuters
Argentina's state-controlled oil company YPF has returned to profit, benefiting from higher global crude oil prices and expanded production from its Vaca Muerta shale formation. The company's improved financial performance reflects both favorable commodity prices and execution of its growth strategy in one of the world's largest shale reserves. This development is significant for Argentina's energy independence and fiscal revenues.
