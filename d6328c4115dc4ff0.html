One in four Colombians start businesses but most fail to sustain operations

17 April 2026 · 01:12 AM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Empresas
Colombia shows one of Latin America's highest business creation rates, with approximately 25% of the population attempting entrepreneurship. However, most ventures fail to achieve sustainability, reflecting structural barriers to long-term business viability. While the high entrepreneurship rate indicates economic dynamism and opportunity-seeking behavior, weak retention suggests challenges in access to capital, business support services, and market conditions for small enterprises across Colombia.
