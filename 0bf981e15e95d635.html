Heineken surprises with price hikes – a relief for Ambev

24 April 2026 · 07:55 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Heineken reported that while its beer sales volume in Brazil declined in the first quarter, revenues increased significantly due to price increases in the high single-digits combined with improved product mix. This development signals a meaningful shift in pricing dynamics for the Brazilian beer market, where volume-to-revenue decoupling is becoming more pronounced. The news is particularly relevant for Ambev, Brazil's dominant brewer, as it suggests the market may be supporting price increases across the sector, potentially offsetting volume pressures.
