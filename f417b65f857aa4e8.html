99Food offered R$300,000 to prevent restaurant from operating with Rappi and Keeta

25 April 2026 · 06:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
99Food, the delivery platform returned to Brazil by the 99 group, allegedly offered R$300,000 to a restaurant to ensure it would not partner with competing platforms Rappi (Colombian) and Keeta (Chinese). The incident highlights competitive dynamics and exclusivity practices in Brazil's food delivery market. This is relevant to investors monitoring competitive strategy, antitrust exposure, and market consolidation trends in Brazil's e-commerce/delivery sector.
