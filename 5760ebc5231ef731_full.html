## Venezuela Government Announces Increase in Bonuses, Not Salaries

Venezuelan citizens are awaiting the announcement from Venezuela's Minister of Labor, Carlos Alexis Castillo, regarding the increase announced by interim president Delcy Rodríguez of 240 dollars for workers and 70 dollars for retirees, through bonuses rather than salary increases to avoid inflation, whose accumulated value was 71.8% during the first quarter of the year.

"At this moment we cannot increase salary and we cannot increase salary because that means inflation shoots up," Castillo assured in an interview on the private radio station Onda.
