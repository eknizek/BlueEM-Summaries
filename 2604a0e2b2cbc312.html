Prediction markets platforms arrive in Brazil amid regulatory uncertainty

05 April 2026 · 07:01 AM  ·  O Globo
Prediction market platforms are entering Brazil as regulatory clarity remains unclear, creating both opportunity and legal risk for operators and users. The platforms enable users to make predictions on various outcomes, blending elements of betting, forecasting, and market trading. Brazilian regulators have not yet established a clear framework governing these platforms, leaving them in a regulatory gray zone that could shift with policy changes. This development is relevant for fintech expansion, online betting regulation, and the broader evolution of digital finance in LatAm's largest economy.
