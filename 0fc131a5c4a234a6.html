Colombia's competition authority files charges against Claro for alleged e-commerce and financing violations

13 April 2026 · 05:02 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombia's antitrust authority, the Superintendence of Industry and Commerce (SIC), filed formal charges against major telecom operator Claro following a preliminary investigation that revealed alleged violations in e-commerce operations and consumer financing activities. The charges follow information requests issued during the investigation phase. The case could result in significant financial penalties or operational restrictions for Claro and reflects heightened regulatory scrutiny of telecom operators' expansion into adjacent digital services.
