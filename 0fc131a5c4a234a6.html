Colombian Competition Authority files charges against Claro for alleged e-commerce and financing irregularities

13 April 2026 · 05:02 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombia's Superintendencia de Industria y Comercio (SIC) has formally charged Claro, the major telecom and services operator, with anti-competitive practices in e-commerce and consumer financing. The charges emerged from a preliminary investigation that included multiple information requests from the regulatory body. These allegations relate to Claro's digital commerce platform and financing offerings, potentially signaling market abuse concerns. The case carries implications for regulatory oversight of telecom operators expanding into adjacent financial services in Colombia.
