Tigo and AWS launch free AI and cloud computing training for youth outside traditional education and labor systems

09 April 2026 · 11:14 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Tigo (a major Colombian telecom operator) and AWS have formed a strategic alliance to deliver free digital training in artificial intelligence and cloud computing to economically disadvantaged youth outside formal education and employment systems. This corporate social investment initiative addresses Colombia's digital skills gap while building long-term talent pipelines and market development for cloud services. For investors in Colombian telecom, technology infrastructure, and workforce development, the partnership demonstrates sectoral commitment to digital inclusion and may signal rising demand for cloud services across Colombian enterprises.
