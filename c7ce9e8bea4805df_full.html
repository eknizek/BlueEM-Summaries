UBS Warns Fiscal Balance Becomes 'Increasingly Difficult' for Mexico

20 April 2026 · 09:29 PM

The Ministry of Finance and Public Credit (SHCP) is focused on supporting the country's economic growth without compromising fiscal consolidation, a balance that appears "increasingly difficult," according to UBS analysts.

Meanwhile, the analysts emphasized that USMCA negotiations add another layer of uncertainty, as policymakers admit that the July deadline will likely not be met.
