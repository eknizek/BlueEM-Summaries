Foot-and-mouth disease: South African delegation visits Argentine animal health laboratory amid health emergency

29 April 2026 · 07:33 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina) - Economia
South Africa's Agriculture Minister John Henry Steenhuisen visited Argentina with agricultural experts and local producers to tour Biogénesis Bagó's vaccine manufacturing facility in Garín. The visit focused on foot-and-mouth disease response and vaccine supply as Argentina works to address the regional health emergency. Biogénesis Bagó has already delivered 2.5 million of the 6 million vaccine doses agreed upon, highlighting the critical role of Argentine pharmaceutical capabilities in regional animal health security.
