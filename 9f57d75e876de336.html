Banco de Brasilia Agrees to Sell Banco Master Assets for US$2.88 Billion to Quadra Capital

21 April 2026 · 10:51 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Banco de Brasilia has agreed to sell the assets of Banco Master to Brazilian investment manager Quadra Capital for US$2.88 billion. The transaction involves creating a new investment fund that will control and monetize the acquired assets. This significant asset sale represents a major portfolio restructuring for both institutions and reflects shifts in Brazil's banking landscape.
