BlackRock sees fixed-income ETFs as key liquidity source for private assets

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
BlackRock has highlighted fixed-income exchange-traded funds (ETFs) as an important solution for addressing liquidity challenges in private asset markets. The asset manager sees ETFs as a bridge mechanism that can unlock capital flows into traditionally illiquid private credit and alternative investment strategies. This positioning reflects growing institutional interest in creating more liquid pathways to private asset exposure, addressing a persistent gap between investor demand for private assets and the limited exit opportunities.
