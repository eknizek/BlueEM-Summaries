# The Paradox of the 'Advent Effect' on Natura

**A private equity fund has a set average price to build a position in the company**

By Maria Luíza Filgueiras — São Paulo 16/04/2026 4:07 PM Updated 20 hours ago

Advent agreed with Natura's controlling block on a shareholding position with governance alignments. But as the stock rose more than 10% since then, the fund has not yet become a shareholder, according to Pipeline sources linked to the beauty company.

The private equity fund plans to build a position of 8% to 10% within six months, making an average price of R$ 9.75. Currently, Natura's stock is worth R$ 10.12.

The paradox is that investors became enthusiastic with the fund's entry, causing the stock to rise, but the fund itself has already determined a threshold for this to happen, and therefore must wait for it to fall.

By reaching this stake, Advent will appoint two board members. When contacted, the fund did not comment.
