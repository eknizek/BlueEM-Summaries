Aneel Initiates Process on Enel Contract Revocation in São Paulo and Gives Company 30 Days to Respond

07 April 2026 · 03:46 PM  ·  O Globo
Brazil's National Electric Energy Agency (Aneel) has initiated formal proceedings to potentially revoke Enel's electricity distribution concession in São Paulo, giving the Italian utility 30 days to respond to charges. This regulatory escalation reflects ongoing service quality issues and regulatory tensions between Enel and Brazilian authorities, with significant implications for utility governance and concessionaire accountability in Brazil. For infrastructure and utility investors, the action signals heightened regulatory enforcement risk in Brazilian power distribution and potential value destruction for Enel shareholders, while also attracting new bidders for a major concession.
