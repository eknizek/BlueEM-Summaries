# Sectors that will receive bonuses and salary increases in May 2026

Bonuses and salary increases in May 2026 reach retirees, pensioners, allowance holders, beneficiaries of social benefits, and workers covered by the minimum wage update. The fifth month brings increases through mobility adjustments, pension supplements, and new reference values for different groups.

The National Social Security Administration (ANSeS) applies a 3.4% increase in pensions, benefits, and allowances, in line with March inflation as measured by INDEC. This percentage defines the update for minimum pension, AUH, AUE, PUAM, and Non-Contributory Pensions.

There are also changes outside the pension system. The minimum wage rises in May to $363,000, according to the current scale established by Resolution 9/2025. This value also impacts labor references and related benefits.

## Main sectors receiving bonuses and increases in May

The main sectors affected by bonuses and increases in May are retirees, pensioners, family allowance holders, AUH, AUE, PUAM, and PNC beneficiaries, and workers covered by the minimum wage.

For ANSeS, the general increase is 3.4%. The increase applies to April benefits and follows the monthly mobility scheme. The pension bonus remains at $70,000 for those earning the lowest benefits. For retirees exceeding the minimum, the supplement is paid proportionally up to the monthly cap.

## Who receives the $70,000 ANSeS bonus

The $70,000 bonus is fully received by retirees and pensioners earning the minimum benefit. It also applies to PUAM holders and Non-Contributory Pensions recipients. Those earning more than the minimum may receive a proportional bonus. The calculation ensures the total does not exceed $463,250.17. The supplement is not incorporated into the benefit—it is paid as an additional amount and does not modify the pension calculation base.

## ANSeS: Allowances increase in May 2026

Allowances also receive the 3.4% increase in May. The update applies to AUH, AUE, AUH for disability, and family allowances.

The Universal Child Allowance (AUH) reaches $141,312.64. ANSeS pays 80% monthly and retains 20% pending AUH Booklet submission. With this scheme, the direct monthly AUH payment is $113,050.11. The accumulated monthly retention is $28,262.53.

The Pregnancy Allowance (AUE) reaches $141,312.64, while AUH for disability increases to $460,133.10.
