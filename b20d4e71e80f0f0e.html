Mixue Opens on 25 de Março Street and Plans First Factory Outside China

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Mixue, the Chinese tea and ice cream chain with scale exceeding McDonald's and Starbucks globally, has opened its first store in Brazil on São Paulo's 25 de Março street and announced plans to build its first manufacturing facility outside China. This expansion marks Mixue's strategic entry into Latin America's largest market and reflects confidence in Brazil's beverage and quick-service restaurant sector. The facility investment signals long-term commitment to the Brazilian market.
