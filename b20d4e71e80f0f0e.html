Mixue arrives at 25 de Março and will open its first factory outside China

24 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Pipeline Valor
Mixue, a Chinese tea and ice cream beverage chain with a market presence larger than McDonald's and Starbucks combined, is expanding into Brazil with its first international factory and retail location on Rua 25 de Março in São Paulo. This marks a significant milestone in the company's international expansion strategy, bringing manufacturing operations outside China for the first time. The opening represents a major retail entry for the brand into one of Latin America's largest consumer markets.
