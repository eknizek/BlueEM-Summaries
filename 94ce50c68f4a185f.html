Investors Are Taking Risk Because the Alternative Is Expensive, Says BlackRock

06 May 2026 · 07:49 PM  ·  BrazilJournal (Brazil)
Francisco Rosemberg, BlackRock's head of wealth management and family office for Latin America, has stated that investors continue to take on portfolio risk despite global geopolitical instability because maintaining purely defensive positions is economically costly. He argues that the current environment of uncertainty actually creates opportunities for capital allocation across diverse markets in the region.
