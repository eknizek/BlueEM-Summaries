# Indecopi Launches Antitrust Investigation Against Five Poultry Companies for Alleged Price-Fixing in Live Chicken Sales

The National Directorate for Investigation and Promotion of Free Competition at Indecopi (DLC) has initiated an administrative sanctioning procedure (PAS) against five companies and three individuals for the alleged commission of an anticompetitive practice to establish coordinated price variations in the live chicken sales market per kilogram in the departments of Lambayeque, La Libertad, Piura, Áncash, and Cajamarca, for events that occurred between February 2020 and March 2021.

This procedure will determine whether the investigated companies coordinated the increase and maintenance of live chicken sale prices per kilogram in various provinces of these departments during the investigated period.

The investigated companies are:

- Avícola Yugoslavia S.A.C.

- Molino La Perla S.A.C.

- Técnica Avícola S.A.

- El Rocío S.A.

- Chimú Agropecuaria S.A.

According to the investigation conducted by the DLC, the coordinations of the alleged agreement would have been conducted through WhatsApp conversations. According to Indecopi, this information would be consistent with sales information reported by the companies during the analyzed periods. Additionally, monitoring compliance with the supposed agreement would have been conducted through telephone calls, WhatsApp messages, and through the companies' own sales representatives.

The combined production of these companies represented more than 80% of total live chicken production in the mentioned departments during 2020 and 2021.

Indecopi clarified that the initiation of an administrative sanctioning procedure is based on the existence of reasonable evidence of an alleged anticompetitive conduct and does not constitute a prejudgment about the outcome of the procedure or the responsibility of those investigated.

Furthermore, in accordance with the Law on Repression of Anticompetitive Conduct, the implicated individuals and legal entities have a period of 30 business days to present their defense arguments.

Indecopi also emphasized that this publication is intended to allow any person with legitimate interest to provide information that contributes to the investigation by contacting the DLC through Indecopi's Information Desk, by email at st-clc@indecopi.gob.pe, or by telephone at (+51) (1) 2247800 - Extension 3101.
