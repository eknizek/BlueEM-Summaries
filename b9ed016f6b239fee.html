'Cheap Blouse Tax' Misses the Mark – It Should Be Called 'Chinese Incentive,' Says Riachuelo CEO

17 April 2026 · 10:26 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's major fashion retailers, including Riachuelo, have criticized the popular term 'cheap blouse tax' (taxa das blusinhas) for being too soft on what they view as a serious threat to domestic industry. The CEOs argue the term should instead be called a 'Chinese incentive' to accurately reflect how tariff and regulatory policies favor low-cost Chinese imports over Brazilian production. The dispute highlights rising protectionist concerns in Brazil's retail and apparel sector amid competition from cheap Chinese goods.
