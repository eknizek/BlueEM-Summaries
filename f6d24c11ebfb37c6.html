Direct employment linked to exports grew 10.5% in February 2026

04 May 2026 · 07:15 PM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Peru's export-related direct employment rose to 1.492 million jobs (annualized basis for March 2025–February 2026), representing a 10.5% increase compared to the prior year-over-year period (March 2024–February 2025). This growth, reported by CIEN-ADEX's Center for Global Economics and Business Research, reflects sustained expansion in Peru's export industries and improved labor market conditions in the trade sector.
