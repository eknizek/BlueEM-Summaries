Decathlon to open three new stores in southern Chile to strengthen sports equipment market leadership

04 May 2026 · 09:11 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Sporting goods retailer Decathlon announced plans to open three new stores in southern Chile—in Temuco, Valdivia, and Osorno—as part of a strategic expansion to consolidate its position as the national leader in sports equipment retail.
