Decathlon to open three new stores in southern Chile to strengthen sporting goods leadership

04 May 2026 · 09:11 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Sporting goods retailer Decathlon announced plans to open three new locations in southern Chile—Temuco, Valdivia, and Osorno—as part of a strategic expansion targeting high-demand regions among both domestic and international consumers. The expansion reinforces Decathlon's ambition to establish itself as the national leader in sports equipment distribution. The company is capitalizing on strong consumer demand in underserved southern territories.
