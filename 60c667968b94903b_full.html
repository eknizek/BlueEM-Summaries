# Gamescom Latam Concludes Sunday with $150 Million in Projected Business Deals

The fair in São Paulo combines game testing, content and business rounds with global participation and 150 thousand visitors

By Bianca Guilherme, Valor — São Paulo

With expectations of 150 thousand visitors, a growth of approximately 15% compared to the 130 thousand registered in 2025, and $150 million in expected business in the corporate area, the third edition of Gamescom Latam 2026 concludes this Sunday (3rd) at the Anhembi District in São Paulo. In addition to the general public, the event brings together, since April 30th, companies and investors around the gaming industry, combining content programming and business rounds.
