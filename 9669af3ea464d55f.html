Court Freezes R$176 Million from Fund Under Investigation in Hidden Carbon Scheme, Managed by Banco Genial

04 May 2026 · 09:04 PM  ·  O Globo
A Brazilian court has issued an asset freeze on R$176 million belonging to a fund under investigation as part of the 'Carbono Oculto' (Hidden Carbon) case, managed by Banco Genial. The action represents a significant regulatory intervention in a major financial fraud investigation, with material implications for the bank's reputation and potential liability. The case highlights risks in carbon credit markets and private fund management in Brazil.
