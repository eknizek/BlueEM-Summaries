Buenos Aires municipality audit reveals only half of road maintenance budget spent

23 April 2026 · 04:29 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
A judicial order has revealed that Baradero, a Buenos Aires province municipality, allocated only $320 million of its $1.1 billion budgeted road maintenance revenue for rural road work. This significant under-execution of infrastructure spending raises questions about municipal fiscal management and capital deployment efficiency. The finding reflects broader concerns about provincial budget execution and infrastructure investment in Argentina.
