São Paulo Court Denies Injunction to Estonian Company in Aviator Game Brand Dispute

20 April 2026 · 09:00 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Brazil's São Paulo Court of Justice denied Spribe Oü's request to suspend use of the Aviator trademark and game elements in Brazil. The Estonian gaming company claims ownership of the trademark and accuses Foggo Entertainment and Aviator Studio Brazil of infringement through blaze.bet.bt. This intellectual property dispute reflects growing litigation in the online gaming sector in Brazil.
