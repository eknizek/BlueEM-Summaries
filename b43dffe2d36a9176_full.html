# Executive Suite: Updates at Carrefour, Ipiranga and More

The shorter week did not slow down the pace of executive movements in companies. Check out who changed positions this holiday week.

Zoetis, a global health company, has just hired Miguel Gallego for the position of Director of Commercial Operations. The executive has worked at Carrefour (Brazil and Colombia), DIA Group, and Ipiranga.

At FlowCredi, the news is the arrival of Rafael Rodrigues Lopes as CFO and Denis Senhorini as Head of New Business.

AXA recruited executive Matheus Fontanelli from Mapfre, who arrives with the mission of leading the insurance company's Cooperatives segment.

Karina Louzada will assume the New York operations of AMG, a specialized agency in positioning major Brazilian brands in the United States, including BB Americas Bank, BEES (AB InBev), and Forno de Minas, among others.

Digital Toll announces the arrival of Rodrigo Zambon as CEO.

At BRQ, the news is the arrival of Silvia Ferreira as new Senior Manager of Alliances and Alexandre Gusson, who assumes the position of Director of Global Alliances.

Fintech Gooroo Crédito registers the arrival of Jonatas Giovinazzo as its new COO.

Those who frequent the exclusive Sweet Secret will now find José Edvandio de Almeida, formerly of Bar des Arts and Coco Bambu, among others. Almeida, as he is better known, will coordinate the entire operation of the establishment, including the teams in the dining room, bar, reception, cashier, and kitchen.

Modec, floating solutions for oil and gas production, has a new CEO in Brazil: Yosuke Kosugi.

With experience at companies such as OLX, Globo Group, and Loft, Guilherme Cirne is the new CTO of Akad Seguros.

Hiker Ventures, the venture capital arm of the Belo Horizonte investment boutique Araújo Fontes, brings Daniel Coimbra as a new partner.

Law firm FAS Advogados expands its Banking & Finance practice with the arrival of partner Thais de Gobbi.

Tere Santos is the new director of People and Management at Tecadi.

Falconi announces Breno Guedes as new vice president of the business unit dedicated to Retail.

Marina de Lima Orsi assumes as Head of Human Resources at Galderma in Brazil.

Rodrigo Peçanha is the new director of the People Management business unit at Flash.

Ana Paula Vitelli is the new director of Columbia Global Center in Rio de Janeiro. With 30 years of experience, the executive will lead one of Columbia University's 11 Global Centers around the world.

Mariana Almeida, formerly of Mattos Filho and McKinsey, has a new address: the Globo building in São Paulo, where she will head institutional relations.

Leonardo Moreira Gomes is the new VP of Marketplaces and Ecosystems at Bemobi.
