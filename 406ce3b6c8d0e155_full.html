# Shortage of Logistics Warehouses in São Paulo

The market for logistics condominiums in São Paulo, the country's largest hub, is experiencing severe scarcity, which could redefine the sector's outlook this year.

According to CBRE data, 58% of the inventory expected to be delivered in 2026 is already pre-leased, reflecting the intensity of demand. "The shortage is already a reality. Clearly there are few options," said Rodrigo Couto, director of industrial & logistics at CBRE Brasil, in an interview with Bloomberg Línea.

The volume of custom-built constructions (a niche known as BTS, in English) has also increased considerably, according to the executive.

In the first quarter, gross absorption of logistics warehouses in the Brazilian market totaled 1.1 million square meters, up 83% compared to the same period in 2025. Net absorption grew 358% on the same basis, reaching 562,000 m².
