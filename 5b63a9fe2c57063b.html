Milei Official Views Bolivia as South American 'Disneyland' If Deregulation Accelerates

04 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Bloomberg Línea
Federico Sturzenegger, Argentina's Minister of Deregulation and State Transformation under President Javier Milei, praised Bolivia's proposed 'Tranca Cero' (Zero Barriers) deregulation initiative, suggesting the country could become a South American tourism hub if the plan succeeds. Sturzenegger's enthusiastic endorsement reflects Argentina's ideological alignment with aggressive deregulation across the region. The Bolivian government's broader liberalization agenda is positioned as a catalyst for economic growth through reduced state intervention.
