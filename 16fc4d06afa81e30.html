Protests against Rappi in north Bogotá: delivery workers denounce arbitrary blocks after SIC fine

20 April 2026 · 09:09 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Delivery workers in northern Bogotá are protesting against Rappi, claiming the platform is arbitrarily suspending their accounts without transparent explanations. The protests follow a fine imposed on Rappi by Colombia's competition regulator (SIC). Delivery partners argue that sudden exclusions from the platform disrupt their livelihoods and lack clear justification or appeal processes.
