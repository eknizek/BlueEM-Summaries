Sagu Chocolates bets on healthy snacks: this line now represents 60% of its sales

13 April 2026 · 11:07 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Colombian chocolate and confectionery company Sagu Chocolates is prioritizing healthy snack product lines, which have become the dominant revenue driver at 60% of total sales. The company projects sales of 2 million units across its portfolio in 2025, reflecting strong market demand for healthier indulgent products. This strategic pivot reflects broader consumer trend toward better-for-you snacking and positions Sagu to capitalize on premiumization and health-conscious purchasing behavior in Colombia and potentially regional markets.
