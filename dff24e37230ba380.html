Sagu Chocolates Bets on Healthy Snacks: Line Now Represents 60% of Sales

13 April 2026 · 11:07 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Sagu Chocolates, a Colombian confectionery firm, is accelerating its pivot toward healthy snack products, which now account for 60% of total sales, and projects commercializing 2 million units in the category this year. The strategic shift reflects consumer demand trends toward better-for-you snacking options in Colombia and the broader Latin American market. This product mix evolution positions Sagu to capture growing health-conscious consumer segments while maintaining revenue scale in competitive chocolate and confectionery markets.
