Colombia Buys Dollars Ahead of $4 Billion Bond Buyback Plan

15 April 2026 · 08:34 PM  ·  Bloomberg.com
Colombia is purchasing US dollars from the market as it prepares to execute a $4 billion bond buyback program aimed at reducing its external debt stock. This strategy allows the Colombian government to reduce debt servicing costs while improving its fiscal position. Bond buybacks are typically executed when a country believes its debt trades at a discount to par value, offering an opportunity to retire debt at a lower cost than the face value of the bonds.
