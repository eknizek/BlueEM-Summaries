B3, XP, and BTG Pactual Launch First Predictive Market Platforms in Brazil

06 April 2026 · 12:28 PM  ·  BNLData
Brazil's primary stock exchange B3, investment platform XP, and investment bank BTG Pactual have jointly launched the country's first predictive market platforms, enabling users to trade contracts based on future outcomes of events. This collaboration represents significant fintech innovation and regulatory progress in Brazil's capital markets. The initiative diversifies trading products available to investors and potentially attracts new market participants. For fintech and capital markets investors, this launch signals Brazil's commitment to modernizing its financial infrastructure and competitive positioning in LatAm.
