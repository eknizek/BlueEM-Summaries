Central Highway in critical condition: Ositrán highlights structural issues on 21 bridges

20 April 2026 · 07:48 PM  ·  Gestion (Peru) - Economia
Peru's transport infrastructure regulator (Ositrán) has identified 21 bridges on the Central Highway (IIRSA Centro – Tramo 2) as structurally critical due to escalating stress from high-tonnage vehicle traffic. Heavy goods vehicles are significantly increasing structural demand on these assets. The alert signals urgent maintenance and investment needs for this critical transport corridor. This has implications for the concessionaire's obligations and potential capex requirements, as well as broader infrastructure risk in Peru's transport network.
