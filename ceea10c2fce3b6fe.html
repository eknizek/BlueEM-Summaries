Rappi issues stern warning to delivery workers after protests: 'platform safety is non-negotiable'

20 April 2026 · 09:46 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Negocios
Rappi has issued a formal statement to its delivery workforce emphasizing that platform safety standards are not subject to negotiation, amid ongoing protests from couriers. Delivery workers have denounced arbitrary account blocks and suspensions, claiming the company provides insufficient explanation for account terminations. The company says it maintains open dialogue channels with its delivery partners. This dispute follows a recent SIC (Colombian competition authority) fine against the platform.
