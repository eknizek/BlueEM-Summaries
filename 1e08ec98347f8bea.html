Trump Administration Submits 80 Questions to Brazil on Tariffs

02 May 2026 · 11:00 AM  ·  Valor Economico
The Trump administration has submitted a detailed 80-question questionnaire to Brazil as part of its tariff review process. The questions are designed to help U.S. officials determine whether additional tariffs will be imposed on Brazilian goods and exports. This inquiry reflects the administration's protectionist trade stance and represents a significant development in U.S.-Brazil trade relations, with potential implications for Brazilian exporters across multiple sectors.
