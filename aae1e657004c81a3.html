Due to imports, industrial and consumer companies earn less, but energy companies' margins rose

04 May 2026 · 10:02 PM  ·  Clarin
Argentina's energy sector, particularly oil companies benefiting from Vaca Muerta development, saw margin expansion in 2025, while industrial and consumer companies faced compressed profitability at decade-low levels. Non-energy firms avoided full pass-through of tariff increases to preserve sales volumes amid import pressures. This divergence reflects the structural impact of energy investment cycles and pricing dynamics on corporate margins across sectors.
