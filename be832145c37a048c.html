Halliburton wins multi-billion-dollar YPF fracking contract

13 April 2026 · 05:30 PM  ·  Buenos Aires Times (Argentina)
Halliburton, the global oilfield services giant, has secured a multi-billion-dollar contract with YPF, Argentina's state-owned petroleum company, for hydraulic fracturing and related unconventional oil development services. The award reflects YPF's aggressive development of Argentina's substantial shale reserves, particularly in the Vaca Muerta formation. This contract deployment represents a significant capital commitment to unconventional hydrocarbon extraction in Latin America's second-largest economy. The engagement underscores Argentina's strategic reliance on international expertise and investment to monetize its hydrocarbon assets.
