Colombia proposes exempting low-displacement motorcycles from mandatory insurance (Soat)

07 May 2026 · 10:05 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia is considering a policy change to exempt low-displacement motorcycles from mandatory Soat (vehicle insurance) requirements in a market growing at over 37% annually. While intended to reduce costs for lower-income riders, the proposal raises concerns about road safety implications and the financial sustainability of the health system, which relies partly on Soat revenue for accident coverage. The initiative reflects a policy tension between affordability and risk management.
