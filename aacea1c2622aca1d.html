CAF, CABEI sign new exposure exchange deal

19 April 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  LatinFinance
The Andean Development Corporation (CAF) and Central American Bank for Economic Integration (CABEI) have signed a second exposure exchange agreement, a financial mechanism that allows development banks to exchange credit exposures and strengthen their balance sheets. These agreements help both institutions diversify concentration risk, optimize capital management, and increase lending capacity across their member countries in Latin America. Such arrangements are increasingly common among multilateral development institutions seeking to enhance financial flexibility.
