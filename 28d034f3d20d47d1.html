Food industry alerts about 'lack of scientific rigor' in new labeling project

25 April 2026 · 08:08 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's food industry chamber (Cámara de la Industria de Alimentos de la Andi) has raised concerns about a new labeling regulation proposal, citing insufficient scientific evidence, potential economic impacts, and risks of creating barriers to trade. The industry group argues the measure lacks proper technical foundation and could disadvantage Colombian food manufacturers. This regulatory dispute has implications for food and beverage companies operating in Colombia and investors in consumer goods exposure to the region.
