Luis Carlos Sarmiento Angulo urges presidential candidates to develop plans for Altillanura region

27 April 2026 · 04:34 PM  ·  Portfolio - Negocios
Luis Carlos Sarmiento Angulo, founder of Grupo Aval, has called on Colombian presidential candidates to propose comprehensive development plans for the Altillanura region, which comprises 53 million hectares suitable for food production. The appeal reflects interest in unlocking agricultural and economic potential in this underdeveloped area. Sarmiento's intervention signals business sector focus on rural development as a policy priority for the next administration.
