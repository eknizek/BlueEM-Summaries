Nuevo León agricultural production falls 11% in 2025 amid drought and profitability challenges

18 April 2026 · 11:01 AM

In 2025, the total agricultural production of the state amounted to 670,616 tons, a decline of 11.1% year-over-year associated with drought and lack of profitability, with declines in orange and forage grain harvests, although increases in walnuts and potatoes, according to an analysis by the Nuevo León Agricultural Council (CEANL).

The Agricultural Markets Consulting Group (GCMA) warned that 2026 could be even more challenging for the agricultural sector.
