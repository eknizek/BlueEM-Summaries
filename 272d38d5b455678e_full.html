# Agricultural Production in Nuevo León Falls 11% in 2025 Due to Drought and Low Profitability

Monterrey, Mexico (April 18, 2026) — 05:00 hrs

In 2025, the total agricultural production of the state reached 670,616 tons, a decline of 11.1% annually associated with drought and lack of profitability, with decreases in orange and forage grain harvests, although with increases in walnuts and potatoes, according to an analysis by the Nuevo León Agricultural Council (CEANL).

The Agricultural Market Consulting Group (GCMA) warned that 2026 could be even more challenging for the agricultural sector.
