Cruz Verde to stop dispensing medicines to Sanitas affiliates starting October 2026 nationwide

12 April 2026 · 02:15 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Cruz Verde, a major pharmacy chain in Colombia, will cease dispensing medications to Sanitas health insurance affiliates beginning October 2026, affecting the entire country. This decision creates operational challenges for Sanitas (the health plan provider) requiring it to reorganize its pharmaceutical distribution network and ensure continuity of medication access for millions of insured patients. The move signals potential shifts in the healthcare supply chain partnerships and may impact both companies' financial performance and market positioning in Colombia's healthcare sector.
