Uber expands to 1,100 municipalities across Colombia with Taxi, Economy and Moto services

14 April 2026 · 11:19 PM  ·  Portfolio (Colombia) - Empresas
Uber is significantly expanding its presence in Colombia, announcing plans to reach more than 1,100 municipalities across all 32 departments of the country. The expansion will include Uber Taxi, Uber Economy, and Uber Moto service options, bringing ride-hailing services to smaller cities and rural areas that previously lacked access to the platform. This aggressive expansion represents a major market penetration strategy for the company in Colombia's transportation sector.
