# Copasa Contract with Belo Horizonte Worth R$1.3 Billion to City with Contested Rate Rules

The City Hall of Belo Horizonte and Copasa, the Minas Gerais state sanitation company, finalized the renewal of the water and sewage services concession contract at the end of March with a clause that provides for a billion-real transfer to the capital. The funds can be used freely by the administration of Álvaro Damião (União Brasil) and will be borne by consumers through the tariff.

By Thiago Bethônico, Folhapress — São Paulo
