Infinix Announces NOTE 60 Pro 5G and NOTE Edge 5G Smartphones in Colombia with AI Focus

24 April 2026 · 04:00 PM

# Infinix Announces NOTE 60 Pro 5G and NOTE Edge 5G Smartphones in Colombia: Prices, Availability and Features

Infinix announced the arrival in the Colombian market of the NOTE 60 Pro 5G and NOTE Edge 5G models, two devices designed to respond to different usage patterns within the current digital environment. According to the brand's statement, the new equipment is based on a market analysis in which the mobile phone performs functions that go beyond communication, integrating into activities such as work, entertainment, learning, and digital transactions.

The company points out that its proposal focuses on addressing needs associated with artificial intelligence, personalization, and user experience—elements that influence purchasing decisions beyond technical performance.

## NOTE 60 Pro 5G Features

The NOTE 60 Pro is aimed at users who prioritize performance, particularly for gaming and multitasking. The device incorporates the Snapdragon 7s Gen 4 processor and a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with 1.5K resolution, 144Hz refresh rate, and brightness up to 4500 nits.

Among its main specifications are included:

- 6000 mAh battery with 90W fast charging

- VC 3D IceCore cooling system

- Dual speakers with JBL technology

- Customizable LED rear interface (Active Matrix Display)

- Integration with Call of Duty: Mobile for optimized gaming experience

## NOTE Edge 5G Proposal

The NOTE Edge 5G is oriented toward users seeking a simpler experience centered on design. It features a 10-bit curved AMOLED display with 1.5K resolution and brightness up to 4500 nits. The device integrates artificial intelligence-based tools, including:

- Folax AI

- SnapMemo Live Photo

These functions are designed to facilitate daily tasks and simplify device interaction.

## Availability in Colombia

According to the company, both devices are already available in the country through chains such as Alkosto, Éxito, Falabella, Olímpica, Metro, Jumbo, and the Wom operator. Launch prices are:

- NOTE 60 Pro 5G: $2,299,900 pesos

- NOTE Edge 5G: $1,151,900 pesos
