Movistar Inaugurates Its Multiteater: Cultural Space to Offer Varied Programming with Simultaneous Shows

23 April 2026 · 08:29 PM

Movistar announced the inauguration of the "Movistar Multiteater," a cultural space that will offer varied programming with simultaneous shows.

The venue has three theaters that will operate simultaneously, allowing for a diverse lineup that includes comedy, theater, and contemporary cultural offerings, bringing live entertainment experiences to more people.

As part of this initiative, Movistar customers will access exclusive benefits through Club Movistar.

During the first month, double entry tickets will be raffled off so customers can attend the different shows, in addition to offering special discounts on ticket purchases.

The company highlighted that it continues expanding its benefits offerings, incorporating new entertainment experiences that complement its value proposition and strengthen its relationship with its customers.
