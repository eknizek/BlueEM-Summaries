Movistar opens Multiteatro: cultural space offering varied programming with simultaneous performances

23 April 2026 · 08:29 PM

Movistar announced the inauguration of "Multiteatro Movistar," a cultural space that will offer varied programming with simultaneous performances.

The venue has three rooms that will operate simultaneously, allowing for a diverse lineup that includes comedy, theater, and contemporary cultural proposals, bringing this type of live entertainment experience to more people.

As part of this initiative, Movistar customers will have access to exclusive benefits through Club Movistar.

Movistar indicated that during the first month, double tickets will be raffled off so customers can attend the different performances, in addition to offering special discounts on ticket purchases.

The company highlighted that it continues to expand its benefits offering, incorporating new entertainment experiences that complement its value proposition and strengthen its relationship with customers.
