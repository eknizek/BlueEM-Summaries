Controversy over proposal to eliminate SOAT insurance requirement for motorcycles up to 250 cc in Colombia

07 May 2026 · 10:03 AM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
A proposed regulatory change in Colombia seeks to eliminate SOAT (mandatory third-party liability insurance) requirements for motorcycles with engines of 250 cubic centimeters or less. The proposal has generated significant controversy, with stakeholders likely divided between cost-saving proponents and insurance industry and road safety advocates. This regulatory debate directly impacts the Colombian insurance sector and motorcycle market.
