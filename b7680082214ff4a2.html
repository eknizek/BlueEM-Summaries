Colombia Surges in Blueberry Production, but 95% Stays Domestic and Slows Export Growth

04 May 2026 · 05:16 PM  ·  El Tiempo - Economia
Colombia's blueberry production sector achieved 12,000 metric tons in 2025, demonstrating significant domestic output growth, yet the industry faces an export constraint with 95% of production consumed locally. The sector plans to expand cultivated area by approximately 20% in 2026, suggesting confidence in future demand, though current supply-chain bottlenecks and market development limit international sales. This represents a growth opportunity for agribusiness investors focused on diversifying Colombia's agricultural exports.
