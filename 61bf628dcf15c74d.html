In Germany, Lula Criticizes 'False Narratives' About Brazilian Agribusiness

19 April 2026 · 08:23 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
At the Hannover Messe industrial fair in Germany, Brazilian President Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva criticized the European Union for promoting what he describes as false narratives about Brazilian agribusiness. The statement reflects ongoing tension between Brazil's agricultural sector and international environmental and sustainability scrutiny. Lula's defense of agribusiness at a major global industrial platform underscores the sector's economic importance to Brazil and the geopolitical dimension of trade relations. The comments are significant given agribusiness comprises a major share of Brazil's exports and the EU's role as a key trading partner.
