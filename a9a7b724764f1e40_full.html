# Eztec Launches Grow 50% and Sales Jump 85% in Q1

The developer Eztec reported four new launches in the first quarter of this year, with a potential gross sales value (VGV) of R$ 924.7 million, an increase of 50.1% over the same period in 2025. Of the launches in the period, 67% were upper-middle standard, 17% middle standard, and 16% from the economic segment.

Net sales for the company rose 84.8% in this interval, to R$ 696.8 million. Eztec highlights that this is the largest value ever recorded by the company in a quarter.

The sales velocity, measured by the sales-to-offer indicator (VSO), rose 6.1 percentage points year-over-year, to 18.2%. The VSO for the last 12 months grew 2.7 points, to 42%.

Eztec recorded R$ 63 million in cancellations, an increase of 71.1% over the first quarter of last year. The proportion of cancellations relative to gross sales fell 0.6 points, to 8.3%.

The developer ended March with an inventory valued at R$ 3.1 billion, a growth of 14.1% year-over-year. Of this total, 36.2% refer to units already completed.
