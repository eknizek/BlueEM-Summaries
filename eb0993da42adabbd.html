Mexico and US Add Intellectual Property to USMCA Technical Talks Agenda

21 April 2026 · 11:23 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
In the second technical meeting ahead of formal USMCA renegotiation proceedings, Mexico and the United States have added intellectual property (IP) rights to the discussion agenda. This represents an expansion of negotiating scope beyond initial topics and reflects strategic interests around patent, trademark, and copyright protection. The move has implications for pharma, biotech, technology, and agribusiness sectors operating under the trade agreement.
