# Mexico tourism receipts hit lowest level in 28 months

During 2026, 8 million 12 thousand 213 international travelers entered Mexico, which represented 8.5 percent more than in the same month of 2025.

In the second month of this year, the annual growth rate of tourism receipts moderated to its lowest figure in 28 months, while that of expenditures accelerated and recorded its best relative variation in a year.
