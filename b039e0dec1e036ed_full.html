Mexico Unemployment Rate Rises to 2.8% in March

24 April 2026 · 04:12 PM

The Unemployment Rate in the country (UR) reported in March was the highest in 27 months, registering a monthly increase of 0.08 percentage points to 2.80 percent of the Economically Active Population (EAP) and marked its third consecutive month of growth.

The unemployed population is defined as those who did not work even one hour during the survey's reference week, but expressed their willingness to do so.
