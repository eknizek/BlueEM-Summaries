# Qualitas and the future of vehicle insurance: prevention, innovation and expansion

The automotive insurance industry is going through a decisive moment. Between the growth of the vehicle fleet, the increase in claims and technological evolution, asset protection stopped being an optional decision to become a key element of financial stability. In this context, Qualitas reaffirms its role as a sector leader, promoting a vision that combines expansion, innovation and financial education.

Currently, the company protects 6.1 million units, which represents nearly a third of the insured market in the country. This data not only reflects leadership, but also evidences the enormous pending challenge: the low penetration of vehicle insurance. In Mexico, barely one out of every three vehicles has protection, a gap that implies economic and social risks for millions of families.

Qualitas' sustained growth confirms market confidence. In recent years, it doubled in size and achieved an increase close to 9.4% in premiums during the last reported period. The incorporation of 350,000 new units demonstrates that asset protection is increasingly positioned as a priority for drivers.

## Financial education as an engine of prevention

Low insurance penetration is not just a statistic: it is a cultural challenge. Financial education and road safety culture are consolidated as strategic pillars to reduce risks and generate social awareness. The reality is clear: an accident can mean million-dollar losses, while a policy represents an accessible preventive investment against the economic impact of a claim.

Qualitas insists that insurance is not only a financial tool, but a social responsibility. Traffic accidents, the exponential growth in motorcycle use and extreme weather events have increased the frequency and severity of claims. In this scenario, prevention becomes the best strategy: the best accident is the one that never happens.

Furthermore, the brand has promoted constant road safety campaigns and training, focused on reducing risks from the root. The combination of training, technology and data analysis makes it possible to anticipate trends and strengthen safety on highways and cities.

## Technology, expansion and human service

Digital transformation is redefining the insurance experience. Thanks to data analysis and artificial intelligence, Qualitas can anticipate risks, optimize claims adjuster positioning and expedite service. Today, approximately 30% of claims are handled remotely, with processes that can be resolved in approximately 20 minutes and high satisfaction levels.

However, technological innovation does not replace the human component. Insurance remains a promise of trust, where empathy and personalized attention are fundamental in critical moments. Technology becomes a tool that enhances service, not a replacement for closeness with the customer.

The growth vision also transcends borders. Qualitas' international presence continues to expand, consolidating its service model in new markets and strengthening its value proposition globally.

In a country with millions of vehicles without protection, the message is forceful: having insurance is an intelligent, responsible and necessary decision. The combination of prevention, innovation and human service sets the course for an industry that evolves to respond to the challenges of the present and the future.
