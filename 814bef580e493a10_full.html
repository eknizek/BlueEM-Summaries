# Quálitas and the Future of Vehicle Insurance: Prevention, Innovation, and Expansion

Quálitas reaffirms its role as a sector benchmark, driving a vision that combines expansion, innovation, and financial education. The automotive insurance industry is at a critical juncture. Amid the growth of the vehicle fleet, increased claims frequency, and technological evolution, patrimony protection has ceased to be an optional decision and has become a key element of financial stability. In this context, Quálitas reaffirms its role as a sector benchmark, promoting a vision that combines expansion, innovation, and financial education.

Currently, the company protects 6.1 million vehicles, representing nearly one-third of the insured market in the country. This figure not only reflects leadership but also evidences the enormous remaining challenge: low vehicle insurance penetration. In Mexico, barely one in three vehicles has protection, a gap that implies economic and social risks for millions of families.

Quálitas's sustained growth confirms market confidence. In recent years, it has doubled in size and achieved an increase of approximately 9.4% in premiums during the last reported period. The addition of 350,000 new units demonstrates that patrimony protection is increasingly positioned as a priority for drivers.

**Financial Education as an Engine for Prevention**

Low insurance penetration is not merely a statistic: it is a cultural challenge. Financial education and road safety culture are consolidated as strategic pillars for reducing risks and generating social awareness. The reality is clear: an accident can mean million-dollar losses, while a policy represents an accessible preventive investment against the economic impact of a claim.

Quálitas insists that insurance is not solely a financial tool but a social responsibility. Traffic accidents, the exponential growth in motorcycle use, and extreme weather events have increased the frequency and severity of claims. In this scenario, prevention becomes the best strategy: the best accident is one that never happens. Additionally, the brand has promoted constant road safety campaigns and training, focused on reducing risks from the root. The combination of training, technology, and data analysis allows for anticipating trends and strengthening safety on roads and in cities.

**Technology, Expansion, and Human Service**

Digital transformation is redefining the insurance experience. Thanks to data analysis and artificial intelligence, Quálitas can anticipate risks, optimize adjuster placement, and expedite service. Today, approximately 30% of claims are handled remotely, with processes that can be resolved in approximately 20 minutes and high satisfaction levels. However, technological innovation does not replace the human component. Insurance remains a promise of trust, where empathy and personalized attention are fundamental in critical moments. Technology becomes a tool that enhances service, not a replacement for customer proximity.

The growth vision also transcends borders. Quálitas's international presence continues to expand, consolidating its service model in new markets and reinforcing its value proposition globally.

In a country with millions of unprotected vehicles, the message is forceful: having insurance is an intelligent, responsible, and necessary decision. The combination of prevention, innovation, and human service sets the course for an industry that evolves to respond to the challenges of the present and the future.
