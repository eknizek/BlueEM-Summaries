Diesel Price Falls R$0.20 Per Liter in Two Weeks

27 April 2026 · 10:05 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
Diesel S-10 prices at Brazilian fuel pumps fell for the second consecutive week after hitting the year's peak in early April, according to ANP (National Agency of Petroleum, Gas and Biofuels) weekly data. The price correction of R$0.20 per liter reduces input costs for transportation and logistics operators. Commodity price movements affect margins for fuel-intensive sectors and influence inflation dynamics in Brazil's economy.
