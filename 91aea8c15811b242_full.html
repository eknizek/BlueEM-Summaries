A Gracie to lift Oncoclínicas off the mat

A Gracie has been part of Oncoclínicas' fight to avoid hitting the mat and declaring financial and operational defeat. The company's crisis, as is already known, has attracted MAK Capital, a New York-based asset manager that may be able to place names on Oncoclínicas' board at Thursday's shareholders meeting. One of the analysts who has participated in discussions about MAK's new investment bid, according to sources, is Brazilian Ronis Gracie. In addition to gold and silver medals in jiu-jitsu competitions, Ronis has experience in venture capital and private equity and joined MAK just over a year ago.

By Maria Luíza Filgueiras — São Paulo

April 29, 2026

Ronis Gracie is an analyst at MAK Capital in New York.
