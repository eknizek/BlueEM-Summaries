Electricity Bills in Aysén to Drop 14% Following Tariff Coupling Implementation

20 April 2026 · 11:55 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
The Chilean National Energy Commission (CNE) confirmed a 14% reduction in electricity bills for the Aysén region following implementation of tariff coupling (acople tarifario) measures. This regulatory decision was documented in the CNE's preliminary technical report and aims to reduce consumer energy costs through improved rate structure alignment. The reduction benefits households and businesses in the southern region.
