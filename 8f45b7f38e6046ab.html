Understanding the Prediction Market, Target of Brazilian Government Blockade

24 April 2026 · 08:42 PM  ·  Folha de Sao Paulo
The Brazilian federal government blocked access to prediction market platforms including Kalshi and Polymarket on Friday, April 24, 2026. These platforms allow users to bet on election outcomes, sporting events, reality shows, and celebrity news. The blockade reflects government concerns about unregulated financial speculation and potential market manipulation, with implications for fintech regulation and digital asset markets in Brazil.
