'Amendment 9 is legal and the TUUA does not affect competitiveness': LAP and MTC respond to airline associations in administrative litigation

09 May 2026 · 11:08 AM  ·  El Comercio - Economia
Lima Airport Partners (LAP) and Peru's Ministry of Transportation (MTC) have responded to airline industry associations challenging Amendment 9 to the airport concession contract and the TUUA (unified tariff) framework. Both entities maintain that the contractual modification is legally sound and assert that the airport continues expanding international connections without competitive harm. The dispute has escalated to administrative litigation as airline groups seek judicial intervention against the airport operator's tariff and operational decisions.
