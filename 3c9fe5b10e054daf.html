Spanish Construction Firm Halts Key Works in Concepción, Citing Financial Difficulties as Authorities Evaluate Contract Liquidation

29 April 2026 · 11:17 PM  ·  Diario Financiero (Chile)
Tapusa, a Spanish construction company, has halted critical infrastructure projects in Chile's Concepción region, citing financial difficulties. Regional authorities held an emergency meeting establishing May 4 as a deadline for the company to present a work recovery plan. The suspended projects include strategically important infrastructure for regional connectivity, including Esmeralda Bridge, Colón Axis, and Perales Bridge. This situation has direct implications for Chile's infrastructure sector and investor confidence.
