Spanish Construction Firm With Stalled Key Works in Concepción Facing Financial Difficulties; Authorities Evaluate Contract Liquidation

29 April 2026 · 11:17 PM  ·  Diario Financiero
Tapusa, a Spanish construction firm, is experiencing severe financial difficulties with major infrastructure projects halted in Concepción, Chile. The stalled projects include critical regional infrastructure such as Puente Esmeralda, Eje Colón, and Puente Perales—essential for regional connectivity.
