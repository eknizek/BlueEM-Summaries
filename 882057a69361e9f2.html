Pluspetrol announces $12 billion investment program in Vaca Muerta field acquired from Exxon

23 April 2026 · 08:51 PM  ·  Clarin (Argentina)
Pluspetrol, Argentina's fourth-largest oil operator after YPF, PAE, and Vista, has announced a $12 billion investment program for the Vaca Muerta shale field acquired from ExxonMobil. The company aims to reach 100,000 barrels per day of production by 2029, leveraging Argentina's RIGI tax incentive regime. This major capital commitment reflects confidence in Vaca Muerta's long-term potential and Argentina's pro-investment policy framework.
