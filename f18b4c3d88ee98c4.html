Chile Targets Faster Mining Approvals to Unlock $100 Billion Investment Pipeline

20 April 2026 · 04:17 PM  ·  Reuters
Chile is implementing measures to streamline and accelerate mining project approvals, aiming to unlock approximately $100 billion in stalled investment pipelines. The initiative addresses regulatory bottlenecks that have constrained mineral sector expansion, particularly critical for lithium and copper projects. Faster approvals could significantly boost foreign direct investment and export revenues in Chile's largest economic sector.
