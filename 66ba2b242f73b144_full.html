BB Seguridade reports 11% higher management profit in Q1 at R$2.2 billion

04 May 2026 · 09:58 PM

BB Seguridade had management profit of R$2.2 billion in the first quarter of this year, representing growth of 11% compared to the same period in 2025. The company attributed the performance to factors such as reduced claims ratio in insurance and improved financial results in the group's pension, brokerage, and capitalization companies.
