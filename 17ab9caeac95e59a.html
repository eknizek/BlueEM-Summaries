Santander fintech Ebury raises £550M to accelerate global expansion

03 May 2026 · 12:00 PM  ·  Startups.com.br
Ebury, a fintech majority owned by Santander (55% stake) and backed by the Spanish bank with an additional £50 million investment, has raised £550 million to fund global expansion. The company plans to increase its presence serving small and medium-sized enterprises and to enter the Brazilian market more aggressively. This capital raise demonstrates strong institutional backing and confidence in Ebury's cross-border payment and trade finance platform.
