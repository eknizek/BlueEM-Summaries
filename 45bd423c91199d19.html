Correios losses more than triple to R$8.5bn in 2025

24 April 2026 · 11:33 AM  ·  O Globo (Brazil)
Correios, Brazil's state-owned postal and logistics company, has reported losses that more than tripled to R$8.5 billion in 2025. This deteriorating financial performance raises questions about the company's operational efficiency, competitive positioning against private logistics providers, and potential need for restructuring or government support. The losses reflect ongoing challenges in the postal sector amid e-commerce growth and changing mail volumes.
