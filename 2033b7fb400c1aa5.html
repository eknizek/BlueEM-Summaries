Federico Sturzenegger Confirms Real Estate Deregulation Bill for Congress in June

02 May 2026 · 10:40 PM  ·  La Nación (Argentina)
Federico Sturzenegger, Argentina's Economy Minister, announced that in June he will submit real estate deregulation reforms to Congress aimed at lowering property transaction costs and improving market efficiency. This initiative is part of the government's broader liberalisation agenda designed to reduce friction in the housing market and attract investment. The regulatory change could materially impact real estate values and transaction volumes.
