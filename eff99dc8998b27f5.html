Indecopi Imposes Definitive Antidumping Rights on Chinese Steel Wire Rod Imports

13 April 2026 · 07:44 PM  ·  El Comercio (Peru)
Peru's Indecopi announced definitive antidumping duties of US$81.3 per ton on steel wire rod imports from China, effective for five years. The measure aims to protect fair competition in Peru's domestic steel market against alleged dumping practices. The decision represents a protectionist trade policy response to Chinese import competition in the metals sector. This regulatory action may reshape supply chains and pricing for steel inputs in Peru and potentially affect regional trade dynamics.
