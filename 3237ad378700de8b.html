SENA Student Develops AI Tool to Facilitate Access to Energy Regulation in Colombia

20 April 2026 · 06:46 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Tech
A student from Colombia's SENA (National Learning Service) has developed an artificial intelligence tool designed to simplify access to and understanding of energy sector regulations in Colombia. The project is supported by Klik Energy and Google Cloud, reflecting collaboration between educational institutions and private tech companies. The initiative aims to reduce operational costs and improve decision-making processes for energy companies by automating regulatory compliance and information management. This demonstrates how AI innovation in emerging markets can address sector-specific regulatory challenges.
