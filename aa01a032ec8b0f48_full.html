# According to Osiptel complaint handling rankings, Claro Peru users receive best sector service

Claro Perú obtained the highest rating in the sector in the Performance Ranking for Public Mobile Service Complaint Handling, prepared by Osiptel, corresponding to the second half of 2025. With this, it consolidates sustained performance in managing user requests and maintains leadership in this ranking for four consecutive semesters (semesters I and II of 2024, and I and II of 2025).

The company achieved 18.1 points, maintaining first place in the ranking and improving its own result compared to the previous semester, when it registered 17.5 points. The indicator evaluates how operating companies handle and resolve complaints filed by mobile service users.

This result gains relevance in a context of general sector improvement. The national global indicator exceeds for the first time the minimum threshold established by the regulator and reaches its highest level (15.1) since this ranking was published in 2019. In this scenario, Claro Perú stood out for maintaining consistent management oriented toward effective complaint resolution.

The Osiptel ranking considers six variables linked to service quality: complaint incidence, anticipated solutions, resolution justification, response speed, founded appeals, and founded complaints. In this analysis, Claro obtained favorable results in five of the six evaluated indicators, demonstrating balanced performance in different aspects of the customer service process.

According to the company, this performance stems from a user-focused strategy that seeks to facilitate access to solutions through various channels and offer clearer and more timely responses to any inconvenience. The goal is to accompany customers throughout the service process and reinforce trust at each brand touchpoint.

The improvement in results also reflects the implementation of more efficient processes and the development of self-management tools that allow user needs to be addressed more agilely, in line with a vision that prioritizes customer experience.

During 2025, Claro concentrated less than 10% of total complaints reported in the public mobile service in the country. Beyond the figure, leadership in the ranking demonstrates its ability to manage these requests under higher service standards.

With this performance, Claro consolidates management focused on generating greater confidence among users, backed by objective indicators and regulatory evaluations. The result reflects attention oriented toward effective complaint resolution and continuous improvement of customer experience in a key sector such as telecommunications.
