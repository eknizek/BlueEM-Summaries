Mexico and US Complete Preparatory Phase for T-MEC Trade Agreement Review

20 April 2026 · 09:05 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Mexico's Secretary of Economy Marcelo Ebrard announced that Mexico and the United States have completed the preparation phase leading up to the next formal review of the T-MEC (USMCA) trade agreement. This milestone signals movement toward substantive negotiation and potential renegotiation of key trade terms affecting North American commerce, manufacturing, agriculture, and labor standards. The review process has material implications for Mexican exporters, cross-border investment, supply chains, and the competitive position of Mexican industries across sectors.
