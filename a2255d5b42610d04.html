Government is indebting Colombia at rates double those paid to the IMF

24 April 2026 · 11:56 PM  ·  El Tiempo (Colombia) - Economia
Colombian government debt issuance is occurring at interest rates approximately double those previously negotiated with the IMF, according to expert analysis. Experts estimate the loss of access to preferential IMF rates generates an annual cost of up to 2 trillion pesos. This shift reflects Colombia's deteriorating fiscal position and reduced creditworthiness in capital markets. The higher borrowing costs have direct implications for fiscal sustainability and the government's debt service capacity.
