Mexican airline sector sees marginal positive impact from World Cup hosting

20 April 2026 · 11:15 PM  ·  Reforma (Mexico) - Portada
Valores Mexicanos Casa de Bolsa forecasts that Mexico's airline sector will experience a modestly positive economic impact from hosting the FIFA World Cup. While the event will drive additional passenger traffic and tourism, the benefit is expected to be marginal given the sector's size relative to the overall economy. Airlines may see temporary boosts in domestic and international route demand, though longer-term impact will depend on broader travel trends.
