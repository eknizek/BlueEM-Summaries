Banco Itaú Chile and Its New York Branch Upgraded to A- by S&P Global Ratings

29 April 2026 · 07:54 PM  ·  GlobeNewswire
S&P Global Ratings has upgraded the credit rating of Banco Itaú Chile and its New York Branch to A-, signaling improved financial strength and creditworthiness. Itaú Chile, a subsidiary of Brazil's Itaú Unibanco, is a major commercial bank in Chile offering retail and institutional banking services. The upgrade reflects solid capital positions, earnings quality, and operational performance in the Chilean market.
