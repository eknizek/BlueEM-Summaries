# Energy Transition with Renewable Sources Recommended to Strengthen Mexico's Competitiveness

An energy diversification plan would allow Mexico to avoid the risks of blackouts and loss of competitiveness, according to a study by Vestas, the global leader in wind energy solutions.

Vestas indicates that 58 percent of the electricity in Mexico is consumed by the industrial sector.
