CMN Reduces Interest Rates and Extends Payment Terms for Reforma Casa Brasil Program

05 May 2026 · 11:07 PM  ·  Valor Economico
The National Monetary Council (CMN) has approved measures to reduce interest rates and extend payment deadlines for the Reforma Casa Brasil program, which finances residential improvements. The policy adjustment is designed to increase accessibility and affordability for Brazilian homeowners. This development is relevant for construction suppliers, building materials companies, mortgage lenders, and real estate investors operating in Brazil's residential sector.
