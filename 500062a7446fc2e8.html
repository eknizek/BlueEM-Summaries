In Defense of Brazil's Achievements: Opposition to Ending Tax Exemption on Foreign E-Commerce Platforms

16 April 2026 · 06:05 PM  ·  O GLOBO
Brazil is considering ending the "taxa das blusinhas" (tax exemption on low-value imports from foreign e-commerce platforms), a long-standing policy that has shielded cross-border e-commerce from tariffs. The debate centers on protecting domestic retail and generating government revenue while managing trade implications. This regulatory change would materially impact foreign e-commerce operators and domestic retailers competing in the Brazilian market.
